Gratin dauphinoise

Leeks (optional)

  1. Cook gently until soft, do not brown
    • olive oil
    • 1 leek thinly sliced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 clove garlic grated

Gratin

  1. Bring to simmer
    • 250ml double cream
    • 100ml milk
    • pinch nutmeg grated
    • seasoning
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins until slightly softened
    • 750g potatoes peeled and sliced (weight after peeling)
  3. Transfer into buttered oven dish arranging potatoes in layers (if including leeks then add leek layer from half way up)
  4. Cover with foil and bake at 160°C for 30 mins
  5. Remove foil, scatter cheese then bake for 10-15 mins until browned
    • 50g Gruyere grated
  6. Leave to cool before serving

Serving

  • salmon & watercress / peas

Notes

  • Fry garlic with leeks if using
  • Could modify ratios so less heavy, e.g. 200ml cream, 150ml milk
  • use waxy potatoes: Charlottes, desiree, estima, maris peer
  • Original recipe
  • Salmon, watercress, gratin
