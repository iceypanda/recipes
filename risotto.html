Risotto

  1. Heat 1.1l of stock
  2. In a separate pan heat
    • 2 tbsp of olive oil
    • 1 knob of butter
  3. Add and fry very slowly for 15 mins without colouring
    • 1 large onion
    • 2 cloves of chopped garlic
    • ½ head of celery
  4. When vegetables have softened turn up heat and add 400g risotto rice and keep stirring
  5. After 1 min when the rice is translucent add 2 glasses of dry white wine
  6. Once the wine has cooked into the rice turn down the heat to a simmer
  7. Keep adding ladles of stock to the rice stirring and allowing each ladle to absorb into the rice before adding the next
  8. Add seasoning and chopped veg, e.g. asparagus at start, peas later to cook in the stock
  9. Keep adding stock until the rice is soft but with a slight bite
  10. Remove from heat and add and stir in
    • 1 knob of butter
    • 90g grated parmesan
  11. Cover pan and leave for 2 mins
