Eggs

Recipes

  • Menemen
  • Custard

Notes

  • Eggs separate better when cold, whisk better when room temperature
  • Old eggs float air space up in water
  • Freeze whites not yolks
  • Use fresh eggs at room temperature, so yolks are more central
  • Use brush to remove egg shell pieces when cracking

Leftovers

  • Whites: freeze for meringue
  • Yolks: fridge for omelette, mayonnaise, custard

Weights

  • Medium: 53-63g = 58g
  • Large: 63-73g = 68g
    • 35g white (52%)
    • 17g yolk (25%)
  • Smaller eggs have larger yolk:white ratio

Serve with

  • Wholemeal / sourdough toast
  • Smoked salmon / sauted spinach / ham
  • Baked beans / chips / coleslaw / salad
  • Hollandaise

Boiled

  • 10 mins hard boiled
  • 6 mins soft boiled

Omelette

Filling

  1. Cook filling separately
  2. Transfer to bowl
  3. Mix with cheese if using
  4. Options: spring onions, mushrooms, peppers

Prep

  1. Whisk till frothy
    • 2 eggs
    • <¼ tsp salt
    • black pepper
  2. Leave eggs for 15 mins
  3. Heat in pan till lightly browned
    • 2 tsp butter
  4. Rewhisk eggs and add to pan
  5. Use spatula to push edges of omelette towards middle of pan so uncooked egg can run underneath (lift-and-tilt)
  6. When almost cooked add filling
  7. Remove heat
  8. Cover with lid for leave for 1 min till egg all cooked

Poached eggs

  • 500ml salted water per egg
  • Bottom of metal pan provides heat to cook eggs, does not work in Pyrex bowl
  • 85°C best constant temperature

Prep

  1. Break egg into cup
  2. Pour into sieve to let loose whites drip through
  3. Return egg to cup

Cooking

  1. Prepare plate with kitchen paper
  2. Bring water to boil in saucepan
  3. Remove heat (85°C) because eggs with sink
  4. Add egg gently in centre of pan to motionless water
  5. Cover pot
  6. Leave, stirring occasionally (times different for different pans)
    • 5:10 medium egg
    • 5:20 large egg
  7. Remove eggs with spider and drain on plate with paper

Scrambled eggs

  • Add milk for fluffy eggs
  • Add butter for creamy eggs

Fluffy scrambled eggs

  1. Whisk till mixed
    • 4 eggs
    • 2 egg yolks
    • 2 tbsp double cream
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Leave for 15 mins
  3. Heat pan and add
    • vegetable oil
    • butter
  4. Cook for 3-4 mins eggs on medium-low heat stirring constantly

Creamy scrambled eggs

  1. Whisk till mixed
    • 2 eggs
    • few drops of lemon juice
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Leave for 15 mins
  3. Melt 6g butter in pan
  4. Cook for 3-4 mins on medium-low heat stirring constantly
  5. Add another 6g butter as eggs cook

Asian fried egg

  1. Heat
    • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  2. Add and fry till cooked
    • 2 large eggs lightly beaten
  3. Drain on paper towel
