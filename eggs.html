Eggs

About

  • Freeze whites not yolks
  • See Meringue

Weights

  • 4 medium: 232g
  • 4 large: 276g = 142g whites, 35g white per egg

Scrambled

  1. Whisk well until completely mixed
    • 2 eggs
    • few drops of lemon juice
    • salt
  2. Melt 6g butter in pan
  3. Cook eggs on medium-low heat stirring constantly
  4. Add another 6g butter as eggs cook

Serve with

  • Wholemeal / sourdough toast
