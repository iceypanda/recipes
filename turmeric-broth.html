Turmeric, mushroom, ginger broth

Noodles

  1. Cook noodles

Broth

  1. Add and heat for 3 mins until mushrooms soft
    • olive oil
    • 1 shallot
    • 150g fancy mushrooms
    • 30g ginger chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic chopped
    • ½ tsp ground tumeric
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 600mlsalted stock
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 20g ginger sliced
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  3. Add and cook for 30 secs
    • 2 pak choi
  4. Add noodles to broth

Serving

  • with udon noodles
  • 3 servings
  • with tofu / tempeh / prawns

Notes

  • Reduced stock from 1l, 500ml too little
