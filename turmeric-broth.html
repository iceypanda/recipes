Turmeric broth

  1. Heat 30g chopped ginger with olive oil for 1 min on low heat
  2. Add and heat for 3 mins until mushrooms soft
    • 150g fancy mushrooms
    • 2 cloves chopped garlic
    • ½ tsp ground tumeric
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 1l vegetable stock
    • 1 tin of chick peas
    • 30g sliced ginger
  4. Add 4 pak choi and 1 shallot for cook for 30 secs
  5. Add cooked noodles
