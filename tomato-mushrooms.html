Tomato & button mushrooms

Mushrooms

  1. Fry 7 mins until softening
    • olive oil
    • 600g button mushrooms whole or halved
  2. Set aside in bowl

Sauce

  1. Heat for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 carrot diced
    • 1 onion diced
    • 2 sticks celery diced
    • ½ tsp rosemary
    • 2 bay leaves
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 50-100ml white wine
  3. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • mushrooms
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • ¼ tsp salt
  4. Add to taste
    • pepper

Serving

  • Khachapuri
  • 3 servings
