Ancho & orange flan

  • Preheat oven to 150°C
  • Set custard

Chilli

  1. Soak in hot water till soft
    • 15g Ancho chilli de-stemmed
  2. Remove stalk
  3. Squeeze out liquid
  4. Scrape out chilli seeds

Caramel

  1. Make caramel with
    • 120g white caster sugar
  2. Pour caramel into baking tray to set

Custard

  1. Blitz in ninja
    • 300ml (309g) whole milk
    • zest 1 orange
    • ancho chilli softened and chopped
  2. Pour into bowl
  3. Whisk in
    • 400g condensed milk
    • 100ml (98g) double cream
    • 3 medium eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Strain into baking tray
  5. Cover tray with foil
  6. Arrange water bath with tray covered up to halfway
  7. Bake for 40-50 mins at 150°C
  8. Remove from water bath and leave to cool
  9. Chill for 3 hours or overnight in fridge

Serving

  1. Go round custard to bottom of tray with sharp knife to help release
  2. Place serving plate on top of tray
  3. Turnover and move quickly to side to release, might take >1 tries
  4. Cut into 6 portions

Notes

  • Original recipe
  • Double caramel flan
