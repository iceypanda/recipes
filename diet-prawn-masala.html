King prawn masala

  1. Blitz to paste
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 1 clove garlic chopped
    • 1 thumb-sized piece (12g) ginger
    • handful fresh coriander
  2. Fry for 1 min
    • 2 tsp coconut oil
    • paste
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
    • ½ tsp turmeric
  4. Add and cook for 15 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
  5. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 2 palm-size amounts (180g) king prawns
  6. Add
    • juice of ¼ lemon
  7. Serve with
    • 30g couscous cooked
    • fresh coriander chopped

Serving

  • 2 portions
