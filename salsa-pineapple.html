Pineapple salsa

  1. Mix so equal amounts
    • 2 tomatoes deseeded & chopped
    • 1 red / yellow pepper chopped
    • pineapple chopped
  2. Macerate separately for 30 mins
    • red onion / shallot finally chopped
    • juice 1 lime
  3. Mix everything together with
    • coriander leaves chopped
    • red chilli chopped
    • salt
    • jalapenos chopped (optional)
