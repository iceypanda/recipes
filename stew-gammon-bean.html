Gammon & bean stew

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Heat to soften in casserole dish for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • 2 onions chopped
    • 2 red / orange / yellow peppers chopped
    • 4 sticks celery chopped
  3. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 4 cloves garlic
  4. Add
    • 500ml low-salt chicken stock
    • 1 butternut squash in 2cm cubes
  5. Add
    • tin cannellini beans rinsed
    • tin red kidney beans in chilli sauce
    • tin tomatoes
    • 4 tbsp tomato puree
  6. Add
    • 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
    • 1 tbsp sweet smoked paprika
    • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 tsp herbs
  7. Add
    • 750g gammon joint trimmed & cut into large pieces
  8. Bring to boil and bake in oven for 1¼ hours
  9. Leave to cool in oven

Notes

  • made: 8 Oct 2022, not better with fennel instead of celery and beans without chilli sauce
  • use bigger casserole dish, 26cm
  • gammon provides all salt needed
  • Original recipe
