Polenta

Polenta

  1. Boil in pan
    • 410ml water
    • ½ salted stock cube
  2. Reduce heat and add 110g polenta whisking constantly to prevent lumps
  3. Remove heat
  4. Stir in 35g parmesan / cheddar grated
  5. Oil smallest steel baking tray
  6. Pour polenta onto tray and smooth out with silicon spatula
  7. Press flat with baking paper
  8. Leave to cool
  9. Chill in fridge for 30 mins

Chips

  1. Cut polenta into chips
  2. Coat chips in olive oil
  3. Roast ½ batch at 190°C for 15-16 mins in air fryer

Serving

  • 2 portions

Notes

  • Cook as chips or slabs not cubes
  • 25 mins in oven, use oven if need to cook full batch
