Rice

  • Rinse 3 times before cooking
  • Wash the rice in cooking pot so know total weight of water
  • Leave for 10 mins after finished cooking
  • Always use rice cooker

Jasmine

  • Water = rice weight x 2.5
  • Ratios
    • 200g rice + 500g water
    • 250g rice + 625g water
    • 300g rice + 750g water

Basmati

  • Water = rice weight x 2.6
  • Ratios
    • 90g rice + 240g water
    • 160g rice + 416g water (2 lunches)
    • 200g rice + 520g water
    • 250g rice + 605g water
    • 300g rice + 780g water

Basmati - in pot

  1. Medium heat 5 mins Basmati rice
  2. Low heat 4 mins
  3. Leave to steam until ready

Brown rice

  • Water = rice weight x 2.6 (for rice cooker)
  • Do not need to wash brown rice
  1. Boil for 25 mins
  2. Leave in pot for 10 mins

Portions

  • 100g lunch for me
  • 300g family
