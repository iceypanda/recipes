Rice

  • Rinse 3 times before cooking
  • Wash the rice in cooking pot so know total weight of water
  • Always use rice cooker
  • Leave for 10 mins in cooked after finished cooking
  • Weigh out equal cooked portions

Jasmine

  • Water = rice weight x 2.5
  • 1 portion = 70g uncooked = 205g cooked
  • Ratios
    • 140g rice + 350g water (2 portions)
    • 210g rice + 525g water (3 portions)
    • 280g rice + 700g water (4 portions)
    • 350g rice + 875g water (5 portions)

Basmati

  • Water = rice weight x 2.6
  • 1 portion = 70g uncooked = 220g cooked
  • Ratios
    • 140g rice + 365g water (2 portions)
    • 210g rice + 545g water (3 portions)
    • 280g rice + 730g water (4 portions)
    • 350g rice + 910g water (5 portions)

Basmati - in pot

  1. Medium heat 5 mins Basmati rice
  2. Low heat 4 mins
  3. Leave to steam till ready

Brown rice

  • Do not need to wash brown rice
  • Different types, different cooking times
    • Parboiled brown long grain rice
    • Brown basmati

Brown rice - rice cooker

  • Water = rice weight x 3.0
  • Stir halfway
  • May need 2nd round of cooking

Brown rice - in pot

  1. Boil for 25 mins
  2. Leave in pot for 10 mins
