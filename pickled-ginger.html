Pickled ginger

Veg

  • ginger
  • shallots
  • cucumber

Salt

  1. Mix and leave for 10 mins
    • 50g ginger in batons or slices
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Pour over boiling water to just cover and leave for 2 mins
  3. Drain water and squeeze out excess liquid
  4. Leave ginger to drain on paper towel

Pickle

  1. Heat in microwave in tall Pyrex container until dissolved, boil for 30 secs then leave to cool
    • 40ml rice vinegar
    • 20g white sugar
    • 20ml water
  2. Place ginger in sterilised jar
    • ginger
    • pickling liquid
  3. Seal jar and leave to cool before putting in fridge
  4. Leave at least 24 hours before eating

Notes

  • Radishes make other things pink
  • Hot liquid and salt take heat out of ginger
