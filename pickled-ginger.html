Pickled ginger

Ginger

  1. Mix and leave for 5 mins
    • 50g ginger in batons or slices
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Pour over boiling water to just cover and leave for 2 mins
  3. Drain water and squeeze out excess liquid
  4. Leave ginger to drain on paper towel

Pickle

  1. Heat until dissolved then boil for 30 secs
    • 30ml rice wine vinegar
    • 15g white sugar
    • 15ml water
  2. Place ginger in sterilised jar and cover with pickling liquid
  3. Seal jar and leave to cool before putting in fridge
  4. Leave at least 24 hours before eating
