Fennel

Prep

  • Thinner slices for less cooking time

Lateral slices

  1. Remove stalks
  2. Cut in half from top
  3. Cut out core in triangle
  4. Slice thinly

Longitudinal slices

  1. Remove stalks
  2. Trim base
  3. Slice thinly

Roasting

  1. Coat slices in olive oil, balsamic vinegar and seasoning
  2. Roast in oven at 180°C for 30 mins
