Potato wedges

  • 200-250g per portion

Coatings

Coating 1 (for 2 portions)

  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ⅓ tsp garlic powder
  • ⅓ tsp onion powder (optional)
  • ⅓ tspsalt

Coating 2

  • sunflower oil
  • cajun spice mix

Cooking - air fryer

  • Roast at 190°C for 23 mins
  • Shake every 6-7 mins

Cooking - oven

  • 200°C for 35 mins
  • Turn wedges half-way, they brown where in contact with pan

Notes

  • Can leave potato skins on or not
  • Cut wedges thick not thin
  • Lay potato halves / quarters on flat side to cut
  • Wedges very bland without salt and seasonings
