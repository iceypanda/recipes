Potato wedges

  1. Cut potatoes into halves
  2. Boil potatoes for 11 min (time from bubbling)
  3. Drain potatoes and cut into wedges
  4. Coat wedges with olive oil
  5. Roast in oven at 200°C for 25 mins until golden

Comments

Can leave potato skins on or not

Turn wedges half-way, they brown where in contact with pan

Cut wedges thick not thin
