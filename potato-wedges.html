Potato wedges

  • 200-250g per portion

Coatings

Coating 1

  • olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • salt and pepper

Coating 2

  • olive oil
  • cajun spice mix

Cooking

  • Oven: 200°C for 35 mins
  • Air fryer: Roast at 190°C for 23 mins

Notes

  • Can leave potato skins on or not
  • Turn wedges half-way, they brown where in contact with pan
  • Cut wedges thick not thin
  • Lay potato halves / quarters on flat side to cut
  • Wedges very bland without salt and seasonings
