Potato wedges

  1. Cut potatoes into quarters
  2. Boil potatoes for 10 min (time from bubbling)
  3. Drain potatoes and cut into wedges
  4. Coat wedges with
    • olive oil
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp onion powder
    • 1 tsp paprika (optional)
    • salt and pepper
  5. Roast in oven at 200°C for 35 mins until golden

Notes

Can leave potato skins on or not

Turn wedges half-way, they brown where in contact with pan

Cut wedges thick not thin

Lay potato halves / quarters on flat side to cut

Wedges very bland without salt and seasonings
