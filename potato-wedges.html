Potato wedges

  • 200-250g per portion

Cooking

  1. Cut potatoes into thick wedges
  2. Heat in pan of cold water till hot and boil for 6 mins
  3. Drain and spread on baking tray to dry
  4. Coat in oil and seasoning
  5. Cook in preheated oven at 190°C for 30 mins

Coatings

  • After coating mix with
    • vegetable/sunflower oil

Coating 1 (3 portions)

  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp oregano
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp chilli powder
  • ¼ tsp caster sugar

Coating 2 (2 portions)

  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ⅓ tsp garlic powder
  • ⅓ tsp onion powder
  • ⅓ tsp salt

Coating 3

  • Cajun spice mix

Notes

  • Leave potato skins on
  • Cut wedges thick not thin
  • Wedges very bland without salt and seasonings
  • Reheat in air fryer for 7 mins at 190°C
