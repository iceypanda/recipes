Mushroom pie

Porcini

  1. Soak for 15 mins
    • 15g porcini mushrooms
    • water boiled
  2. Drain and chop

Mushrooms

  1. Fry till lightly browned
    • 700g mixed mushrooms chopped
    • olive oil
  2. Transfer to bowl

Filling

  1. Fry till soft
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 1 clove garlic
    • olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins stirring constantly
    • 25g plain flour
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • ½ tsp white wine vinegar
  4. Mix in little by little
    • 200ml veg stock (½ salted cube)
  5. Bring to simmer
  6. Mix in
    • mixed mushooms
    • porcini mushrooms
    • 75ml double cream
    • ½ tsp Marmite
    • black pepper

Pie

  1. Pour filling into roasting dish
  2. Cover with
    • ready-rolled puff pastry
  3. Bake at 180°C for 20 mins till pastry golden

Serving

  • 4 portions
