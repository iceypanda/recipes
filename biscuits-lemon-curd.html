Lemon curd biscuits

  • Preheat oven to 170°C
  1. Add to bowl and mix
    • 90g caster sugar
    • zest of 1 lemon
  2. Add
    • 240g plain flour
    • 150g unsalted butter cubed
    • ¼ tsp salt
  3. Blitz to crumbs
  4. Add and mix to form dough
    • 2 eggs yolks
    • 1 tsp water
  5. Divide into 30g pieces
  6. Arrange on lined baking tray
  7. Make indent with thumb on each biscuit
  8. Cook in oven for 18-20 mins
  9. Fill holes with lemon curd / jam
  10. Dust with icing sugar
