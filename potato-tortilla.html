Potato tortilla

Potatoes & onion

  1. Peel, slice with mandoline and pat dry
    • 300g potatoes
  2. Fry gently for 25-30 mins until potatoes cooked, don't stir too much
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • potatoes
  3. Remove from heat and leave to cool

Tortilla

  1. Mix and beat in bowl
    • 5 eggs
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Add the onions and potatoes and mix gently
  3. Cook gently for 20 mins until almost no runny egg on top
  4. Carefully flip with plate and cook other side for 5 mins

Serving

  • Spanish-style roast chicken pieces
  • Tomato salsa

Notes

  • Allow eggs to reach room temperature before using
  • Flip tortilla using a large plate
  • Waxy potatoes recommended
