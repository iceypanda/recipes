Potato tortilla

Potatoes

  1. Simmer gently for 15-20 mins until potatoes cooked
    • 500g potatoes sliced with mandoline
    • milk
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Drain potatoes and leave to cool

Tortilla

  1. Fry until soft
    • ½ onion (or shallots)
    • olive oil
  2. Leave to cool
  3. Combine in bowl and leave for 15 mins
    • 4 eggs beaten
    • onions
    • potatoes
    • ¼ tsp salt
  4. Pour carefully into frying pan and cook 6-8 mins each side

Serving

  • Spanish-style roast chicken pieces
  • Tomato salsa

Notes

  • Allow eggs to reach room temperature before using
  • Flip tortilla using a large plate
  • Waxy potatoes recommended
