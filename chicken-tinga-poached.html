Poached chicken tinga

Chicken

  1. Brind to boil then simmer for 15 mins
    • 500g chicken breast
    • ½ onion
    • 3 bay leaves
    • ½ stock cube
  2. Remove chicken to cool, reserving the liquid
  3. Shred chicken with two forks when cool enough

Sauce

  1. Blend
    • 2 large plum tomatoes
    • ½ onion
    • 50ml cooking liquid
    • 50g chipotle sauce
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • ½ tsp smoked paprika
    • ¼ tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp salt

Tinga

  1. Fry for 10 mins
    • 2 onions finely sliced
    • 50g chorizo chopped
  2. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • tomato sauce
  3. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • shredded chicken
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin pinto beans drained

Serving

  • Tortillas
  • Rosated sweetcorn
  • Roasted sweet potato / Rice
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
