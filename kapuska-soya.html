Soya kapuska

Red pepper

  1. Roast
    • 1 red pepper
  2. Leave to cool
  3. Remove skins
  4. Blitz with
    • 1 tsp tomato puree

Kapuska

  1. Fry for 5 mins
    • 1 tsp olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 1 clove garlic grated
  2. Fry for 5 mins
    • 150g soya mince
  3. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • 2 tomatoes skinned & chopped
    • large handful cabbage shredded
    • 350ml water
    • 6 olives sliced
    • pepper paste
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tsp fennel seeds
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
    • black pepper
  4. Add
    • 4 dried apricots chopped

Serving

  • 2 portions
