Honey soy chicken rice

  1. Mix in cast iron pot
    • 45g pineapple juice
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1½ tsp dark soy sauce
    • ½ tsp sesame oil
    • ½ tsp five spice powder
    • ½ tsp ground ginger
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Add and marinate for 30 mins
    • 5 chicken thighs
  3. Cook for 5 mins
  4. Remove chicken
  5. Add and heat till hot
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
    • 25g butter
    • 300g jasmine rice rinsed
    • 150g sweetcorn
    • 1 tin green lentils drained
    • 400ml chicken stock (1 salted stock cube)
  6. Place chicken into the rice
  7. Cover pot
  8. Bake at 160°C for 40 mins
  9. Leave to stand for 5 mins

Serving

  • 5 portions
