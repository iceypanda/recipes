Pineapple relish

Version 1

  1. Simmer in pan for 15 mins
    • 400g chopped fresh pineapple
    • 100g caster sugar
    • 100ml cider vinegar
    • 50ml water
    • zest of 1 lime
    • 1 star anise
    • pinch cayenne pepper
  2. Drain pineapple to remove vinegar
  3. Blitz in blender
    • pineapple flesh from pan
    • 20ml lime juice
  4. Leave to cool

Notes

  • Last time too sharp
  • Reduced vinegar from 150ml
