Pineapple relish

Version 2

  • 2 portions
  • R not like
  1. Simmer in pan for 5 mins
    • 1 small tin pineapple drained
    • 20g golden caster sugar
    • 1 tbsp cider vinegar
    • zest ½ lime
    • ½ tsp lime juice
    • ¼ tsp Chinese 5 spice
    • pinch salt
    • pinch chilli flakes
  2. Blitz till no chunks

Version 1

  1. Simmer in pan for 15 mins
    • 400g chopped fresh pineapple
    • 100g caster sugar
    • 100ml cider vinegar
    • 50ml water
    • zest 1 lime
    • 1 star anise
    • pinch cayenne pepper
  2. Drain pineapple to remove vinegar
  3. Blitz in blender
    • pineapple flesh from pan
    • 20ml lime juice
  4. Leave to cool

Notes

  • Last time too sharp
  • Reduced vinegar from 150ml
