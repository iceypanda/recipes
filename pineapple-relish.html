Pineapple relish

  1. Simmer in pan for 15 mins
    • 400g chopped fresh pineapple
    • 100g caster sugar
    • 150ml cider vinegar
    • zest of 1 lime
    • 1 star anise
    • pinch cayenne pepper
  2. Blitz in blender, add more syrup if required
    • pineapple flesh from pan
    • 1 tbsp syrup from pan
    • 20ml lime juice
  3. Leave to cool
