Croutons

  1. Remove crusts from old bread
  2. Cut bread into 1cm chunks
  3. Mix with olive oil, 1 tbsp per 100g bread
  4. Spread on baking tray and bake in oven at 200°C for 10 mins or until crispy, turning halfway

Serving

  • 50g portion
  • 100g if only bread

Notes

  • Could mix with 1 tbsp dried oregano
