Kalamata olive & feta pasta

  1. Fry until soft:
    • olive oil
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 2 medium onions
  2. Add and simmer for 20 mins:
    • 2 tins of tomatoes
    • 2 corgettes
    • 8 mushrooms
    • 8 tbsp basil
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • 6 bay leaves
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins:
    • 1 jar of kalamata olives
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins:
    • 1 large tin of tuna
  5. Add:
    • 1 pack of chopped feta
