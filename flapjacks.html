Flapjacks

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Use large saucepan so do not need extra mixing bowl
  3. Heat gently till melted and dissolved
    • 125g unsalted butter
    • 90g golden syrup
    • 90g light muscovado sugar
  4. Mix well till all butter incorporated
  5. Stir in and mix well
    • 250g rolled oats
  6. Add optional extra ingredients
    • 80g dried blueberries
    • 6 dates & 40g walnuts, 2:1 ratio by weight
  7. Line tin with baking paper
  8. Spoon mixture into 20x20cm baking tin and smooth the surface with wooden spoon at flat angle
  9. Bake at 160°C for 30 mins
  10. Leave to cool for 20 mins
  11. Cut into segments
  12. Leave to cool completely

Serving

  • Calories = 2500, 1/16 = 150

Notes

  • 32 mins circotherm: flapjacks too hard and crunchy
  • 27 mins and flapjacks not quite chewy enough, nice but fall apart
  • 35 mins and flapjacks crunchy and stay together
