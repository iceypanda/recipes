Flapjacks

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Combine in large saucepan and heat gently until melted and dissolved
    • 125g butter
    • 90g golden syrup
    • 90g light muscovado sugar
  3. Stir in 250g rolled oats and mix well
  4. Lightly butter tin or line with greaseproof paper
  5. Spoon mixture into tin and smooth the surface with wooden spoon
  6. Bake at 160°C for 30 mins
  7. Leave to cool for 10 mins then mark into segments
  8. Leave to cool completely

Notes

Use big pan to heat butter and syrup so room to stir in oats

27 mins and flapjacks not quite chewy enough, nice but fall apart

35 mins and flapjacks crunchy and stay together
