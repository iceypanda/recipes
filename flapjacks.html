Flapjacks

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Use large saucepan so do not need extra mixing bowl
  3. Heat gently until melted and dissolved
    • 125g butter
    • 90g golden syrup
    • 90g light muscovado sugar
  4. Stir in and mix well
    • 250g rolled oats
  5. Add extra ingredients (optional), 455g is half mixture
  6. Lightly butter tin or line with greaseproof paper
  7. Spoon mixture into tin and smooth the surface with wooden spoon at flat angle
  8. Bake at 160°C for 31 mins
  9. Leave to cool for 10 mins then mark into segments with paring knife
  10. Leave to cool completely

Extra ingredients

  • 80g dried blueberries
  • 6 dates & 40g walnuts, 2:1 ratio by weight

Serving

  • Calories = 2500, 1/16 = 150

Notes

  • 27 mins and flapjacks not quite chewy enough, nice but fall apart
  • 35 mins and flapjacks crunchy and stay together
