Rhubarb Crumble

  1. Heat at 180°C for 10 mins
    • 5 sticks rhubarb
    • 3 tbsp sugar
    • 2 tbsp water
  2. Mix in bowl and cover fruit
    • 70g butter
    • 70g muscavado/demerara sugar
    • 110g plain flour
    • 35g oats
    • 1 tsp ginger
  3. Heat at 180°C for 45 mins

Notes

If topping is too floury add more butter and sugar

Apple cooks quicker than rhubarb so do not preheat. It also collapses down when cooked to apple sauce.
