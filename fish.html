Fish

Salmon

Baked

  1. Air fry on bake at 160°C for 15 mins skin side up (same for oven)
  2. Take out and rest for 2 mins
  3. Remove skin and grey flesh before serving

Poached

  1. Bring to simmer
    • 500ml stock
    • dill chopped
    • 1 bay leaf
    • pepper
  2. Cook salmon fillets for 5-6 mins (check cooked, might need longer)
  3. Can keep warm in foil until ready to serve

Notes

  • serve skin-side down, even if removed
  • Salmon goujons

Cod

Pan-fried

  1. Start on low-medium heat
  2. Heat fillets for 5 mins in oiled frying pan without lifting (flip before cooked through so fish does not flake or break apart)
  3. Turn over and add butter
  4. Baste the fillets in melted butter until cooked

Pan-fried with lime and herb crust

  1. Press firmly fish on both sides to coat with
    • 3 tbsp breadcrumbs
    • 2 tbsp dill / parsely chopped
    • zest of 1 lime
  2. Melt 30g butter in pan
  3. Add 1 tbsp olive oil
  4. When bubbles appear around pan add fish and cook for 5-6 mins
  5. When crumbs are golden turn and cook other side

Notes

  • hard to overcook cod because is oily fish

Tuna

Seared

  1. Plenty of sesame oil in pan
  2. Fry for up to 2 mins each side making sure not cooked all the way through
  3. Rest for short time then cut across grain

Serve with

  • Udon noodles and broccoli cooked in stock; teriyaki sauce; spring onions

Sea bass

Pan-fried

  1. Heat fillets for 4 mins in 1½ tsp olive oil skin side down without lifting, press gently so doesn't curl
  2. Turn over and fry for another 90 seconds
