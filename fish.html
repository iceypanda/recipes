Fish

Baked salmon

  1. Air fry on bake at 160°C for 15 mins skin side up (same for oven)
  2. Take out and rest for 2 mins
  3. Remove skin and grey flesh before serving

Pan-fried cod

  1. Heat cod fillets for 5 mins in oiled frying pan without lifting
  2. Turn over and add butter
  3. Baste the fillets in melted butter until cooked

Notes

  • hard to overcook cod because is oily fish

Seared tuna

  1. Plenty of sesame oil in pan
  2. Fry for up to 2 mins each side making sure not cooked all the way through
  3. Rest for short time then cut across grain

Serve with

  1. Udon noodles and broccoli cooked in stock; teriyaki sauce; spring onions
