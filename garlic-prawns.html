Garlic prawns

  1. Heat until butter melted
    • plenty of olive oil
    • large knob butter
  2. Add and fry until browning
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 red chilli finely chopped (optional)
  3. Add (optional)
    • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  4. Add until cooked, 4 mins, stir regularly
    • 200g king prawns
    • fresh parsely chopped (added half way through)
  5. Remove from heat and add
    • fresh parsely chopped
    • lemon juice
    • pinch salt

Serving

  • Potato tortilla

Notes

  • can butterfly prawns
  • Original recipe
