Garlic prawns

  1. Fry for 30 seconds
    • plenty of olive oil
    • 1 red chilli finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic
  2. Add (optional)
    • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  3. Add until cooked, 4 mins
    • 200g king prawns
    • fresh parsely chopped (added half way through)
  4. Remove from heat and add
    • fresh parsely chopped
    • squeeze lemon juice
    • pinch salt

Serving

  • Potato tortilla
