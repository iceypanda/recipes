Salmon Goujons

  1. Cut salmon into goujon shaped pieces
  2. For coating:
    • 80g nice waitrose breadcrumbs
    • 30g cheddar cheese
    • 20g cornflakes
  3. Dip salmon pieces in plain flour then beaten egg and roll once in coating
  4. Fry goujons in sunflower oil

Comments

Fry in plenty of oil, group goujons together at one end of the frying pan

Use plenty of heat to cook the goujons

Coat fish in flour first so coats better with breadcrumbs
