Sea bass & tomato sauce

Fish spice mix

  • Mix
    • 1 tsp cardamom
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp tumeric
    • ½ tsp paprika

Fish

  1. Mix
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp spice mix
    • ½ tsp salt
    • cracked black pepper
  2. Add and marinate for up to 2 hours
    • 350g (4) sea bass fillets scored
  3. Pan-fry after tomato sauce cooked

Tomato sauce

  1. Cook until softened and browned
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 5 cloves garlic
    • 25g ginger grated
    • 2 green chillis sliced with seeds
  3. Add and cook for 30 seconds
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1½ tsp spice mix
  4. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • tin plum tomatoes blitzed
    • 1 tsp caster sugar
    • 100ml stock (½ cube)
    • 1½ tsp dill
    • 15g coriander chopped
    • black pepper
  5. Add lemon juice to taste

Cherry tomatoes

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • 200g cherry tomatoes (pomodorino)
  2. Add to hot frying pan and heat on high for 7 mins until soft but not mush
  3. Combine with tomato sauce

Serving

  • couscous / bulgar wheat
  • roasted aubergine

Notes

  • Can use prawns instead of fish
