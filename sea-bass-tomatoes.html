Sea bass & tomato sauce

Fish spice mix

  • Mix
    • 1 tsp cardamom
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp tumeric
    • ½ tsp paprika

Fish

  1. Mix
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp spice mix
    • ½ tsp salt
    • cracked black pepper
  2. Add and marinate for up to 2 hours
    • 350g (4) sea bass fillets scored
  3. Pan-fry after tomato sauce cooked

Tomato sauce

  1. Cook until softened and browned
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 5 cloves garlic
    • 25g ginger grated
    • 1 green chilli chopped with seeds
  3. Add and cook for 30 seconds
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1½ tsp spice mix
  4. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • tin plum tomatoes blitzed
    • 1½ tsp caster sugar
    • 400ml stock (1 cube)
    • 1½ tsp dill
    • 15g coriander chopped
    • black pepper

Cherry tomatoes

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • 200g cherry tomatoes
  2. Add to hot frying pan and heat on high for 4 mins until charred
  3. Add and heat for 3 mins
    • ½ lemon deseeded and sliced into thin rounds
  4. Combine with tomato sauce

Serving

  • Cover fish with tomato sauce
  • aubergine pilaf

Notes

  • Marinate and fry new sea bass fillets each time if reheating pilaf and sauce
