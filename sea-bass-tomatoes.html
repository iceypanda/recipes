Sea bass / prawns & tomato sauce

Fish spice mix

  • Mix
    • 1 tsp cardamom
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp tumeric
    • ½ tsp paprika

Fish / prawns

  1. Mix
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • ⅓ spice mix
    • salt
  2. Cover and marinate
    • 350g (4) sea bass fillets scored, marinate 2 hours
    • 300g prawns
  3. After sauce is cooked
    • Pan-fry sea bass
    • Cook prawns
  4. Season
    • black pepper

Tomato sauce

  1. Cook till softened and browned
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • ½ green pepper
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 5 cloves garlic
    • 25g ginger grated
    • 1 green chilli sliced with seeds
  3. Add and cook for 30 seconds
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • ⅔ spice mix
  4. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • 1 tin plum tomatoes blitzed
    • 1 tsp caster sugar
    • 100ml stock (½ cube)
    • 1½ tsp dill
    • 15g coriander chopped
  5. Add
    • salt to taste, depends on stock
    • lemon juice
    • 1 aubergine roasted
  6. Sprinkle to serve
    • Aleppo pepper

Cherry tomatoes

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • 200g cherry tomatoes
  2. Roast or fry cherry tomatoes
  3. Combine with tomato sauce

Serving

  • Couscous / bulgar wheat
  • Aubergine bulgar pilaf
  • Roasted aubergine
