Broccoli

  • Dry in oven after boiling

Broccoli

Blanched

  • 10 mins in steamer
  • 6 mins in boiling water (try after 6 mins)

Roasted

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C with baking tray lined with baking paper
  2. Cut broccoli florets in half lengthways
  3. Toss with olive oil
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Tip broccoli on to tray so most are lying flat
  6. Roast for 10 mins until charred and tender
  7. Sprinkle with lemon juice and toasted breadcrumbs (optional)

Sprouting broccoli

  • 10 mins in steamer
  • Few mins in microwave with 1 tbsp water to steam
  • 6 mins in boiling water
    • Try after 6 mins
    • Shake all water out of florets before serving
