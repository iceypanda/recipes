Tofu & mushroom broth

Veg

  1. Heat in frying pan for 10-15 mins until tofu lightly brown on all sides
    • 2 tbsp vegetable / rapeseed oil
    • firm tofu chopped in 1-2cm pieces
    • mushrooms sliced
  2. Add and heat in pan for 5 mins
    • 2 pak choi leaves separated

Broth

  1. Add to large saucepan
    • ½ carton Itsu broth
    • 1 portion noodles
  2. Heat until noodles are cooked
  3. Add half the cooked veg (save other half for another day)
  4. Remove heat and leave to cool for 5 mins before serving (to allow pak choi stems to finish cooking)

Notes

  • 100g dry noodles = 1 portion
  • 150g udon noodles = 1 portion
  • One block of tofu is a lot so could either freeze ⅓ or eat raw as snack while cooking
