Minestrone soup - veg

  1. Soften in a pan without colouring
    • olive oil
    • 1 red onion
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • plenty of chopped rosemary
  2. Add and soften for 3 mins
    • 1 swede
    • 1 carrot
    • 2 celery sticks
    • peas
  3. Add and heat for 15 mins
    • 1.5l of good chicken stock
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tin borlotti or cannellini beans
  4. Add and heat for 7 mins
    • 100g spaghetti
  5. Add and heat for 8 mins
    • ½ cabbage

Notes

Can use any seasonal veg for step 2: corgette, broad beans, fennel, potato
