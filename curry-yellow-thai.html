Yellow Thai curry

Paste (2 portions)

  1. Chop then blend until smooth
    • 2 shallots
    • 1 whole garlic peeled and chopped
    • 2 sticks lemongrass outer leaves removed
    • 40g ginger
    • 4 chillies
    • 4 lime leaves
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 4 tbsp coconut milk

Potatoes

  1. Boil 300g new potatoes until cooked

Curry

  1. Stir fry curry paste in 1½ tbsp oil for 5 mins until darkens
  2. Add and bring to simmer
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 100ml water (rinse out coconut milk tin)
    • 1 tsp cornflour in 2 tsp water
  3. Add and cook for 8 mins until beans cooked
    • 200g green beans
    • potatoes
  4. Add and cook for 3-4 mins
    • 200g king prawns

Serving

  • jasmine rice

Notes

  • made: 24 Aug 2022
  • freeze one of the paste portions
