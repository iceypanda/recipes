Yellow Thai curry

Potatoes

  1. Boil 300g potatoes until cooked

Paste (2 portions)

  1. Chop then blend until smooth
    • 2 shallots
    • 1 whole garlic peeled and chopped
    • 2 sticks lemongrass outer leaves removed
    • 4cm x 4cm piece ginger
    • 4 chillies
    • 4 lime leaves
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 4 tbsp coconut milk

Curry

  1. Stir fry curry paste in 2 tbsp oil for 5 mins until darkens
  2. Add and bring to simmer
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 200ml water
  3. Add and cook for 5 mins until beans cooked
    • 200g green beans
    • potatoes

Serve with

  • rice

Notes

  • freeze one of the paste portions
