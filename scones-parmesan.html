Parmesan scones

  • Preheat oven to 180°C

Milk & apple

  1. Mix
    • 1 apple grated
    • 1 egg
    • 50ml milk

Dough

  1. Blitz till sandy
    • 25g butter cold & cubed
    • 100g full-fat cream cheese cold
    • 100g parmesan chopped
    • 300g self-raising flour
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Add and blitz till dough
    • milk mixture

Scones

  1. Flatten out dough on floured surface till 2cm thick
  2. Cut out 11 7cm circles
  3. Place on baking tray lined with baking paper
  4. Brush with
    • egg beaten
  5. Sprinkle with
    • parmesan grated
  6. Bake at 180°C for 15-18 mins till cooked through
  7. Cool on wire rack
