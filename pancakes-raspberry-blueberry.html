Raspberry & blueberry pancakes

1 portions

  1. Blitz
    • 4 egg whites
    • 1 tsp vanilla paste
  2. Add and blitz
    • 3 tbsp oats
    • 12 blueberries
  3. Fold in
    • 3 tbsp full-fat cottage cheese
  4. Cook 4 pancakes with
    • coconut oil
  5. Serve each pancake topped with
    • 2 or 3 raspberries

2 portions

  1. Blitz

    • 2 tsp vanilla paste
  2. Add and blitz
    • 6 tbsp oats
    • 24 blueberries
  3. Fold in
    • 6 tbsp full-fat cottage cheese
  4. Cook 8 pancakes with
    • coconut oil
