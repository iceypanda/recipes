Fish stew

  1. Fry till soft
    • ½ onion chopped
    • 1 tsp olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic
  2. Add and cook for 15 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 6 black olives sliced
  3. Add
    • handful parsely chopped
  4. Transfer to baking dish and add
    • 2 cod fillets covered by sauce
  5. Bake at 160°C for 15 mins
  6. Add and bake for 5 mins
    • handful spinach

Serving

  • 2 portions
