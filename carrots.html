Carrots

Notes

  • Carrots should have bite after cooking

Cooking

Steam

  • Steam for 17-19 mins

Roast

  • Parboil before cooking
  • Roast at 180°C for 40 mins (less if parboiled)
  • Lower shelf and high sided tin so don't burn
  • Mix halfway and remove any liquid

Recipes

Honey & butter roasted

  • 250g carrots
  • 1½ tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 12g butter melted
  • black pepper

Roasted with coriander & cumin seeds and mandarin

  • 250g carrots
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp coriander seeds
  • ½ tbsp honey
  • mandarin zest and juice
