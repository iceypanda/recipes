Carrots

Notes

  • Roast at 170-180°C for 30-40 mins
  • Pack close so cook evenly
  • Lower shelf and high sided tin so don't burn
  • Mix halfway and remove any liquid
  • Carrots do not overcook, tolerant of extra cooking

Honey & butter roasted

  • 250g carrots
  • 1½ tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 12g butter melted
  • black pepper

Roasted with coriander & cumin seeds and mandarin

  • 250g carrots
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp coriander seeds
  • ½ tbsp honey
  • mandarin zest and juice

400g - 35 min - 170°C
