Raspberry panna cotta

Raspberries

  1. Mash and sieve to remove pips
    • 375g raspberries

Panna cotta

  1. Warm until honey dissolved
    • 240ml (235g) double cream
    • 210ml (216g) whole milk
    • 50g honey
  2. Whisk in until dissolved
    • 10g gelatine presoaked if leaves
  3. Allow mixture to cool but not set
  4. Stir in one of
    • raspberries pureed
    • 50g raspberry coulee
  5. Pour into 8 small ramekins
  6. Cover and chill for 2-3 hours
  7. Pour more raspberry coulee on top

Notes

  • Made: 22 Jul 2023, very firm with 10g gelatine
  • Warm mixture in microwave in Pyrex jug
  • Halve amounts to make 4 portions
  • To make with semi-skimmed milk
    • 260ml (255g) double cream
    • 190ml (195g) semi-skimmed milk
