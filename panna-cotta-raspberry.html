Raspberry panna cotta

Raspberries

  1. Mash and sieve to remove pips
    • 375g raspberries

Panna cotta

  1. Warm until honey dissolved
    • 240ml (235g) double cream
    • 210ml (216g) whole milk
    • 50g honey
  2. Stir in until dissolved
    • 10g gelatine presoaked
  3. Allow mixture to cool but not set
  4. Stir in pureed raspberries
  5. Lightly grease 4 ramekins / small cups with neutral oil
  6. Cover and chill for 2-3 hours

Serving

  1. Dip each mould in warm water for 15 secs
  2. Go round sides with knife to loosen
  3. Tip onto plate and tap base to release

Notes

  • Can warm mixture in microwave in Pyrex bowl
  • To make with semi-skimmed milk
    • 260ml (255g) double cream
    • 190ml (195g) semi-skimmed milk
