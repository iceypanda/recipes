Aubergine bulgar pilaf

Aubergine

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 aubergines cubed
  2. Roast with veg meatballs (if using)

Pilaf

  1. Cook until softened
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add then bring to boil the reduce and simmer for 20 mins
    • 1 or 2 green chillies chopped with seeds
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 150g bulgar wheat
    • 500ml water with ½ stock cube
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tsp caster sugar
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  4. Remove heat and leave covered for 10 mins
  5. Lemon juice to taste
  6. Stir in the aubergines

Serving

  • vegetarian meatballs / spiced king prawns
  • pickled beetroot
  • red pepper hummus / ajvar
  • mint yogurt / tzatziki
  • harissa / Aleppo pepper
