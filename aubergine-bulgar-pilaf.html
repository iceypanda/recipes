Aubergine bulgar pilaf

Aubergine

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 aubergines cubed
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Roast

Pilaf

  1. Cook until softened
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add and stir for 30 secs
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  4. Add then bring to boil
    • 2 green chillies chopped with seeds
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 200ml water
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
    • ½ tsp salt
  5. Add then reduce heat and cook for 15 mins at low heat
    • 150g bulgar wheat
  6. Remove heat and leave covered for 10 mins
  7. Stir in the aubergines

Serving

  • yogurt / fresh coriander / chilli flakes
