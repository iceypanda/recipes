Vegetable ragu

  1. Heat for 20 mins stirring often
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 aubergine
    • 1 red onion
    • 1 red pepper
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • 2 courgettes
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • ½ tbsp oregano
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  4. Add
    • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
    • zest of ½ lemon grated
    • parsley chopped

Serving

  • Buckwheat / couscous

Notes

  • Can include black olives, mushrooms, capers
  • Can change oregano for basil, thyme, marjoram, dill
