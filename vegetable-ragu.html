Vegetable ragu

  1. Heat for 20 mins stirring often
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 aubergine
    • 1 red onion
    • 2 corgettes
    • 1 red pepper
  2. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • tin of chopped tomatoes
    • ½ tbsp oregano
    • dash balsamic vinegar
    • 1 tsp sugar
  3. Add
    • grated lemon zest
    • chopped parsley

Notes

Can also use black olives, mushrooms

Can change oregano for basil, thyme, marjoram, dill
