Mediterranean cod

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 3 tbsp lemon juice
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 20g melted butter
  2. In another bowl mix
    • ⅓ cup flour
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • ¾ tsp paprika
    • ¾ tsp ground cumin
    • ¾ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp black pepper
  3. Pat 4 pieces of cod dry then dip in lemon juice mixture followed by the flour mixture
  4. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in pan and sear cod on both sides to give colour but don't fully cook
  5. Add 3 cloves chopped garlic to lemon juice mixture and pour over cod
  6. Bake uncovered in same skillet the fish was fried in preheated oven at 200°C for 10 mins or until fish flakes
  7. Sprinkle over chopped parsley

Notes

Reduced amount of garlic and lemon juice mixture

Eat fish the same day it is cooked

Don't use thick cover of flour or will be wet, light cover is best
