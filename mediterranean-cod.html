Mediterranean cod

  1. Mix in bowl:
    • 5 tbsp lemon juice
    • 5 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp melted butter
  2. In another bowl mix:
    • ⅓ cup flour
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • ¾ tsp paprika
    • ¾ tsp ground cumin
    • ¾ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp black pepper
  3. Pat 4 pieces of cod dry then dip in lemon juice mixture followed by the flour mixture
  4. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in pan and sear cod on both sides to give colour but don't fully cook
  5. Add 5 cloves chopped garlic to lemon juice mixture and pour over cod
  6. Bake in preheated oven at 200°C for 10 mins or until fish flakes
  7. Sprinkle over chopped parsley
