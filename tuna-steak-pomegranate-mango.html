Tuna steak with pomegranate & mango

Relish

  1. Mix in bowl
    • ½ cucumber deseeded & chopped
    • 2 spring onions chopped
    • 4 cherry tomatoes quartered
    • ½ red chilli chopped
    • 1 heaped tbsp pomegranate
    • 1 heaped tbsp mango diced
    • parsely
    • 1 tsp olive oil
    • juice of ½ lime

Tuna

  1. Fry
    • 2 tuna steaks
  2. Serve with relish spooned over

Serving

  • 2 portions
