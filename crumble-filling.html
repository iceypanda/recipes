Crumble filling

Plum & apple

  • 22x22cm pan
  1. Cook on low heat for 5 mins
    • 700g firm plums stoned & chopped
    • 300g (after peeling, 3) Braeburn apples peeled & cored, 2cm dice
  2. Remove heat, cover and leave for 3 mins
  3. Drain excess liquid
  4. Mix in
    • 50g unsalted butter
    • 50g golden caster sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 tsp cinnamon / ginger

Apples & blackberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Melt then cook on medium heat for 3 mins
    • 30g unsalted butter
    • 30g dark brown sugar
  2. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 300g (after peeling, 3) Braeburn apples peeled & cored, 2cm dice
  3. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 150g blackberries (apple peeled 2 : 1 blackberry ratio)
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  4. Remove heat, cover and leave for 3 mins
  5. Drain excess liquid

Peach & lime

  • 22x22cm pan
  1. Place in pan
    • 3 tins peaches drained
    • ½ peach syrup from tin
  2. Drizzle
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • juice of lime
  3. Add to crumble topping
    • zest of lime

Peach & ginger

  • 22x22cm pan
  1. Place in pan
    • 3 tins peaches drained
    • ½ tsp ground ginger
    • 1 tbsp light brown sugar
  2. Add to crumble topping
    • ½ tsp cinnamon

Cherries & blackberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 250g cherries whole, pitted
    • 150g blackberries
    • 20g light brown sugar
  2. Drain excess liquid

Cherries & strawberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 400g strawberries hulled & halved
    • 200g cherries whole, pitted
    • 20g light brown sugar
  2. Drain excess liquid

Rhubarb

  1. Simmer for 10 mins
    • 5 sticks rhubarb
    • 3 tbsp light brown sugar
    • 2 tbsp water
  2. Drain excess liquid

Notes

  • Try: plums
