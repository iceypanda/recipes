Sausage carbonara

  1. Cook 230g of tagliatelle, then drain saving some water
  2. Squeeze sausage meat out of 3 sausages
    • roll into 18 balls
    • coat in black pepper
    • fry with olive oil until cooked through
    • turn off heat
  3. Beat 1 egg and add
    • finely chopped bunch of parsley with stalks
    • some pasta cooking water
    • 25g parmesan
  4. Add pasta into sausage pan
  5. Add egg mixture into pan and let cook in residual heat
  6. Loosen with pasta water, season and grate over more parmesan

Notes

Start frying sausages on low heat so don't stick

Kids like parsley but not parmesan

Will be some plain pasta left over
