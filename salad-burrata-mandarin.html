Carrot, chicory & mandarin salad with burrata

Veg

  1. Heat fan oven to 180C
  2. Toss thoroughly and heat in roasting tin for 30 mins:
    • 250g carrots
    • olive oil
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
    • ½ tsp coriander seeds
    • ½ tbsp honey
    • mandarin zest and juice
  3. Mix thoroughly and heat in roasting tin for 30 mins:
    • 1 tin of chickpeas (should be half)
    • pinch chilli flakes
    • 2 sprigs rosemary
    • olive oil
    • seasoning

Salsa

  1. Blitz 25g walnuts in food processor
  2. Add and blitz again
    • 20g anchovies
    • ¼ tbsp harissa
    • ½ tbsp chopped parsley
    • ½ tbsp mandarin & lemon juice
    • 50ml olive oil
  3. Season to taste

Mandarins

  • Fry 2 mandarins until caramelised and chared

Making the salad

  1. Put in bowl
    • 100g cooked fregola
    • 1 chicory head split into leaves
  2. Add mandarin & lemon juice and toss to coat
  3. Top with carrots, chickpeas, madarins and burrata
  4. Dress with salsa
