Maple treacle tart

Pastry base

  • Pastry

Tart

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C
  2. Warm until combined
    • 280ml maple syrup
    • 80g golden syrup
    • 50g unsalted butter
    • ⅛ tsp salt
  3. Leave to cool for 2 mins
  4. Mix in
    • 1 egg beaten
    • 2 tbsp double cream
  5. Mix in
    • 120g brown breadcrumbs (10 small slices with crusts removed)
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  6. Pour mixture into pastry base
  7. Bake for 20-25 mins at 170°C
