Yellow pea & coconut daal

Peas

  1. Soak 250g yellow split peas in to cold water for 12-16 hours
  2. Boil hard for 10 mins with lid off
    • yellow split peas
    • 800ml water (might need to add more later)
    • ½ tsp turmeric
  3. Cook for 40 mins more
  4. Add and cook until peas are falling apart
    • 2 tsp ground coriander
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 2 tsp sugar

Spices

  1. Fry in pan for 30 seconds
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • 5 lightly crushed cardamom pods
    • ½ tsp brown mustard seeds
    • 1 dried red chilli
    • 2 bay leaves
  2. Add 3 heaped tbsp desiccated coconut until turns golden brown
  3. Mix spices and coconut into the peas
  4. Leave to cool and infuse before serving
  5. Add warm water if too stodgy

Notes

Need to boil hard for period to soften

Cannot slow cook, took 9 hours
