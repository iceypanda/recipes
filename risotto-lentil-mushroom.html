Lentil & mushroom risotto

  1. Fry till soft
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • 2 shallots sliced
    • 2 handfuls mushrooms chopped
    • ½ stick celery sliced
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
    • sage leaves chopped
  2. Add and cook
    • 2 tins green lentils drained & rinsed
    • 6 cherry tomatoes quartered
    • 2 tsp cumin
  3. Add and cook till wilted
    • 1 handful spinach
  4. Garnish with
    • black pepper
    • 1 tbsp tzatziki per portion

Serving

  • 2 portions
