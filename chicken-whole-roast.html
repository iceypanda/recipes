Whole roast chicken

Accompaniments

  • Stuffing
  • Cook roast potatoes, stuffing and veg in oven while chicken is resting

Notes

  • Cook in 4L Pyrex roaster in black oven roasting tray
  • Times at 180°C
    • 20 mins + 20 mins per 450g
    • Medium 1.5kg, 1h 20m, 4 portions & leftovers
    • Medium 1.6kg, 1h 30m

Cooking

  1. Insert into chicken
    • Lemon pricked all over with knife and heated in microwave
    • 2 sticks celery chopped and heated in microwave
    • Slabs of butter 1 inside, 1 under skin at front
  2. Cook at 180°C uncovered
  3. 75°C in meat when done
  4. Baste before resting
  5. Cover with foil and rest for 30 mins
