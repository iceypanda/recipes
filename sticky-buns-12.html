Sticky buns

Buttermilk

    Whisk
    • 140ml milk
    • 40ml single cream
    • ¾ tsp lemon juice / white vinegar
  2. Warm in microwave
  3. Whisk in
    • 7g packet dried yeast

Dough

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 3 large eggs
  2. Add and whisk in
    • buttermilk mixture
    • 80g light brown sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
  3. Gradually whisk in
    • 90ml (82g) unsalted butter melted
  4. Mix in
    • 720g flour
  5. Knead dough for 2 mins to combine
  6. Leave to rise in bowl for 2 hours at room temperature till volume doubled

Baking dish sauce

  1. Heat in pan till sugar disolved and mixture bubbling
    • 60ml (55g) unsalted butter melted
    • 140g light brown sugar
  2. Mix in
    • 50ml (49g) single cream
    • 1 cup pecans toasted, chopped (optional)
  3. Pour evenly over base of 13"x9" baking dish

Cinnamon sugar

  • Mix
    • 140g light brown sugar
    • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon

Buns

  1. Roll out dough to 16"x12" with short edge towards you
  2. Brush with leaving 1" border at top
    • 27g unsalted butter melted
  3. Sprinkle evenly with cinnamon sugar
  4. Roll up tightly using bench scraper to help
  5. Put seam side down and even out shape
  6. Cut into 12 slices by sawing with sharp chef's knife
  7. Arrange in sauced baking dish
  8. Cover with cling film
  9. Leave for 2 hours till volume doubled

Baking

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Cook for 30 mins rotating tray once
  3. Rest for 5 mins
  4. Invert buns onto rack and cover with scraped out sauce

Notes

  • Increased cream and reduced sugar in sauce
  • Original recipe: The Food Lab, p170
  • Made: 8 Feb 2023
