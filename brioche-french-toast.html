Brioche custard French toast

  • 8 slices

Custard

  1. Whisk to mix
    • 50g custard powder
    • 30g icing sugar
  2. Add and whisk
    • 225ml whole milk
    • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste

French toast

  1. Dip in custard
    • thick slice brioche
  2. Heat in pan till hot
    • knob butter
  3. Add brioche slice
  4. Turn down heat
  5. Cook brioche for 2 mins on each side

Serving

  • Blitzed pistachios
  • Drizzled maple syrup
  • Strawberries

Notes

  • Original recipe: Fast Flavours, p103
