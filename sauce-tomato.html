Tomato sauce

  1. Heat over medium-heat heat until foaming subsides
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp butter
  2. Add and cook for 3 mins, stirring frequently
    • 1 onion
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • pinch chilli flakes
    • 1 anchovy chopped (optional)
  4. Add and simmer for 30 mins
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • 1 stem fresh basil chopped (leaves reserved for garnish)
  5. Season to taste
    • salt
    • black pepper

Serving

  • Gnocchi
  • Aubergine pasta
