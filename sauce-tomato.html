Tomato sauce

  1. Heat over medium-heat heat till foaming subsides
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp butter
  2. Add and cook for 8 mins, stirring frequently
    • 1 onion
  3. Add and cook for 2 min
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • ¼ chilli flakes
    • 1 anchovy chopped (optional)
  4. Add and simmer for 30 mins (or slow cooker for 2-3 hours)
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • ½ tsp basil
    • 1 stem fresh basil chopped (leaves reserved for garnish)
    • ½ tsp sugar
    • ¼ salt
  5. Season to taste
    • sugar
    • salt
    • black pepper
  6. Blitz half to smoothen
  7. Stir in while hot
    • 100g spinach (optional)

Serving

  • Gnocchi
  • Aubergine pasta
  • 3-4 portions

Notes

  • Will taste fishy if use anchovy
