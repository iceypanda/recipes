Tomato sauce

  1. Fry until soft
    • olive oil
    • ½ onion or red onion or 1 shallot
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 anchovy (optional)
  3. Transfer to slow cooker and add
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp basil or oregano
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
    • 1 bay leaf
    • salt
  4. Cook on high for 2 hours
  5. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
  6. Remove bay leaf and blitz

Serving

  • with gnocchi
