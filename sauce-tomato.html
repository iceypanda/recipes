Tomato sauce

  1. Fry
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 anchovy
  3. Transfer to slow cooker and add
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tsp basil or oregano
    • 1 tsp sugar
  4. Cook on high for 2 hours
  5. Add
    • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
    • cracked black pepper
    • salt
