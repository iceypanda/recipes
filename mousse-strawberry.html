Strawberry mousse

  1. Cook for 3 mins until strawberries soft enough to mash
    • 250g chopped strawberries
    • 25g caster sugar
    • 50ml water
  2. Remove from heat and mash strawberries with fork
  3. Add 100g mini marshmallows and stir in until dissolved
  4. Whip 200ml double cream until stiff
  5. Fold cream into cooled strawberry mix
  6. Pour into bowls and chill for 2 hours
  7. Serve with half strawberry and 1 marshmallow on top
