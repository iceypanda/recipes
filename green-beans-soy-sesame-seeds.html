Green beans, soy & sesame seeds

Sesame dressing

  1. Dry fry till toasted
    • 3 tbsp sesame seeds
  2. Grind in pestle and mortar till about half are ground
  3. Mix in
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp mirin
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
    • 1 tsp sugar (optional or less)

Beans

  1. Boil till cooked
    • 175g green beans
  2. Cool under running water
  3. Cut beans in half
  4. Mix beans with dressing
