Sweet & sour sprouts, pineapple stir fry

Sauce

  1. Mix
    • pineapple juice from tin
    • 1 tbsp cornflour
    • 1 tbsp cider vinegar
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 4 tbsp water

Stir fry

  1. Toast 2 tsp sesame seeds in pan then set aside
  2. Dry heat for 1 min 1 small tin pineapple chunks
  3. Add and heat for 1 min 30g cashew nuts chopped
  4. Add and heat for 4 min 4 spring onions chopped into 2cm pieces
  5. Add and heat for 30 seconds
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 2 cloves garlic finely sliced
    • 1 red chilli sliced into discs
    • 15g ginger roughly chopped
  6. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 200g sugar snap peas halved lengthways
    • 200g beansprouts
  7. Add sauce, bring to boil and thicken for 2 min
  8. Serve stir fry onto noodles then add splash of boiled kettle water to pan, stir for 1 min, then pour over stir fry

Serving

  • 100g fine rice noodles
  • Tofu / tempah / prawns

Notes

  • Use less vinegar next time
