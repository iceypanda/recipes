Blondies

  • Preheat oven to 160°C
  1. Mix in bowl
    • 110g butter melted
    • 220g dark brown sugar
  2. Mix in
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Mix in
    • 130g plain flour
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • ½ tsp baking powder
  4. Mix in
    • 60g white chocolate chips
    • 60g walnuts chopped
  5. Pour into 20x20cm baking pan lined with baking paper
  6. Bake for 20-25 mins at 160°C until tester comes out clean
  7. Leave to cool
  8. Cut into 16 pieces

Serving

  • 16 portions

Notes

  • Made: 7 Apr 2023, ¼ tsp salt was too salty, currants chewy but not nice
  • Freeze well
  • Original recipe
