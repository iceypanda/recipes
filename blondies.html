Blondies

  • Preheat oven to 160°C
  1. Mix in bowl
    • 110g unsalted butter melted
    • 220g dark brown sugar
  2. Mix in
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract (slightly heaped tsp)
  3. Mix in
    • 130g plain flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder (slightly heaped tsp)
  4. Mix in
    • 60g white chocolate chips
    • 60g walnuts chopped
  5. Pour into 23x23cm (9") baking pan lined with baking paper
  6. Bake for 26 mins at 160°C until tester comes out clean
  7. Leave to cool
  8. Cut into 16 pieces

Serving

  • 16 portions

Notes

  • Freeze well
