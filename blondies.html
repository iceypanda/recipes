Blondies

  • Preheat oven to 160°C
  1. Mix in bowl
    • 110g unsalted butter melted
    • 220g dark muscavado sugar
  2. Mix in
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract (slightly heaped tsp)
  3. Mix in
    • 130g plain flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder (slightly heaped tsp)
  4. Mix in
    • 60g white chocolate chips
    • 60g walnuts chopped
  5. Pour into 23x23cm (9") baking pan lined with baking paper
  6. Bake at 160°C for 26 mins till tester comes out clean (don't turn)

Dividing

  1. Leave to cool in tray for 30 mins on cooling rack
  2. Tip whole tray onto 2nd baking tray (cake upside down)
  3. Remove paper, saving for cutting into pieces later
  4. Tip back into 1st tray
  5. Tip onto 2nd tray (cake upside down)
  6. Tip onto rack
  7. Cut into 16 pieces on saved paper

Notes

  • Freeze well
