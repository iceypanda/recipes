Veggie chilli

  1. Chop 500g sweet potato into bite-size chunks
  2. Bake at 200°C for 45 mins after tossing with
    • drizzle of olive oil
    • pinch of cayenne pepper
    • pinch of cumin
    • pinch of cinnamon
    • pinch of salt and pepper
  3. Heat for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 2 mixed-colour peppers
    • 2 cloves garlic
  4. Cook for 8 mins
    • 2 mixed-colour chillies
    • coriander stalks
    • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
  5. Add and cook for 25 mins
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • 1 tin kidney beans
    • 1 tin black beans
  6. Stir in
    • sweet potatoes
    • coriander leaves
    • lemon or lime juice to taste

Notes

30g coriander in total

can use pinto or cannellini beans
