Veggie chilli

Prep before cooking

  • Weigh and start rice
  • Prep all veg
  • Take leaves off coriander

Sweet potatoes

  1. Chop 500-700g sweet potato into bite-size chunks
  2. Bake at 200°C for 45 mins after tossing with
    • drizzle of olive oil
    • pinch of cayenne pepper
    • pinch of cumin
    • pinch of cinnamon
    • pinch of salt and pepper

Chilli

  1. Heat for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 2 mixed-colour peppers
    • 2 cloves garlic
  2. Cook for 8 mins
    • 2 mixed-colour chillies
    • coriander stalks very finely chopped (optional)
    • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • 1 tsp smoked paprika (optional)
  3. Add and cook for 25 mins
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • 1 tin kidney beans
    • 1 tin black beans
  4. Stir in
    • sweet potatoes
    • chopped coriander leaves (how much g?)
    • lemon or lime juice to taste

Serve with

  • sour cream
  • salsa

Notes

  • 30g coriander in total, weigh and do not use too much
  • can use pinto or cannellini beans
