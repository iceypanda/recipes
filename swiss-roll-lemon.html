Lemon Swiss roll

  • Preheat oven to 160°C

Cake

  1. Whisk together for 8-10 mins until creamy
    • 100g caster sugar
    • 4 large eggs
  2. Fold in gently
    • 100g self-raising flour sieved
  3. Spread the mix evenly with knife in a 34x27cm butterd and lined baking tray
  4. Bake for 10 mins until golden and springy
  5. Take out of oven and quickly flip onto a sheet of baking paper sprinkled with 2 tbsp caster sugar
  6. Carefully peel off the baking paper attached to the sponge
  7. Allow to cool for 2 mins then roll up while still warm with the paper inside
  8. Allow to cool
  9. Unroll and spread filling up to last 1"
    • lemon curd
    • 150ml double cream whipped
  10. Roll back up, peeling away paper as you go
