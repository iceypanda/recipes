Pak choi

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil
  2. Add and heat for 2 mins:
    • 2 pak choi with roots removed and leaves separated
    • 1 cm piece of chopped ginger
  3. Add water for steam and heat 2 mins
  4. Pak choi done when leaves wilted and stems cooked but crisp
  5. Optional to serve:
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • salt
