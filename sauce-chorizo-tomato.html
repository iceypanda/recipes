Chorizo tomato sauce

  1. Cook for 5 mins
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • ½ chorizo chopped
  2. Cook until soft
    • 1 large onion chopped
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp chilli powder
  4. Add and simmer for 30 mins
    • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
    • ¼ cup (?g) capers rinsed & chopped
    • ¼ cup (?g) black olives rinsed & chopped
    • fresh coriander chopped
    • 1 tsp sugar
  5. Season to taste
    • salt
    • black pepper

Serving

  • Pan-fried chicken
  • Brown rice
