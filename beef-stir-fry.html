Stir fry beef

Alkalise

  1. Cut 100g per person beef skirt / medallions into strips 2" long and ⅛" wide
  2. Put in bowl with
    • cold water
    • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  3. Leave for 30 mins
  4. Drain in strainer and rinse well
  5. Press to remove excess water

Marinade

  1. Mix in bowl and stir well
    • beef
    • 2 tsp shoyu
    • 2 tsp Shaoxing wine
    • 2 tsp cornflour
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  2. Leave for 15 mins to overnight
  3. Drain well before cooking

Serving

  • 100g beef per person
