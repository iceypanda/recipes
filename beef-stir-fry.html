Stir fry beef

Marinade

  1. Cut 100g per person beef skirt / medallions into strips 2" long and ⅛" wide
  2. Put in bowl with cold water and rinse vigourously
  3. Drain in strainer and press hard to remove excess water
  4. Put in bowl and roughly massage in for 1 min
    • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  5. Add and mix well
    • 2 tsp shoyu
    • 2 tsp Shaoxing wine
    • 2 tsp cornflour
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp sugar
  6. Leave for 15 mins to overnight
  7. Drain before cooking

Serving

  • 100g beef per person
