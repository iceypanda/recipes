Quinoa & black bean chilli

  1. Fry until soft
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 1 red chilli chopped
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp hot smoked paprika
  3. Add and simmer for 30 mins
    • 600ml stock
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 1 tin black beans
    • salt
  4. Top with
    • avocado sliced
    • fresh coriander leaves chopped
