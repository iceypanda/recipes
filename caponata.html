Caponata

Aubergine

  • Fry 2 large (600g) aubergines diced in 2cm pieces until brown on all sides

Stew

  1. Fry for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 2 large onions chopped
    • 6 sticks celery chopped
  2. Add for 2 mins
    • 6 cloves garlic
  3. Add and simmer for 15 mins with lid off
    • 1 tins of tomatoes
    • 50g capers rinsed
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
    • aubergine
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins until all liquid evapourated
    • 70ml red wine vinegar
    • 30g sugar
    • seasoning

Serve with

  • orzo, couscous

Notes

  • optional veg: corgette (replace 1 aubergine and fry with other), peppers (fry with onions)
  • optional with tomatoes: 50g raisins (halve sugar), green olives, herbs, anchovies, artichokes
  • optional at end: 50g toasted pine nuts
  • capers reduced from 150g, sugar halved
  • original recipe
