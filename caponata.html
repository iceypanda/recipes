Caponata

Aubergine & courgette

  • Fry or roast at 200°C till brown on all sides
    • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 medium aubergines diced in 2cm pieces
    • 1 courgette chopped

Stew

  • Chop onions and celery together in ninja
  1. Fry for 10-15 mins till celery soft
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 peppers chopped
    • 3 medium onions chopped
    • 5 sticks celery chopped
  2. Add for 2 mins
    • 6 cloves garlic
    • 1 chilli
  3. Add and simmer for 30 mins with lid off
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 75g capers rinsed
    • 100g black olives rinsed, quartered
    • aubergine & courgette
  4. Add and simmer for 15 mins with lid off
    • 25g dark brown Muscavado sugar
    • ¼ tsp fish sauce
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes (if no fresh chilli)
  5. Add
    • 40ml red wine vinegar
    • 50g pine nuts toasted
    • ¼ tsp salt (taste as olives may add enough)
    • black pepper

Serving

  • Orzo couscous
  • 3 portions
