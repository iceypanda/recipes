Caponata

Aubergine & corgette

  • Fry or roast at 200°C until brown on all sides
    • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 medium aubergines diced in 2cm pieces
    • 1 corgette chopped

Stew

  1. Fry for 10-15 mins until celery soft
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 large (4 small) onions chopped
    • 1 pepper chopped
    • 6 sticks celery chopped
  2. Add for 2 mins
    • 6 cloves garlic
    • 1 chilli
    • 1 anchovy (optional)
  3. Add and simmer for 15 mins with lid off
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 75g capers rinsed
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes (if no fresh chilli)
    • aubergine & corgette
  4. Add
    • 40ml red wine vinegar
    • 25g dark brown Muscavado sugar
    • ¼ tsp fish sauce
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • cracked black pepper
  5. Simmer gently for 30 mins with lid off until all liquid evapourated
  6. Scatter
    • 50g pine nuts toasted

Serving

  • orzo, couscous
  • 3 portions

Notes

  • Optional: 50g raisins (halve sugar), green olives, herbs, artichokes
  • Air fry veg on roast at 190°C for 20-24 mins
  • Original recipe
