Aubergine donburi

Sauce

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 4 tbsp cooking sake / sherry
    • 4 tbsp soy sauce
    • 2½ tbsp mirin
    • 2 chopped cloves garlic
    • pinch of ginger from topping

Topping

  1. Heat gently for 8 mins
    • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 6 chopped spring onions
    • 50g ginger chopped into matchsticks
  2. Remove heat and add
    • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
    • ½ tsp salt

Aubergines

  1. Cut 3 aubergines into slices lengthways
  2. Fry 3 mins each side till golden brown

Combine

  1. Place fried aubergine slices in layers in pan
  2. Pour over sauce and bubble for 2 mins
  3. Add water if required

Notes

  • Serve on top of rice
  • Original recipe
