Meringue

Whisking

  1. Whisk on medium speed until peaks forming
    • 4-5 egg whites room temperature
  2. Whisk on faster speed and add in 1 tbsp at a time, 5 seconds between each
    • 220g white caster sugar (1.22 x egg white weight)
    • 1 tsp cornflour
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  3. Whisk until soft peaks form and meringue is glossy

Serving

  • strawberries and raspberries
  • pear & toffee, cream around sides with chopped hazelnuts
  • tinned peaches and raspberries / blackberries

Notes

  • Whisk eggs whites at room temperature
  • 50-60g sugar per egg white
  • Ratio, egg white 1 : 1.22 sugar
  • Beading on meringue because sugar not dissolved in egg white, whisk for longer to allow time to dissolve
  • Acid prevents meringue breaking down after stiff peaks
  • Don’t go higher than medium-high on a stand mixer, you’ll get smaller and more stable bubbles in the foam if you don't go full speed
  • How to beat egg whites

Used in

  • Roulade
  • Pavlova
