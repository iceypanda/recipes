Coq au vin

Chicken

  1. Marinate in fridge for up to 12 hours
    • 600g chicken thighs
    • 500ml riesling
    • 4 cloves garlic halved lengthways
    • 1 bay leaf
    • thyme/dill/marjoram

Stew

  1. Fry on low heat till golden then remove
    • 150g bacon lardons
    • 15g butter
  2. Fry till soft then remove
    • 4 leeks sliced
    • 15g butter
  3. Fry till soft then remove
    • 250g chestnut mushrooms chopped
    • 10g butter
  4. Scatter and fry stirring constantly for 2 mins
    • 20g butter
    • 1½ tbsp plain flour
  5. Stir in
    • 200ml stock (1 salted stock cube)
  6. Mix in
    • chicken
    • wine
    • garlic
    • herbs
  7. Cook in oven at 160°C for 90 mins

Finish

  1. Remove chicken
  2. Mix in bowl
    • 100ml single cream
    • 1 egg yolk
  3. Stir into stew
  4. Simmer gently till sauce thickens
  5. Season to taste
    • salt
    • black pepper
  6. Stir in and warm
    • chicken
    • leeks
    • mushrooms
    • bacon

Serving

  • 5 portions

Notes

  • Brown chicken if skin on
  • Original recipe
