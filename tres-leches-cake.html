Tres leches cake

  • Preheat oven to 170°C the turn down to 160°C when put cake in
  • Baking
  • 4 large eggs / 5 medium eggs (304g with shells)
  • Cooked till 90°C but still some shrinkage, try 93°C for less shrinkage

Egg whites

  1. Whisk till foamy
    • 4 egg whites
  2. Whisk in one spoonful at a time
    • 150g caster sugar

Bake

  1. Mix for 2 mins
    • 4 egg yolks
    • 50g caster sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  2. Mix in
    • 100ml milk
  3. Sieve in gradually while combining with hand whisk
    • 200g self-raising sponge flour
  4. Add one spoonful of egg white to loosen
  5. Fold in rest of egg whites
  6. Pour mixture into 20x20cm cake tin lined with baking paper
  7. Bake at 160°C for 45 mins till skewer comes out clean or 92°C in centre

Milks

  1. Mix
    • 410g can evapourated milk
    • 200ml sweetened condensed milk
    • 45ml (45g) double cream

Milk cake

  1. Leave to cool for 10 mins
  2. Run knife around sides
  3. Poke 16 holes in surface with chop stick, one hole in centre of each piece
  4. Pour over half milk mixture
  5. Leave for 5 mins
  6. Transfer to plate
  7. Remove baking paper from underneath
  8. Pour over more milk till cake cannot absorb more
  9. Leave to cool
  10. Cut into 16
  11. Pour over rest of milk mixture
  12. Whisk to soft peaks
    • 235ml double cream
    • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  13. Spread cream over cake
  14. Sprinkle with a little cinnamon
  15. Transfer to container
