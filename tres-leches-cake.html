Tres leches cake

  • Preheat oven to 160°C

Egg whites

  1. Whisk until foamy
    • 4 egg whites
  2. Whisk in one spoonful at a time
    • 150g caster sugar

Bake

  1. Mix for 2 mins
    • 4 egg yolks
    • 50g caster sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  2. Fold in
    • 200g plain flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 100ml milk
  3. Add one spoonful of egg white to loosen
  4. Fold in rest of egg whites
  5. Pour mixture into 20x20cm cake tin lined with baking paper
  6. Bake at 160°C for 40-45 mins until skewer comes out clean

Milks

  1. Mix
    • 410g can evapourated milk
    • 200ml sweetened condensed milk
    • 45ml (45g) double cream

Milk cake

  1. Leave to cool for 10 mins
  2. Run knife around sides
  3. Poke holes in surface with skewer
  4. Pour over half milk mixture
  5. Leave for 5 mins
  6. Transfer to plate
  7. Pour over more milk until cake cannot absorb more
  8. Leave to cool
  9. Cut into 16
  10. Pour over rest of milk mixture
  11. Whisk to soft peaks
    • 235ml double cream
    • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  12. Spread cream over cake
  13. Sprinkle with a little cinnamon
