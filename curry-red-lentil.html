Fish/chicken red lentil curry

Lentils

  • Soak for 1-3 hours
    • 350g water
    • 150g red lentils

Curry

  1. Fry till seeds start to sizzle
    • vegetable oil
    • 1 tsp black mustard seeds
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 1 red onion
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 250g passata
    • 10g ginger
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1½ tbsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • ¾ tsp chilli powder
  4. Add and simmer for 15 mins till lentils are soft
    • ½ tin coconut milk
    • 150ml single cream (or whole tin coconut milk)
    • lentils & water
    • 1 tin chickpeas (optional)
    • squash roasted
    • 1 cauliflower roasted
    • ½ tsp salt

Serving

  • Rice, bread, mango chutney

Notes

  • Lentils will thicken the sauce when cooked
  • White fish: hake, cod, haddock
  • Original recipe
