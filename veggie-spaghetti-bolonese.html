Veggie spaghetti Bolonese

  1. Heat for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped fine
    • 1 carrot chopped fine
    • 2 sticks celery chopped fine
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add and reduce for 2 mins
    • 187ml red wine
  4. Add
    • 300g veg mince
    • 100g mushrooms chopped
    • 1 aubergine chopped
    • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
    • 1 stock cube in 200ml water
  5. Add
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 tsp oregano
    • 3 bay leaves
  6. Bring to boil then simmer for 45 mins

Serving

  • Mix in chopped fresh basil
  • Pasta / buckwheat

Notes

  • Could slow cook for 4 hours, add everything from mince onwards while soffritto is cooking
  • Original recipe
