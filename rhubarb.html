Rhubarb

Roasted

  1. Wash and dry 500g rhubarb
  2. Cut into 3cm pieces
  3. Toss with
    • 100g golden caster sugar
    • orange rind strips from potato peeler (optional)
  4. Leave to mixture to stand for hour (optional)
  5. Roast at 180°C for 15 mins in air fryer

Notes

  • recommended sugar from 100g - 300g

Stewed

  1. Heat at 170°C for 10-15 mins
    • 200g rhubarb (how many sticks?)
    • 55g caster sugar
    • juice of ½ lemon
  2. Remove from oven and leave for two hours

Serve with

  • crepes
  • sugar custards
