Tuna, mushroom & sweetcorn quesadillas

Filling

  • For 1 quesadilla
  1. Heat in pan for 8 mins
    • olive oil
    • 50g mushrooms sliced
    • ½ pepper chopped
  2. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 50g sweetcorn
  3. Mix in and warm for 2 mins
    • 1 small tin (102g drained weight) tuna
    • 6 tbsp salsa
  4. Add
    • 50g cheese grated
