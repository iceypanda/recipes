Parkin

  1. Heat gently in pan
    • 225g unsalted butter
    • 175g golden syrup
    • 100g black treacle
    • 85g light muscavado sugar
  2. Mix in bowl
    • 250g plain flour
    • 100g oatmeal
    • 1 tbsp ground ginger
    • 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  3. Combine butter and flour mixtures
  4. Mix separately
    • 1 egg
    • 3 tbsp milk
  5. Mix egg and milk into main mixture
  6. Pour into 3" tall 20 x 20cm tin lined with baking paper, with sides buttered
  7. Bake for 60 mins at 140°C, do not open oven or move tray
  8. Cool in tin for 1 hour

Serving

  • Calories = 4180, 1/16 = 260
  • 16 portions

Notes

  • do not open oven while cooking or middle will collapse
