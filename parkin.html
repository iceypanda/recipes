Parkin

  1. Heat gently in pan
    • 225g butter
    • 175g golden syrup
    • 100g black treacle
    • 85g light muscavado sugar
  2. Mix in bowl
    • 250g plain flour
    • 100g oatmeal
    • 1 tbsp ground ginger
    • 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  3. Combine butter and flour mixtures
  4. Mix separately
    • 1 egg
    • 3 tbsp milk
  5. Mix egg and milk into main mixture
  6. Pour into lined 23x23cm tin and bake for 50-60 mins at 140°C until firm and a little crusty on top
  7. Cool in tin

Serving

  • Calories = 4180, 1/16 = 260
  • 16 portions
