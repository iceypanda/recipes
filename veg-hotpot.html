Vegetable hotpot

Potatoes

  1. Boil in water for 2 mins to soften
    • 900g (after peeling) potatoes peeled and sliced with mandoline 2
  2. Allow to cool so easier to handle

Filling

    1. Heat for 8 mins
    • olive oil
    • 2 onions
  2. Add and cook for 10 mins with lid on
    • 4 carrots diced
    • ½ swede diced
    • 200g parsnips diced
  3. Sprinkle 25g plain flour and stir to coat veg
  4. Add and simmer for 20 mins
    • 650ml stock
    • 55g pearl barley
    • 3 sprigs thyme
    • ½ tsp thyme
    • ¼ garlic salt
    • ¼ celery salt

Hotpot

  1. Pour filling into oven dish
  2. Arrange potato slices on top, seasoning between layers
  3. Pour over 25g melted butter
  4. Cook at 180°C for 45-50 mins till potatoes are golden brown
