Creme caramel

  1. Preheat oven to 120°C
  2. Heat 175g caster sugar gently in stainless steel pan until dissolved
  3. Turn up heat and boil without stirring until liquid turns golden brown
  4. Stir periodically for 15 mins with wooden spoon until turned colour of dark honey
  5. Take off heat and add 2 tbsp warm water then heat to dissolve any lumps
  6. Pour into 8 inch cake tin and allow to set
  7. Butter sides of tin
  8. Whisk by hand
    • 50g caster sugar
    • 4 eggs
    • 2 egg yolks
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Heat 600ml full-fat milk until hot to touch
  10. Pour milk into bowl with eggs and whisk rapidly
  11. Pour custard through sieve into caramel tin
  12. Put caramel tin onto 4 sheets of baking paper into a roasting tin
  13. Pour boiling water in roasting tin so halfway up sides of caramel tin
  14. Put tin in preheated 120°C oven for 50-60 mins
  15. Leave caramel tin in roasting tin for 30 mins to cool
  16. Chill in fridge for 12 hours
  17. Run knife around caramel tin and invert onto a plate

Notes

Cake tin must not touch roasting tin as this will conduct too much heat (also why water added to tin)

Caramel will not brown in non-stick saucepan

Eggy taste of creme goes after 48 hours
