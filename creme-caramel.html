Creme caramel

  • Preheat oven to 150°C
  • Set custard

Try

  • 2 large eggs & 1 yolk
  • 5 ramekins
  • Custard with cream

Caramel

  1. Make caramel with
    • 80g white caster sugar
  2. Divide caramel into 4 ramekins to set

Custard

Custard with condensed milk

  1. Mix gently with fork to prevent bubbles forming
    • 397g condensed milk
    • 257g (247g if large eggs) whole milk warmed
    • 3 medium eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  2. Strain custard into 1L jug
  3. Pour into 4 larger ramekins
  4. Pop any air bubbles on top with kitchen towel

Custard with cream

  • Only warm the cream enough to dissolve the sugar so the eggs don't cook when combined
  1. Warm in pan until sugar dissolved
    • 300ml single cream
    • 250g (240g if large eggs) milk warmed
    • 40g light brown sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • zest 1 lemon/lime (optional)
  2. Mix in jug
    • 4 medium eggs / 3 large eggs beaten
  3. Gradually whisk cream mixture into the eggs
  4. Pour egg/cream mixture back into pan and mix
  5. Sieve custard back into jug for pouring into 6 ramekins

Cooking

  • While cooking
    • Foil must not touch custard or might burn
  1. Space out ramekins in baking dish
  2. Cover ramekins with large foil squares
  3. Pour in 1l warm water (~70C) up to half way on ramekins
  4. Heat in oven at 150°C for 50-60 mins

Cooling

  1. Take ramekins out of water bath and place on cooling rack
  2. Cool at room temperature for 2 hours without foil covers
  3. Cool in fridge overnight with cling film square covers

Serving

  1. Go round custard to bottom of ramekin with sharp knife to help release
  2. Place serving plate on top of ramekins
  3. Turnover and move quickly to side to release, might take >1 tries

Notes

  • Water necessary around ramekins or caramel will bubble underneath custard
  • Can replace some milk with cream
  • Creme will wobble a lot when removed from oven, trust it will set when cooled
  • Whole eggs make firmer set than just yolks
  • Caramel is sweet so less sugar needed in creme, condensed milk is already sweetened
  • Zest lemon/lime before cutting for juice
