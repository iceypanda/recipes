Lokanta stew

  1. Heat for 1 hour in roasting tin in oven preheated to 180C
    • 1 onion
    • 3 red and yellow peppers
    • 200g assorted tomatoes
    • 10 thyme sprigs
    • 4 tbsp olive oil
  2. Add, mix well and cook for 30 mins
    • 1 tin butter beans
    • 1 tsp ground allspice
  3. Add 180g cooked green beans
  4. Season with salt and ground black pepper

Serve with

  • Couscous
