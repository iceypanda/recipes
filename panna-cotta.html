Panna cotta

Panna cotta

  1. Warm in microwave in Pyrex jug
    • 150ml double cream
    • 75ml (77g) semi-skimmed milk
    • 25g caster sugar
    • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  2. Whisk in until dissolved
    • 4.0g gelatine powder
  3. Allow mixture to cool but not set
  4. Pour into 4 small ramekins
  5. Cover and chill for 2-3 hours

Raspberry panna cotta

  1. Mash and sieve to remove pips
    • 160g raspberries
  2. Combine with
    • 225ml cream & milk
    • 25g honey

Notes

  • Made: 22 Jul 2023, very firm with 10g gelatine & 500ml mixture
  • 2:1:0.33 cream:milk:sugar
