Orange & poppy seed pancakes

  1. Blend until frothy
    • 4 egg whites
    • 1 tsp vanilla paste
  2. Blend in
    • 3 tbsp oats ground to flour
    • zest ½ orange
    • juice ½ orange
    • zest ½ lemon
    • juice ½ lemon
    • 1 tbsp poppy seeds
  3. Fold in
    • 3 tbsp cottage cheese
  4. Garnish with
    • 8 pistachios chopped
    • 1 tsp yogurt

Serving

  • 1 portion
