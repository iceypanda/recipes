Sticky salmon

Salmon

  1. Mix
    • 1 clove garlic grated
    • 1 piece ginger grated
    • 1 red chilli chopped
    • 2 tsp honey
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
  2. Coat
    • 2 salmon fillets
  3. Bake at 160°C for 15 mins

Salad

  1. Mix
    • 100g bulgar cooked
    • ½ carrot diced
    • 2 spring onions chopped
  2. Place on top
    • salmon
  3. Garnish
    • lemon juice
    • black pepper

Serving

  • 2 portions
