Creme caramel

  • Preheat oven to 160°C

Caramel

  • While warming
    • Do not stir or mix
    • Use silicon brush in water to clean sides of pan to prevent sugar burning
    • Swirl gently if need to mix
  1. Warm in pan on medium heat for 10-12 mins until medium amber
    • 110g caster sugar
    • 3 tbsp water
    • squeeze lemon juice
  2. Pour caramel into 4 ramekins and leave to cool in fridge

Custard

  1. Warm in pan to dissolve sugar and melt chococlate, don't boil or bubbles will form
    • 397g condensed milk
    • 400ml (410g) whole milk
    • 50g caster sugar / white chocolate
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • pinch salt
  2. Mix gently with fork to prevent bubbles forming
    • 3 large eggs
  3. Pour milk slowly into the eggs
  4. Strain custard
  5. Pour into 4 larger ramekins lightly buttered or oiled
  6. Pop any air bubbles on top with kitchen towel

Cooking

  1. Put ramekins in baking dish with higher sides
  2. Cover ramekins with foil
  3. Pour in warm water (~70C) up to half way on ramekins
  4. Heat in oven at 160°C for 45 mins

Cooling

  1. Take ramekins out of water bath to cool
  2. Cool at room temperature for 2 hours
  3. Cool in fridge overnight

Serving

  1. Push round top of custard with fingers to make release easier
  2. Place serving plate on top of ramekins
  3. Turnover and move quickly to side to release, might take >1 tries
