Creme caramel

  • Preheat oven to 150°C

Caramel

  • While warming
    • Do not stir or mix
    • Use silicon brush in water to clean sides of pan to prevent sugar burning
    • Swirl gently if need to mix
  1. Warm in pan on medium heat for 10-12 mins until medium amber
    • 70g white caster sugar
    • 2½ tbsp water
    • squeeze lemon juice (optional)
  2. When dark add one at a time 2 tbsp cold water to stop cooking
  3. Quickly divide caramel into 4 ramekins while still bubbling
  4. Leave to cool in fridge

Custard

  1. Warm in pan to dissolve sugar and melt chococlate, don't boil or bubbles will form
    • 397g condensed milk
    • 300ml (310g) whole milk
    • 20g golden caster sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • pinch salt
  2. Mix gently with fork to prevent bubbles forming
    • 3 medium eggs
  3. Pour milk slowly into the eggs stiring with fork
  4. Strain custard
  5. Pour into 4 larger ramekins lightly buttered or oiled
  6. Pop any air bubbles on top with kitchen towel

Cooking

  1. Put ramekins in baking dish with higher sides
  2. Cover ramekins with foil
  3. Pour in warm water (~70C) up to half way on ramekins
  4. Heat in oven at 150°C for 45 mins

Cooling

  1. Take ramekins out of water bath to cool
  2. Cool at room temperature for 2 hours without foil covers
  3. Cool in fridge overnight with cling film square covers

Serving

  1. Push round top of custard with sharp knife to make release easier
  2. Place serving plate on top of ramekins
  3. Turnover and move quickly to side to release, might take >1 tries

Notes

  • Use white sugar not golden so can tell when colour changes
  • Can use white chocolate instead of sugar
  • Creme is eggy if overcooked
  • Whole eggs make firmer set than just yolks
  • Caramel is sweet so less sugar needed in creme
  • Changes
    • Eggs: from 3 large eggs
    • Temperature: from 160°C
    • Milk: from 400ml because leftover custard
    • Caramel: sugar from 100g
  • Made: 25 Mar 2023, 160°C 45 mins, used sweetened evapourated milk which was too sweet
