Creme caramel

  • Preheat oven to 140°C

Caramel

- 3 tbsp water + 1/2 cup sugar + lemon juice = medoium amber; 2:1 sugar:water; medium heat; don't mix or get sugar on sides which will burn; silicon brush in water to clean sides; 10-12 mins; can swirl a little - sugar should set in bottom

Custard

custard cold -> strain custard and push through - pinch salt + 50g white chocolate + sugar and warm to melt and dissolve - don't boil milk because don't want bubbles - mix eggs but don't put air in - add milk to eggs and vanilla, don't use whisk use spoon so no bubbles

Cooking

- pop air bubbles on top - half way with hot water ~70C - cover dish with foil skewer should come out clean

Cooling

- out of water bath to stop cooking - cool room temp 2 hours, straight in fridge and get air bubbles - best to cool overnight

Serving

- push round top with fingers to make easier to release - place pasta bowl on top - plate on top then move quickly to side to release
