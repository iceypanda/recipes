Crab lettuce wraps

  1. Add to bowl
    • 1 grapefruit chopped in small pieces
  2. Mix in
    • 2 portions white crab
    • 1 avocado chopped
    • 1 red chilli chopped
    • 2 tbsp Thai basil
    • juice 1 lime
    • black pepper
  3. Spoon into
    • 6 leaves little gem lettuce

Serving

  • 2 portions
