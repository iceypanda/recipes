Quesadillas - Chicken

  1. Spread 2 tbsp each of hot salsa onto 2 tortillas
  2. Top one tortilla with
    • 50g cooked chicken
    • 1 tin of mashed kidney beans
    • 1 spring onion
    • 85g grated Cheddar cheese
    • fresh chopped coriander
  3. Heat in pan on medium heat for 4 mins each side

Notes

To turn over slide onto plate then place under frying pan and flip
