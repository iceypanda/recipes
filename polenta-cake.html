Lemon polenta cake

Cake

  1. Sift into bowl
    • 175g polenta
    • 50g plain flour
    • 1½ tsp baking powder
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Mix in cup
    • 5 tbsp (71g) sunflower oil / vegetable oil
    • 5 tbsp (85g) natural yogurt
    • 2 tbsp lemon juice
    • zest of 2 lemons
  3. Beat in separate bowl until creamy
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 egg whites
    • 200g caster sugar
  4. Beat yogurt mixture into egg mixture until smooth
  5. Fold in polenta mixture
  6. Pour into 1.2l loaf tin, oiled and lined with baking paper
  7. Bake at 160°C for 40-45 mins until skewer comes out clean
  8. Cool cake on wire rack for 15 mins
  9. Slice in half then quarters then twelths

Syrup

  1. Bring to boil and simmer for 10 mins
    • 30g caster sugar
    • 30ml water
    • 4 sprigs rosemary lightly bashed

Serving

  • Make holes in the cooled cake with a skewer then pour over syrup through a strainer
  • Calories = 2500 cake + 120 syrup, 1/12 = 220
