Lemon polenta cake

Cake

  1. Sift into bowl
    • 175g polenta
    • 50g plain flour
    • 1½ tsp baking powder
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Mix in cup
    • 5 tbsp sunflower oil / vegetable oil
    • 5 tbsp natural yogurt
    • 2 tbsp lemon juice
    • zest of 2 lemons
  3. Beat in separate bowl until creamy
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 egg whites
    • 200g caster sugar
  4. Beat in yogurt mixture until smooth
  5. Fold in polenta mixture
  6. Pour into 1.2l loaf tin, oiled and lined with baking paper
  7. Bake at 160°C for 40-45 mins until skewer comes out clean
  8. Cool cake on wire rack for 15 mins

Syrup

  1. Bring to boil and simmer for 10 mins
    • 100g caster sugar
    • 100ml water
    • 2 sprigs rosemary lightly bashed

Serving

  • Pour syrup over cooled cake
  • Calories = 2500 cake, 390 syrup
