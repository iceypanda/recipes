Prawn & tomato stew

Cherry tomatoes

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 250g cherry tomatoes
  2. Add to hot frying pan and heat on high for 7 mins until charred
  3. Set aside

Prawns

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 400g king prawns patted dry
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Fry until cooked

Tomato sauce

  1. Cook until softened and browned
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 15g ginger grated
    • 1 green chilli finely chopped
    • 2 tsp coriander seeds ground in pestle and mortar
    • 1 ½ tsp cumin seeds crushed in pestle and mortar
    • 8 cardamom pods bashed in pestle and mortar
    • 20g dill finely chopped
    • 2 tsp tomato puree
  3. Add and cook for 25 mins until sauce has thickened
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 2 tomatoes chopped
    • 100ml stock (½ cube)
  4. Add prawns and cherry tomatoes

Coriander pesto

  1. Blitz until pine nuts crumbled
    • 30g coriander chopped
    • 1 green chilli chopped
    • 50g pine nuts toasted
  2. Mix with
    • 80ml olive oil
    • zest of 1 lemon
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • black pepper

Serving

  • broad bean & dill rice
