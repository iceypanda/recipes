Potato raclette

Potatoes

  1. Cut 250g unpeeled (per circle) potatoes into ¾cm slices
  2. Boil for 10-15 mins until cooked
  3. Cut into cubes and remove excess skin

Circles

  1. Mix
    • potatoes
    • 1 tsp (per circle) whole-grain mustard
    • 40g (per circle) Gruyere / Cheddar grated
    • black pepper
  2. Place two layers in circles and top each with more cheese
  3. Cook in oven at 180°C for 15 mins

Serving

  • Smoked salmon
  • Pan-fried cod
  • Seared tuna

Notes

  • 1 tbsp English mustard for 2 circles overpowered relish, don't use mustard in this case
  • Plenty of cheese as will melt to much less volume
