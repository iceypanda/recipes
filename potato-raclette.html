Potato raclette

Potatoes

  1. Mix
    • 500g baked new potatoes diced
    • 2 tbsp whole-grain mustard
    • rosemary chopped
    • seasoning
  2. Place in circles and top each with
    • Gruyere grated
  3. Warm in oven at 160°C until melted

Pepper dressing

  1. Mix
    • 1 roasted red pepper in olive oil, diced
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Serving

  • smoked salmon

Notes

  • plenty of cheese as will melt to much less volume
