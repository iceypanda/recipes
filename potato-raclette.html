Potato raclette

Potatoes

  1. Mix
    • 250g baked new potatoes diced
    • 3 tbsp whole-grain mustard
    • rosemary chopped
    • seasoning
  2. Place in circles and top each with
    • Gruyere grated
  3. Warm in oven at 160°C until melted

Pepper dressing

  1. Mix
    • 1 roasted red pepper in olive oil, diced
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Serve with

  • smoked salmon
