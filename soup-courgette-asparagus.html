Courgette, asparagus & pea soup

  1. Cook on low heat for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 courgette diced
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 250g asparagus chopped
  4. Add and bring to boil, then turn off heat
    • 300g peas defrosted
    • 750ml veg stock
    • ½ tsp herb (fresh tarragon?)
  5. Blend half of soup

Notes

  • Original recipe
  • too much pepper so removed
  • originally with cinnamon, try with herbs instead
