Mushroom & Parmigiano Reggiano pasta

    Hazlenuts

  1. Roast 40g hazelnuts or walnuts at 180°C for 15 min, then chop

    2. Sauce

  2. Melt 10g butter in pan
  3. Gradually sprinkle and stir in 10g plain flour to make roux, then cook for 1 min
  4. Slowly stir in 250ml warmed milk and stir until sauce thickens (lower heat if sauce bubbles)
  5. Add 60g grated Parmigiano Reggiano

    6. Mushrooms

  6. Heat for 10 mins
    • butter or olive oil
    • 200g of mixed mushrooms
    • 1 chopped shallot
    • ½ tsp thyme

    7. Pasta

  7. Mix sauce, nuts and mushrooms with 200g pasta

Notes

  • Use tagliatelle or parpadelle
  • Serve with chopped veggie sausages
  • Original recipe
