Mushroom & Parmigiano Reggiano pasta

    Hazlenuts

  1. Roast 40g hazelnuts or walnuts at 180°C for 15 min, then chop

    2. Mushrooms

  2. Heat for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 200g mixed mushrooms
    • 1 shallot chopped
    • ½ tsp thyme

    3. Sauce

  3. Melt 15g butter in pan on lowest heat
  4. Gradually sprinkle and stir in 15g plain flour to make roux, then cook for 1 min
  5. Slowly stir in 250ml warmed milk and stir until sauce thickens (lower heat if sauce bubbles)
  6. Add 60g Parmigiano Reggiano or Gruyere grated
  7. Remove from heat and add mushrooms and hazelnuts

    8. Pasta

  8. Mix sauce, nuts and mushrooms with
    • 200g tagliatelle or parpadelle
    • pasta cooking water (if required)

Serve with

  • ground pepper
  • parsely sprinkled for colour
  • chopped sausages

Notes

  • need to add some milk before all flour or else dough forms
  • hazelnuts will burn and be bitter if overcooked, check every few mins
  • parpadelle best
