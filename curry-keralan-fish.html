Keralan fish curry

  1. Heat until seeds begin to pop
    • vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds
    • 15 dried curry leaves
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 4 shallots
    • 20g ginger
    • 1 green chilli
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • ½ tsp turmeric
  4. Add and simmer for 20 mins until fish cooked
    • 300g white fish cut into chunks
    • ½ tin coconut milk
    • ½ tin tomatoes

Notes

  • 280g frozen pollock good
  • 10 curry leaves if fresh
  • Original recipe
