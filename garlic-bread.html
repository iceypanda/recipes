Garlic bread

  • Preheat oven to 220°C

Bread

  1. Slice lengthways across widest diameter
    • soft french stick
  2. Cut bread into slices

Garlic bread

  1. Heat in pan until butter melts
    • 60ml olive oil
    • 55g butter
  2. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 6 cloves garlic chopped (optional)
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
  3. Remove heat
  4. Dip each slice of bread in garlic butter and arrange on baking tray
  5. Sprinkle over each slice
    • parmesan grated
    • salt
  6. Heat in oven at 220°C for 10 mins until toasted
