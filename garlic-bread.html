Garlic bread

Bread

  1. Slice lengthways across widest diameter
    • soft french stick
  2. Cut bread into slices

Garlic bread

  1. Heat in pan until butter melts
    • 60ml olive oil
    • 55g butter
  2. Add and heat for 1 min (garlic will cook when toasted)
    • 6 cloves garlic minced
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
  3. Remove heat
  4. Dip each slice of bread in garlic butter and arrange on baking tray
  5. Sprinkle over each slice
    • parmesan grated
    • salt
  6. Toast
    • Oven, 200°C for 10 mins
    • Air fryer,180°C for 3-4 mins

Notes

  • 15g oil & 15g butter per large slice or 2 french stick slices
