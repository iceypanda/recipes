Stilton & fig wholemeal scones

Flour

  1. Mix in big bowl
    • 225g wholemeal flour
    • 225g self-raising flour
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp cracked black pepper
  2. Rub in until crumbs
    • 110g unsalted butter cubed and chilled
  3. Mix in
    • 150g stilton chopped
    • 6 dried figs roughly chopped

Batter

  1. Mix
    • 1 egg
    • 150ml whole milk
  2. Reserve 1 tbsp of milk mixture
  3. Pour milk mixture into flour and mix with metal spoon careful not to overwork dough
  4. Shape dough on surface so 3 cm thick
  5. Cut dough into 7cm circles and arrange on tray lined with baking paper
  6. Brush top of scones with milk mixture
  7. Bake in preheated oven at 160°C for 16-20 mins
  8. Cool on rack

Serving

  • Winter tomato soup

Notes

  • Can use dates / apricots instead of figs
  • Original recipe
