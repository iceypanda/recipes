King of puddings

Custard

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C
  2. Spread 6 tbsp of jam over bottom of pie dish
  3. Mix
    • 600ml (620g) whole milk
    • 30g caster sugar
    • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  4. Separate 3 eggs
  5. Slowly mix in
    • 3 egg yolks
    • 1 whole egg
    • ½ cup (40g) of fine breadcrumbs
  6. Stir well and then pour mixture over jam
  7. Bake in oven for 1 hour or until set

Meringue

  1. Whisk with stand mixer (4-5) to soft peaks
    • 3 egg whites
    • ½ tsp white wine vinegar
    • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  2. Add slowly and whisk for few mins to stiff peaks
    • 40g caster sugar
  3. Spread over base while hot to seal the meringue to the base
  4. Bake in oven for 20 mins at 150°C until meringue is set and lightly browned
  5. Leave to cool for 2 hours to set, then chill in fridge for 1 hour

Notes

  • Made: 10 Sep 2022, blackcurrant jam, 40g sugar in meringue, 25g sugar in milk = almost no sweetness
  • try 100ml single cream for milk, 50g breadcrumbs
