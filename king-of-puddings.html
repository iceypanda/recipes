King of puddings

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C
  2. Spread 4 tbsp of jam over bottom of pie dish
  3. Separate 3 eggs
  4. Mix yolks with 1 whole egg
  5. Mix with:
    • 2½ cups of milk
    • ¼ cup of sugar
  6. Slowly mix in ½ cup of fine breadcrumbs
  7. Pour mixture over jam
  8. Bake in oven for 1 hour or until set
  9. Whisk egg whites then slowly add 100g sugar
  10. Bake in oven for 15-20 mins until meringue is set and lightly browned
