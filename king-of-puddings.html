King of puddings

Custard

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C
  2. Spread 5 tbsp of jam over bottom of pie dish
  3. Separate 3 eggs
  4. Mix yolks with 1 whole egg
  5. Mix with
    • 600ml (680g) whole milk
    • ¼ cup (42g) caster sugar
  6. Slowly mix in ½ cup of fine breadcrumbs
  7. Pour mixture over jam
  8. Bake in oven for 1 hour or until set

Meringue

  1. Whisk egg whites (in food processor on 6) then slowly add 50g caster sugar as meringue starts to form but before stiff peaks form
  2. Spread over base while hot to seal the meringue to the base
  3. Bake in oven for 20 mins until meringue is set and lightly browned
  4. Leave to cool for 2 hours to set, then chill in fridge for 1 hour

Notes

  • Beading on meringue because sugar not dissolved in egg white, whisk for longer to allow time to dissolve
  • Increased jam from 4 tbsp
  • Reduced sugar from 100g in meringue
