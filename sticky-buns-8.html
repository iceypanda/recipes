Sticky buns

Buttermilk

    Whisk
    • 100ml (103g) milk
    • 20ml (20g) single cream
    • ½ tsp lemon juice / white vinegar
  2. Warm in microwave
  3. Whisk in
    • 7g packet dried yeast

Dough

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 2 large eggs
  2. Add and whisk in
    • buttermilk mixture
    • 55g light brown sugar
    • ½ tsp salt
  3. Gradually whisk in
    • 60ml (55g) unsalted butter melted
  4. Mix in
    • 480g flour
  5. Knead dough for 2 mins to combine
  6. Leave to rise in bowl for 2 hours at room temperature till volume doubled

Baking dish sauce

  1. Heat in pan until sugar disolved and mixture bubbling
    • 90g light brown sugar
    • 40ml (37g) unsalted butter melted
  2. Mix in
    • 35ml (34g) single cream
    • ⅔ cup pecans toasted, chopped (optional)
  3. Place small ramekin face-down in one corner of 23x23cm (9") baking tray
  4. Pour sauce evenly over base of baking tray

Cinnamon sugar

  • Mix
    • 90g light brown sugar
    • 2 tsp ground cinnamon

Buns

  1. Roll out dough to 12"x12" with short edge towards you
  2. Brush with leaving 1" border at top
    • 18g unsalted butter melted
  3. Sprinkle evenly with cinnamon sugar
  4. Roll up tightly using bench scraper to help
  5. Put seam side down and even out shape
  6. Cut into 8 slices by sawing with sharp chef's knife
  7. Arrange in sauced baking tray
  8. Cover with cling film
  9. Leave for 2 hours till volume doubled

Baking

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Cook for 30 mins rotating tray once
  3. Rest for 5 mins
  4. Invert buns onto rack and cover with scraped out sauce

Notes

  • Increased cream and reduced sugar in sauce
  • Buns go hard quickly next day
  • Original recipe: The Food Lab, p170
  • Made: 8 Feb 2023
