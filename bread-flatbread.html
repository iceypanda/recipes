Flatbreads

Plain flatbread

  1. Knead in bowl in make dough
    • 250g plain flour
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp maple syrup
    • 1½ tsp baking powder
    • ½ tsp salt
    • 170ml water
  2. Leave to stand for 10 mins
  3. Divide into 6 pieces
  4. Gently flatten each piece
  5. Cook on skillet for 2-3 mins each side

Notes

  • Thicker pieces after rolling with make fluffier bread

Yogurt flatbread

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 300g self-raising flour
    • 300g natural yogurt
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • ½ tsp caster sugar
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Knead dough to bring together
  3. Divide into 8 balls
  4. Flatten each ball with a rolling pin until 2-3mm thick (or maybe better to do by hand so less even)
  5. Cook each flatbread on gridle pan on high heat for 2-3 mins on each side
  6. Brush each flatbread with melted butter when cooked

Notes

  • Make more or less by changing amount of flour and yogurt - 150g for 4 people
  • Can add fresh herbs to butter, e.g. parsley, tarragon, basil, dill
  • Added salt and sugar

Coconut flatbread

  1. Knead in bowl in make dough
    • 200g tin coconut milk
    • 220g self-raising flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Divide into 8
  3. Cook on skillet for 2-3 mins each side in butter

Notes

  • Make bread when need it so doesn't dry out

Chapati

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 70g wholemeal flour
    • 70g plain flour
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 90ml warm water
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Knead dough to bring together
  3. Divide into 4 balls
  4. Flatten each ball with a rolling pin until very thin
  5. Cook each chapati in oiled frying pan until has brown spots on underside, about 30 secs

Notes

  • halve amounts to make 2 for lunch
  • Original recipe

Tortilla

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 200g plain flour
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 100ml warm water
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Knead dough to bring together
  3. Divide into 8 balls
  4. Flatten each ball with a rolling pin until very thin
  5. Cook each flatbread in oiled frying pan for 2 mins each side
