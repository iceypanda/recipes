Red lentil stew

Lentils

  1. Chop tomatoes and celery before starting
  2. Rinse 1 cup dry red lentils until water clear
  3. Add 2 cups water and bring to boil then turn off heat and leave for 10 mins with lid on

Soup

  1. Saute in large pot
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 4 cloves garlic
  2. Add 3 stalks celery untils softens
  3. Stir in
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 1 tsp chilli powder
    • ½ tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp turmeric
    • 5 dashes hot sauce
  4. Add
    • drained cooked lentils
    • 1l vegetable stock
  5. Simmer for 15 mins then remove from heat
  6. Add
    • chopped fresh coriander
    • juice from ½ lime
