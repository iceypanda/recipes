Chana masala

Puree

  1. Blitz in food processor until puree
    • 2 chopped onions
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • thumb sized piece of chopped ginger

Sauce

  1. Heat until seeds begin to pop
    • vegetable oil
    • 2 tsp black mustard seeds
  2. Add puree and simmer gently for 15 mins until thickened
  3. Add and heat for 15 mins until thickened
    • tin of chopped tomatoes
    • 2 tins of chickpeas
    • ½ tsp brown sugar
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  4. Add
    • ½ tsp garam masala
    • juice of ¼ lemon
    • chopped bunch of coriander
