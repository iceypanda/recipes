Tamarind sauce

Sauce

  1. Heat for 10 mins
    • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
    • 1 onion
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 20g chopped ginger
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins stirring constantly
    • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
    • 1 chopped chilli
    • 2 tsp garam masala
    • ½ tsp turmeric
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins stirring
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
    • ½ tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp salt
  4. Add 200ml water, bring to simmer and cook for 5 mins
  5. Blitz sauce till smooth

Notes

Serve with duck
