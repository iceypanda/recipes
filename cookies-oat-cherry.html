Oaty cherry cookies

  1. Beat together until fluffy
    • 250g butter softened
    • 50g caster sugar
    • 100g light muscavado sugar
  2. Mix in
    • 225g oats
    • 150g self-raising flour
  3. Stir in
    • 200g glace cherries ¾ chopped
    • 50g raisins
  4. Divide into 18 balls and arrange on 2 baking trays
  5. Lightly flatten each ball
  6. Bake at 160°C for 15-20 mins
  7. Allow to cool on the sheets then on trays

Serving

  • Calories = 4520, 1/18 = 250
