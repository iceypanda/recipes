Fluffy pancakes

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 135g self-raising flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 2 tsp golden caster sugar
  2. Whisk in
    • 2 large eggs
    • 16g butter melted
    • 135ml (139g) milk
  3. Cook 10cm rounds for 1 min 30 secs on medium-low heat on each side
  4. Stack pancakes on plate in oven to keep warm
  5. Serve when all cooked

Serving

  • 2 portions
