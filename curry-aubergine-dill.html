Aubergine & dill curry

  1. Heat for 2 mins
    • 4 tbsp oil
    • 5 cloves garlic
  2. Add and cook for 15 mins (try less mins instead)
    • tin of chopped tomatoes
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • 1 ¼ tsp chilli powder
    • 1 ¼ tsp dried dill
    • 1 ¼ tsp curry powder
  4. Add 3 aubergines chopped into semi-circles and cook for 20 mins, stirring every 5 mins
  5. Add tin of black-eyed beans and cook for 3 mins until hot
  6. Add 40g chopped dill
  7. Serve with yogurt and naan or chapatis

Notes

Added extra dill when cooking

Added curry powder and more turmeric
