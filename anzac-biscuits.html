Anzac biscuits

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 85g porridge oats
    • 85g desiccated coconut
    • 100g plain flour
    • 100g caster sugar
  2. Melt gently in pan
    • 100g butter
    • 1 tbsp golden syrup
  3. Mix in cup
    • 2 tbsp boiling water
    • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  4. Stir soda water into butter mixture
  5. Make well in dry ingredients and pour in butter mixture
  6. Mix well
  7. Put dessert spoons of mixture onto trays lined with baking paper
  8. Bake for 9-11 mins at 160°C
  9. Cool on wire rack

Serving

  • Calories = 2150, 1/20 = 110
  • 20 portions

Notes

  • made: 5 Oct 2022
  • Why soda into boiling water? try mixing with dry ingredients next time
