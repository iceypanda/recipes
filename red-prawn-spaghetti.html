Red prawn spaghetti

Prawns

  1. Brine and alkalise prawns

Broccoli

  1. Heat pan of water to boiling
  2. Cook for 2-3 mins
    • 100g sprouting broccoli thick stems sliced in half
  3. Drain
  4. Place in bowl and cover with plate
  5. When tender rinse with cold water to cool

Spaghetti

  1. Heat pan of fresh water to boiling
  2. Cook 190g spaghetti
  3. Save some of the water after draining

Sauce

  1. Fry on medium heat for 8 mins until almost cooked
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 150g cherry tomatoes
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 red chilli chopped (if using red pesto)
  3. Add and cook for 4 mins
    • 150g raw king prawns
  4. Remove heat and add
    • 2 tbsp harissa or 3 tbsp red pesto
    • zest of ¼ lemon
    • juice of lemon
    • broccoli cooked
    • pinch of salt
  5. Add spaghetti
    • spaghetti cooked
    • pasta cooking water to make smooth

Serving

  • 3 portions: 270g spaghetti, 225g tomatoes, 150g broccoli, 225g prawns, 3 garlic, 3 tbsp harissa
  • 4 portions: 360g spaghetti, 300g tomatoes, 200g broccoli, 300g prawns, 4 garlic, 4 tbsp harissa

Notes

  • Adjust amount of lemon zest depending on how lemony you want it
  • Don't fully drain spaghetti so keep liquid in the pot
