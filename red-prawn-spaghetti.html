Red prawn spaghetti

Broccoli & spaghetti

  1. Heat pan of water to boiling
  2. Cook 100g sprouting broccoli for 2 mins then remove
  3. Cook 200g spaghetti
  4. Save some of the water after draining

Sauce

  1. Fry on medium heat for 8 mins until almost cooked
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 150g cherry tomatoes
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 red chilli chopped (if using red pesto)
  3. Add 150g raw king prawns and cook for 2-4 mins until pink
  4. Remove heat and add
    • 2 tbsp harissa or 3 tbsp red pesto
    • zest of ¼ lemon
    • sprouting broccoli chopped
  5. Add spaghetti
  6. Mix with some pasta cooking water to make smooth

Notes

  • Can use 150g broccoli
  • Slice thick broccoli stems in half (or cook these for longer)
  • Adjust amount of lemon zest depending on how lemony you want it
  • Don't fully drain spaghetti so keep liquid in the pot
  • 3 portions: 300g spaghetti
  • 4 portions: 330g spaghetti, 300g tomatoes, 200g broccoli, 200g prawns, 2 garlic
