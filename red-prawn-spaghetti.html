Red prawn spaghetti

Broccoli & spaghetti

  1. Heat pan of water to boiling
  2. Cook 100g sprouting broccoli for 2 mins then remove
  3. Cook 200g spaghetti
  4. Save some of the water after draining

Sauce

  1. Fry on medium heat for 5 mins
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 150g cherry tomatoes
  2. Add 150g raw king prawns and cook for 2-4 mins until pink
  3. Remove heat and add
    • 2 tbsp harissa or red pesto
    • zest of ¼ lemon
    • sprouting broccoli chopped
  4. Add spaghetti
  5. Mix with some pasta cooking water to make smooth

Notes

Use 300g spaghetti for 3 portions

Can use 150g broccoli

Adjust amount of lemon zest depending on how lemony you want it
