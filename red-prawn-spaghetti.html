Red prawn spaghetti

Broccoli & spaghetti

  1. Heat pan of water to boiling
  2. Cook 100g sprouting broccoli for 90 seconds then remove
  3. Cook 200g spaghetti
  4. Save some of the water after draining

Sauce

  1. Fry on medium heat for 5 mins
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 150g cherry tomatoes
  2. Add 150g raw king prawns and cook for 2 mins until pink
  3. Add and warm
    • 2 tbsp harissa or red pesto
    • zest of ½ lemon
  4. Add spaghetti and mix with cooking water to make smooth

Notes

Use 300g spaghetti for 3 portions

Can use 150g broccoli

Don't use all lemon zest if don't want too lemony

Belazu rose harissa (Sainsbury)
