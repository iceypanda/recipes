Red prawn spaghetti

Prawns

  1. Brine and alkalise prawns

Broccoli

  1. Cook for 2-3 mins
    • 100g sprouting broccoli stems sliced in half
  2. Drain
  3. Transfer to Pyrex bowl and cover with lid

Spaghetti

  1. Cook 190g spaghetti
  2. Drain spaghetti but keep some liquid in the pot

Sauce

  1. Fry on medium heat for 8 mins till almost cooked
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 200g cherry tomatoes
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 red chilli chopped (if using red pesto)
  3. Add and cook for 4 mins
    • 150g raw king prawns
  4. Remove heat and add
    • 2 tbsp harissa or 3 tbsp red pesto
    • zest ¼-½ lemon
    • juice lemon
    • broccoli cooked
    • pinch of salt
  5. Add spaghetti
    • spaghetti cooked
    • pasta cooking water to make smooth

Serving

  • 3 portions
    • 300g tomatoes
    • 270g spaghetti
    • 225g prawns
    • 150g broccoli
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 3 tbsp harissa
  • 4 portions
    • 400g tomatoes
    • 360g spaghetti
    • 300g prawns
    • 200g broccoli
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 4 tbsp harissa
