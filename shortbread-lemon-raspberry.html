Lemon & raspberry shortbread

Dough

  1. Mix till fluffy
    • 110g caster sugar
    • 50g unsalted butter softened
  2. Mix in
    • zest 1 lemon
    • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  3. Add and mix to form dough without kneading
    • 400g plain flour
  4. Flatten into round
  5. Wrap in cling film
  6. Chill in fridge for 30 mins

Rounds

  1. Roll dough into a sheet 3mm thick
  2. Cut out rounds 6cm diameter
  3. Place on trays with baking paper 1cm apart
  4. Prick surface of each biscuit with fork
  5. Chill trays in fridge for 30 mins

Biscuits

  1. Generously sprinkle each tray with caster sugar
  2. Bake in oven at 160°C for 10-12 mins
  3. Leave to cool on tray till cool
  4. Mix
    • 4 tbsp raspberry jam
    • 50g white chocolate grated
  5. Spread jam on one side of a round and join to another till all rounds are paired

Notes

  • Original recipe: Nadia, p36
