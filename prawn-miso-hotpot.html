Prawn miso hotpot

  1. Simmer for 10 mins
    • 1l chicken stock
    • 2 tbsp miso
    • 1½ tsp tomato puree
    • 2 star anise
    • ¼ bunch fresh coriander
    • juice of ½ lime
  2. Sieve broth and then bring to boil
  3. Add and heat until veg cooked
    • 20 king prawns cooked
    • tin water chestnuts
    • 1 carrot ribboned with peeler
    • 100g mangetout halved
    • 100g baby corn
    • 50g ginger pickled
    • 200g noodles cooked

Serving

  • 2 portions
