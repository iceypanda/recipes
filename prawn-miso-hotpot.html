Prawn miso hotpot

Broth

  1. Simmer until desired anise flavour
    • 600ml chicken stock (1 salt cube)
    • ½ tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 star anise smashed
  2. Sieve broth

Hotpot

  1. Stir fry or add to broth and bring to boil then simmer until cooked
    • 150g mangetout halved diagonally
    • 150g baby corn
    • 1 tin water chestnuts
    • 1 carrot ribboned with peeler
  2. Add and heat until everything is warmed through
    • 400g udon noodles cooked
    • 225g king prawns / tiny prawns cooked
    • 50g ginger pickled, rinsed thoroughly
  3. Remove heat
  4. Pour out some broth into a cup and mix in 1 tbsp at a time to dissolve
    • 3 tbsp miso
  5. Add gradually to taste
    • juice of ½ lime
  6. Serve with chopped
    • coriander leaves chopped (optional)
    • chilli oil

Serving

  • 4 portions
