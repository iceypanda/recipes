Prawn miso hotpot

  1. Simmer for 10 mins
    • 1l chicken stock (1 salt + 1 no-salt)
    • ½ tbsp tomato puree
    • ¼ bunch fresh coriander chopped
    • 2 star anise
  2. Sieve broth
  3. Add to broth and bring to boil then simmer for 2 mins
    • 150g mangetout halved diagonally
    • 150g baby corn
    • 1 tin water chestnuts
    • 1 carrot ribboned with peeler
  4. Add and heat until veg cooked
    • 400g udon noodles cooked
    • 225g king prawns cooked
    • 50g ginger pickled
    • 3 tbsp miso
  5. Remove heat and stir in
    • juice of ½ lime
  6. Serve with chopped coriander leaves

Serving

  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Made: 19 Nov 2022
  • can strain miso in to help dissolve
  • could use more pickled ginger
  • Original recipe
