Toad in the hole

  • Preheat oven to 230°C

Batter

  • Blitz till smooth
    • 285ml (295g) milk
    • 115g plain flour
    • 3 large eggs

Toad in the hole

  1. Pour into square 22x22cm roasting dish
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  2. Heat in oven at 230°C till hot
  3. Arrange in dish
    • 8 sausages
  4. Cook at 230°C till for 15-20 mins till sausages browning
  5. Pour over batter
  6. Cook at 230°C for 15-20 mins till batter is golden and has risen, don't open the oven till done

Serving

  • Sainsbury's onion gravy
  • Mash
  • Peas, broccoli
  • 4 portions
