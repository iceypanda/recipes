Iced buns

Dough

  1. Mix in stand mixer with dough hook
    • 250g strong bread flour
    • 20g caster sugar
    • 7g yeast sachet
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Mix in to form dough
    • 80ml (82g) milk warmed
    • 20g butter softened
    • 1 large egg beaten
    • 25ml water warmed
  3. Slowly add in 25ml more water to form sticky dough
  4. Mix on high for 8 mins until smooth and elastic
  5. Place dough in oiled bowl and leave to prove for 45 mins until doubled in size

Buns

  1. Kock back dough on floured surface
  2. Knead for 2 mins until smooth
  3. Divide into 6
  4. Roll each piece into sausage 5" long
  5. Place on oiled baking tray leaving space so they will just touch each other when risen
  6. Cover and leave to rise for 30 mins

Baking

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C
  2. Bake 10 mins until golden brown
  3. Leave to cool

Icing

  1. Mix
    • 200g fondant icing sugar
    • 2-3 tbsp water as required
    • yellow food colouring
  2. Smooth over buns
  3. Decorate with sugar sprinkles if required
