Raita

  1. Mix
    • 200g yogurt
    • 40g tomato deseeded and finely diced
    • 40g cucumberd eseeded and finely diced
    • 25g (¼) red onion finely diced
    • ¼ tsp caster sugar
    • 8 mint leaves finely chopped
    • fresh coriander leaves finely chopped
  2. Leave for 30 mins before serving
  3. Toast cumin seeds and sprinkle on top to serve

Notes
