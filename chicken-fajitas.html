Chicken fajitas

  1. Fry on low heat for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 red onion
    • 1 red pepper
  2. Cook until chicken is done
    • 1 chicken breast
    • chopped red chilli
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
    • ¾ tbsp ground coriander
    • pinch cumin
    • dashes of hot sauce
    • juice of 1 lime
  3. Cook in pan
  4. Serve with
    • warm tortillas
    • soured cream / natural yogurt
    • grated cheddar cheese
    • salsa

Notes

  • Reduced from 2 chicken breast, 1 heaped tbsp paprika, 1 tbsp coriander
  • Try roasting veg in oven / air fryer (with spices) instead of frying
