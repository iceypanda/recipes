Chicken fajitas

  1. Add to bowl 1
    • 1 red onion thinly sliced
    • 1 yellow pepper thinly sliced
    • 2 sweet red peppers thinly sliced
  2. Add to bowl 2
    • 300-400g chicken breast sliced
  3. Divide equally between both bowls
    • 2 red chillies chopped
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 1½ tbsp smoked paprika
    • 1 tbsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp cumin
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ¼ tsp mild chllli powder
    • juice and zest 1 lime
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
  4. Roast peppers in air fryer at 180°C for 13 mins
  5. Add chicken and roast for 12 mins

Serving

  • 3 portions
  • tortillas warmed
  • sour cream / natural yogurt
  • cheddar cheese grated
  • Pico de gallo
  • lettuce
