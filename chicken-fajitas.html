Chicken fajitas

  1. Chop:
    • 1 red onion
    • 1 red pepper
    • 2 chicken breasts
  2. Marinade for 5 mins in:
    • 1 heaped tbsp smoked paprika
    • 1 tbsp ground coriander
    • pinch cumin
    • ¼ tsp chilli powder
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • juice of 1 lime
    • olive oil
    • salt & pepper
  3. Cook in pan
  4. Serve with:
    • warm tortillas
    • soured cream / natural yogurt
    • grated cheddar cheese
    • salsa
