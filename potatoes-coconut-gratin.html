Coconut gratin potatoes

  • Preheat oven to 180°C

Potatoes & onions

  1. Heat for 10 mins
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 5 banana shallots thinly sliced
    • 3 cloves garlic sliced
  2. Transfer to bowl
  3. Add and mix well so each slice coated
    • 1.4kg potatoes unpeeled & thinly sliced
    • juice 3 limes
    • 200ml coconut milk
    • pepper
  4. Transfer to buttered baking tin and arrange potato slices in overlapping pattern
  5. Pour over
    • 200ml water boiled
    • 1 salted stock cube

Aromatics

  1. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 100ml olive oil
    • 4 cloves garlic sliced
    • 2cm ginger julienned
    • 2 chillis sliced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins until garlic is golden
    • 5 spring onions sliced at angle
  3. Separate oil and aromatics

Baking

  1. Cover baking tin with foil
  2. Bake at 180°C for 45 mins
  3. Remove foil
  4. Pour over
    • aromatic oil
  5. Bake at 200°C for 45 mins until top is golden and crispy
  6. Garnish with
    • aromatics
    • zest 3 limes
