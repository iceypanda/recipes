Potato, carrot & pea curry pies

Veg

  1. Boil for 2 mins
    • 110g carrots in 1cm cubes
  2. Add, bring to boil and cook for 11 mins till veg is tender
    • 375g potatoes in 1cm cubes
  3. Add for 30 seconds
    • 110g peas defrosted
  4. Drain with cold water to cool

Filling

  1. Heat till seeds crackle for 30 seconds
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  2. Gently heat till soft
    • 1 red onion
  3. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 1 green chilli finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 5g ginger grated
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
    • ¼ tsp garam masala
    • ¼ tsp ground cumin
    • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
    • ¼ tsp amchur
    • ½ tsp salt
  5. Add
    • 1 tbsp lime / lemon juice (will taste too sour till pies cooked)
    • 15g coriander leaves chopped
  6. Add potatoes, carrot and peas then remove heat
  7. Allow mixture to cool and chill in fridge

Notes

Serving

  • Chicken tikka
