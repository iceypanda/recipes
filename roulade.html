Roulade

Meringue

  1. Heat oven to 160°C
  2. Whisk meringue
  3. Spread over a 30x20cm baking tin lined with baking paper
  4. Cook for 1 hour mins until crisp on outside, turning down heat to 140° after 10 mins
  5. Remove from oven and leave to cool
  6. Flip over meringue onto another sheet of baking paper
  7. Remove baking paper from meringue

Roulade

  1. Whisk 300g sweetened cream to soft peaks
  2. Spread cream evenly over meringue
  3. Pour or place fruit onto the cream
  4. Roll up the roulade from shortest side
  5. Dust with icing sugar to finish

Serving

  • 200g blueberry compote plus 100g blackberries

Notes

  • Meringue will have a crisp side (for outside the roulade) and a soft side (for inside the roulade)
  • Tiramisu roulade
