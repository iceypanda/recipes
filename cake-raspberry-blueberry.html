Raspberry & blueberry lime drizzle cake

Cake

  1. Cream together
    • 225g butter softened
    • 225g golden caster sugar
  2. Beat in one at a time
    • 4 eggs
  3. Beat in
    • 50g self-raising flour
  4. Beat in
    • zest of 2 limes
  5. Fold in
    • 200g self-raising flour
    • 25g polenta
  6. Mix in
    • 3 tbsp lime juice
  7. Fold in
    • 75g blueberries
    • 75g raspberries
  8. Pour into 20x20cm tin lined with baking paper
  9. Smooth then scatter on top
    • 25g blueberries
    • 25g raspberries
  10. Bake at 160°C for 45-50 mins

Syrup

  1. Warm to dissolve sugar without bubbling
    • remaining lime juice
    • zest of 1 lime
    • 30g golden caster sugar
  2. While cake is hot prick all over with skewer and spoon over syrup

Serving

  • Calories = 3900, 1/16 = 245
  • 16 portions

Notes

  • made: 15 Oct 2022
  • can use up to 150g of each fruit
  • polenta replaces almond flour
