Pasta puttanesca

  1. Gently heat until almost cooked
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion or 3 shallots
  2. Heat for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Heat until sizzling
    • 1 red chilli chopped
    • 5 anchovies
    • 50g black olives chopped
    • 1 tbsp capers
  4. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 100ml passata
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • chilli flakes (optional for heat)
  5. Add cooked spaghetti and mix well
  6. Mix in some spaghetti cooking water
  7. Top with fresh parsley

Notes

  • 120g spaghetti per portion (200g for me & R)
