Pasta puttanesca

  1. Gently heat until cooked
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion or 3 shallots
    • 3 cloves garlic
  2. Heat until sizzling
    • 1 red chilli chopped
    • 5 anchovies
    • 50g black olives chopped
    • 1 tbsp capers
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  3. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 100ml passata
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  4. Add cooked spaghetti and mix well
  5. Mix in some spaghetti cooking water
  6. Top with fresh parsley

Notes

  • 120g spaghetti per portion (200g for me & R)
  • Too hot with ground black pepper
