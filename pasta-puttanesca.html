Pasta puttanesca

  1. Gently heat until cooked:
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Heat until sizzling:
    • 2 cloves of garlic
    • 1 red chilli
    • 4 anchovies
    • 50g chopped black olives
    • 1 tbsp capers
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  3. Simmer for 5 mins:
    • 100ml passata
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  4. Add spaghetti and mix well
  5. Top with fresh parsley

Comments

120g spaghetti per portion

Too hot with ground black pepper
