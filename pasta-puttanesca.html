Pasta puttanesca

  1. Heat until cooked (optional):
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Heat until sizzling:
    • 2 cloves of garlic
    • 4 anchovies
    • 50g chopped black olives
    • 1 tbsp capers
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  3. Simmer for 5 mins:
    • 100ml passata
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  4. Add 200g spaghetti and mix well
  5. Top with fresh parsley
