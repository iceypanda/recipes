Sweet potato curry

  1. Heat for 5 mins
    • 2 red onions
    • thumb piece of chopped ginger
  2. Add
    • 2 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp allspice
    • 1 tsp chilli flakes
    • tin of tomatoes
    • 200g coconut milk
    • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
    • vegetable stock cube
  3. Add and simmer for 20-25 mins until veg is tender
    • 3 sweet potatoes cut into large cubes
    • 2 red peppers
    • 325g white cabbage

Notes

  • Serve with rice and peas
