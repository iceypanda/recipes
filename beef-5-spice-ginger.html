Ginger & 5-spice beef

Beef

  1. Brown in batches
    • 750g beef chunks
    • vegetable oil
  2. Transfer to slow cooker

Sauce

  1. Blitz
    • 2 onions
    • 50g ginger
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • splash of water
  2. Gently fry without oil for 5 mins
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 tsp Chinese 5-spice
    • 6 star anise
  4. Add and bring to simmer
    • 100g dark brown muscavado sugar
    • 50ml dark soy sauce
    • 50ml shoyu
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • black pepper cracked
  5. Transfer to slow cooker
  6. Cook on high for 4 hours
  7. Reduce sauce to thicken

Serving

  • Jasmine rice
  • Pickled cucumber / radish
  • Green beans with sesame seeds
