Ginger & 5-spice beef

Beef

  1. Brown all in one go in big frying pan
    • 750g beef chunks
    • vegetable oil
  2. Transfer to slow cooker

Sauce

  1. Blitz
    • 2 (300g unpeeled) onions
    • 30g ginger
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • splash of water
  2. Gently fry without oil for 5 mins
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 1¼ tsp Chinese 5-spice
    • 4 star anise
  4. Add and bring to simmer
    • 60g dark brown muscavado sugar
    • 30ml dark soy sauce
    • 30ml shoyu
    • 1¼ tbsp tomato puree
    • black pepper cracked
  5. Transfer to slow cooker
  6. Cook on high for 4 hours
  7. Reduce sauce to thicken

Serving

  • Jasmine rice
  • Pickled cucumber / radish
  • Green beans with sesame seeds
  • Steamed pak choi
  • 3 portions
