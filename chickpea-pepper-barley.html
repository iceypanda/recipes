Chickpea, pepper & pearl barley

    Roasted veg

  1. Finely chop and roast at 200°C for 25 mins
    • 1 tin of chickpeas
    • 1 corgette
    • 1 red onion
    • 3 sweet pointed red peppers
    • olive oil
    • splash of maple syrup
    • salt

    2. Pearl barley

  2. Cook pearl barley (amount?)
  3. Stir in
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • splash of maple syrup
    • sprinkle of chilli flakes
  4. Mix with roasted veg
  5. Add rocket (optional) and serve with hot smoked salmon

Notes

  • May want more balsamic and olive oil
