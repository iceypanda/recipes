Tortino alla Fiorentina

Notes

  • Can also use artichoke hearts or corgettes

Aubergine

  1. Bake at 180°C for 15 mins till lightly golden
    • 1 aubergine peeled & cut into 5mm slices
    • 1 tbsp olive oil

Potatoes

  1. Pat dry
    • 4 kiwi-sized potatoes peeled & cut into 5mm slices
  2. Heat till sizzling in cast-iron pot
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • potatoes
  3. Heat on low for 15 mins till soft
  4. Remove to plate and keep warm

Eggs

  1. Lightly beat in bowl
    • 6 eggs
    • 60ml (62g) milk
    • 2 tbsp cheese grated
    • 2 tbsp parsely chopped
    • 1 tsp oregano / marjoram

Tortino

  1. Arrange evenly in pot
    • potatoes
    • aubergine
  2. Pour over
    • egg mixture
  3. Bake at 160°C for 15 mins till cooked

Serving

  • Mango chutney
  • Salad
  • 3 portions
