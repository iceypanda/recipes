Chicken

Recipes

  • Chicken goujons

Roast chicken pieces

  1. 400g chicken breast cut into thick pieces and marinated
  2. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 14 mins

Spanish style

  • Marinate for 2 hours
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 sprigs thyme leaves
    • 2 tsp sherry vinegar (or rice / red wine / white wine vinegar)
    • 1 tsp sweet smoked paprika
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp ground coriander

Tikka

  1. Blitz till smooth
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 30ml rice vinegar
    • 25g ginger
    • 2 green chillis deseeded
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Marinate chicken for 6-24 hours
  3. Allow to reach room temperature then mix in 10g melted butter

Chicken in sauce

  • Barbecue sauce and 3 chicken breasts, 200°C for 27 mins

Notes

  • 350g for 4 people for tea
