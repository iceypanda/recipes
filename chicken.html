Chicken

Recipes

  • Chicken goujons

Notes

  • Salt / brine chicken 1-3 hours before cooking
  • Cook to 65°C then rest
  • Don't marinate in acids because meat will lose more moisture after cooking
  • Freezing damages cell structure and can cause interior juices to leak out. If packaging has lots of juices then probably because meat was frozen

Pan-fried chicken breasts

  1. Trim chicken breasts
  2. Season on both sides
  3. Heat frying pan with olive oil
  4. Once oil is hot heat chicken breasts on one side for 5 mins
  5. Add knob of butter
  6. Turn chicken breasts and baste in butter for 2 mins
  7. Roast in air fryer at 180°C for 10-15 mins until 68°C inside
  8. Rest for 5 mins before serving

Chicken cutlets

  1. Slice chicken breasts into two horizontally (don't saw)
  2. Place each chicken slice between cling film and bash with pan bottom until 6-7mm thick
  3. Season cutlets with salt and pepper
  4. Coat in plain flour shaking off excess
  5. Add 1½ tbsp vegetable oil to 30cm pan
  6. Heat over high-heat until smoking
  7. Add cutlets and cook without moving for 2½ mins until browned on bottom
  8. Flip and cook for 3 mins
  9. Transfer to plate and cover to rest

Chicken in ham

  1. Wrap chicken breast in Prosciutto ham then lightly brush with oil
  2. Spread thin layer of pesto on top and cover that with panko breadcrumbs
  3. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 15-20 mins? until 68°C
  4. Rest for 5 mins before serving

Roast whole chicken

  1. Stuffing: 170g, 12 balls, 200°C for 25 mins
  2. Insert into chicken
    • Lemon pricked all over with knife and heated in microwave
    • 2 sticks celery chopped
    • Slabs of butter 1 inside, 1 under skin at front
  3. 20 mins + 20 mins for each 450g
  4. Cook at 180°C uncovered
    • Medium 1.5kg, 1h 20m, 4 portions & leftovers
  5. 75°C in meat when done
  6. Baste before resting
  7. Cover with foil and rest for 30 mins

Roast chicken pieces

  1. 400g chicken breast cut into similar sized pieces
  2. Marinate 2-6 hours
  3. Allow to reach room temperature then cook
  4. Roast in air fryer at 180°C for 8-10 mins until 68°C
