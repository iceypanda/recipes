Asparagus

Notes

  • To trim cut where stalk begins to fade to white, don't snap stem
  • Peel from below tip

Steam

  • 10 mins bottom tray, try higher tray or 9 mins

Blanch

  • Boil in water for 5 mins

Roasting

  1. Mix bunch with
    • olive oil
    • lemon juice / balsamic vinegar
    • ground black pepper
    • grated parmesan / cheddar
  2. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 8 mins
