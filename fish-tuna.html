Tuna

Seared

  • Remove from pan as soon as cooked or will overcook
  1. Plenty of sesame oil in pan
  2. Fry on low-medium heat for up to 2 mins each side making sure not cooked all the way through
  3. Rest for short time off pan then cut across grain

Serve with

  • Udon noodles and broccoli cooked in stock; teriyaki sauce; spring onions
