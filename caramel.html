Caramel

Notes

  • While warming do not stir or mix
  • If need to mix swirl gently
  • Pile sugar in middle so don't get crystals up the side
  • Lid on for first stage so condensation from the water washes down the sides of the pan, preventing any crystallization
  • Use silicon scraper / brush in water if need to clean sides of pan to prevent sugar burning
  • Warm ramekins / tray so does not cool immediately and can be swirled to coat base evenly
  • Use white caster sugar so can see colour change
  • 185-190C for caramel

Cooking

  1. Warm in pan on medium-low heat until sugar dissolved
    • 1.00 white caster sugar
    • 0.25 water
  2. Turn up high and remove lid
  3. Cook until dark and just smelling smoky
  4. Quickly divide caramel into warm ramekins / tray while still bubbling
  5. Leave to cool
