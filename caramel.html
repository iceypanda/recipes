Caramel

Notes

  • While warming
    • Do not stir or mix
    • Use silicon brush in water to clean sides of pan to prevent sugar burning
    • Swirl gently if need to mix
    • May want to warm ramekins / tray so does not cool immediately and can be swirled to coat base evenly
    • Use white caster sugar so can see colour change
    • 185-190C for caramel

Cooking

  1. Warm in pan on medium heat for 10-12 mins until medium amber
    • 1.00 white caster sugar
    • 0.25-0.35 water (try 0.25)
  2. When dark add one at a time same amount of cold water again to stop cooking (Try not doing this)
  3. Quickly divide caramel into ramekins / tray while still bubbling
  4. Leave to cool
