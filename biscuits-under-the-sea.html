Under the sea biscuits

Dough

  1. Put in bowl
    • 150g butter cold & cubed
  2. Add
    • 75g caster sugar
    • 225g plain flour
    • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  3. Blitz till dough forms
  4. Roll into ball
  5. Chill in fridge for 15 mins

Biscuits

  • Preheat oven to 170°C
  1. Roll in portions on floured surface till ½ cm thick
  2. Cut into biscuits
  3. Bake at 170°C for 15-20 mins till golden
  4. Cool on rack
