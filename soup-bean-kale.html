Bean & kale soup

  1. Cook on low heat for 15 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 1 carrot
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 sticks celery finely chopped
    • 2 sprigs rosemary
  2. Add then cover pan and cook for 20 mins
    • 1 potato diced
    • 1 tomato chopped
  3. Add and simmer for 20 mins
    • 1 tin of black eyed beans drained
    • 1l stock
    • 1 tsp majoram (optional)
  4. Add 150g kale leaves
  5. Blend ⅓ of soup
  6. Serve with chopped parsley (optional)
