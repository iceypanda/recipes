Bean & kale soup

  1. On low heat for 15 mins:
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 1 carrot
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 stick finely chopped celery
    • 2 sprigs rosemary
  2. Add then cover pan and cook for 20 mins:
    • 1 diced potato
    • 1 chopped tomato
  3. Add simmer for 20 mins:
    • 1 tin of black eyed beans
    • 1l stock
  4. Blend ⅓ of soup
  5. Add 150g kale into the hot soup
  6. Serve with chopped parsley and drizzle of olive oil
