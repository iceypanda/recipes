Aubergine pasta

Tomato sauce

  1. Tomato sauce
  2. Chorizo tomato sauce

Aubergines

  1. Cut lengthwise into 1cm half moons
    • 2 small aubergines
  2. Toss with 1 tsp salt and leave for 30 mins in strainer
  3. Dry thoroughly with paper towels
  4. Fry in 2? batches in 30cm pan
    1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil on medium-low heat
    2. Cook aubergine in single layer for 7-10 mins until brown on both sides
    3. Transfer to paper-lined plate and season with salt

Aubergine pasta

  1. Mix pasta, tomato sauce and aubergine

Notes

  • Made: Aug 2023, Air fry 180°C, 1 aubergine & ½ corgette = 1 large portion
  • Original recipe: The Food Lab, p702
