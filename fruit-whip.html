Fruit whip

Blueberry compote

  1. Simmer for 4 mins in medium pan
    • 120g blueberries
    • 80g caster sugar
    • 15g water

Custard

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 3 egg yolks
    • 25g cornflour
  2. Whisk in slowly
    • 370ml (381g) whole milk / cream warmed

Pots

  1. Pour custard into fruit
  2. Blitz
  3. Heat over medium heat stirring constantly till thickened
  4. Remove heat
  5. Spoon into ramekins
  6. Chill for 2 hours in fridge

Serving

  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Try whisking after mixing or heating to make fluffy
  • Try with 150ml single cream, try white chocolate
  • Tastes best on same day when fruit is freshest
