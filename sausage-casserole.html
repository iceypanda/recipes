Sausage casserole

Slow cooked

  1. Fry 12 sausages until browned all over
  2. Add to slow cooker
    • 200g green lentils
    • 300ml chicken stock
    • 1 finely sliced fennel bulb
    • ½ tsp fennel seeds
    • 2 bay leaves
  3. Add sausages to slow cooker
  4. Fry 2 small onions until soft
  5. Add 2 cloves garlic for 1 min
  6. Add 125ml white wine, turn up heat and bubble for 1 min
  7. Add to slow cooker
  8. Cook all for 4 hours on high

Colman's mix

  • 1 hour cooking time
  • Use 350ml water instead of 300ml (add hot water at end if sauce too thick)
  • Use 8 sausages
  • Tried with corgette but mushy
  • Use fine beans instead
  • Use cold water for sauce mix, if use hot then sauce will thicken in lumps

Notes

Serve with yorkshire puds
