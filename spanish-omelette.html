Spanish omelette

Potatoes

  1. Peel, slice with mandoline (number 2) and pat dry
    • 300g potatoes
  2. Bake in air fryer at 160°C for 20 mins
  3. Remove from heat and leave to cool

Onion

  1. Fry gently until soft and browned
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Remove from heat and leave to cool

Tortilla

  1. Mix and beat in bowl
    • 5 eggs
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • pepper
  2. Add the onions and potatoes and mix gently
  3. Leave for 15 mins
  4. Cook gently for 20 mins until almost no runny egg on top
  5. Carefully flip with plate and cook other side for 5 mins

Serving

  • Spanish-style roast chicken pieces
  • Tomato salsa
  • hollandaise sauce

Notes

  • Allow eggs to reach room temperature before using
  • Flip tortilla using a large plate
  • Use metal frying pan with steamer lid
  • Waxy potatoes recommended
