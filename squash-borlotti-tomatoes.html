Squash, borlotti beans & tomatoes

  1. Cook in baking dish at 180°C for 1 hour
    • 1 tin borlotti beans with liquid
    • 6 (400g) medium tomatoes halved
    • 500-600g butternut squash deseeded, cut into 1½ cm wedges with skin on
    • 5 cloves garlic sliced
    • 150ml white wine
    • 6 tbsp olive oil
    • 20 sage leaves
  2. Season
    • salt
    • black pepper
    • lemon juice

Serving

  • couscous
  • hot-smoked salmon

Notes

  • beans should be under the liquid so don't burst
