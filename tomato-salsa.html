Tomato salsa

  1. Stir in bowl and blitz half
    • 6 (400g) medium tomatoes finely chopped
    • ½ red onion very finely chopped
    • 1 clove garlic
    • juice of ½ lime
    • 1 tsp white/red wine vinegar
    • fresh coriander (optional)
  2. Leave to chill for 1 hour
  3. Sieve to remove excess liquid

Notes

  • Coriander optional depending on what being served with, could use other herb
  • use lime zest in lime and coriander rice
  • use best tomatoes, not pale, basic ones
