Tomato salsa

  1. Stir in bowl and blitz portion
    • 4-6 medium tomatoes finely chopped
    • ½ red onion very finely chopped
    • 1 clove garlic
    • splash white/red wine vinegar
    • juice of ½ lime
    • fresh coriander (optional)
  2. Sieve to remove excess liquid

Notes

  • Coriander optional depending on what being served with, could use other herb
