Tomato salsa

  1. Stir in bowl and maybe blitz portion
    • 4-6 medium tomatoes finely chopped
    • ½ red onion very finely chopped
    • 1 clove garlic
    • splash white wine vinegar
    • ½ juice of 1 lime
    • fresh coriander (optional)

Comments

Coriander optional depending on what being served with

Could use other herb, e.g. dried coriander and leave to soak in salsa before serving
