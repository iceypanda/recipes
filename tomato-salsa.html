Tomato salsa

  1. Leave in bowl for 20 mins
    • ½ red onion very finely chopped
    • juice of ½ lime
    • 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  2. Add and blitz half
    • 6 (400g) medium tomatoes cored, deseeded and chopped
    • zest of 1 lime
    • salt
  3. Add
    • fresh coriander chopped
  4. Drain off liquid
  5. Leave to chill for 1 hour
  6. Leave to strain in sieve over cup to remove excess liquid

Notes

  • use best tomatoes, not pale, basic ones
  • changed so onion macerates in acid before mixing
  • too much raw onion will be overpowering and leave long onion after taste
  • omited raw garlic
