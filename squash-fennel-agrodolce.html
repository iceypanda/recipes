Squash & fennel agrodolce

  • Preheat oven to 220°C

Squash

  1. Mix
    • 1000g butternut squash peeled, deseeded, cubed
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Arrange on a lined baking tray
  3. Bake at 220°C for 25-30 mins till browned

Capers, pine nuts & raisins

  1. Heat in pan till hot
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 2 tbsp capers washed & drained
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 tbsp pine nuts
    • 2 tbsp raisins
  4. Set aside in bowl

Agrodolce

  1. Cook for 20 mins
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 fennel trimmed, halved, ½ cm slices
    • 1 red onion diced
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Add and cook for 8 mins
    • 2 large tomatoes diced
  3. Add and warm
    • squash
    • 2 tbsp balasmic vinegar
    • 30g mint chopped
  4. Serve topped with
    • capers, pine nuts & raisins

Serving

  • 4 portions
