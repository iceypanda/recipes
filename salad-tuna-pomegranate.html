Tuna & pomegranate salad

Salad

  1. Mix in bowl
    • green leafy salad
    • green beans in thirds
    • 1 corgette in ribbons
    • 2 spring onions
    • 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
    • 2 palm-size amounts tinned tuna

Dressing

  1. Mix
    • 12 raspberries pushed through sieve
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • ¼ tsp mixed herbs
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes

Serving

  • 2 portions
