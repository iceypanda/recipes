Roasted cauliflower

Air fry

  • 190°C for 20 mins

Oven

  1. Preheat oven & baking tray to 200°C
  2. Roast for 20 mins flipping halfway

Basic

  • Mix
    • cauliflower
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • salt
    • pepper

Spiced

  • Mix
    • 400-500g (after trimming) cauliflower
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1 tsp Garam masala
    • ½ tsp ground cardamom

Honey & vinegar

  1. Mix
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp capers rinsed, drained and choped
    • 1 tbsp sherry vinegar
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • ¼ cup (?g) raisins soaked
    • ¼ cup (?g) pine nuts toasted
    • 2 tbsp fresh parsely leaves chopped
  2. Spoon over cauliflower after cooking

Cauliflower cheese

  1. Heat ½ cauliflower broken into pieces for 5 mins in boiling water until cooked
  2. Drain cauliflower and put in oven dish
  3. Heat in saucepan
    • 25g butter
    • 250ml milk
    • 2 tbsp flour
  4. Whisk mixture until sauce thickens
  5. Turn off heat and stir in 50g grated cheddar
  6. Pour over the cauliflower
  7. Scatter 2-3 tbsp breadcrumbs
  8. Bake in oven at 200°C for 20 mins
