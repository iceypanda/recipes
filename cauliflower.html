Roasted cauliflower

Cauliflower

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C with baking tray
  2. Toss together
    • 1 whole cauliflower trimmed and cut into 8 pieces
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • salt
    • pepper
  3. Roast in oven for 20 mins flipping halfway

Dressing

  1. Mix
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp capers rinsed, drained and choped
    • 1 tbsp sherry vinegar
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • ¼ cup (?g) raisins
    • ¼ cup (?g) pine nuts toasted
    • 2 tbsp fresh parsely leaves chopped
  2. Spoon over cauliflower before serving

Cauliflower cheese

  1. Heat ½ cauliflower broken into pieces for 5 mins in boiling water until cooked
  2. Drain cauliflower and put in oven dish
  3. Heat in saucepan
    • 25g butter
    • 250ml milk
    • 2 tbsp flour
  4. Whisk mixture until sauce thickens
  5. Turn off heat and stir in 50g grated cheddar
  6. Pour over the cauliflower
  7. Scatter 2-3 tbsp breadcrumbs
  8. Bake in oven at 200°C for 20 mins
