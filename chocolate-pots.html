White chocolate pots

  1. Heat till steaming
    • 150ml double cream
  2. Remove heat and add
    • 150g white chocolate
  3. Whisk till combined
  4. Pour into 4 small pots
  5. Cool in fridge for 3 hours till set

Serving

  • Roasted nectarines on top

Notes

  • Could add gelatine
  • Made: Aug 2023, 113g white chocolate, 1 tsp sugar, ¼ tsp vanilla, did not set
