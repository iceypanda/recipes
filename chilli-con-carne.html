Chilli con carne

Sun-dried tomatoes

  1. Soak for 10 mins in warm water
    • 80g sun-dried tomatoes
  2. Blitz in chopper

Onions

  1. Fry till soft
    • 2 medium (270g) onions
    • olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 4 cloves garlic minced
    • 1 chopped red chilli
    • 1½ tsp cumin

Chilli con carne

  1. Fry till brown
    • 500g minced beef
    • olive oil
  2. Add
    • 2 tins of tomatoes
    • 150ml of salted stock (½ cube)
    • onions
    • sun-dried tomatoes
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  3. Bring to boil
  4. Transfer to slow cooker and cook for 3 hours
  5. Add and cook for 1 hour
    • 1 tin red kidney beans rinsed
    • 1 tin black/pinto beans rinsed
    • ½ tsp fish sauce
  6. Add
    • ½ tsp shoyu

Serving

  • 6 portions

Notes

  • Try 3 tins beans kidney/black/pinto
  • Can mix in hot salsa at end
