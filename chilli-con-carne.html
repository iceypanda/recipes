Chilli con carne

Sun-dried tomatoes

  1. Soak for 10 mins in luke-warm water
    • 80g sun-dried tomatoes
  2. Blitz in chopper

Onions

  1. Fry until soft
    • 2 medium (270g) onions
    • olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 4 cloves garlic minced
    • 1 chopped red chilli
    • 1½ tsp cumin

Chilli con carne

  1. Fry until brown
    • 500g minced beef
    • olive oil
  2. Add
    • 2 tins of tomatoes
    • 200ml of salted stock
    • onions
    • sun-dried tomatoes
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • ½ tsp oregano
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  3. Bring to boil
  4. Transfer to slow cooker and cook for 3 hours
  5. Add and cook for 1 hour
    • 2 tins of red kidney beans rinsed & warmed in microwave
    • ½ tsp shoyu
    • ½ tsp fish sauce

Serving

  • 6 portions

Notes

  • Made: 23 Jul 2023, 10 Feb 2023, Jul 2023
  • Cook everything in same large skillet
