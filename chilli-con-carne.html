Chilli con carne

  1. Fry until soft
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 2 medium onions
  2. Add
    • 1 chopped red chilli
    • 1 heaped tsp cumin
    • 2 level tsp chilli powder (optional for heat)
  3. Fry until brown
    • 500g minced beef
  4. Add
    • 150g chopped sun-dried tomatoes
    • 2 tins of tomatoes
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 200ml water with ½ salted stock cube
    • ½ tsp oregano
  5. Bring to boil then simmer for 1 hour
  6. Add and simmer for 30 mins
    • 2 tins of red kidney beans
  7. Remove heat and add
    • ½ tsp shoyu
    • ½ tsp fish sauce

    8. Notes

    • Can slow cook
