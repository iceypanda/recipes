Lasagne

  1. Fry till soft
    • ½ red pepper chopped
    • ½ red onion chopped
    • handful mushrooms chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 tsp olive oil
  2. Add and cook 5 mins
    • 100 ml water hot
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 palm-size amounts (100g) soya mince
    • 12 basil leaves chopped
    • ½ tsp basil
    • ½ tsp oregano
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
    • pinch dill
    • pinch white pepper
  3. Layer in oven dish
    1. courgette strips (1 courgette overall)
    2. mince mixture
    3. courgette strips
    4. mince mixture
    5. courgette strips
    6. 1 tbsp cottage cheese
    7. black pepper
  4. Cook at 180°C for 20 mins
  5. Serve with
    • rocket leaves

Serving

  • 2 portions
