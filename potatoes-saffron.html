Saffron potatoes

Roux

  1. Melt in microwave
    • 2 tbsp butter
  2. Mix in
    • 1 tbsp plain flour

Stock

  1. Heat for 2 mins
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic
  2. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • 250 ml stock
    • large pinch saffron crushed
  3. Add roux and bring to boil till starts to thicken
  4. Taste and add seasoning
  5. Pour over and simmer or roast till cooked
    • potatoes sliced
