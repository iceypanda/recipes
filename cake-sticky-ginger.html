Sticky ginger cake

  • Preheat oven to 170°C

Date treacle

  1. Soak in bowl for 10 mins
    • 250g dates pitted, chopped
    • 200ml water boiled
    • 1 tsp bicarbonate soda
  2. Blitz
  3. Add and blitz
    • 100g light brown sugar
    • 80g vegetable oil
    • 75g golden syrup
    • 75g molasses / black treacle

Cake

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 200g plain flour
    • 1 tbsp ground ginger
    • 1 tbsp cinnamon
    • 2 tsp baking powder
    • ¼ salt
  2. Fold in
    • date treacle
  3. Pour into 23x23cm baking tin
  4. Bake at 170°C for 45-50 mins
  5. While warm brush with
    • stem ginger syrup
  6. Scatter
    • 20g stem ginger finely chopped
  7. Leave to cool completely
  8. Remove from tin

Notes

  • Good but aftertaste of vegetable oil
