Smoked haddock & coconut soup

  1. Heat for 5 mins
    • 1 onion diced
    • olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp coriander
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • ½ tsp paprika
  3. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • ½ red pepper chopped
    • 1 carrot in ½ moons
    • 2 spring onions chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
    • 5g ginger minced
    • ½ tsp thyme
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 150ml veg stock
  5. Add and simmer for 15 mins
    • 200g squash
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  6. Add and simmer for 7 mins till fish cooked
    • 300g smoked haddock in pieces
    • 200g sweetcorn
    • salt to taste
  7. Garnish
    • parsely / chives chopped
