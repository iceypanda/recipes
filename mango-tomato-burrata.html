Mango, tomato & burrata

Tomatoes

  1. Mix and leave for 10 mins
    • ½ long shallot
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 red chilli chopped
    • juice of 2 limes
  2. Add and leave for 10 mins
    • 225g tomatoes room temperature
    • 40g soft brown sugar
    • 40ml fish sauce
    3. Add
    • 1 spring onion sliced

Mango

  1. Griddle until charred
    • 1 large mango cut into wedges
  2. Toss with
    • 10ml lime juice
  3. Arrange on plate and scatter with tomatoes
  4. Break open and arrange
    • burrata

Serving

  • 2 portions
