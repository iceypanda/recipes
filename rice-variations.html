Rice variations

Broad bean and dill rice

  1. Cook
    • 200g basmati rice
    • 520g water
    • 10g fresh dill chopped / 2 tsp dried dill
  2. Bring to boil
    • tin broad beans / black-eyed beans
  3. Mix rice and beans

Saffron rice

  • Grind large pinch of saffron to powder with salt in pestle and mortar
  • Add 2 tbsp boiling water and leave for 5 mins
  • Add to rice as cooking

Coriander & lime rice

  1. Cook
    • 200g basmati rice
    • 520g water
    • 1 clove garlic minced
    • zest of 1 lime
  2. Mix in chopped leaves from 20g coriander (⅔ of 30g bag)

Notes

  • 20g of coriander enough so can taste
  • omitted 3 tbsp lime juice because too sour
