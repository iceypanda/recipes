Rice variations

Broad bean and dill rice

  • Mix rice before cooking with
    • 10g fresh dill chopped
  • Mix with
    • tin broad beans peeled and cooked

Saffron rice

  • Grind large pinch of saffron to powder with salt in pestle and mortar
  • Add 2 tbsp boiling water and leave for 5 mins
  • Add to rice as cooking
