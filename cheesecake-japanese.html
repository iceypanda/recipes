Japanese cheesecake

Batter

  1. Separate 4 eggs
  2. Gently warm in microwave to melt and combine
    • 180g cream cheese
    • 30g unsalted butter
    • 50ml (51g) full-fat milk
  3. Whisk batter on medium speed so no lumps
  4. Whisk in
    • 4 egg yolks
  5. In separate bowl sift and combine
    • 35g plain flour
    • 10g cornflour
    • pinch salt
  6. Gradually whisk flour mixture into cheese mixture

Cake

  1. Whisk
    • 4 egg whites
    • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  2. Mix in to form meringue with soft peaks
    • 50g caster sugar
  3. Fold meringue into batter
  4. Pour into 15cm cake pan greased and lined with baking paper
  5. Bake in water bath at 140°C for 30 mins
  6. Reduce temperature to 110°C for 60 mins
  7. Flip onto plate then back up to get out of pan

Serving

  • Calories = 1530, 1/8 = 190
  • 8 portions
  • Coulis / maple syrup
