Chorizo & prawn quesadillas

  1. Add and fry until oozes oil
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 70g chorizo finely chopped
  2. Heat for 10 mins
    • 1 red onion sliced
    • 1 red pepper chopped
  3. Add and heat for 2 mins
    • 100g raw king prawns chopped
    • 1 clove garlic grated
    • 1 tbsp chipotle paste
    • ½ tsp cumin
    • ½ tsp coriander
  4. Add to taste
    • juice of 1 lime
