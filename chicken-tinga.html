Chicken tinga

Onions

  1. Fry on low heat for 7 mins
    • olive oil
    • 2 onions (1 red, 1 white)
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1¼ tsp sweet smoked paprika
    • ¾ tsp cumin

Slow cooker

  1. Add to slow cooker with onions
    • 6 boneless and skinless chicken thighs (400g after trimming)
    • 50g chipotle paste
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp brown sugar
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Cook on high for 3½ hours
  3. Remove chicken and use two forks to tidy and shred on chopping board
  4. Add and heat for 30 mins
    • 1 tin black beans drained
    • shredded chicken

Serving

  1. Serve with in wraps with
    • coriander rice with lime
    • fresh salsa
    • lettuce finely chopped (for crunch)
    • cheese (for flavour)
    • soured cream

Notes

  • Made: 30 Aug 2022
  • Try draining when hot to reduce liquid or make well in sauce and remove collected water
  • chipotle paste in small 100g bottle
  • Reduced chipotle and added paprika and cumin for more flavour less heat
  • Original recipe
