Chicken tinga

  1. Fry on low heat for 7 mins
    • olive oil
    • 2 onions
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 tsp smoked paprika
    • ½ tsp cumin
  3. Add 50ml water and transfer to slow cooker
  4. Add
    • 6 boneless and skinless chicken thighs
    • 2 tbsp chipotle paste
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp brown sugar
    • ¼ tsp salt
  5. Cook on high for 3½ hours
  6. Use forks to tidy and shred chicken then add back to sauce
  7. Add drained tin of black beans and heat for 30 mins

Serve with

  1. Serve with in wraps with
    • lettuce finely chopped
    • coriander rice with lime
    • fresh salsa
    • cheese

Notes

  • lettuce for crunch, cheese for flavour
  • 400g thigh fillets
  • Try draining when hot to reduce liquid, or add less water
  • Reduced chipotle and added paprika and cumin for more flavour less heat
  • Could add roasted peppers for more flavour
  • Original recipe
