Chicken tinga

  1. Fry on low heat for 8 mins
    • olive oil
    • 2 onions
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Add ½ cup of water and transfer to slow cooker
  4. Add to slow cooker
    • 4 boneless and skinless chicken thighs
    • 3 tbsp chipotle paste
    • tin chopped tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  5. Heat for 4 hours until chicken cooked
  6. Use forks to shred chicken and add back to sauce

Serve with

  1. Serve with in wrap with
    • lettuce finely chopped
    • coriander rice with lime
    • black beans
    • fresh salsa
    • cheese

Notes

  • Thighs reduced from 6
  • Original recipe
