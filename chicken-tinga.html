Chicken tinga

  1. Fry on low heat for 7 mins
    • olive oil
    • 2 onions
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 tsp smoked paprika (try extra ¼ next time?)
    • ½ tsp cumin (try extra ¼ next time)
  3. Transfer to slow cooker
  4. Add
    • 6 (400g) boneless and skinless chicken thighs
    • 2 tbsp chipotle paste
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp brown sugar
    • ½ tsp salt
  5. Cook on high for 3½ hours
  6. Remove chicken and use two forks to tidy and shred on chopping board
  7. Add and heat for 30 mins
    • tin of black beans drained
    • shredded chicken

Serve with

  1. Serve with in wraps with
    • coriander rice with lime
    • fresh salsa
    • lettuce finely chopped (for crunch)
    • cheese (for flavour)

Notes

  • Try draining when hot to reduce liquid or make well in sauce and remove collected water
  • Reduced chipotle and added paprika and cumin for more flavour less heat
  • Could add roasted peppers for more flavour
  • Original recipe
