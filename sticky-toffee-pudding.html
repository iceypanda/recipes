Sticky toffee pudding

  • Preheat oven to 160°C

Dates

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 200g dates chopped
    • 200ml water boiling
    • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  2. Leave for 10 mins then squish dates

Pudding

  1. Cream together
    • 75g unsalted butter softened
    • 2 tbsp (42g) black treacle
  2. Mix in
    • 50g dark muscavado sugar
  3. Beat in one at a time
    • 2 eggs room temperature
  4. Mix in gently until smooth
    • 150g plain flour
    • 2 tsp baking powder
  5. Mix in dates and liquid
  6. Pour into buttered 23cm x 23cm tray
  7. Bake at preheated 160°C for 30-35 mins
  8. Prick all over with cocktail stick and pour over ¼ of sauce
  9. Leave for 1 hour to cool

Sauce

  1. Melt together gently
    • 113g unsalted butter
    • 225g dark muscavado sugar
    • ¾ tbsp (18g) black treacle
  2. Stir in
    • 150ml double cream
  3. Heat until bubbling hot then remove heat

Serving

  • 9 portions
