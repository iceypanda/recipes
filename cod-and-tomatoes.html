Cod with Prosciutto and tomatoes

Cod

  1. Wrap 400g cod fillets in Prosciutto ham then lightly brush with oil
  2. Spread thin layer of pesto on top and cover that with panko breadcrumbs

Tomatoes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Mix in bowl
    • 600g cherry tomatoes
    • 2 clove garlic
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • 1 tsp thyme
    • Salt and pepper
  3. Place tomatoes on tray and bake for 5 mins
  4. Place cod amongst tomatoes and bake for 19 mins until fish is cooked

Serving

  • Parmentier potatoes
  • veg, eg fine beans, baby carrots
