Cod with Prosciutto and tomatoes

Cod

  1. Wrap 2 cod fillets in 2 slices each of Prosciutto ham
  2. Spread thin layer of pesto on top
  3. Cover with panko breadcrumbs

Tomatoes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Mix in bowl
    • 250g cherry tomatoes
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • ½ tsp oregano
    • ½ tsp thyme
    • pepper
  3. Place tomatoes on tray and bake for 5 mins
  4. Place cod amongst tomatoes and bake for 19 mins till fish is cooked

Serving

  • Parmentier potatoes
  • Veg, eg fine beans, baby carrots
