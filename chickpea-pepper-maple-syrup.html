Chickpea, peppers & maple syrup

    Roasted veg

  1. Finely chop and roast at 180°C for 30 mins
    • 1 tin of chickpeas
    • 1 corgette
    • 1 red onion
    • 3 sweet pointed red peppers
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp maple syrup
    • salt

    2. Serve

  2. Stir into veg
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  3. Serve with
    • hot smoked salmon
    • cooked rice / pearl barley / buckwheat

Notes

  • chilli flakes best added per person on serving
