Hot smoked salmon, chickpeas, peppers & maple syrup

Notes

  • Use small measuring jug for syrup, oil and vinegar
  • Use big bowl for veg, chickpeas and bulgar wheat
  • Can roast salmon in oven with veg

Roasted veg

  1. Mix in big bowl
    • 1 courgette
    • 1 yellow pepper
    • 2 sweet pointed red peppers
    • 1 (200g) large red onion chopped in medium chunks
  2. Add
    • 4 tbsp (60g) maple syrup
    • 3 tbsp (42g) olive oil
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes (no more)
    • ¼ tsp salt
  3. Cook in large deep roasting tin at 180°C for 10 mins
  4. Mix in
    • 1 tin chickpeas drained
  5. Roast at 180°C for 20 more mins
  6. Add to roasting tin and stir in
    • 2½ tbsp (38g) balsamic vinegar
  7. Add to roasting tin and mix till have right amount
    • 210g bulgar wheat / rice / pearl barley / buckwheat
  8. Break up and mix in
    • 2-3 fillets of hot smoked salmon

Serving

  • 3 portions

Notes

  • Cook too long and chickpeas go hard
  • Double up
    • Red pepper & yellow pepper & 3 sweet peppers
