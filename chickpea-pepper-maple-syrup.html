Chickpea, peppers & maple syrup

    Roasted veg

  1. Mix in big bowl
    • 1 tin of chickpeas
    • 1 corgette
    • 1 red onion
    • 3 sweet pointed red peppers
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp maple syrup
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
    • salt
  2. Roast at 180°C for 30 mins
  3. Remove from oven and return to bowl
  4. Stir in
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • 130g bulgar wheat

Serving

  • hot smoked salmon
  • 130g bulgar wheat / rice / pearl barley / buckwheat

Notes

  • double up if want leftovers for 2 people next day
  • if double up then use red pepper & yellow pepper & 3 sweet peppers
  • chilli flakes can be added per person on serving
