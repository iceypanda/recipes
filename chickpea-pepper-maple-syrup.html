Hot smoked salmon, chickpeas, peppers & maple syrup

Roasted veg

  1. Mix in big bowl
    • 1 corgette
    • 1 red onion
    • 1 yellow pepper
    • 2 sweet pointed red peppers
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 4 tbsp maple syrup
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes (no more)
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Roast at 180°C for 10 mins
  3. Mix in
    • 1 tin chickpeas
  4. Roast at 180°C for 20 more mins
  5. Add to roasting tin and stir in
    • 2½ tbsp balsamic vinegar
  6. Add to roasting tin and mix till have right amount
    • 160g bulgar wheat / 160g rice / pearl barley / buckwheat
  7. Break up 2-3 fillets hot smoked salmon and mix in roasting tin

Serving

  • 3 portions

Notes

  • Made: Apr 2023, Aug 2023: forgot balsamic, used extra pepper instead of pointed peppers
  • Cook too long and chickpeas go hard
  • Double up
    • use red pepper & yellow pepper & 3 sweet peppers
    • use large metal bowl to mix
    • roast all in steep sided roasting tin
