Chickpea, peppers & maple syrup

Roasted veg

  1. Mix in big bowl
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 1 corgette
    • 1 red onion
    • 1 yellow pepper
    • 2 sweet pointed red peppers
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp maple syrup
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Roast at 180°C for 30 mins
  3. Add to roasting tin and stir in
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  4. Add to roasting tin and mix until have right amount
    • bulgar wheat / rice / pearl barley / buckwheat
  5. Break up 180g hot smoked salmon and distribute on top after serving into bowls

Serving

  • 3 portions

Notes

  • cook too long and chickpeas go hard
  • 130g bulgar wheat
  • double up
    • use red pepper & yellow pepper & 3 sweet peppers
    • use large metal bowl to mix
    • roast all in steep sided roasting tin
