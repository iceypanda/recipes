Hot smoked salmon, chickpeas, peppers & maple syrup

Roasted veg

  1. Mix in big bowl
    • 1 corgette
    • 1 red onion
    • 1 yellow pepper
    • 2 sweet pointed red peppers
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 4 tbsp maple syrup
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Roast at 180°C for 10 mins
  3. Mix in
    • 1 tin chickpeas
  4. Roast at 180°C for 20 more mins
  5. Add to roasting tin and stir in
    • 2½ tbsp balsamic vinegar (too much or not enough?)
  6. Add to roasting tin and mix until have right amount
    • 130g bulgar wheat / 160g rice / pearl barley / buckwheat
  7. Break up 4 fillets hot smoked salmon and mix in roasting tin

Serving

  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Made: 1 Apr 2023
  • Increased maple from 2 tbsp and balsamic from 1 tbsp
  • Cook too long and chickpeas go hard
  • Double up
    • use red pepper & yellow pepper & 3 sweet peppers
    • use large metal bowl to mix
    • roast all in steep sided roasting tin
