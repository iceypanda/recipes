Beef Cobbler

  • Preheat oven to 140°C

Beef

  1. Season beef with salt and ground black pepper
  2. Brown 800g stewing steak in batches
    1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in pan
    2. Fry beef for 5 mins until brown
    3. Transfer to casserole dish
  3. Deglaze the pan with
    • 187ml red wine
  4. Pour wine into casserole dish

Stew

  1. Add to casserole dish
    • 2 carrots
    • 10 shallots
    • 350ml stock
    • 250ml tomato passata
    • 3 bay leaves
    • 1 star anise
  2. Bring to simmer on hob
  3. Cook in oven at 140°C for 2½ hours

Scones

  1. Add to bowl
    • 175g self-raising flour
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Rub in until resembles course breadcrumbs
    • 75g butter chilled and cubed
  3. Stir in
    • 75g Cheddar grated
    • 1 tsp English mustard
  4. Beat separately
    • 125ml milk
    • 1 large egg
  5. Stir milk into flour mix until soft dough
  6. Roll into equal 9 scone-sized circles
  7. Brush tops with beaten egg

Cobbler

  1. Place scones on top of stew
  2. Bake uncovered in oven at 200°C for 20-25 mins until golden brown

Notes

  1. Recipe uses no garlic, could include whole garlic cloves
  2. Did not add much wine first time
