Beef Cobbler

Stew

  1. Preheat oven to 140°C
  2. Brown 800g stewing steak in batches
    • Add 1 tbsp olive oil to pan, season with black pepper and fry for 5 mins until meat is brown
    • Transfer to casserole dish
  3. Deglaze the pan using ½ glass red wine
  4. To casserole add
    • 2 carrots
    • 10 shallots
    • 350ml beef stock
    • 250ml tomato passata
    • 3 bay leaves
    • 1 star anise
  5. Bring to simmer on hob
  6. Cook in oven at 140°C for 2½ hours

Scones

  1. Sift 175g self-raising flour and ½ tsp salt into bowl
  2. Rub in 75g chilled butter until resembles course breadcrumbs
  3. Stir in 75g Cheddar and 1 tsp English mustard
  4. Beat 1 large egg with 125ml milk
  5. Stir into flour mix until until soft sticky dough
  6. Roll into equal 9 scone-sized circles and brush tops with beaten egg

Cobbler

  1. Place scones on top of stew
  2. Bake uncovered in oven at 200°C for 20-25 mins until golden brown

Notes

  1. Recipe uses no garlic, could include whole garlic cloves
  2. Did not add much wine first time
