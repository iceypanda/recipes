Fish & red lentil curry

  1. Fry until seeds start to sizzle
    • vegetable oil
    • 1 tsp black mustard seeds
  2. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 1 red onion
  3. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 1 large knob ginger
  4. Add and heat for 2 mins
    • 2 tomatoes chopped
  5. Add and mix in
    • 150g red lentils
    • 2 tsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • ¾ tsp chilli powder
    • ½ tsp salt
  6. Add and simmer for 10 mins until lentils are soft
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 100ml boiling water
  7. Add and cook on medium heat for 5 mins
    • 400g white fish

Serving

  • rice, bread, mango chutney

Notes

  • white fish: hake, cod, haddock
  • Original recipe
