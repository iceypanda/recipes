Baked new potatoes

  1. Boil new potatoes until almost tender, about 15 mins
  2. Drain potatoes and coat with
    • olive oil
    • salt & pepper
  3. Roast in oven at 200°C for 30 mins until golden

Notes

  • Let potatoes cool for 10 mins before serving
  • Do not need to boil hard, maybe don't even need to boil if bake longer
  • Try without olive oil, and serve with butter

Potato varieties

  • Celandine - not good for baking, sweet and slimy when cooked
