Berry compote

  1. Simmer for 4 mins on medium heat
    • blueberries / blackberries
    • 1 tbsp golden caster sugar per 100g fruit
    • 1 tbsp water (if no liquid with fruit)
  2. Add to taste
    • lemon juice

Serving

  • 50-70g fruit per portion

Notes

  • Must be made fresh on day, next day fruit will taste off
  • Lemon juice important for taste
  • Water helps the berries to cook and dissolves the sugar
  • Can add cornflour or more sugar to thicken if necessary
