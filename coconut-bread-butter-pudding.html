Coconut bread & butter pudding

  • Preheat oven to 160°C

Raisins

  1. Soak in hot water for 10 min
    • 50g raisins
  2. Drain

Eggs

  1. Beat for 3-5 mins
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1½ tbsp caster sugar

Custard

  1. Heat to 70°C
    • 250ml (258g) milk
    • 160g coconut cream
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  2. Pour slowly over eggs stirring constantly so they do not curdle

Bread & butter

  1. Remove crusts and cut into slices then triangles
    • ½ (200g without slices) loaf bread
  2. Butter one side of each slice
  3. Make one layer of bread with butter side up
  4. Sprinkle half raisins
  5. Add second layer of bread with butter side up
  6. Sprinkle remaining raisins
  7. Pour custard over the bread, push bread down to absorb liquid
  8. Sprinkle over
    • 2 tsp light brown sugar
  9. Cook in oven at 180°C for 40-45 mins
  10. Leave to cool and set for 1 hour
