Beef Cobbler

Stew

  1. Brown 800g stewing steak in batches by frying in olive oil then transferring to slow cooker
  2. Deglaze the pan using 125ml red wine, boiling off alcohol
  3. To casserole add:
    • 2 carrots
    • 10 shallots
    • 350ml beef stock
    • 250ml tomato passata
    • 3 bay leaves
    • 1 star anise
  4. Cook in slow cooker on high for 4 hours

Scones

  1. Sift 200g self-raising flour into bowl
  2. Rub in 55g chilled butter until resembles course breadcrumbs
  3. Stir in 85g Cheddar
  4. Beat 1 egg with 100ml full-fat milk
  5. Stir into flour mix until until soft sticky dough
  6. Roll into equal 9 scone-sized circles and brush tops with beaten egg
  7. Bake uncovered in oven at 200°C for 20-25 mins until golden brown

Comments

  1. Recipe uses no garlic, could include whole garlic cloves
