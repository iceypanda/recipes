Roast potatoes

  • Preheat oven to 200°C

Potatoes

  1. Cut potatoes into 1-2" pieces and dry
  2. Mix potatoes with
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • ½ tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp smoked paprika
    • ¼ tsp salt
  3. Mix in
    • olive oil
    • 3 bay leaves

Roasting

  1. Turn heat down from 200°C to 180°C
  2. Roast till golden, moving potatoes around halfway

Notes

  • Space potatoes out so can get dry and crispy
  • Mix potatoes with spices first so can coat well before adding oil
