Venison & red cabbage stew

Venison

  1. Fry for 5 mins until browned then transfer to slow cooker
    • olive oil
    • 350g venison cubed

Veg

  1. Fry for 7 mins
    • 10g butter
    • ⅛ red cabbage finely shredded
    • 110g shallots
  2. Add and fry for 1 min then transfer to slow cooker
    • 1 clove garlic

Gravy

  1. Stir in and bring to boil
    • 100ml red wine
    • 110ml beef stock
    • 1½ tsp tomato puree
    • 1½ tsp cranberry sauce
    • 60g dried cranberries
    • 1 tsp thyme
    • 1 tbsp plain flour sprinkled over
  2. Pour gravy over venison and cook for 5 hours on low

Notes

  • Serve with green beans and mash
