Venison & red cabbage stew

  1. Fry for 5 mins until browned then transfer to slow cooker
    • 350g cubed venison
    • olive oil
  2. Fry for 7 mins then transfer to slow cooker
    • 10g butter
    • ⅛ finely shredded red cabbage
  3. Add 110g shallots to slow cooker
  4. Fry for 1 min
    • 1 clove garlic
    • olive oil
  5. Sprinkle over 1 tbsp plain flour
  6. Stir in and bring to boil
    • 100ml red wine
    • 110ml beef stock
    • 1½ tsp tomato puree
    • 1½ tsp cranberry sauce
    • 60g dried cranberries
    • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  7. Pour gravy over venison and cook for 10 hours on low

Comments

Serve with green beans and mash
