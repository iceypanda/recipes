Venison & red cabbage stew

Venison

  1. Fry for 5 mins until browned then transfer to slow cooker
    • olive oil
    • 350g venison cubed

Veg

  1. Fry for 7 mins
    • 10g butter
    • 110g red cabbage finely shredded
    • 4 shallots finely chopped
  2. Add and fry for 2 mins then transfer to slow cooker
    • 2 cloves garlic

Gravy

  1. Stir together
    • 100ml red wine
    • 110ml beef stock (½ rich beef stock pot)
    • 1½ tsp tomato puree
    • 1½ tsp cranberry sauce
    • 60g dried cranberries
    • 1 tsp thyme
  2. Add 1 tbsp plain flour sprinkled over in tea strainer gradually so mixes in without forming lumps
  3. Bring to boil
  4. Pour gravy over venison and cook for 3 hours on high

Notes

  • Served with green beans and anya potatoes
  • Used ½ tsp thyme but could use more
