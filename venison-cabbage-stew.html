Venison & red cabbage stew

Venison

  1. Fry for 5 mins till browned then transfer to slow cooker
    • olive oil
    • 400-500g venison cubed

Veg

  1. Fry for 7 mins
    • 20g butter
    • 150g red cabbage finely shredded
    • 6 shallots finely chopped
  2. Add and fry for 2 mins then transfer to slow cooker
    • 3 cloves garlic

Gravy

  1. Stir together
    • 150ml red wine
    • 150ml beef stock (½ rich beef stock pot)
    • 2 tsp tomato puree
    • 2 tsp cranberry sauce
    • 90g dried cranberries
    • 1½ tsp thyme
    • seasoning
  2. Strain 1 tbsp plain flour very slowly over so mixes in without forming lumps
  3. Bring to boil
  4. Pour gravy over venison and cook for 3 hours on high

Serving

  • Green beans
  • New potatoes

Notes

  • Timings: 65 mins till everything in slow cooker
  • Made: 18 Mar 2022
  • Try cornflour in water instead of flour to avoid lumps
