Eggs on potato

  • Preheat oven to 180°C
  1. Grate with food processor
    • 500g potatoes unpeeled
  2. Leave for 5 mins
  3. In muslin cloth wring potato till no more water comes out
  4. Mix in bowl
    • potato
    • 1 small onion
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp turmeric
  5. Spread into roasting dish lined with baking paper
  6. Bake at 180°C for 40 mins
  7. Crack into separate indents in potato
    • 2 eggs
  8. Bake at 180°C for 10 mins
    9. Scatter with
    • coriander leaves chopped

Serving

  • Tomato chutney
  • 2 portions
