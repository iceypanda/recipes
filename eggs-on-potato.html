Eggs on potato

  • Preheat oven to 180°C
  • Stir potato every 10 mins to release steam
  1. Grate with food processor
    • 500g potatoes unpeeled
  2. Leave for 5 mins
  3. Wring potato till no more water comes out
  4. Mix in bowl
    • potato
    • 1 small onion
    • 2 tbsp (27g) vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp turmeric
  5. Spread into an oiled roasting dish
  6. Bake at 180°C for 40 mins
  7. Crack into separate indents in potato
    • 2 eggs
  8. Bake at 180°C for 10-14 mins till eggs cooked
  9. Scatter with
    • coriander leaves chopped
    • Aleppo pepper
    • black pepper

Serving

  • Tomato chutney
  • Smoked tofu paella
  • 2 portions
