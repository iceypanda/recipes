Pineapple pancakes

Mixture

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 125g self-raising flour
    • 1 tbsp golden caster sugar
    • pinch salt
  2. Whisk in
    • 1 egg
    • 85ml (88g) milk

Pancakes

  1. Heat in frying pan
    • vegetable oil
  2. Pour out 3 pineapple ring size amounts of mixture
  3. Place on top
    • 1 pineapple ring
    • 1 glace cherry
  4. Cook for 3 mins
  5. Scatter with
    • golden caster sugar
  6. Turn over
  7. Cook till pineapple browned
  8. Serve pineapple up with
    • dollop yogurt
    • drizzle honey

Serving

  • 6 portions

Notes

  • Original recipe: Nadia, p36
