Butter bean & avocado spread

  1. Roughly mash in bowl
    • 1 tin butter beans drained
    • 1 large avocado
  2. Mix in
    • 100g kale / spinach finely sliced
  3. Mix in
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • zest of 1 lemon
    • juice of 1 lemon
  4. Add to taste
    • salt
    • pepper
  5. Leave in sealed container overnight
  6. Spread on toast

Serving

  • 2 portions

Notes

  • Original recipe: Healthy Made Simple, p28
