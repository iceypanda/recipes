Potato salad

  1. Cook 800g new potatoes and cut into chunks
  2. Add potatoes to
    • 3 finely chopped shallots
    • 1 tbsp capers
    • 2 tbsp finely chopped cornichons
    • up to 3 tbsp mayonnaise to bind
  3. Mix and add to give sharpness
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  4. Add chopped parsley

Alternative ingredients

  • soured cream for mayonnaise
  • red onion / spring onions / chives for shallots
  • lemon juice for vinegar
  • Dijon mustard / whole grain mustard for capers
  • finely chopped radishes / kohl rabi
  • ground black pepper
