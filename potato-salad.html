Potato salad

Dressing

  1. Mix and leave to macerate for 20 mins
    • 3 finely chopped shallots
    • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
    • 1 tbsp capers
    • 2 tbsp finely chopped cornichons
  2. Add and mix
    • 3 tbsp creme fraiche

Salad

  1. Cook 800g new potatoes and cut into chunks
  2. Mix with dressing
  3. Add chopped parsley

Alternative ingredients

  • mayonnaise: soured cream / creme fraiche
  • shallots: red onion / spring onions / chives
  • vinegar: lemon juice
  • capers: Dijon mustard / whole grain mustard / horseradish
  • finely chopped radishes / kohl rabi
  • ground black pepper
