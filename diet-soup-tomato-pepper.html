Roasted tomato & red pepper soup

Veg

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 4 tomatoes halved
    • 1 red pepper chopped
    • 1 red onion chopped
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 4 tsp smoked paprika
    • 2 tsp olive oil
  2. Bake at 180°C for 15 mins

Soup

  1. Heat for 5 mins
    • veg
    • 3 palm-size silken tofu
    • 400g water hot
  2. Add
    • 1 tsp white pepper
    • fresh basil
    • black pepper
  3. Blitz

Serving

  • 2 portions
