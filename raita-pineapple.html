Raita - Pineapple

  1. Roast spices
    • 1 tsp cumin powder
    • ½ tsp marsala
  2. Add spices and blitz with mixer
    • 1 cup pineapple chunks
    • 1 cup natural yogurt
    • 2 tsp sugar
    • 30g mlik
    • ½ tsp black pepper powder
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
    • 1 tbsp coriander leaves

Notes

Can replace coriander with mint

Don't need to chop pineapple better to blitz with mixer
