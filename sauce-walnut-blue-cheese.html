Walnut & blue cheese sauce

  1. Cook gently until darkens whisking constantly
    • 15g butter
    • 15g plain flour
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins to reduce
    • 90ml white wine
    • ½ stock cube
  3. Lower heat and add then simmer for 5 mins
    • 50ml single cream
    • 60g walnuts roasted for 5 mins in air fryer
  4. Thin with water
  5. Remove heat and add
    • 30g roquefort
  6. Stir in more roquefort in 5g portions to taste

Serving

  • Pan-fry chicken breasts
  • Roast potatoes
  • Peas
  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Don't chop walnuts, leave as halves
