Walnut & blue cheese sauce

  1. Cook gently until darkens whisking constantly
    • 20g butter
    • 20g plain flour
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins to reduce
    • 90ml white wine
    • ½ stock cube
  3. Lower heat and add then simmer for 5 mins
    • 75ml single cream
    • 75g walnuts roasted
  4. Remove heat and add
    • 75g roquefort

Serving

  • Pan-fry chicken breasts
  • Roast potatoes
