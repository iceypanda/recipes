Blackberry crumble bars

Crumble base

  1. Mix in big silver bowl
    • 300g plain flour
    • 70g caster sugar
    • 50g light brown sugar
    • 60g oats
  2. Add and rub until like course breadcrumbs
    • 220 salted butter cold and chopped into small cubes
  3. Set aside ¼ (175g) of crumble
  4. Tip the remaining ¾ of crumble into 20x20cm baking tin and press down (can line with paper)
  5. Chill both for 20 mins
  6. Bake base for 20 mins at 180°C until set
  7. Leave to cool

Fruit bars

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 350-450g blackberries
    • 45g caster sugar
    • 1 tsp cornflour
    • juice of ½ lemon
  2. Pour fruit and liquid over cooled base
  3. Top fruit with remaining ¼ of crumble and press down
  4. Bake for 40 mins at 190°C
  5. Leave to cool for 2 hours
  6. Cut into portions, 4s then 4s

Serving

  • 12 or 16 portions
  • Calories = 3700, 1/12 = 307, 1/16 = 230

Notes

  • Oven takes 10 mins to heat to 190°C
  • Careful do not overbake the base
  • Could add walnuts / hazelnuts / more oats to topping
  • Original recipe
  • Made: 14 Apr 2023, 7 Nov 2022 with 50g blueberries, 6 Sep 2022
