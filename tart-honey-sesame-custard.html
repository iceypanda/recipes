Honey & sesame custard tart

Pastry

  1. Blitz fine
    • 175g plain flour
    • 95g unsalted butter
    • 40g icing sugar
    • pinch salt
  2. Add and pulse to ball
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 1 tbsp cold water
  3. Add more water if dry
  4. Bring together on floured surface
  5. Chill 3 hours

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C
  2. Roll pastry to 2-3mm thin on floured surface
  3. Line 23cm fluted tart tin with pastry
  4. Prick all over with fork
  5. Line with scrunched up baking paper
  6. Fill with baking beans
  7. Bake for 20 mins at 170°C
  8. Remove paper and beans
  9. Bake for 15 mins at 170°C
  10. Brush with little egg white
  11. Bake for 1 min at 170°C

Custard

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 6 large egg yolks
    • 1 large egg
  2. Heat in saucepan and heat until steaming
    • 600ml whipping cream
    • 150g honey
  3. Remove heat and add
    • ¼ tsp salt
  4. Slowly whisk cream mixture into eggs
  5. Leave for 2 mins
  6. Skim off foam
  7. Sieve into jug

Tart

  1. Pour half custard into the pastry base
  2. Transfer to oven
  3. Pour in rest of custard
  4. Bake at 110°C for 40-45 mins
  5. Remove from oven and leave to cool

Topping

  1. Bring to boil and heat while stirring for 4 mins
    • 80g sesame seeds
    • 70g caster sugar
    • 60g water
  2. Reduce heat and cook until water evaporates, seeds brown and clump and sugar starts to caramelise
  3. Tip on to tray
  4. Sprinkle with salt
  5. Leave to cool
  6. Place around edges of tart
    • 8 dollops creme fraiche
  7. Sprinkle around edges of tart

Serving

  • 12 portions
