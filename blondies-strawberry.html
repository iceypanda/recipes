Strawberry blondies

Strawberries

  1. Lay on baking tray flat side up
    • 400g strawberries hulled & halved
  2. Roast at 150°C for 35-40 mins
  3. Leave to cool

Dough balls (optional)

  1. Blitz until balls start to form
    • 110g plain flour
    • 60g unsalted butter
    • 20g caster sugar
    • 1 tsp water
    • pinch salt
  2. Roll into lots of little balls of dough of different sizes
  3. Bake at 170°C for 10-12 mins until slightly golden
  4. Leave to cool

Batter

  1. Brown in pan on medium heat
    • 150g unsalted butter
  2. Leave to cool a little
  3. Mix in bowl
    • 335g soft light brown sugar
    • butter
  4. Mix in
    • 3 large eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Mix in
    • 270g plain flour sifted
    • ¼ tsp baking powder
    • ⅛ tsp salt
  6. Gently fold in with spatula
    • 150g white chocolate pieces chilled
    • 100g dough balls

Blondies

  1. Spread half batter in lined 23x23cm tin
  2. Cover with roasted strawberries
  3. Spread remaining batter
  4. Place on top
    • 4 strawberries hulled & thinly sliced
  5. Scatter over remaining dough balls
  6. Bake at 170°C for 40 mins

Serving

  • 16 portions
