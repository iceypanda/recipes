Strawberry cake

  • Preheat oven to 160°C

Strawberry reduction

  1. Blitz
    • 225g strawberries
  2. Cook in saucepan for 5 mins till thick puree
  3. Leave to cool

Strawberry mixture

  1. Melt
    • 200g butter
  2. Leave to cool
  3. Mix in
    • strawberry reduction
    • 75g milk
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • zest of 1 lemon
    • drops of red/pink food colouring

Cake

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 4 medium eggs
    • 250g golden caster sugar
  2. Whisk till double size, thick and moussey
  3. Sieve over
    • 250g self-raising flour
  4. Pour down side of bowl so sinks to the bottom
    • strawberry mixture
  5. Gently fold together with big metal spoon till all mixed with no streaks
  6. Divide batter between two 20cm cake tins lined with baking paper
  7. Bake at 160°C for 25 mins till risen and cake tester comes out clean
  8. Leave tins for 10 mins then tip onto wire rack

Icing

  1. Whisk till creamy
    • 150g butter
  2. Whisk in as two portions
    • 100g soft full-fat cheese
    • 400g icing sugar
    • 1 tsp milk
    • drops of red/pink food colouring
    • 10g freeze-dried strawberry powder
  3. Spread over cake with palette knife

Serving

  • 10 portions
