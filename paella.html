Paella

  1. Heat for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 1 red pepper sliced
    • 1 yellow pepper sliced
  2. Add and heat for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Add and mix well
    • 300g paella rice
    • 4 sun-dried tomatoes chopped
    • large pinch of saffron
    • 1 tsp sweet smoked paprika
    • 1 tsp paprika
  4. Add and simmer for 15-30 mins until rice is cooked
    • 850ml stock
  5. Add towards end before rice is cooked
    • 300g prawns
    • 100g peas defrosted
  6. Scatter with parsely chopped

Notes

  • optional veg: artichokes, corgette
  • optional: chorizo, clams, mussels
