Paella

  1. Heat for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 1 red pepper sliced
    • 1 yellow pepper sliced
  2. Add and heat for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Add and mix well
    • 250g paella rice
    • 3 sun-dried tomatoes chopped
    • large pinch of saffron
    • 1 tsp sweet smoked paprika
    • 1 tsp paprika
  4. Add and simmer for 20 mins until rice is cooked
    • 750ml stock
  5. Add and warm for 1 min
    • 150g peas defrosted (cooked if no prawns)
  6. Add and cook until hot
    • 300g prawns
  7. Scatter with
    • parsely chopped
    • 2 tsp lemon juice

Serving

  • Chicken pieces

Notes

  • maybe cook prawns & peas before adding
  • optional veg: artichokes, corgette
  • optional: chorizo, clams, mussels
