Paella

  1. Heat for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 1 red pepper sliced
    • 1 yellow pepper sliced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Add and mix well
    • 250g paella rice
    • large pinch of saffron
    • 1 tsp sweet smoked paprika
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • ¼ tsp mild chilli powder
  4. Add and simmer for 25 mins until rice is cooked
    • 750ml stock (53g concentrated stock)
    • 3 sun-dried tomatoes soaked & blitzed
  5. Mix in
    • 150g peas cooked
  6. Add
    • 2 tsp lemon juice

Serving

  • Parsely chopped
  • Prawns
  • Chicken pieces
  • Pan-fried cod

Notes

  • Made: 22 Jul 2023
  • Cook prawns & peas before adding
  • Optional veg: artichokes, corgette
  • Optional: chorizo, clams, mussels, cooked seafood mix
