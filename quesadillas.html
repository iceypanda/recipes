Quesadillas

  1. Mix for filling:
    • grated Cheddar cheese
    • choped spring onions
    • fresh coriander
    • 1 finely chopped red pepper
    • 1 finely chopped red chilli
  2. Place filling between 2 tortillas and heat in pan on medium heat
  3. Serve in quarters with guacamole

Comments

Divide filling between 4 pairs of tortillas
