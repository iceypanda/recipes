Quesadillas

Cooking

  1. Place on half of 1 tortilla
    • cooked filling
    • handful cheddar / mozzarella grated
  2. Fold over so half-moon
  3. Heat for 3 mins
  4. Press plate on top for 10 secs and use to flip
  5. Heat for 3 mins
  6. Press plate on top for 10 secs
