Borscht

  1. Heat in a pan for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 2 carrots diced
    • 3 beets diced
  2. Add and cook for 2 min
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add
    • 1l stock
    • 1 potato diced
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • ½ cabbage
    • 1½ tbsp dried dill (if no fresh)
  4. Bring to the boil then simmer for 30 mins
  5. Remove heat and add
    • 1½ tbsp lemon juice
    • ¼ cup fresh dill chopped (if not added dried)
  6. Blitz some of the soup
  7. Serve with soured cream / natural yogurt

Notes

  • Dice veg to 5mm cubes
  • Stock
    • Try 2 beef oxo & 1 no salt veg
    • 1 chicken & 1 no salt veg
  • Optional
    • Red wine
    • Celery, broccoli
