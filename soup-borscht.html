Borscht

  1. Heat in a pan for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 2 carrots
    • 3 diced beets
  2. Add 2 garlic cloves and heat for 1 min
  3. Add
    • 1l vegetable stock
    • 1 diced potato
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • ½ cabbage
    • 1 tbsp dried dill (if no fresh)
  4. Bring to the boil then simmer for 20 mins
  5. Remove heat and add
    • 1½ tbsp lemon juice
    • ¼ cup fresh chopped dill
  6. Blitz some of the soup
  7. Serve with soured cream
