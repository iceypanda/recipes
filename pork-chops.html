Pork chops

  1. Trim skin and fry in pan with oil for 1 min each side
  2. Cook pan in oven at 180° for 15 mins

Notes

Serve with apple sauce

Apple sauce

  1. Cook chopped apples with little water for 15 mins
  2. Add sugar to sweeten
  3. Add honey to sweeten and for taste
  4. Add lemon juice (optional)
