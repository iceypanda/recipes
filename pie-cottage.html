Cottage pie

Filling

  1. Heat in pan until browned all over
    • olive oil
    • 500g beef mince
  2. Pour away liquid from mince
  3. Add and cook for 10 mins with lid on
    • 1 large onion
    • 2 carrots diced
    • 1 red pepper chopped
    • 2 sticks celery diced
    • 3 cloves garlic
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 90ml red wine (optional)
  5. Add and simmer for 20 mins with lid off to reduce
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 60ml salted veg stock (½ cube)
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 1 tsp thyme
    • 1 tsp brown sugar
    • ¾ tsp celery salt
    • ¾ tsp rosemary
    • ¾ tsp Worcestershire sauce
    • ½ tsp fish sauce
  6. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 100g peas defrosted
  7. Pour into warmed oven dish (3.1L Pyrex roaster)
  8. Flatten with spoon
  9. Preheat oven to 190°C

Potato

  1. Cook
    • 1000g (peeled) potatoes mashed
  2. Mix in
    • 30g butter
    • 50g milk
    • 15g parsely leaves chopped (optional)
    • ¼ tsp (small) salt

Pie

  1. Spoon mash over filling
  2. Sprinkle over
    • 60g cheddar / Parmesan grated
  3. Bake at 190°C for 25 mins until golden
  4. Leave for 10 mins before serving

Serving

  • Greens / sweetcorn
  • Roasted carrots / parsnips cooked with pie in oven
  • 6 portions
