Lentils & beans

  • Wash and rinse thoroughly before cooking

Yellow split peas

  1. Soak in to cold water for 12 hours
  2. Simmer for 1 hour to cook

Brown lentils

  1. Soak in to cold water for 12 hours
  2. Simmer for 45 mins to cook

Green lentils

  1. Soak in to cold water for 12 hours
  2. Simmer for 20 mins to cook

Puy lentils

Type of green lentil

  1. Simmer for 20 mins to cook

Red lentils

  1. Simmer for 30 mins to cook
