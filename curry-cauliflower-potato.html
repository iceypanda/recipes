Cauliflower & potato curry

  1. Heat for 8 mins then set aside
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1 onion
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
    • 3cm piece ginger grated
  2. Toast in dry pan for 30 secs
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  3. Add and cook over medium heat for 5 mins
    • ½ tbsp vegetable oil
    • 2 medium tomatoes grated
    • ½ green chilli chopped
    • ½ tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp cumin
    • ½ tsp turmeric
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 450g (weight after peeling) potatoes
    • 600ml water boiled
  5. Add and simmer for 10 mins till potatoes and cauliflower cooked
    • ½ large cauliflower cut into florets
  6. Add
    • 40g natural yogurt

Serving

  • 3 portions
