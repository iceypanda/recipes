Chickpea pork

  1. Drain 480g jar of red peppers and dice into 1cm chunks
  2. Season 400g pork fillet with black pepper
  3. Sear pork fillet in pan for 5 mins with olive oil
  4. Remove pork and fry for 2 mins:
    • chopped peppers
    • 1 heaped tsp fennel seeds
  5. Add and bring to boil:
    • 2 tins of chick peas
    • water (not too much)
  6. Add back pork so it is touching base of pan
  7. Add 200g spinach
  8. Cover and simmer gently for 12 mins until pork is cooked through
  9. Rest for 2 mins
  10. Add splash of red wine vinegar and drizzle with olive oil

Comments

Do not simmer too hot or pork will overcook
