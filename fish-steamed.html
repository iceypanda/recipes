Steamed fish

  • can either air fry or microwave
  • could microwave in Pyrex bowl

Thai

  1. Place on square of parchment paper
    • 200g cod / salmon fillets
  2. Scatter with
    • knob of ginger
    • 1 clove of garlic
    • 1 red chilli
    • zest and juice of ½ lime
  3. Place next to fish
    • 2 pak choi quartered
  4. Pour over pak choi
    • 1½ tbsp soy sauce
  5. Seal paper leaving air above fish for steam to circulate
  6. Air fry on bake at 160°C for 15 mins

Chinese

  1. Place in bowl
    • 200g cod / salmon fillets
  2. Squeeze over
    • ?g ginger crushed
  3. Add
    • ?g ginger chopped
    • salt & pepper
  4. Leave for up to 1 hour
  5. Cook in mircowave for 4-5 mins on high
  6. Pour over
    • ?tbsp soy sauce
  7. Top with
    • spring onions shredded
  8. Heat in pan till starts smoking
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 2 tsp sesame oil
  9. Pour oil over fish
