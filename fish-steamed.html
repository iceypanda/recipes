Steamed fish

Cooking

  • Air fry on bake at 160°C for 15-20 mins
  • In oven 180°C for 20 mins

Thai

  1. Place on square of parchment paper
    • 200g cod / salmon fillets
  2. Scatter with
    • piece ginger
    • 1 clove mgarlic
    • 1 red chilli
    • zest and juice ½ lime
  3. Place next to fish
    • 2 pak choi quartered
  4. Pour over pak choi
    • 1½ tbsp soy sauce
  5. Seal paper leaving air above fish for steam to circulate
  6. Cook

Chinese

  1. Place in parchment paper
    • 200g cod / salmon fillets
  2. Add
    • ginger chopped
    • salt & pepper
  3. Seal paper
  4. Cook
  5. Pour over
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Top with
    • spring onions shredded
  7. Heat in pan till starts smoking
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 2 tsp sesame oil
  8. Pour oil over fish
