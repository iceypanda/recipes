Duck legs - Confit

  • Oil does not have to cover meat
  • Skin down to start
  • Turn over halfway
  • Duck confit heavy so don't need heavy sauce with it
  1. Add to 1.8l slow cooker
    • 2 duck legs
    • 100-150ml olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic chopped
  2. Herbs from
    • fresh rosemary
    • thyme
    • bay leaves
  3. Cook on high for 2-3 hours
  4. Strain to allow oil to drain off
  5. Cook in oven or grill to crisp up skin for 5-10 mins

Duck legs - Roasted

  1. Pat legs dry and season
  2. Leave till room temperature
  3. Prick skin all over
  4. Cook in oven at
    • 180°C 40 min (difficult to shred)
    • 170°C 60 min (try this instead)
    • 160°C 90 min
  5. Cover and rest for 10 mins

Duck breast - Pieces

  1. Cut 2 duck breasts (340g) into 2-3cm pieces
  2. Alkalise and marinate in fridge for 1 hour
  3. Fry in hot oil for 2-3 mins till lightly coloured on all sides

Duck breast - Seared

  1. Remove excess fat
  2. Score cross-hatch on skin
  3. Pat dry
  4. Salt and pepper both sides
  5. Place on cold frying pan skin side down with no oil
  6. Heat for 6 mins pouring off fat
  7. Turn over and heat other side for 4 mins
  8. Heat on sides for colour
  9. Air fry on roast at 200°C for 5 mins to finish
  10. Cover with foil and rest for 5 mins
  11. Slice

Notes

  • Keep fat for potatoes
  • Use tongs to move meat
