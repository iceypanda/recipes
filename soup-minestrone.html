Minestrone soup

  1. Heat in a pan
    • 1 red onion
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • plenty of rosemary
  2. Add and heat for 3-4 mins
    • 250g pancetta
  3. Add and heat for 2 mins
    • 1 swede
    • 1 carrot
    • 1 leak
    • 2 celery sticks
  4. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  5. Add and heat for 15 mins
    • 2 litres chicken stock
  6. Add and heat for 7 mins
    • 100g spaghetti
  7. Add and heat for 8 mins
    • ½ cabbage
    • 250g mange tout
  8. Add
    • 15 cherry tomatoes
