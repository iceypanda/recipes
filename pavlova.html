Strawberry Pavlova

Meringue

  1. Heat oven to 150°C
  2. Mark out circle size of dinner plate (20cm) on baking paper
  3. Whisk 4 egg whites on slow speed separate yolks with hands
  4. Whisk in 230g white caster sugar on faster speed 1 tbsp at a time for 5 seconds each
  5. Whisk until soft peaks form and meringue is glossy can use food mixer for 10 mins
  6. Whisk on low speed
    • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
    • 1 tsp cornflour
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Mound meringue in middle of circle and spread with sides little higher than middle
  8. Turn oven down to 140°C and bake for 1 hour on lower shelf or 120°C for 1 hour 15 mins for softer meringues in middle
  9. Let meringue completely cool inside oven

Sauce

  1. Blitz until smooth
    • 100g strawberries hulled and halved
    • 100g raspberries
    • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  2. Sieve to collect puree

Pavlova

  1. Whip
    • 300ml double cream
    • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  2. Spread cream into middle of meringue
  3. Put onto cream
    • 300g strawberries hulled and halved
    • 100g raspberries

Comments
