Strawberry Pavlova

Meringue

  1. Heat oven to 150°C
  2. Mark out circle size of dinner plate (20cm) on baking paper
  3. Whisk on low speed
    • 4 egg whites
    • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Whisk in on faster speed 1 tbsp at a time for 5 seconds each
    • 1 tsp cornflour
    • 230g white caster sugar
  5. Whisk until soft peaks form and meringue is glossy
  6. Mound meringue in middle of circle and spread with sides little higher than middle
  7. Turn oven down to 140°C and bake for 1 hour on lower shelf or 120°C for 1 hour 15 mins for softer meringues in middle
  8. Let meringue completely cool inside oven

Sauce

  • Raspberry & strawberry coulis with 200g fruit

Pavlova

  1. Whip
    • 300ml double cream
    • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  2. Spread cream into middle of meringue
  3. Put onto cream
    • 300g strawberries hulled and halved
    • 100g raspberries

Notes

  • Sugar reduced from 250g
  • Strawberries reduced from 500g total
