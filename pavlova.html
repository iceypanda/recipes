Strawberry Pavlova

Meringue

  1. Heat oven to 150°C
  2. Mark out circle size of dinner plate on baking paper
  3. Whisk 4 egg whites until stiff in non-plastic bowl
  4. Whisk in 230g caster sugar 1 tbsp at a time for 5 seconds each
  5. Whisk in
    • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
    • 1 tsp cornflour
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Spread meringue in circle with sides little higher than middle
  7. Turn oven down to 140°C and bake for 1 hour
  8. Let meringue completely cool inside oven

Sauce

  1. Blitz until smooth
    • 100g strawberries hulled and halved
    • 100g raspberries
    • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  2. Sieve to collect puree

Pavlova

  1. Whip
    • 250ml double cream
    • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  2. Spread cream into middle of meringue
  3. Put onto cream
    • 300g strawberries hulled and halved
    • 100g raspberries

Comments

Sugar reduce from 250g

Cream reduced from 300ml

Strawberries reduced from 500g total
