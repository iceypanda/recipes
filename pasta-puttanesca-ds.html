Pasta puttanesca - DS

  1. Gently heat until almost cooked
    • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion or 3 shallots
  2. Heat for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic grated
  3. Heat until sizzling
    • 1 red chilli chopped
    • 5 anchovies
    • 50g black olives quartered
    • 1 tbsp capers
  4. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 100ml passata
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 small tin (150g drained) tuna
    • chilli flakes (optional for heat)
  5. Mix in spaghetti cooking water until smooth
  6. Add cooked spaghetti and mix
  7. Top with
    • fresh parsley chopped
    • parmesan grated

Serving

  • 2 portions

Notes

  • 90-100g spaghetti per portion
