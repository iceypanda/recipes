Carrot, chickpea & mandarin salad

Veg

  1. Heat fan oven to 180C
  2. Roasted carrots with coriander & cumin seeds and mandarin
  3. Mix thoroughly and heat in roasting tin for 30 mins
    • 1 1 tin chickpeas
    • pinch chilli flakes
    • 2 sprigs rosemary
    • olive oil
    • seasoning

Salsa

  1. Blitz 25g walnuts in food processor
  2. Add and blitz again
    • 20g anchovies
    • ¼ tbsp harissa
    • ½ tbsp chopped parsley
    • ½ tbsp mandarin & lemon juice
    • 50ml olive oil
  3. Season to taste

Mandarins

  • Dry fry slices from 2 mandarins until caramelised and chared

Making the salad

  1. Put in bowl
    • 50g fregola (cooked)
    • Mandarin & lemon juice and toss to coat
    • Carrots, chickpeas, mandarins
    • Half the salsa
  2. Dress with remaining salsa

Serving

  • salad leaves
  • burrata

Notes

  • chickpeas increased to whole tin instead of half
