Carrot, chickpea & mandarin salad

  • Preheat oven to 180°C

Veg

  1. Roasted carrots with coriander & cumin seeds and mandarin
  2. Mix thoroughly and heat in roasting tin at 180°C for 30 mins
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 2 sprigs rosemary chopped
    • ¼ chilli flakes
    • olive oil
    • seasoning

Salsa

  1. Blitz in food processor
    • 25g walnuts
  2. Add and blitz again
    • 50ml olive oil
    • 20g anchovies
    • ½ tbsp chopped parsley
    • ½ tbsp mandarin & lemon juice
    • ¼ tbsp harissa
  3. Season to taste

Mandarins

  • Dry fry supremes from 2 mandarins till caramelised and chared

Making the salad

  1. Combine in bowl
    • 50g fregola (cooked)
    • Mandarin & lemon juice and toss to coat
    • Carrots, chickpeas, mandarins
    • Half the salsa
  2. Dress with remaining salsa

Serving

  • salad leaves
  • burrata

Notes

  • chickpeas increased to whole tin instead of half
