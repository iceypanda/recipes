Lemon meringue pie

Pastry base

  • Pastry

Orange juice

  1. Squeeze out
    • juice 1 orange
  2. Add water to make total liquid 200ml

Filling

  1. Mix in pan
    • zest 2 lemons
    • 100g golden caster sugar
    • 2 tbsp cornflour
  2. Strain in and combine
    • 125ml (from 2-3 lemons) lemon juice
  3. Strain in and combine
    • orange juice
  4. Heat till thickened, stirring constantly
  5. Remove heat
  6. Mix in till melted
    • 85g unsalted butter cubed
  7. Beat together separately then mix in
    • 3 egg yolks
    • 1 whole egg
  8. Return to heat for 5 mins till thickened, stirring constantly

Meringue

  • Meringue

Pie

  1. Pour the hot filling into the pastry case
  2. Pipe over the meringue starting from the edges
  3. Heat in oven at 160°C for 20 mins till meringue is crisp
  4. Leave to cool for 1 hour
