Blue cheese & mushroom sauce

Sauce

  1. Cook for 6 mins
    • olive oil
    • knob butter
    • 3 shallots
    • 250g mushrooms
  2. Add 2 cloves garlic and cook for 4 mins
  3. Remove heat
  4. Melt in 70g blue cheese
  5. Scatter chopped parsely

Serving

  • Pasta / gnocchi

Notes

  • Roquefort cheese used
  • Try adding white wine
  • Mushrooms produce a lot of liquid which needs to evaporate
