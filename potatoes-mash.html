Mashed potatoes

Portions

  • 250g after peeling per person

Cooking

  1. Cut potatoes into 1-2" pieces
  2. Rinse under cold water
  3. Cover with salted cold water to 1" above
  4. Bring water to boil then reduce to simmer
  5. Simmer for 20-25 mins until cake tester goes through with resistance
  6. Drain then return to saucepan
  7. Let steam to dry out without lid on

Finishing

  1. Mash in per portion
    • 7g butter melted
    • 1½ tbsp creme fraiche
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. If required add warmed milk until smooth

Serving

  • Honey mustard over mash
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • 1 tsp whole grain mustard

Notes

  • All chunks similar size so cook evenly, no big chunks
  • Allow steam to escape as retained water will make potatoes gummy
  • If mash is lumpy then potatoes were probably not cooked long enough
  • Take out potato and cut in half and taste to make sure is cooked enough to mash

Varieties

  • King Edwards - best
  • Maris Piper - v. good
  • Desiree - good
  • Marfona - not good, too waxy
