Mashed potatoes

Portions

  • 250g after peeling per person

Cooking

  1. 20-25 mins boiling in salted water (better longer time to ensure cooked)
  2. drain then return to saucepan and let steam to dry out
  3. stir in 7g melted butter per portion
  4. stir in 1½ tbsp creme fraiche per portion
  5. if required add warmed milk until smooth

Notes

  • all chunks similar size so cook evenly, no big chunks
  • if mash is lumpy then potatoes were probably not cooked long enough
  • take out potato and cut in half and taste to make sure is cooked enough to mash

Varieties

  • King Edwards - best
  • Maris Piper - v. good
  • Desiree - good
  • Marfona - not good, too waxy
