Mashed potatoes

Mash - cooking

  • 20-25 mins boiling
  • better longer time to ensure cooked
  • after draining return to saucepan and heat for 1-2 mins to dry out
  • mash in 20g melted butter
  • add 50g warmed milk

Mash - comments

  • R does not like
  • Take out potato and cut in half and taste to make sure is cooked enough to mash
  • If mash is lumpy then potatoes were probably not cooked long enough

Varieties

  • Marfona - not good for mash, too waxy
  • Desiree - good for mash
  • Maris Piper - v. good for mash
