Chicken balti

Cauliflower

  • Roast ½ cauliflower

Chicken

  1. Mix
    • 400g chicken cut into chunks
    • 2 tsp tikka spices
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Add and mix
    • 50g yogurt
  3. Marinade for 2 hours
  4. Fry or air fry until just cooked
  5. Cover and set aside

Curry

  1. Fry for 10 mins
    • 1 onion diced
    • 1 red pepper chopped
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 10g ginger grated
    • 1 tbsp curry powder
  3. Add and cook for 15 mins
    • 150g mushrooms chopped (optional)
    • 400ml passata (or 1 tin tomatoes)
    • 400ml water
    • 50g red lentils
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin chickpeas drained
    • cauliflower (roasted)
    • chicken (cooked)
  5. Add
    • 100g natural yogurt

Serving

  • Basmati rice
  • Naan
