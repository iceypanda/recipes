Chicken balti

Chicken

  1. Fry until just cooked
    • 400g chicken cut into chunks
  2. Cover and set aside

Curry

  1. Fry for 10 mins
    • 1 onion diced
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 10g ginger grated
    • 1 tbsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp coriander
  3. Add and cook for 15 mins
    • 150g mushrooms chopped
    • ½ cauliflower cut into florets
    • 300ml water
    • ½ chicken stock cube
    • 200ml passata
    • 50g red lentils
    • 2 tbsp balti paste
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin chickpeas drained
    • chicken
  5. Add
    • 100g natural yogurt

Serving

  • Basmati rice
  • Naan
