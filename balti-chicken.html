Chicken balti

Cauliflower

  • Roast
    • 1 cauliflower cut into chunks

Chicken

  1. Mix
    • 400g chicken cut into chunks
    • 2 tsp tikka spices
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Add and mix
    • 50g yogurt
  3. Marinade for 2 hours
  4. Air fry at 180°C for 9 mins till just cooked
  5. Cover with foil and leave to rest in juices

Curry

  1. Fry for 10 mins
    • 1 onion diced
    • 1 red pepper chopped
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 10g ginger grated
    • 2 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp mild chilli powder
  3. Add and cook for 15 mins
    • 400ml passata (or 1 tin tomatoes)
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 200g butternut squash
    • 50g red lentils
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin chickpeas drained
    • cauliflower (roasted)
    • chicken (cooked)
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • ½ tsp ground cardamom
  5. Add
    • juice ½ lemon
    • ½ tsp salt

Serving

  • Basmati rice
  • Naan
