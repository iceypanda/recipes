Chicken balti

Chicken

  1. Fry until just cooked
    • 400g chicken cut into chunks
  2. Cover and set aside

Cauliflower

  • Roast ½ cauliflower

Curry

  1. Fry for 10 mins
    • 1 onion diced
    • 1 red pepper chopped
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 10g ginger grated
    • 2 tbsp curry powder (2?)
  3. Add and cook for 15 mins
    • 150g mushrooms chopped (optional)
    • 400ml passata
    • 50g red lentils
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin chickpeas drained (optional)
    • cauliflower
    • chicken
  5. Add
    • 100g natural yogurt

Serving

  • Basmati rice
  • Naan
