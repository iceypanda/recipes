Tomato & mascarpone pasta sauce

Sauce - passata

  1. Fry till soft
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
  3. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • ½18.7cl bottle white wine
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 500g passata
    • ½ tsp dried basil
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  5. Add and simmer for 2 mins
    • 125g mascarpone
  6. Add
    • 25g Parmesan grated

Sauce - fresh tomatoes

  1. Fry till soft
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 3 cloves garlic minced
  3. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • ½18.7cl bottle red / white wine
  4. Add, simmer and reduce for 30 mins
    • 16 (1000g) medium tomatoes blitzed
    • 80g black olives halved (optional)
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • ¼ tsp sugar
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  5. Add and simmer for 2 mins
    • 125g mascarpone

Pasta

  1. Add sauce to pasta and mix

Serving

  • Italian chicken
  • 90g per person Spaghetti / Tagliatelle
  • Sprouting broccoli / salad
  • 4 portions
