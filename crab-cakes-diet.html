Crab cakes

  • Preheat oven to 180°C

Sweet potatoes

  1. Boil till soft
    • 2 handfuls sweet potato peeled & cubed
  2. Drain
  3. Mash
  4. Leave to cool

Cakes

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 2 palm-size amounts white crab meat
    • 1 spring onion chopped
    • 1 red chilli deseeded & chopped
    • zest 2 limes
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • 2 tsp fresh coriander chopped
  2. Mix in
    • sweet potato mashed
  3. Shape into 6 cakes
  4. Bake in oven at 180°C for 30 mins

Serving

  • Serve with
    • coleslaw
    • tzatziki
    • salad leaves
  • 2 portions
