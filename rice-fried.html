Fried rice

Rice

  1. Break up into individual grains
    • 300g jasmine rice cooked >12 hours before

Egg

  1. Heat
    • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  2. Add and fry till cooked
    • 2 large eggs lightly beaten
  3. Drain on paper towel

Fried rice

  1. Cook ingredients separately in wok and set aside
    • 100g small prawns
    • salmon cooked / hot smoked
    • carrots / peppers / corgette in 6mm dice
    • mushrooms chopped
    • cabbage thinly sliced
    • tofu marinated & cooked
    • sweetcorn
    • green beans blanched and chopped into thirds
    • mange tout halved
    • cucumber deseeded, thickly sliced, cut into quarters
  2. Stir fry and break up any clumps
    • vegetable oil
    • rice
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 spring onions sliced
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
  4. Add sauce and cook for 1 min
    • 1 tbsp nasi goreng
  5. Add everything to wok and break up egg
  6. Add
    • salt
    • pepper

Serving

  • 2 portions

Notes

  • Can use 1 tsp shoyu and 1 tsp sesame oil / fish sauce for sauce
  • Original recipe: The Wok, p274
  • Made: Aug 2023: sticky rice in clumps not nice, try basmati or extra rice flour next time
