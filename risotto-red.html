Red risotto

Sauce

  1. Cook for 30 mins, adding water if sticking
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 carrots chopped
    • 2 red onions chopped
    • 2 red peppers chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic
  2. Blitz until smooth with 2 tins of tomatoes
  3. Season with salt and brown sugar and taste until good
  4. Cool and keep in fridge overnight

Risotto

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C
  2. Bring to boil and simmer for 5 mins
    • sauce
    • 250g risotto rice
    • 1 tin cannellini beans with liquid
  3. Cook on bottom shelf of oven for 20 mins with lid off until rice is soft to eat
  4. Dot in 150g of mozzarella balls quartered
