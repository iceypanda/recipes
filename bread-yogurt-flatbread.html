Yogurt flatbread

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 300g self-raising flour
    • 300g natural yogurt
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • ½ tsp caster sugar
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Knead dough for minute to bring together
  3. Divide into 8 balls
  4. Flatten each ball with a rolling pin until 2-3mm thick
  5. Cook each flatbread on gridle pan on high heat for 2 mins on each side
  6. Brush each flatbread with melted butter when cooked

Notes

Make more or less by changing amount of flour and yogurt - 150g for 4 people

Can add fresh herbs to butter, e.g. parsley, tarragon, basil, dill

Added salt and sugar
