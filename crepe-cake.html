Crepe cake

Crepes

  1. Blitz
    • 330ml milk
    • 125g plain flour
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 tbsp icing sugar
    • 2 tbsp (16g) unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  2. Make 10-11 20cm crepes with 60ml batter and a of bit butter each

Cream filling

  1. Beat till smooth
    • 40g unsalted butter
  2. Mix in
    • 140g cream cheese
  3. Mix in
    • 100g white chocolate melted
  4. Mix in
    • 140ml double cream
  5. Whisk till soft peaks
  6. Chill at least 1 hour

Cake

  1. Layer crepes and filling till all crepes used
  2. Chill overnight
