Apple pudding

Apples

  1. Simmer for 15 mins
    • 2 apples thinly sliced
    • water hot
  2. Drain

Pudding

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 2 eggs
    • 3 tbsp (54g) cottage cheese
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • 3 drops vanilla extract
  2. Mix in
    • apples
    • 6 walnut halves chopped
  3. Bake in loaf tin at 160°C for 30 mins
  4. Leave to cool 10 mins
  5. Garnish
    • 2 tsp honey

Serving

  • 2 portions
