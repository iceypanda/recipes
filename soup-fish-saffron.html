Saffron fish soup

  1. Heat until soft
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 medium onion chopped
    • 1 leek sliced
    • 1 stick celery chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 187ml white wine
    • 550ml water
    • ½ veg stock cube
    • strip of orange peel
  3. Add and heat for 18 mins
    • 200g (after peeling) potatoes cubed
  4. Add and heat for 6-8 mins
    • 450g fish fillets cubed
    • ½ tsp saffron
  5. Remove orange peel
  6. Mix in
    • 150ml single cream
  7. Blitz portion

Serving

  • Bread

Notes

  • Fish: hake / monkfish / cod / haddock
  • Can swap 100g fish for prawns
  • Original recipe
