Saffron fish soup

  1. Heat until soft
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 medium onion
    • 1 leek sliced
    • 1 stick celery chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 250ml white wine
    • 500ml water
    • strip of orange peel
    • seasoning
  3. Add and heat for 15 mins
    • 200g potatoes cubed
  4. Add and heat for 8-10 mins
    • 600g fish fillets
    • ½ tsp saffron
  5. Cut fish into pieces
  6. Remove orange peel
  7. Add and heat for 1-2 mins
    • 150ml double cream

Serving

  • bread

Notes

  • fish: hake / monkfish / cod / haddock
  • can swap 200g fish for prawns
  • Original recipe
