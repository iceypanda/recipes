Moroccan veg soup

  1. Cook for 10 mins
    • oil
    • 1 onion
    • 3 sticks celery diced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • rind from 2 preserved lemons (flesh discarded)
    • 2 red chillis chopped
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  4. Brind to boil the simmer for 30 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 350g potatoes in chunks
    • 1l stock
  5. Add and cook for 1 min until spinach wilted
    • 80g spinach
    • parsely chopped
    • lemon juice

Serving

  • flatbreads / rice

Notes

  • use frozen spinach
  • Original recipe
