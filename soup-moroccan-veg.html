Moroccan veg soup

  1. Cook for 10 mins
    • oil
    • 1 onion
    • 3 sticks celery diced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • rind from 1½ preserved lemons (flesh discarded)
    • 2 red chillis chopped
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  4. Brind to boil the simmer for 30 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 350g (after peeling) potatoes in chunks
    • 1l stock
    • 30g red lentils
  5. Add and cook for 1 min until spinach wilted
    • 80g spinach (can use frozen)
    • parsely chopped
    • juice of ¼ lemon / lime
  6. Blitz ¼ of soup

Serving

  • flatbreads / rice
