Chicken supreme

  1. Fry for 8-10 mins
    • 1 small onion
    • 400g chicken breast chopped
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp butter
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 50g bacon chopped
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • clove garlic
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tbsp plain flour
  5. Add and simmer for 2 mins
    • 70ml white wine
  6. Add and simmer on low heat for 5 mins
    • 150ml double cream
    • 1 tsp Dijon mustard

Serving

  • greens
  • rice / potatoes

Notes

  • used ¼ tsp mustard but couldn't taste it
  • can pan-fry chicken instead
