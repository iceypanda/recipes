Chicken supreme

  1. Fry for 8-10 mins
    • 1 small onion
    • 400g chopped chicken breast
    • olive oil
    • butter
  2. Add 50g chopped bacon and cook for 5 mins
  3. Add 1 clove garlic and cook for 1 min
  4. Add 1 tbsp plain flour and cook for 2 mins
  5. Add 70ml white wine, bring to boil and simmer for 2 mins
  6. Add and simmer on low heat for 5 mins
    • 150ml double cream
    • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
