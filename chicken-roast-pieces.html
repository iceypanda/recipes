Roast chicken pieces

Cooking

  1. 400g chicken breast cut into similar sized pieces
  2. Marinate
  3. Roast in air fryer at 180°C for 8-10 mins till 70°C

Honey, mustard & garlic

  • Coat and cook
    • 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
    • 1½ tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • 1 clove garlic minced

Honey & ginger

  • Marinate and cook
    • 20g honey
    • 10g ginger
    • 7g soy sauce
    • 7g sake / shaoxing wine

Spanish style

  • Marinate and cook
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tsp sherry vinegar (or rice / red wine / white wine vinegar)
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 tsp thyme
    • 1 tsp sweet smoked paprika
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp ground coriander

Tikka

  • Marinate and cook
    • 75g greek yogurt
    • 25g ginger
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1 tsp mild chilli powder
    • ½ tsp salt

Tikka - ready mixed

Honey & mustard

Italian

  • Coat and cook
    • 4 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp lemon juice
    • 30g parmesan grated (new)
    • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary chopped
    • zest ½ lemon grated
