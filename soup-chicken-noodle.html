Chicken noodle soup

  1. Add to pot
    • 900ml chicken or vegetable stock
    • 1 chicken breast
    • 1 tsp chopped root ginger
    • 1 clove garlic
  2. Bring to boil then simmer for 20 mins
  3. Remove chicken and shread
  4. Stir in and simmer for 4 mins
    • shredded chicken
    • 50g noodles
    • 2 tsp sweet corn
    • 3 thinly sliced mushrooms
    • 1 chopped spring onion
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • chopped red chilli (optional)
  5. Serve with basil leaves

Notes

Try double amount of noodles
