Keema Madras

  1. Cook for 14 mins (do not drain)
    • 75g red lentils
    • 160g small cubes of potatoes
    • 480ml water
  2. Toast for 2-3 mins
    • cooking oil
    • ½ tsp ground pepper
    • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
    • ½ tsp ground cumin
    • ½ tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp poppy seeds
    • ½ tsp sage
    • 1 tsp fennel seeds
    • 1 tsp rice
  3. Add
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 onion
  4. Blend together
    • 2 medium tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp desiccated coconut
    • 2 tbsp water
  5. When onions are cooked add tomato and coconut mixture and cook for 6 mins
  6. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 40g walnuts
    • 40g grated carrots
  7. Add potatoes and lentils and ½ cup of cooking water and cook for 5 mins
  8. Garnish with
    • lime juice
    • pepper flakes or cayenne
    • chopped fresh coriander
