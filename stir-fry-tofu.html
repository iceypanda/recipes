Tofu stir fry

  1. Toast 3 tbsp cashew nuts in pan
  2. Toast 2 tbsp sesame seeds in pan
  3. Cut 175g firm tofu into cubes and dust with cornflower and pepper
  4. Fry tofu in vegetable oil until crisp and golden
  5. Set tofu aside and drizzle with honey and the nuts
  6. Stir fry for 30 secs:
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 5 cm piece chopped ginger
    • 4 spring onions
    • 2 red chillies
  7. Add for 4 mins:
    • ½ head of broccoli
    • ½ red pepper
    • 4 baby sweetcorn
    • 80g sweetcorn
  8. Add:
    • juice of ½ lime
    • splash of soy sauce
