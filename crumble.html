Crumble

  • Make topping first
  • Crumble topping
  • Crumble filling

Amounts

  • Weight ratio fruit 1.7 : 1.0 topping
  • 22x22cm (2.4L) dish = 10 portions
  • 22x17cm (1.5L) pan = 4 portions

Crumble

  1. Put fruit into oven dish / ramekins
  2. Flatten
  3. Cover with crumble mix but don't press down
  4. Top with
    • 10g hazelnuts (optional)
  5. Cook at 180°C for 30 mins or till topping golden
