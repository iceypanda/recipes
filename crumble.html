Crumble

Crumble

  • Ratios
    • 4 portions = 450g fruit : 150g crumble
  • Fruit cooked first to remove excess liquid

Topping amounts

  • 22x22cm pan = 6 portions
  • Ratios (total 150g = 4 portions = 35g per portion)
    • 55g plain flour
    • 35g unsalted butter cubed
    • 35g light brown sugar
    • 25g oats
  • Ratios (1 portion)
    • 14g plain flour
    • 9g unsalted butter cubed
    • 9g light brown sugar
    • 6g oats

Topping

  1. Mix in bowl
    • plain flour
    • light brown sugar
    • unsalted butter cubed
  2. Blitz in portions until breadcrumbs
  3. Mix in
    • oats
    • 1 tsp ginger (optional)
    • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)

Apples & blackberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Melt then cook on medium heat for 3 mins
    • 30g unsalted butter
    • 30g dark brown sugar
  2. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 300g (after peeling, 3) Braeburn apples peeled & cored, 2cm dice
  3. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 150g blackberries (apple peeled 2 : 1 blackberry ratio)
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  4. Remove heat, cover and leave for 3 mins
  5. Drain excess liquid

Rhubarb

  1. Simmer for 10 mins
    • 5 sticks rhubarb
    • 3 tbsp light brown sugar
    • 2 tbsp water
  2. Drain excess liquid

Cherries & blackberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 250g cherries whole, pitted
    • 150g blackberries
    • 20g light brown sugar
  2. Drain excess liquid

Cherries & strawberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 400g strawberries hulled & halved
    • 200g cherries whole, pitted
    • 20g light brown sugar
  2. Drain excess liquid

Crumble

  1. Put fruit into oven dish / ramekins
  2. Flatten
  3. Cover with crumble mix
  4. Top with
    • hazelnuts (optional)
  5. Cook at 180°C for 30 mins or until topping golden
