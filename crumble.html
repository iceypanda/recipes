  • See Crumble filling

Crumble

  1. Cook fruit first to remove excess liquid if required
  2. Put fruit into oven dish / ramekins
  3. Flatten
  4. Cover with crumble mix
  5. Top with
    • hazelnuts (optional)
  6. Cook at 180°C for 30 mins or till topping golden

Topping

  1. Mix in bowl
    • plain flour
    • light brown sugar
    • unsalted butter cubed
  2. Blitz in portions till breadcrumbs
  3. Mix in
    • oats
    • 1 tsp ginger (optional)
    • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)

Topping amounts

  • Ratio fruit 1.7 : 1.0 topping
  • 22x22cm pan = 10 portions: 380g topping & 650g fruit
  • Blitz without oats
    • Try
    • Melt butter and mix
    • More oats
  • Ratios (10 portions = 380g)
    • 140g plain flour
    • 90g unsalted butter cubed
    • 90g light brown sugar
    • 60g oats
  • Ratios (4 portions = 150g)
    • 55g plain flour
    • 35g unsalted butter cubed
    • 35g light brown sugar
    • 25g oats
  • Ratios (1 portion = 38g)
    • 14g plain flour
    • 9g unsalted butter cubed
    • 9g light brown sugar
    • 6g oats
