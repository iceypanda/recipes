Crumble

Crumble

  1. Cook fruit first to remove excess liquid if required
  2. Put fruit into oven dish / ramekins
  3. Flatten
  4. Cover with crumble mix
  5. Top with
    • hazelnuts (optional)
  6. Cook at 180°C for 30 mins or until topping golden

Topping

  1. Mix in bowl
    • plain flour
    • light brown sugar
    • unsalted butter cubed
  2. Blitz in portions until breadcrumbs
  3. Mix in
    • oats
    • 1 tsp ginger (optional)
    • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)

Topping amounts

  • Ratio fruit 1.7 : 1.0 topping
  • 22x22cm pan = 10 portions: 380g topping & 650g fruit
  • Blitz without oats
    • Try
    • Melt butter and mix
    • More oats
  • Ratios (10 portions = 380g)
    • 140g plain flour
    • 90g unsalted butter cubed
    • 90g light brown sugar
    • 60g oats
  • Ratios (4 portions = 150g)
    • 55g plain flour
    • 35g unsalted butter cubed
    • 35g light brown sugar
    • 25g oats
  • Ratios (1 portion = 38g)
    • 14g plain flour
    • 9g unsalted butter cubed
    • 9g light brown sugar
    • 6g oats

Filling

Peach & lime

  1. Place in pan
    • 2 tins peaches
    • ½ peach syrup in tin
  2. Drizzle
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • juice of lime
  3. Top crumble with
    • zest of lime

Apples & blackberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Melt then cook on medium heat for 3 mins
    • 30g unsalted butter
    • 30g dark brown sugar
  2. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 300g (after peeling, 3) Braeburn apples peeled & cored, 2cm dice
  3. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 150g blackberries (apple peeled 2 : 1 blackberry ratio)
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  4. Remove heat, cover and leave for 3 mins
  5. Drain excess liquid

Rhubarb

  1. Simmer for 10 mins
    • 5 sticks rhubarb
    • 3 tbsp light brown sugar
    • 2 tbsp water
  2. Drain excess liquid

Cherries & blackberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 250g cherries whole, pitted
    • 150g blackberries
    • 20g light brown sugar
  2. Drain excess liquid

Cherries & strawberries

  • 4 portions
  1. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 400g strawberries hulled & halved
    • 200g cherries whole, pitted
    • 20g light brown sugar
  2. Drain excess liquid
