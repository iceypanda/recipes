Parmentier potatoes

Prep

  1. Cut potatoes into cubes
  2. Pat cubes dry with paper towel
  3. Toss in oil and herbs and seasoning

Oven

  1. Roast in oven at 180°C for 30 mins

Air fryer

  1. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 22 mins (try 24 mins to see if crispier)

Notes

  • optional coatings: garlic powder, salt
  • no point adding cracked black pepper until cooked as cooking with remove heat
    • crispy
