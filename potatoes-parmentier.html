Parmentier potatoes

Prep

  1. Cut potatoes into cubes
  2. Pat cubes dry with paper towel
  3. Toss in herbs / spices
  4. Coat in olive oil
  5. Add salt

Oven

  1. Roast in oven at 180°C for 30 mins

Air fryer

  1. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 18-22 mins

Notes

  • Careful don't overcook the potatoes
  • Don't use garlic powder, bad aftertaste
  • No point adding cracked black pepper until cooked as cooking with remove heat
