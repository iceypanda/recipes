Parmentier potatoes

Oven

  1. Cut potatoes into cubes
  2. Fry in pan for 10 mins with sunflower oil
  3. Mix potato cubes with chopped parsely and rosemary
  4. Roast in oven at 180°C for 30 mins

Air fryer

  1. Cut potatoes into cubes
  2. Toss in
    • sunflower oil
    • chopped parsely and rosemary
  3. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 20 mins

Notes

  • nice at 190°C for 20 mins in air fryer on roast, try slightly longer or hotter or get more brown and crispy
