Salad

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 1 avocado chopped
    • 8 cherry tomatoes quartered
    • ½ carrot grated
    • ½ cucumber deseeded & chopped
    • ½ yellow pepper chopped
  2. Mix in
    • salmon
    • 60g bulgar cooked
    • 40g pinenuts / sunflower seeds roasted
    • squeeze of lemon or dressing
    • black pepper
  3. Garnish with
    • coriander chopped
