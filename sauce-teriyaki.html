Teriyaki sauce

Sauce

  1. Mix in small saucepan
    • 80g cold water
    • 1 tbsp cornflour
  2. Mix in
    • 100g shoyu
    • 50g dark brown sugar
    • 45g mirin
    • 15g honey
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 15g ginger chopped
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
  3. Heat for 5 mins while whisking

Serving

  • Stir fry & noodles
  • Meat, rice & greens
  • Better with rice than noodles

Notes

  • Too salty so diluted from ½ cup shoyu and ¼ cup water, sugar reduced from ¼ cup
