Teriyaki sauce

Sauce

  1. Mix
    • ½ cup soy sauce
    • ¼ cup brown sugar
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 15g ginger chopped
    • 3 tbsp mirin
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
    • 1 tbsp cornflour mixed into ¼ cup cold water
  2. Heat for 5 mins

Notes

Serve with duck / over rice with greens
