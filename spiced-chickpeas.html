Spiced chick peas

  1. Heat until soft:
    • 1 medium onion
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • 2 tsp cumin seeds
    • 2 tsp ground coriander
    • 2 tsp ground paprika
    • 1 tsp tumeric
    • ¼ tsp tomato paste
  2. Bring to boil and simmer for 30 mins:
    • 1 can of chick peas
    • 1 tbsp tomato paste
    • ⅔ cup of water
