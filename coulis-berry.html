Berry coulis

Strawberry & raspberry

  1. Blitz till smooth
    • 100g strawberries hulled and halved
    • 100g raspberries
    • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  2. Sieve to remove seeds

Raspberry

  1. Warm in pan till raspberries break down
    • 1 tsp water in pan first
    • 250g raspberries
    • 1 tsp golden caster sugar
  2. Sieve to remove seeds

Notes

  • Can try lemon juice or orange juice instead of water
  • Sweetness of coulis should depend on sweetness of dessert it is served with
  • Freezes well
