Berry coulis

Strawberry &raspberry

  1. Blitz until smooth
    • 100g strawberries hulled and halved
    • 100g raspberries
    • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  2. Sieve to collect puree

Raspberry

  1. Warm in pan until raspberries break down
    • 250g raspberries
    • 20g golden caster sugar
    • 1 tbsp water
  2. Blitz with hand blender
  3. Sieve to remove seeds

Notes

  • Can try lemon juice ir orange juice instead of water
  • Sweetness of coulis should depend on sweetness of dessert it is served with
  • Freezes well
