Hoisin & soy sauce

  1. Mix
    • 60g shoyu
    • 2 tsp cornflour
  2. Add
    • 60g hoisin sauce
    • 60g spring onions chopped
    • 1 clove garlic minced
    • 5g ginger minced
    • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
    • 2 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp chilli flakes
    • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  3. Add to stir fry and allow to thicken
