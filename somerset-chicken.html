Somerset chicken

Chicken

  1. Season 4 chicken breasts with salt and freshly ground black pepper
  2. Fry chicken breasts for 2 mins until golden all over in:
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 20g butter
  3. Cook chicken for 25 mins in high-sided roasting tin in oven preheated to 200C

Sauce

  1. Fry until soft:
    • 1 onion
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 10g butter
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins:
    • 2 tbsp plain flour
    • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  3. Add and cook for 1 min:
    • 50g mushrooms
    • 1 dessert apple peeled, cored, sliced into batons
  4. Add 125ml chicken stock and bring to boil
  5. Add 150ml cider and bring to boil and cook for 2 mins
  6. Lower heat and add then simmer for 6 mins:
    • ½ tbsp chopped fresh sage
    • 125ml double cream

Assemble

  1. Pour sauce over chicken, cover with grated cheddar and grill for 5 mins until cheese bubbling
  2. Serve with potatoes
