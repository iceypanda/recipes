Monkfish vindaloo

  1. Mix
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic grated
    • 1 tsp ginger grated
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • ¼ tsp chilli powder
  2. Coat
    • 2 portions monkfish
    • ½ red onion big chunks
    • 6 baby tomatoes
  3. Bake at 160°C for 10-15 mins
  4. Serve with
    • salad leaves
    • raita
    • lemon wedges

Serving

  • 2 portions
