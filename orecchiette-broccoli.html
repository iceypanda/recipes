Orecchiette & broccoli

Broccoli

  1. Cook 3 mins
    • florets of 2 heads of broccoli

Pasta

  1. Cook 10 mins
    • 250g orecchiette
    • salt
  2. Retain some cooking water

Sauce

  1. Cover and cook for 8 mins
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • stalks of 2 heads of broccoli finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 6 anchovy fillets chopped
    • zest of ¼ lemon
    • 1 knob butter
  2. Mix in
    • pasta
    • broccoli florets
    • pasta water
  3. Mix in
    • parmesan grated
    • aleppo pepper
    • 1 knob butter
    • lemon juice
    • black pepper

Serving

  • xxx
  • 3 portions
