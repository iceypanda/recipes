Patatas bravas

Tomatoes

  1. Add to slow cooker and set to low
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • ½ tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp thyme
    • ½ tsp sweet smoked paprika
    • ¼ stock cube dissolved in hot water

Sauce

  1. Fry for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 1 carrot diced
    • 2 chillies chopped
  3. Add to slow cooker with tomatoes
  4. Cook for 4 hours
  5. Blitz quickly so not too smooth
  6. Add to taste
    • 2 tsp red wine vinegar
    • salt
    • black pepper

Serving

  • Serve over Parmentier potatoes
  • 3 portions
