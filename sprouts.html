Brussels sprouts

Roasted sprouts

  • Sprouts need breathing space to crisp up

Prep

  1. Cut off bottom of each sprout
  2. Remove outer leaves
  3. Cut each sprout in half

Roasting

  1. Mix sprouts with
    • olive oil
    • salt
  2. Arrange on baking try
  3. Roast in oven at 200°C for 20-25 mins
  4. Halfway sprinkle with
    • parmesan (optional)
  5. Done when offer little resistance to fork and browned
