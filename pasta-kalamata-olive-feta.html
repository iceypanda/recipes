Kalamata olive tuna, & feta pasta

  1. Fry until soft
    • olive oil
    • 2 medium onions
    • 3 cloves garlic
  2. Add and simmer for 20 mins
    • 2 tins of tomatoes
    • 2 corgettes chopped into semi-circles
    • 8 mushrooms chopped
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tbsp basil
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • 6 bay leaves
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 1 jar of kalamata olives, halved and destoned
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 1 large tin tuna
  5. Add
    • 1 pack of feta, chopped into cubes

Slow cooker

  1. Add corgettes, mushrooms, tomatoes and herbs to cooker then turn on
  2. Chop onions and fry
  3. Chop garlic and add to onions
  4. Chop olives and add back to jar
  5. Add onions to cooker then wait 2 hours
  6. Add olives, stir and wait 1 hour
  7. Add tuna, stir and wait 1 hour
  8. Turn off cooker
  9. Add feta and stir
