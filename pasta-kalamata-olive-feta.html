Kalamata olive, tuna, & feta pasta

On hob

  1. Fry till soft
    • olive oil
    • 2 medium onions
    • 3 cloves garlic
  2. Add and simmer for 20 mins
    • 2 tins of tomatoes
    • 2 courgettes chopped into semi-circles
    • 8 mushrooms chopped
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tbsp basil
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • 6 bay leaves
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 1 jar kalamata olives, halved and destoned
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 1 large tin tuna
  5. Add
    • 1 pack feta, chopped into cubes

Slow cooker

  1. Add courgettes, mushrooms, tomatoes and herbs to cooker then turn on
  2. Chop onions and fry
  3. Chop garlic and add to onions
  4. Chop olives and add back to jar
  5. Add onions to cooker then wait 2 hours
  6. Add olives, stir and wait 30 mins
  7. Add tuna, stir and wait 30 mins
  8. Turn off cooker
  9. Add feta and stir
