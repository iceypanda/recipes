Kalamata olive, tuna, & feta pasta

Noteser

  • Chop olives and add back to jar

On hob

  1. Fry till soft
    • olive oil
    • 2 medium onions
    • 3 cloves garlic
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 2 tins of tomatoes
    • 8 mushrooms chopped
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tbsp basil
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • 6 bay leaves
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 2 courgettes chopped into semi-circles
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 1 jar kalamata olives, halved and destoned
    • ½ tsp fish sauce
  5. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 1 large tin tuna
  6. Add
    • 1 pack feta, chopped into cubes

Slow cooker

  1. Fry onions and garlic
  2. Add tomatoes and mushrooms and heat
  3. 0:00 Starting cooking onions, tomatoes & mushrooms
  4. 1:30 Add corgettes & herbs
  5. 2:30 Add olives
  6. 3:00 Add tuna
  7. 3:30 Add feta
