Tomato & haricot bean stew

  1. Fry for 8 mins
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 sprigs rosemary
  2. Fry for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
  3. Add and cook covered for 20 mins
    • 300g cherry tomatoes
    • 150g kale stalks removed & leaves shredded
    • 2 tins haricot beans drained
  4. Mix in
    • juice of ½ lemon
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • black pepper
  5. Serve with
    • couscous
    • pesto
