Haricot bean & tomato stew

  1. Fry for 8 mins
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 sprigs rosemary
  2. Fry for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
  3. Add and cook covered for 20 mins
    • 400g cherry tomatoes
    • 150g kale stalks removed & leaves shredded
    • 2 tins haricot beans drained
    • ¼ stock cube in 100ml water
    • ¼ tsp fish sauce
  4. Mix in
    • juice ½ lemon
    • ⅛ tsp salt
    • black pepper

Serving

  • couscous
  • basil pesto
  • pickled radishes
  • 4 portions
