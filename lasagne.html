Beef

    • 500g minced beef

Tomato sauce

  1. Cook for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 1 carrot diced
    • 4 mushrooms chopped
    • 1 stalk celery diced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
  3. Add and cook for 5 mins to reduce
    • 187ml red wine
  4. Add and simmer for 15 mins reducing if necessary
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 100ml of salted beef stock (½ cube)
    • mince
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
    • ½ tsp herbs (optional)

White sauce

  1. Make Bechamel sauce with
    • 50g unsalted butter
    • 50g plain flour
    • 550ml milk

Lasagne

  1. Repeat layers x 3 in roasting tray
    1. ⅓ tomato sauce
    2. lasagne sheets
    3. ⅓ white sauce
  2. Scatter over top
    • 150g Cheddar grated
    • 50g Parmasan grated
  3. Bake in oven at 180°C for 45 mins
