Spinach & chickpeas

Paste

  1. Heat in pan until bread crispy on all sides
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 slices bread crusts removed & cubed
    • 15g hazelnuts / cashews / pistachios
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic chopped
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  3. Add
    • 2 tbsp sherry vinegar
  4. Blend until paste adding water if needed

Stew

  1. Heat in pan for 5 mins
    • 2 tins chickpeas drained
    • 60ml tomato sauce
    • bread paste
    • 1 tsp paprika
  2. Add and heat until wilted
    • 250g spinach
  3. Season to taste
    • salt
    • black pepper

Serving

  • Garnish with olive oil
  • 4 portions
