Baked potatoes & peppers

  1. Heat for 1 hour in roasting tin in oven preheated to 200C
    • 500g waxy potatoes in 1 inch chunks
    • 1 red pepper in 1 inch pieces
    • ½ tbsp coriander seeds
    • 4 tbsp olive oil

Notes

If peppers aren't cooked then try pre-cooked ones preserved in oil as Nigella recommended
