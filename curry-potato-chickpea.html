Potato and chickpea curry

Potatoes

  1. Cut 600g potatoes into bite size chunks and steam for 15 mins

Curry

  1. Fry on high heat until seeds start to crackle
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  2. Fry on medium heat for 5 mins
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 2 large onions
    • 1 large knob ginger
    • 2 green chillies
  3. Add and heat for 10 mins until onion is soft
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 2 tsp ground coriander
    • 2 tsp garam masala
  4. Add and simmer for 20 mins (need hot enough to cook potatoes)
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • potatoes
    • ½ tin of water
    • salt and pepper
  5. Add chopped fresh coriander

Notes

Serve with rice, breads, yogurt and mango chutney
