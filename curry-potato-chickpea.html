Potato and chickpea curry

  1. Cut 6 large potatoes into bite size chunks and steam for 10 mins
  2. Fry on high heat until seeds start to crackle
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  3. Fry on medium heat for 5 mins
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 2 large onions
    • 1 large knob ginger
    • 1 green chilli
  4. Add and heat for 10 mins until onion is soft
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 2 tsp ground coriander
    • 2 tsp garam masala
  5. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • salt and pepper
  6. Add potatoes and half-a-tin of water cook for 10 mins until potatoes tender
  7. Add chopped fresh coriander

Comments

Serve with breads, yogurt and mango chutney
