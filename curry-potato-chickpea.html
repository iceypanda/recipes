Potato & chickpea curry

  1. Fry on high heat until seeds start to crackle
    • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  2. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 2 large onions
    • 1 large knob ginger
    • 2 green chillies
  3. Add and heat for 5 mins until onion is soft
    • 2 tsp ground coriander
    • 2 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  4. Add and simmer for 35-40 mins until potatoes cooked (needs to be hot enough to cook potatoes)
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 600g potatoes cut into bite size chunks
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • ½ tin water & ¼ stock cube
    • pinch chilli flakes
    • ground black pepper
  5. Add 5 mins before end
    • 100g spinach stalks removed
  6. Add to taste
    • lemon juice
    • salt
    • fresh coriander chopped (optional)

Serving

  • rice, bread, mango chutney
  • 4 portions
