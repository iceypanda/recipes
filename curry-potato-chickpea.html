Potato & chickpea curry

  1. Fry on high heat until seeds start to crackle
    • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  2. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 2 large onions
    • 1 large knob ginger
    • 2 green chillies
  3. Add and heat for 5 mins until onion is soft
    • 2 tsp ground coriander
    • 2 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  4. Add and simmer for 30 mins until potatoes cooked (need hot enough to cook potatoes)
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 600g potatoes cut into bite size chunks
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • ½ tin of water
    • salt and pepper
  5. Add 100g spinach 5 mins before end (optional)
  6. Add chopped fresh coriander (optional)

Serve with

  • rice, bread, mango chutney
