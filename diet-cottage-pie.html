Cottage pie

Potato

  1. Boil until soft
    • 500g (after peeling) sweet potato
  2. Drain
  3. Add
    • 1 tsp white pepper
    • fresh dill (if curried)
  4. Mash

Filling

  1. Cook till soft
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • 1 pepper chopped
    • 1 red onion chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
  2. Add
    • 4 palm-size amounts (200g) soya mince
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  3. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 1 tsp basil
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • 1 tsp allspice
    • ¼ tsp dill
    • 1 bay leaf
    or
    • 1 tsp Jamaican curry powder
    • 1 tsp medium curry powder
    • 1 tsp garam masala
  4. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes

Pie

  1. Flatten filling in square 2.4L Pyrex dish
  2. Spread over mash and level with fork
  3. Roast in oven at 190°C for 25 mins

Serving

  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Mash will not brown as too wet
