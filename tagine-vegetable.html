Vegetable tagine

Spices

  • Spices 1
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 1½ tsp ground ginger
    • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Spices 2
    • 1 tsp ras el hanout
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • Spices 3
    • 1 tsp ras el hanout
    • ½ tsp ground cumin
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • ½ tsp ground cinnamon

Vegetable tagine

  1. Heat until soft in big pot
    • 2 large onions
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 4 cloves of garlic
    • pinch of saffron threads
    • spices
  3. Add and simmer on low heat for 25 mins
    • 600g sweet potatoes cut into large cubes
    • 400g corgettes / aubergine / squash / cauliflower / mushrooms / peppers
    • 500ml vegetable stock
    • 250g apricots & raisins halved
    • skin of 1 preserved lemon finely chopped
  4. Add 1 tin chick peas drained and cook for 10 mins
  5. Add
    • 50g green olives halved
    • 75g roasted cashew nuts chopped
    • 1 tbsp harissa
    • small bunch coriander or parsely

Notes

  • Try slow cook
  • Use 1kg veg, try different combinations
  • Hazelnuts or cashews
  • Serve with couscous

Changes to original recipe

  • Added harissa
  • Increased nuts from 50g
  • Replaced turnips
  • Too sweet, removed honey and replaced prunes, reduced fruit from 300g to 250g
