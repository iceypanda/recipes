Vegetable tagine

Sweet potatoes

  1. Roast at 160°C for 10 mins
    • 500g (after peeling) sweet potatoes cubed

Tagine

  1. Heat until soft in 5L stock pot
    • olive oil
    • 2 large onions
    • 1 pepper
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 2 tbspras el hanout
  3. Add and simmer on low heat for 30 mins
    • sweet potatoes
    • corgette / aubergine / squash / cauliflower
    • 500ml vegetable stock (with salt)
    • 200ml passata
    • 200g apricots cut into 3s with scissors
    • 50g raisins
    • skin of 1 preserved lemon finely chopped
    • 1 tsp Alleppo pepper
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin artichokes quartered
    • 1 tin chickpeas drained
    • 100-150g black olives rinsed & halved
  5. Add
    • 2 tbsp harissa
    • juice of ½ lemon

Serving

  • Couscous / buckwheat
  • Roasted cashew nuts / hazelnuts
  • Parsely chopped
  • 7 portions

Notes

  • Use 1kg veg, try different combinations
  • Slow cook 4 hours: 3 hours then add chickpeas and artichokes, use stock to rinse pan after onions
