Vegetable tagine

  1. Heat until soft in big pot
    • 2 large onions
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 4 cloves of garlic
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 1½ tsp ground ginger
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • ¼ tsp saffron threads
  3. Add and simmer on low heat for 25 mins
    • 600g sweet potatoes cut into large cubes
    • 400g corgettes / aubergine / squash
    • 500ml vegetable stock
    • 250g apricots / raisins
    • finely chopped skin from 1 preserved lemon
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin chick peas
  5. Add 50g green olives
  6. Sprinkle over
    • small bunch coriander
    • 50g roasted chopped cashew nuts

Comments

  • Replaced turnips
  • Hazelnuts or cashews
  • Serve with lots of harissa
  • Too sweet, removed honey and replaced prunes, reduced fruit from 300g to 250g
  • Try with 1 potato
