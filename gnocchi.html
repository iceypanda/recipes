Gnocchi

Potatoes

  1. Boil 500g potatoes with skins on
  2. Drain and save water for cooking gnocchi
  3. Leave to cool then remove skins

Gnocchi

  1. Mash potatoes
  2. Make well in mash to beat egg and add
    • 1 egg
    • ½ tsp salt
  3. Mix in 170g flour to form soft dough that does not stick to your fingers
  4. Roll dough into ropes and cut into 2cm pieces
  5. Arrange on baking trays, sprinkling with flour so don't stick together

Cooking

  1. Cook in pan of salted, boiling water
  2. Gnocchi are cooked when they have floated for 1-2 mins

Serving

  • Tomato sauce
  • Blue cheese & mushroom sauce

Notes

  • Prepare ahead of time before needed for cooking
  • Try thinner ropes
  • Can freeze before cooking
