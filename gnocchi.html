Gnocchi

  1. Boil 500g potatoes with skins on
  2. Drain and save water for cooking gnocchi
  3. Leave to cool then remove skins and push through potato ricer or mash
  4. Make well in mash to beat egg and add
    • 1 egg
    • ½ tsp salt
  5. Mix in 150g flour to form soft dough that does not stick to your fingers
  6. Roll dough into ropes and cut into 2cm pieces
  7. Sprinkle gnocchi with flour so don't stick together
  8. Cook in pan of salted, boiling water, gnocchi are cooked when they float

Serve with

  • Tomato sauce
  • Blue cheese & mushroom sauce

Notes

  • Alternative recipe says gnocchi are cooked after floating for 2-3 mins
  • Try thinner ropes
  • Can freeze before cooking
