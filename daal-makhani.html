Kidney bean & brown lentil daal

  1. Fry for 10 mins
    • oil
    • 10g butter
    • 1 large onion
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 20g ginger
    • 2 tbsp curry powder
    • ½ tsp chilli powder
  2. Add, bring to the boil then reduce to simmer for 30 mins
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 1 tin kidney beans
    • Cooked brown lentils (100g before cooking)
    • Lots of black pepper
  3. Add spinach 5 mins before end of cooking (optional)
  4. Leave to completely cool before serving

Notes

  • Butter reduced from 75g
