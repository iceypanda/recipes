White chocolate mousse

  1. Put in heat proof bowl over simmering water, making sure bowl does not touch water
    • 250g chopped white chocolate
    • 80ml milk
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  2. Heat until chocolate just melted, stirring regularly
  3. Cool for 5 mins
  4. Add 3 egg yolks one by one and beat well after each addition
  5. Whip 300ml double cream into soft peaks and fold into chocolate mixture until just combined
  6. Whisk 3 egg whites until peaks form
  7. Fold egg whites into chocolate mixture in two batches with large spoon
  8. Divide mousse into 6 or 8 pots leaving space to cover
  9. Swirl puree from 250g raspberries over mousses
  10. Cover and chill for 4 hours until set
