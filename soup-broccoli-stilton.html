Broccoli & stilton soup

  1. Fry gently for 8 mins (until soft)
    • 1 tbsp butter
    • 2 shallots
  2. Add chopped stalks of 600g broccoli for 1 min
  3. Add and bring to simmer (don't boil) for 12 mins (when stalk almost tender)
    • 600ml stock
    • 450ml milk
  4. Add and simmer for 8 mins
    • broccoli florets
    • 150g stilton
  5. Blend soup
  6. Season with ground nutmeg and black pepper to taste
