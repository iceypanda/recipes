South West Stew

  1. Heat in a pan for 7 mins
    • 1 tbsp water
    • 1 onion
    • 1 bell pepper
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 6 mushrooms
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 1 tbsp chilli powder
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • 2 tsp cumin
  3. Add, bring to boil then simmer for 10 mins
    • 1100ml water (or stock)
    • 350g potatoes in chunks
    • 1 tin black beans
    • 1 tin pinto beans
    • 1 tin sweetcorn
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • ⅓ cup of tomato paste
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 4 cups of greens
  5. Add chopped coriander
