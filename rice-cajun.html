Cajun rice

  1. Fry for 3 mins
    • 25ml (23g) coconut oil
    • 1 medium onion finely diced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 10g garlic minced
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • ½ yellow pepper diced
    • ½ chilli chopped
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 100g veg mince
  5. Add and stir to coat
    • 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • ½ tsp dried parsely
    • ½ tsp thyme
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
    • 1 bay leaf
  6. Add and simmer for 8 mins
    • 60ml veg stock
    • 15ml soy sauce
  7. Remove bay leaves
  8. Mix in
    • 300g basmati rice cooked & warm
  9. Stir till heated through

Serving

  • 2 portions
  • Piri piri chicken
