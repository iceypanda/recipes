Cobbler

  • See Crumble filling

Milk

  1. Whisk together then set aside
    • 100ml (103g) milk
    • 1 egg

Cobbler

  1. Blitz until breadcrumbs
    • 280g plain flour
    • 140g unsalted butter cold & cubed
  2. Mix in
    • 140g golden caster sugar
    • 1 tbsp baking powder
    • ½ tsp cinnamon
    • pinch salt
  3. Mix in to form batter
    • milk mixture
  4. Spoon dough over fruit in large dollops leaving spaces to expand
  5. Sprinkle over
    • 2 tbsp demerara sugar
  6. Bake at 170°C for 35-40 mins
