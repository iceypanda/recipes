Cherry chocolate cake

  • Preheat oven to 160°C
  1. Mix in bowl until smooth
    • 175g caster sugar
    • 3 large eggs
  2. Mix in
    • 200g plain flour
    • 150g unsalted butter melted
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Fold in
    • 250g cherries frozen, pitted, halved
    • 100g dark chocolate in chunks
  4. Mix in
    • 37½ml (40g) milk
  5. Pour into 23x23cm high-sided tin lined with baking paper
  6. Bake at 160°C for 45 mins until cake tester comes out clean
  7. Allow to cool in tin

Serving

  • 9 portions
