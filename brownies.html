Brownies

  1. Melt together then leave to cool
    • 350g dark chocolate
    • 250g unsalted butter
  2. Whisk
    • 3 large eggs
  3. Whisk in until well combined
    • 250g dark muscavado sugar
  4. Fold in melted chocolate mixture
  5. Sift in
    • 85g plain flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Pour into lined 23cm x 23cm baking tin
  7. Bake at 160°C for 30-35 mins
  8. Cool in tin for 1 hour

Serving

  • Calories = 5400, 1/16 = 340
