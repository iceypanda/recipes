Brownies

Chocolate

  1. Melt together in microwave on low stirring every 30 secs
    • 285g chocolate
    • 185g unsalted butter cubed
  2. Leave to cool

Brownies

  1. Whisk together
    • 3 large eggs
  2. Whisk well with electric whisker until creamy
    • 275g golden caster sugar
  3. Gently fold in melted chocolate mixture
  4. Sift in with medium strainer
    • 85g plain flour
    • 40g cocoa powder
    • 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Pour into lined 20cm x 20cm baking tin
  6. Bake at 160°C for 30-35 mins
  7. Cool in tin for 1 hour

Serving

  • Calories = 5400, 1/16 = 340

Notes

  • Can use mixture of milk / white / dark chocolate but usually mainly dark
