Sweet potatoes

Boiled

  • Boil in water for 15-20 mins

Roasted

  • Can easily burn and then taste bitter
  • 1cm chunks
  • 160°C for 40 mins (maybe up to 180°C at end to finish)

Spanish roasted

  1. Mix
    • 550g (before peeling) sweet potatoes 1cm cubes
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp maple syrup
    • ½ tsp smoked paprika
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. 160°C for 40 mins
  3. To serve
    • drizzle maple syrup
    • to taste salt
