Apple & blackberry muesli cake

Oats

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 3 tbsp oats
    • 1 egg beaten
    • 1 tsp vanilla paste
    • 6 blackberries halved
    • 6 pecan halves chopped
    • ½ tsp cinnamon

Apple

  1. Simmer in pan for 10 mins
    • 1 apple peeled & cut into cubes
    • 200ml soya/pea milk
    • 3 cloves
  2. Remove cloves
  3. Mash apple a little

Cake

  1. Mix
    • oat mixture
    • apples & milk
  2. Pour into baking dish
  3. Bake at 180°C for 30 mins
  4. Serve with
    • 1½ tbsp (23g) yogurt per portion
