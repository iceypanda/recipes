Chorizo, chickpea & spinach soup

  1. Add to pan and heat for 5 mins (stiring often)
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 160g chorizo chopped
  2. Add and cook slowly for 15 mins
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1/2 onion
    • 2 sticks celery chopped
  3. Add and simmer for 40 mins
    • 250g spinach
    • 2 ripe tomatoes chopped
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 1 tin chick peas
    • 1l chicken stock
  4. Add
    • 55g prosciutto (optional)
  5. Blitz at least half the soup till thick

Notes

  • Better without prosciutto
