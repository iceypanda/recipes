Chorizo soup

  1. Add to pan and heat for 5 mins (stiring often):
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 160g chopped chorizo
  2. Add and cook slowly for 15 mins:
    • 1 garlic clove
    • 1 onion
    • 2 sticks of chopped celery
  3. Add and simmer for 40 mins:
    • 500g spinach
    • 8 chopped tomatoes
    • 1 tin chick peas
    • 2 pints chicken stock
  4. Add:
    • 55g prosciutto
    • more olive oil

Comments

Can replace celery with 2 more onions

Can replace prosciutto with more chorizo
