Chorizo, chickpea & spinach soup

  1. Add to pan and heat for 5 mins (stiring often)
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 180g chorizo chopped
  2. Add and cook on low heat for 15 mins
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 1 onion
    • 3 sticks celery chopped
  3. Add and simmer for 40 mins
    • 250g spinach
    • 2 ripe tomatoes chopped
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 1 tin chick peas with liquid
    • 1l chicken stock
  4. Blitz at least half the soup till thick
  5. Add lemon juice to taste

Serving

  • 6+ portions

Notes

  • spinach can be frozen or fresh
