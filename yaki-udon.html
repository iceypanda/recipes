Yaki udon

Chicken

  1. Marinade for 15 mins
    • 1 chicken breast sliced
  2. Velvet in water

Noodles

  1. Cook in water
    • 300g udon noodles
  2. Allow to cool
  3. Beat
    • 2 eggs
  4. Mix eggs and noodles to coat

Yaki udon

  1. Heat for 7 mins
    • vegetable oil
    • 1 red pepper sliced
    • 1 onion sliced
    • 30g ginger grated
    • 1 clove garlic grated
  2. Add and heat for 3 mins
    • 4 spring onions sliced 1cm pieces (save some finely sliced green for garnish)
    • 150g small prawns cooked
    • 75g beansprouts
    • chicken
  3. Add, mix well and cook for 4 mins stirring frequently
    • noodles & egg
  4. Add sauce over food (may need more) and mix well
    • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 2 tbsp shoyu
    • 1 tbsp mirin (new)
    • 2 tsp brown sugar (new)
    • 1 tsp oyster sauce (new)
  5. On plate garnish with
    • sesame seeds toasted
    • spring onion thinly sliced (for colour)
    • pickled ginger (optional)
    • chilli oil (optional)

Serving

  • 2 portions
