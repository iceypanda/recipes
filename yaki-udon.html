Yaki udon

Noodles

  1. Cook in water
    • 300g udon noodles
  2. Allow to cool
  3. Beat
    • 2 eggs
  4. Mix eggs and noodles to coat

Chicken

  1. Marinade for 15 mins
    • 1 chicken breast sliced
  2. Velvet in water

Yaki udon

  1. Heat for 7 mins
    • vegetable oil
    • 1 red pepper sliced
    • 1 onion sliced
    • 2.5 cm ginger grated
  2. Add and heat for 3 mins
    • 4 spring onions finely sliced
    • 150g small prawns cooked
    • 75g beansprouts
  3. Add, mix well and cook for 4 mins stirring frequently
    • noodles & egg
  4. Add sauce over food (may need more) and mix well
    • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 tbsp shoyu
  5. On plate garnish with
    • sesame seeds toasted
    • pickled ginger
    • chilli oil

Serving

  • 2 portions
