Yaki udon

Sauce

  1. Mix
    • 4 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 3 tbsp mirin
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp water
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp dashi powder

Stir fry

  1. Heat for 2 mins
    • vegetable oil
    • chicken / pork / tofu
  2. Heat for 5 mins
    • vegetable oil
    • 4 mushrooms sliced
    • 1 red pepper sliced
    • 1 onion sliced
    • 1 carrot julienned
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • udon noodles
    • sauce
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • ⅛ green cabbage sliced
    • 3 spring onions in ½" pieces

Serving

  • 2 portions
