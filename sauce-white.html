White sauce

Roux

  • Roux = cooked mixture and butter & flour
  • Roux should remain liquid, if solid then too much flour
  • Darker roux has less thickening ability
  • White < blond 2-10 mins < brown 10-15 mins
  • Remove heat if sauce too hot

Milk

  1. Warm until almost simmering
    • 250ml milk
    • ½ onion
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 2 cloves
  2. Remove heat
  3. Leave to infuse for 20 mins
  4. Strain

Bechamel

  1. Melt in pan
    • 25g unsalted butter
  2. Mix in gradually with small whisk
    • 2 tbsp plain flour
  3. Cook roux for 2-10 mins
  4. Mix in gradually
    • 250ml milk warmed

Mornay sauce

  • 2 portions
  • Need less of a stronger cheese
  • ~2:1 ratio bechamel to cheese
  • Roux = 20g butter : 1⅔ tbsp flour : 200ml milk
  1. Whisk into roux until tastes cheesey
    • 70g Gruyere
    • 90g Cheddar
