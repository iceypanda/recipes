Cheesy quiche

  1. Beat in bowl
    • 4 eggs
  2. Stir in
    • 2 tbsp cottage cheese
    • 1 handful spinach
    • 4 cherry tomatoes quartered
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  3. Line 1 or 2 oven proof dish(es) with
    • 2 wholemeal wraps
  4. Pour in egg mixture
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Bake at 160°C for 35 mins
  7. Remove foil
  8. Bake at 160°C for 5 mins

Serving

  • 2 portions
