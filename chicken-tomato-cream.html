Creamy tomato chicken

Chicken

  1. Coat in plain flour
    • 4 chicken breasts seasoned
  2. Fry for 10 mins till golden but not cooked through
    • 2 tbsp sun-dried tomato oil
    • chicken breasts
  3. Set aside on plate and cover with foil

Creamy tomato chicken

  1. Fry till soft but not golden
    • 1 tbsp sun-dried tomato oil
    • 1 red onion diced
  2. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 3 cloves garlic grated
  3. Mix in
    • 100g sun-dried tomatoes chopped
    • ½ tsp thyme
    • ½ tsp wholegrain mustard
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  4. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • chicken breasts
    • 200ml chicken stock (½ salted cube)
    • 150ml double cream
    • 1 tsp cornflour in cold water
  5. Mix in
    • 35g parmesan

Serving

  • 4 portions
