Mujaddara

  1. Fry 2 onions onions in olive oil on low heat for 15 mins until caramelised
  2. Saute with onions for 1 min
    • ½ tsp cumin
    • ½ tsp allspice
  3. Add
    • 740ml vegetable stock
    • ⅔ cup puy lentils
    • ⅔ cup jasmine rice
  4. Bring to boil then simmer for 30 mins
  5. Rest for 10 mins with lid on

Notes

A complete protein meal

Took out ¼ tsp cloves because too strong

Reduced amount to ⅔
