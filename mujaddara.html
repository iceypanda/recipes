Mujaddara

  1. Soak 1 cup brown lentils for 30 mins
  2. Fry 3 onions onions in olive oil on low heat for 15 mins until caramelised
  3. Saute with onions for 1 min:
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp allspice
    • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  4. Add:
    • 1.1l vegetable broth
    • soaked lentils
    • 1 cup jasmine rice
  5. Bring to boil then simmer for 30 mins
  6. Rest for 10 mins with lid on

Comments

A complete protein meal

Could cook onions with spiced chicken/fish/lamb

Careful not too much ground cloves

Use puy lentils which don't need soaking
