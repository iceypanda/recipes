Polenta chips

  1. Cook 100g polenta according to instructions on packet, whisk constantly to prevent lumps
  2. Remove from heat and stir in 10g grated parmesan
  3. Pour on baking tray and leave to set
  4. Cut polenta into chips and toss in little olive oil
  5. Roast at 180°C for 50 mins until crips and golden
  6. Add fresh rosemary leaves for last 10 mins (optional)
