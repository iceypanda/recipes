Polenta chips

Polenta

  1. Boil 400ml water in a pan
  2. Gradually add 100g polenta whisking constantly to prevent lumps
  3. Remove from heat and stir in 10g grated parmesan
  4. Pour on baking tray and leave to set

Chips

  1. Cut polenta into chips and toss in olive oil
  2. Roast at 190°C for 16 mins in air fryer until crisp and golden

Notes

  • Try 16 mins then more if not crispy enough
  • Can sprinkle grated cheddar over for last 5 mins in oven
  • 25 mins in oven
