Polenta chips

Polenta

  1. Boil 400ml water in a pan
  2. Gradually add 100g polenta whisking constantly to prevent lumps
  3. Remove from heat and stir in 10g grated parmesan
  4. Pour on baking tray and leave to set

Chips

  1. Cut polenta into chips and toss in little olive oil
  2. Roast at 180°C for 50 mins until crisp and golden
  3. Add fresh rosemary leaves for last 10 mins (optional)
