Polenta chips

Polenta

  1. Boil in pan
    • 400ml water
    • ½ stock cube
  2. Reduce heat and add 100g polenta whisking constantly to prevent lumps
  3. Remove heat and stir in 20g parmesan / cheddar grated
  4. Pour on baking tray and leave to set

Chips

  1. Cut polenta into chips
  2. Coat chips in olive oil
  3. Roast ½ batch at 190°C for 16 mins in air fryer

Serving

  • 2 portions

Notes

  • Cook as chips not cubes
  • 25 mins in oven, use oven if need to cook full batch
