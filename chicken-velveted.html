Velveted chicken

Marinade

  1. Cut 400g chicken breast / pork loin into ⅛" strips
  2. Put in bowl, cover with cold water and agitate vigourously
  3. Drain in strainer and press to remove excess water
  4. Mix in bowl and stir well
    • chicken
    • 2 tsp shoyu
    • 2 tsp Shaoxing wine
    • 2 tsp cornflour
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
    • 1 large egg white
  5. Leave for 15 mins

Velveting

  1. Bring 1l water to boil on high-heat in wok
  2. Add chicken 1 piece at a time so doesn't stick
  3. Remove after 60s
  4. Rinse under tap to remove egg white
  5. Place in bowl to dry
  6. Discard water and dry wok
