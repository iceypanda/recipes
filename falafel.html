Falafels

Falafels

  1. Blend in food processor
    • 400g tin chick peas
    • ½ onion finely chopped
    • 1 clove garlic finely chopped
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp cinnamon
    • small handful chopped coriander
    • small handful chopped parsley
    • 2 tbsp wholemeal flour
    • splash of lemon juice
  2. Shape into burgers
  3. Fry in sunflower oil for 2-3 mins on both sides until brown and crispy
  4. Drain on paper
  5. Serve in pittas with tzatiki, tomato salsa, vine leaves, salad

Tzatziki

  1. Coursely grate ½ deseeded cucumber
  2. Add pinch of salt and squeeze all liquid out
  3. Mix well with
    • 170g Greek yogurt
    • 1 garlic clove
    • handful fresh mint leaves

Comments

Try grilling instead of frying

Plenty of oil gives falafels more flavour and colour

Use largest ring of cooker for frying pan

More spice better than too little
