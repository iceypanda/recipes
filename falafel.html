Falafels

  1. Blend in food processor
    • 400g tin chick peas
    • ½ onion finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic finely chopped
    • 2 tbsp wholemeal flour
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp chilli powder
    • ½ tsp baking powder
    • small handful chopped fresh coriander
    • small handful chopped parsley
  2. Shape into burgers
  3. Fry in sunflower oil for 2-3 mins on both sides until brown and crispy
  4. Drain on paper

Serving

  • pittas, wraps
  • tzatziki, humous, tahini, tomato salsa
  • lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, pomegranate, pickled turnip / beetroot
  • splash of lemon or lime juice

Notes

  • Air fry for 20 mins at 200°C
  • Want as little liquid as possible otherwise insides will be wet
  • Too much herbs will make dough too wet
  • Plenty of oil gives falafels more flavour and colour
  • More spice better than too little
  • Try 1 tbsp ras el hanout instead of other spices
