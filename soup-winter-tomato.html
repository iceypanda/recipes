Winter tomato soup

Dried tomatoes

  1. Leave to soaked in covered bowl
    • 1 cup (?) sun-dried tomatoes (no oil)
    • 500ml water boiling

Tinned tomatoes

  1. Drain and save liquid
    • 2 tins whole peeled tomatoes halved
  2. Mix tomatoes with
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp thyme
  3. Roast at 190°C for 30 mins

Soup

  1. Cook for 5 mins
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 red onion thinly sliced
    • 1 carrot diced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tbsp garlic chopped
  3. Add and reduce for 5-10 mins
    • reserved liquid from soaked tomatoes
    • reserved and roasting liquid from roasted tomatoes
    • 1 tsp sugar
  4. Add and bring to boil then simmer for 30 mins
    • 950ml stock
    • soaked tomatoes roughly chopped
    • roasted tomatoes chopped

Serving

  • Stilton & fig wholemeal scones
