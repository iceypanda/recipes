Kedgeree

  1. Fry for 5 mins
    • 2 smoked mackerel fillets
    • 1 tsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp hot curry powder
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 100g peas
    • 40g couscous cooked
    • juice ½ lemon
  3. Mix in
    • 2 tsp fresh parsely
    • 2 tsp fresh dill
    • black pepper
  4. Serve on top
    • eggs poached

Serving

  • 2 portions

Notes

  • More curry powder and too salty
