Kedgeree

Eggs

  1. Hard boil for 5 mins
    • 3 eggs
  2. Drain boiling water and replace with cold
  3. Leave to cool
  4. Remove shells

Kedgeree

  1. Fry for 5 mins
    • 2 smoked mackerel fillets
    • 1 tsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp curry powder
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 100g peas
    • 40g couscous cooked
    • juice ½ lemon
  3. Mix in
    • 2 tsp fresh parsely
    • 2 tsp fresh dill
    • black pepper
  4. Serve on top
    • eggs chopped

Serving

  • 2 portions
