Bacon & parmesan biscuits

  • Preheat oven to 200°C

Bacon

  • 8 rashers in two batches
  1. Place 4 rashers bacon flat in 30 cm pan
  2. Heat on medium heat for 4 mins until sizzling
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low heat
  4. Cook for 12 mins until fat is rendered, flipping as required
  5. Drain rashers on paper towel

Buttermilk

  • Try milk & vinegar instead next time
  • Whisk together
    • 240ml (233g) soured cream
    • 160ml (165g) Greek yogurt
    • 80ml (82g) milk

Biscuits

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 360g plain flour
    • 1 tbsp baking powder
    • ¾ tsp salt
    • ¼ tsp baking soda
  2. Add and rub until crumbs
    • 110g unsalted butter cold and cubed
  3. Add buttermilk mixture and fold until combined
  4. Roll dough to make 30cm square
  5. Fold twice into thirds to make 9 layers
  6. Roll dough to make 30cm square
  7. Sprinkle over
    • 8 rashers bacon cooked, chopped & blitzed
    • 60g parmesan finely grated
  8. Fold twice into thirds to make 9 layers
  9. Roll dough to make 30cm square
  10. Cut out into 10cm rounds
  11. Roll out remaining dough and cut into rounds

Baking

  1. Place rounds on baking paper
  2. Brush tops with beaten egg or melted butter
  3. Bake at 200°C for 15 mins until golden brown and risen
  4. Rotate trays and switch between shelves halfway through cooking
