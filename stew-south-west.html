South West Stew

  1. Heat in a pan for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • ¼ (50-60g) chorizo chopped (optional)
  2. Heat in a pan for 8 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 1 pepper
    • 6 mushrooms
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp mild chilli powder
    • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  4. Add, bring to boil then simmer for 30 mins till potatoes cooked
    • 1l stock 1 salt & 1 no-salt cube
    • 350g (after peeling) potatoes in chunks
    • courgette diced (optional))
    • 1 tin black beans
    • 1 tin pinto / black-eyed beans
    • 1 tin (275g drained) sweetcorn
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • ⅓ cup (100g) tomato puree
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • Cheese rind (optional)
  5. Add
    • coriander leaves chopped, stalks removed (optional)

Notes

  • Slow cook need 5 hours on high
  • Chilli reduced from 2 tsp
