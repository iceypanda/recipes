South West Stew

  1. Heat in a pan for 8 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 1 bell pepper
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 6 mushrooms
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • 1 tsp mild chilli powder
    • 2 tsp cumin
  3. Add, bring to boil then simmer for 20 mins
    • 1l stock
    • 350g potatoes in chunks
    • 1 tin black beans
    • 1 tin pinto beans
    • 1 tin sweetcorn
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • ⅓ cup of tomato paste
  4. Add chopped coriander

Notes

Could try using 1 tsp smoked paprika and less or in addition to the cumin

Chilli reduced from 2 tsp
