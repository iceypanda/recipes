Fruity fish salad

Cod

  1. Bake at 180°C for 15 mins
    • 2 portions cod
    • zest 1 lime
    • juice ½ lime

Salad

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 1 avocado cubed
    • 6 cherry tomatoes quartered
    • 2 spring onions chopped
    • handful rocket leaves
    • ½ red chilli chopped
    • ½ mango cubed
    • handful coriander
  2. Plate salad
  3. Place cod on top
  4. Squeeze over
    • juice of ½ lime

Serving

  • 2 portions
