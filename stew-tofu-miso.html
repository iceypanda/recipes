Tofu & miso stew

  1. Add to pan
    • dashi (1l water + 30g kombu soaked for 2 hours)
    • 1 sheet nori cut into squares
    • 1½ tbsp white miso paste
    • 1 chilli sliced (optional)
  2. Add and simmer gently for 5 mins
    • 4 spring onions finely sliced
    • 200g tofu cubed
    • 160g pak choi / tenderstem broccoli
    • 160g exotic mushrooms
  3. Add lime juice

Serving

  • noodles
