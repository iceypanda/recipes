Tofu & miso stew

Dashi

  1. Soak for two hours
    • 40g kombu washed
    • 800ml hot water
  2. Remove kombu and discard

Stew

  1. Add to pan and simmer for 5 mins
    • dashi
    • 2 sheets nori cut into squares
    • 2 tbsp white miso paste
    • ½ chilli sliced (optional)
    • 160g exotic mushrooms
  2. Add and simmer gently for 5 mins
    • 4 spring onions finely sliced
    • 200g silken tofu cubed
    • 160g pak choi
  3. Add 1 tsp? lime juice

Serving

  • noodles in bowl first, stew on top

Notes

  • measure lime juice
  • instead of mushrooms
    • broad beans / edamame beans / mangetout / tenderstem broccoli
    • sweetcorn / grated carrot
    • daikon / radish
