Tofu & miso stew

Dashi

  1. Soak for two hours
    • 40g kombu washed
    • 600ml hot water
  2. Remove kombu and discard

Stew

  1. Add to pan and simmer for 5 mins
    • dashi
    • 2 sheets nori cut into squares
    • 2 tbsp white miso paste
    • 1 chilli sliced (optional)
    • 160g exotic mushrooms
  2. Add and simmer gently for 5 mins
    • 4 spring onions finely sliced
    • 200g tofu cubed
    • 160g pak choi / tenderstem broccoli
  3. Add lime juice

Serving

  • noodles

Notes

  • instead of mushrooms could use, broad beans / edamame beans / sweetcorn / grated carrot / mangetout / daikon / radish
