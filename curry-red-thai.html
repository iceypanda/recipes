Thai red curry

  1. Heat for 10 mins until meat is browned
    • sunflower oil
    • 400g chicken / duck
    • 6 shallots chopped
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 400ml coconut milk
    • 300g new potatoes cut into bite-size pieces
    • 1 red pepper chopped
    • 4 tbsp thai red curry paste (use whole small pot)
    • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins, until potatoes cooked
    • 150g green beans cut into thirds
  4. Add fresh chopped basil leaves

Serving

  • Spring rolls

Notes

  • Made: 15 Apr 2022, 1 Jun 2022
  • Use 300g chicken if for lunch
  • 1 tsp cornflour if need to thicken
  • Use good coconut milk (Blue Dragon), not essential kind

Changes to original recipe

  • Removed fish sauce as included in the paste
  • Paste added later, not with chicken
  • Tried pineapple but not great
