Thai red curry

  1. Heat for 10 mins until meat is browned
    • sunflower oil
    • 400g chicken / duck
    • 3 tbsp thai red curry paste
    • 6 peeled and quartered shallots
  2. Add 400ml coconut milk and bring to boil
  3. Add and stir in
    • 1 tbsp fish sauce
    • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  4. Add
    • 1 red pepper
    • 300g halved new potatoes
    • 100g green beans
  5. Cook for 25 mins until potatoes soft (or 4 hours on high in slow cooker)
  6. Add fresh chopped basil leaves
