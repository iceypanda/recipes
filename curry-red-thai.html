Thai red curry

  1. Heat for few mins until meat is browned
    • sunflower oil
    • 500g chicken / duck
    • 4 tbsp thai red curry paste
    • 6 peeled and quartered shallots
  2. Add 400ml coconut milk and bring to boil
  3. Add the pour into slow cooker
    • 1 tbsp fish sauce
    • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  4. Add to slow cooker
    • 1 red pepper
    • 400g potatoes
    • small tin pineapple
  5. Cook on high heat for 4 hours in slow cooker
