Thai red curry

Chicken

  1. Heat till meat just cooked outside
    • sunflower oil
    • 400g chicken / duck in pieces
  2. Set aside in covered bowl

Curry

  1. Heat for 8 mins
    • sunflower oil
    • 6 shallots chopped
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 400ml coconut milk
    • 300g new potatoes cut into bite-size pieces
    • 1 red pepper chopped
    • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Add and simmer for 15-20 mins, till potatoes and beans cooked
    • 150-200g green beans cut into thirds
    • 4 tbsp thai red curry paste (use whole small pot)
    • chicken (add with 5 mins to go)
    • 1½ tsp cornflour mixed with 3 tsp water (or more as required)
    • ¼ tsp fish sauce

Serving

  • Spring rolls
  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Made: 4 Jun 2023, 15 Apr 2022, 1 Jun 2022, 19 Feb 2023
  • Use good coconut milk (Blue Dragon), not essential kind
  • For prawns cook separately and add at end
  • Tried pineapple but not great
