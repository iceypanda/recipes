Tofu, kombu & miso stew

Dashi

  1. Soak for 2 hours
    • 40g kombu washed
    • 800ml hot water
  2. Strain to remove kombu and discard

Stew

  1. Add to pan and simmer for 5 mins
    • dashi
    • 2 sheets nori cut into squares
    • ½ chilli sliced (optional)
    • 160g exotic mushrooms (optional)
  2. Add and simmer gently for 5 mins
    • noodles soaked in hot water
    • 4-6 spring onions finely sliced
    • 200g silken tofu cubed
    • 2 pak choi chopped
    • 2 tbsp white miso paste
  3. Add lime juice to taste (measure)

Serving

  • Per portion: 350ml dashi, 1 tbsp miso
  • 2-3 portions

Notes

  • If need more dashi add more water when soaking, enough kombu already
