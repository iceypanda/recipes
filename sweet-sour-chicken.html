Sweet & sour chicken / pork

Prep

  • Chop pineapple and return to tin
  • Chop peppers and set aside
  • Grate ginger and garlic
  • Mix cornflour solution in small cup
  • Mix sauce in small bowl
  • Mix marinade in large container
  • Chop meat
  • Roast cashews

Sauce

  • Mix
    • small tin pineapple juice
    • 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
    • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
    • 2 tbsp sugar
    • 1 tsp shoyu

Cornflour

  • Mix
    • 1 tbsp water
    • 2 tsp cornflour

Meat marinade

  1. Cut 400g chicken breast / pork loin into ⅛" strips
  2. Put in bowl, cover with cold water and agitate vigourously
  3. Drain in strainer and press to remove excess water
  4. Mix in bowl and stir well
    • chicken
    • 2 tsp shoyu
    • 2 tsp Shaoxing wine
    • 2 tsp cornflour
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp sugar
    • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
    • 1 large egg white
  5. Leave for 15 mins

Velveting

  1. Bring 1l water to boil on high-heat in wok
  2. Add chicken 1 piece at a time so doesn't stick
  3. Remove after 60s
  4. Rinse under tap to remove egg white
  5. Place in bowl to dry
  6. Discard water and dry wok

Stir fry

  1. Rub wok with thin film of oil and heat until smoking
  2. Add and swirl to coat
    • 2 tbsp veg oil
  3. Add and cook
    • 1 red pepper
    • 1 yellow pepper
  4. Make space in middle and heat until fragrant, 30s
    • 1 tbsp veg oil
    • 4 cloves garlic minced
    • 5g ginger minced
  5. Add
    • chicken
    • pineapple chunks
    • 75g cashews roasted
  6. Add sauce around edges
  7. Add cornflour liquid till sauce good consistency
  8. Cook tossing until chicken cooked through

Serving

  • Rice

Notes

  • Original recipe: The Wok, p75
