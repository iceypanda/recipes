Sweet & sour chicken / pork

Prep

  • Chop pineapple and return to tin
  • Chop peppers and set aside
  • Grate ginger and garlic
  • Mix cornflour solution in small cup
  • Mix sauce in small bowl
  • Mix marinade in large container
  • Chop meat
  • Roast cashews
  • Velvet chicken

Sauce

  • Mix
    • small tin pineapple juice
    • 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
    • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
    • 2 tbsp sugar
    • 1 tsp shoyu

Cornflour

  • Mix
    • 1 tbsp water
    • 2 tsp cornflour

Stir fry

  1. Rub wok with thin film of oil and heat until smoking
  2. Add and swirl to coat
    • 2 tbsp veg oil
  3. Add and cook
    • 1 red pepper
    • 1 yellow pepper
  4. Make space in middle and heat until fragrant, 30s
    • 1 tbsp veg oil
    • 2 cloves (max) garlic minced
    • 5g (max) ginger minced
  5. Add
    • chicken
    • pineapple chunks
    • 80g cashews roasted
  6. Add sauce around edges
  7. Add cornflour liquid till sauce good consistency
  8. Cook tossing until chicken cooked through

Serving

  • Rice, 220g for 3 portions
  • 2-3 portions

Notes

  • Could add julienned carrots with peppers
  • Original recipe: The Wok, p75
