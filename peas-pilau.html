Peas pilau

  • Cook in rice cooker
    • 350g basmati rice
    • 875g water
    • 80g peas

Spices

Spices 1

  • Add
    • 4 cardamom pods
    • 4 cloves
    • 1 tsp caraway seeds lightly bashed
    • ¼ tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp rose water

Spices 2

  • Add
    • 4 cardamom pods
    • 1 tsp caraway seeds lightly bashed
    • ¼ tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp turmeric
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon

Serving

  • 5 portions
