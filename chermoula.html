Chermoula

  1. Blitz
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • ½ tsp paprika
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Add and blitz
    • 30g packet fresh coriander large stalks removed
    • 30g packet fresh parsley large stalks removed
  3. Add and blitz
    • 1 preserved lemonchopped, pips removed
    • juice ½ lemon
  4. Add and blitz
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
  5. Season to taste

Serving

  • 4 portions
  • Marinade for grilled fish
