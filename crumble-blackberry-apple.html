Blackberry & apple crumble

Topping

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 100g plain flour
    • 60g caster sugar
  2. Rub till breadcrumbs
    • 60g unsalted butter cubed
  3. Mix in
    • 3 tbsp (25g) oats
  4. Spread over baking tray and cook at 180°C for 15 mins

Fruit

  1. Melt then cook on medium heat for 3 mins
    • 30g unsalted butter
    • 30g dark brown sugar
  2. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 300g (after peeling, 3) Braeburn apples peeled & cored, 2cm dice
  3. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 120-150g blackberries (apple peeled : blackberry ratio)
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  4. Remove heat, cover and leave for 3 mins
  5. Drain excess liquid

Crumble

  1. Put fruit into oven dish / ramekins
  2. Cover with crumble mix
  3. Top with
    • 1 tbsp hazelnuts (optional)
    • 1½ tsp dark brown sugar
  4. Cook at 180°C for 5 mins

Serving

  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Flour reduced from 120g
  • Original recipe
