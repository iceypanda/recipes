Blackberry & apple crumble

Topping

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 120g plain flour
    • 60g caster sugar
  2. Rub till breadcrumbs
    • 60g unsalted butter cubed
  3. Spread over baking tray and cook at 170°C for 15 mins

Fruit

  1. Melt then cook on medium heat for 3 mins
    • 30g unsalted butter
    • 30g dark brown sugar
  2. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 300g (before peeling) Braeburn apples peeled & cored, 2cm dice
  3. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 120-150g blackberries (apple peeled : blackberry ratio)
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  4. Remove heat, cover and leave for 3 mins

Crumble

  1. Put fruit into oven dish / ramekins
  2. Top with crumble mix
  3. Cook for 5-10 mins (optional)

Serving

  • 4 portions
