Pilau rice

Spices

  1. Grind ½ tsp coriander seeds in pestle and mortar until course powder
  2. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds and pound lightly to mix
  3. Add 6 cardamom pods and grind until husks split and seeds lightly crushed
  4. Add
    • 8 cloves
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 2 bay leaves
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • ½ tsp black mustard seeds

Rice

  1. Fry until soft
    • sunflower oil
    • 20g butter
    • 1 onion / shallots
  2. Add and fry gently for 2 mins
    • spices
    • 1 clove garlic
  3. Add 300g basmati rice and coat with spices
  4. Add, bring to boil then simmer for 15 mins
    • 680ml vegetable or chicken stock (1 cube, ½ salt ½ non-salt )
    • pinch black pepper
  5. When rice is cooked leave to cool for 10 mins
  6. Remove all cloves, pods, leaves, etc from top of rice
  7. Stir and fluff up

Notes

  • Shallots better than onion
  • Originally 75g butter
