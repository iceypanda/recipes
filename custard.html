Custard

Yolks

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 4 large egg yolks
    • 2 tbsp cornflour
    • 75g golden caster sugar (try 60g)
    • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste (try 1¼ tsp)

Custard

  1. Heat in pan to ~60°C
    • 150ml (98g) double cream
    • 525ml (540g) milk
  2. Slowly pour milk & cream into egg mixture whisking constantly
  3. Return custard to the pan
  4. Remove foam
  5. Heat gently until thickened stirring constantly
  6. Leave to cool

Serving

  • 6 portions
