Custard

Yolks

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 6 large (100g) egg yolks
    • 65g golden caster sugar
    • 1¼ tbsp cornflour
    • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste

Custard

  1. Heat in pan to ~50°C
    • 150ml (147g) double cream
    • 525ml (540g) milk
    or
    • 300ml (294g) single cream
    • 375ml (385g) milk
  2. Slowly pour milk & cream into egg mixture whisking constantly
  3. Return custard to the pan
  4. Remove foam into mixing bowl
  5. Heat gently stirring constantly
  6. At 85°C heat for 2 mins stirring constantly
  7. Remove heat
  8. Add
    • ¼ tsp rose water (optional)
  9. Continue stirring gently till cooled a little
  10. Place pan in wok of cold water to cool
  11. Serve or pour into containers

Serving

  • 6 portions: 150g per portion

Notes

  • Eggs
    • 9 yolks (150g) & 1 tbsp cornflour: too thick, like scrambled eggs
    • 6 yolks (94g) & 1 scant tbsp cornflour, 82°C for 4 mins: didn't thicken
  • Cornflour
    • 2 or 1½ tbsp too thick
    • 1 scant tbsp too thin
    • 1¼ tbsp good
  • Sugar
    • 75g too sweet
    • 60g nice, not too sweet
    • 65g best
    • 65g light Demerara, brown with caramel taste
    • 20g light Demerara & 45g golden caster, nice
  • Vanilla
    • 1½ tsp too much, not nice
    • 1 tsp good
