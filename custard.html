Custard

Yolks

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 4 large egg yolks
    • 1½ tbsp cornflour
    • 65g golden caster sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste

Custard

  1. Heat in pan to ~60°C
    • 150ml (147g) double cream
    • 525ml (540g) milk
  2. Slowly pour milk & cream into egg mixture whisking constantly
  3. Return custard to the pan
  4. Remove foam
  5. Heat gently till thickened stirring constantly
  6. Cover with parchment paper and pan lid to prevent skin
  7. Leave to cool

Serving

  • 6 portions

Notes

  • Sugar: 75g too sweet, 60g nice and not too sweet
  • Vanilla: 1½ tsp too much not nice
  • Cornflour: 2 tbsp too much too thick
