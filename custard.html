Custard

Yolks

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 3 large egg yolks
    • 2 tbsp & 1 tsp cornflour
    • 75g caster sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Custard

  1. Heat in pan until almost boiling
    • 150ml double cream
    • 525ml (540g) milk
  2. Slowly pour milk & cream into egg mixture whisking constantly
  3. Return custard to the pan
  4. Heat gently until thickened stirring constantly

Serving

  • 6 portions
