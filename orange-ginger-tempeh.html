Orange & ginger tempeh

Tempeh

  1. Marinade in bowl
    • 200g tempeh in pieces
    • 2 red chillies deseeded & chopped
    • 1 heaped tsp ginger chopped
    • 5 cloves garlic sliced
    • 1 handful basil chopped
    • juice 1 orange
    • zest 1 orange

Veg

  1. Boil for 1 min
    • 1 handful green beans
    • 1 handful tenderstem broccoli
    • 1 handful baby corn
  2. Add and boil for 1 min
    • 1 pak choi
  3. Drain

Stir fry

  1. Fry for 5 mins till coloured
    • 4 tsp sesame oil
    • tempeh
    • marinade
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • veg
    • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  3. Garnish with
    • segments of 2 oranges
    • coriander chopped

Serving

  • 3 portions
