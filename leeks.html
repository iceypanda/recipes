Leeks

Notes

  • Don't slice lengthways or will come apart too easily

Prep

  1. Remove outer skin and top green section
  2. Rinse and pat dry
  3. Cut into 5cm sections

Braised

  1. Heat olive oil in pan on medium high heat until shimmering
  2. Add leeks cut side down and heat without moving for 5 mins until browned
  3. Place leeks in baking dish browned side up
  4. Dot top of leeks with butter
  5. Pour over leeks to cover halfway
    • stock mixture
  6. Cover with foil
  7. Cook in oven at 180°C for 30 mins
  8. Allow to cool for 5 mins
  9. Add to pan juices and pour over leeks
    • garnish & seasoning
  10. Serve browned side up

Thyme & lemon

  1. Stock mixture
    • 150ml stock
    • ½ tsp thyme
  2. Garnish & seasoning
    • 2 tsp lemon zest
    • 2 tsp lemon juice
    • pepper

Miso & maple

  1. Stock mixture
    • 150ml stock
    • 2 garlic cloves sliced
    • 2 tsp miso
    • 1 tsp mirin
    • ½ tsp sesame oil
    • pinch chilli flakes
    • knob of butter
  2. Garnish & seasoning
    • 1 tsp maple syrup
    • 1 tsp soy sauce
    • lemon zest

Braised with peas

  1. Simmer in pan for 15 mins
    • 1 leek outer skin removed & cut into 5cm sections
    • 300ml stock
    • clove garlic sliced
    • ¼ tsp thyme
    • pepper
  2. Add and cook for 5 more mins
    • 100g peas
  3. Drizzle with olive oil (optional) and season with pepper
