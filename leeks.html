Leeks

Roasted

  1. Cover so don't burn
  2. Cook at 180°C for 20-30 mins

Braised

  1. Split leeks in half lengthways
  2. Remove outer skin
  3. Rinse and pat dry
  4. Heat olive oil in pan on medium high heat until shimmering
  5. Add leeks cut side down and heat without moving for 5 mins until browned
  6. Place leeks in baking dish
  7. Dot top of leeks with butter
  8. Pour over leeks
    • 250ml stock
    • ½ tsp thyme
    • pepper
  9. Cover with foil and cook in oven at 190°C for 30 mins
  10. Allow to cool for 5 mins
  11. Add to pan juices and pour over leeks
    • 2 tsp lemon zest
    • 2 tsp lemon juice

Braised with peas

  1. Simmer in pan for 15 mins
    • 1 leek outer skin removed and quartered
    • 300ml stock
    • clove garlic sliced
    • ¼ tsp thyme
    • pepper
  2. Add and heat for 5 more mins
    • 100g peas
  3. Drizzle with olive oil (optional) and season with pepper
