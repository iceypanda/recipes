Menemen

  1. Heat in small frying pan for 8 mins
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 green pepper chopped
  2. Add and heat until tomatoes darken
    • 2 tomatoes grated & skins discard
    • pinch chilli flakes
  3. Season with
    • salt
    • black pepper
    • sugar (optional)
  4. Turn heat low, add and leave on top for 2 mins
    • 3 large eggs
  5. Gently stir eggs but do not mix in, heating for 5 mins
  6. Remove heat and leave to finish cooking
  7. Sprinkle over
    • Aleppo pepper / pul biber
    • Feta (optional)

Serving

  • 2 portions
  • Dark honey & soft cheese (mozzarella / burrata)
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Green olives
  • Turkish bread / pittas

Notes

  • Eggs should cook on sauce not base of pan
  • Original recipe
