Sugar custards

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Whisk to combine in a bowl
    • 80g brown sugar
    • 5 egg yolks
  3. Bring to boil then remove from heat
    • 500ml milk
    • 3 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Gradually add milk to yolks whisking constantly
  5. Place 4 ramekins in a baking dish
  6. Divide mixture between ramekins
  7. Pour hot water into baking dish to halfway up sides of ramekins
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 45 mins until custards are set
  9. Leave to cool for 2 hours
