Sugar custards

Milk

  1. Add to pan and warm to dissolve sugar
    • 350ml full-fat milk
    • 150ml single cream
    • 80g light brown muscavado sugar
    • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
    • pinch of salt

Eggs

  1. Whisk to combine in a bowl
    • 5 egg yolks (120g)
  2. Gradually add milk mixture to yolks whisking constantly
  3. Divide mixture equally between 4 ramekins in a baking dish
  4. Remove any foam
  5. Cover each ramekin with foil (squares with corners chopped off)

Bake

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Pour warm water into baking dish to halfway up sides of ramekins
  3. Bake for 45 mins until custards are set
  4. Leave to cool for 90 mins (without foil)
  5. Chill in fridge for 1 hour (with foil)

Serving

  • roasted rhubarb

Notes

  • Reduced heat to 150°C but slightly runny at bottom
  • Too much heat and custard will split, only warm milk in pan until sugar is disolved
