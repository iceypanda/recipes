Sugar custards

  1. Preheat oven to 140°C
  2. Whisk to combine in a bowl
    • 5 egg yolks (120g)
  3. Warm to dissolve sugar
    • 500ml milk
    • 80g brown sugar
    • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
    • pinch of salt (optional)
  4. Gradually add milk to yolks whisking constantly
  5. Place 4 ramekins in a baking dish
  6. Divide mixture between ramekins
  7. Pour warm water into baking dish to halfway up sides of ramekins
  8. Cover each ramekins with foil and bake for 45 mins until custards are set
  9. Leave to cool for 2 hours

Notes

  • Reduced heat from 160°C
  • Too much heat and custard will split
  • Try using half milk and half cream (or 300ml cream)
