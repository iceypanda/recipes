Sugar custards

Milk

  1. Add to pan and warm just enough to dissolve sugar
    • 350ml (360g) full-fat milk
    • 150ml single cream
    • 80g light brown muscavado sugar
    • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
    • pinch of salt

Custard

  1. Whisk to combine in a bowl
    • 5 egg yolks (120g)
  2. Gradually add milk mixture to yolks whisking constantly
  3. Remove any foam
  4. Divide mixture equally between 4 ramekins in a baking dish
  5. Cover each ramekin with foil squares with corners chopped off (size 1½ width of ramekins)
  6. Pour warm water into baking dish to halfway up sides of ramekins

Bake

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Bake for 45 mins until custards are set, rotate tray 180°C halfway
  3. Leave to cool for 90 mins (without foil)
  4. Chill in fridge for 1 hour (with foil)

Serving

  • roasted rhubarb
  • Calories = 1000, 1 portion = 250

Notes

  • try: 150C with less water
  • made: 24 Sep 2022
  • Reduced heat to 150°C but slightly runny at bottom
  • Too much heat and custard will split, only warm milk in pan until sugar is disolved
