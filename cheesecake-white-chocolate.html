White chocolate cheesecake

Base

  1. Break into bits then blitz till almost all crumbs
    • 175g gingernut biscuits
  2. Add add blitz again
    • 50g unsalted butter softened
  3. Press into 20cm circular baking dish with wooden spoon then flatten with one hand
  4. Cool in fridge

Filling

  1. Melt in bowl over saucepan of boiled water over low heat
    • 200g white chocolate
  2. Cool for 10 mins (make base)
  3. Beat in bowl with wooden spoon till soft
    • 300g full-fat cream cheese at room temperature
    • 1 tsp lemon juice
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Gently fold in melted chocolate
  5. Whip but stop before peaks soft peaks
    • 300ml double cream
  6. Fold cream into cheese mixture in two batches
  7. Scrape mixture onto base
  8. Tidy by going around edge with finger wrapped in kitchen roll
  9. Cover with cling film
  10. Refridgerate overnight

Serving

  • Calories = 4100, 1/10 = 410
  • 10 portions

⅔ amounts

  • In 20cm round baking tin
    • 115g biscuits
    • 33g butter
    • 133g white chocolate
    • 200g cheese
    • 196g double cream
    • 3.33g vanilla extract
    • 3.33g lemon juice

Notes

  • Can flavour with liqueur / spirit / flavoured white chocolate
  • Original recipe
  • Made: Oct 2022, Sep 2023
