Gingerbread men

  1. Cream together in bowl
    • 100g butter
    • 100g light brown/muscavado sugar
  2. Mix in
    • 3 tbsp (60g) golden syrup
    • 1 medium egg (optional)
  3. Sift in
    • 200g plain flour
    • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
    • 2 tsp ginger
    • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  4. Knead the dough together
  5. Roll out to 0.5-1.0cm thick
  6. Cut out gingerbread men
  7. Bake at 150°C for 10-15 mins
  8. Leave to cool for 5 mins
  9. Move to cooling rack

Serving

  • 10 men

Notes

  • Can switch 1 tbsp of golden syrup for black treacle
  • Adding an egg will make a softer, cakey bread, no egg for crunchy biscuit
