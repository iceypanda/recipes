Naan

Yeast

  1. Put in jug and leave for 15 mins until frothy
    • 120ml water 43°C
    • 7g sachet of yeast
    • 1 tsp golden caster sugar

Mix

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 300g strong white bread flour
    • 1 tbsp nigella seeds
    • 1 tsp golden caster sugar
    • ¾ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp baking powder
  2. Make well in middle and pour in
    • 25g melted unsalted butter
    • 150ml natural yogurt
    • yeast mixture
  3. Stir well, if dry add more warm water, if wet add more flour

Knead and rise

  1. Put in bread maker on dough setting for 1½ hours
  2. Or knead for 10 mins then leave for 1 hour until doubled in size

Cook

  1. Very gently tip dough onto floured board
  2. Divide into 2 then 3 to get 6 pieces with knife
  3. Shape each piece into tear drop shape
  4. Cook two at a time in skillet for 3 mins each side

Serving

  • Calories = 1450, 1/6 = 240
