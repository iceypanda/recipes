Naan

Yeast

  1. Put in jug and leave for 15 mins until frothy
    • 125ml water
    • 7g sachet of yeast
    • 1 tsp golden caster sugar

Mix

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 300g strong white bread flour
    • ½ tsp baking powder
    • 1 tsp golden caster sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
  2. Make well in middle and pour in
    • 25g melted butter
    • 150ml natural yogurt
    • 1 tbsp nigella seeds
    • yeast mixture
  3. Stir well, if dry add more warm water, if wet add more flour

Knead and rise

  1. Put in bread maker on dough setting for 1½ hours
  2. Or knead for 10 mins then leave for 1 hour until doubled in size

Cook

  1. Divide into 6 balls with knife
  2. One by one shape balls into tear drop shape
  3. Cook two at a time in skillet for 3 mins each side

Notes

Needs salt for flavour
