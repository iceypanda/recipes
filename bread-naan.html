Naan

  1. Put in jug and leave for 15 mins until frothy
    • 125ml water
    • 7g sachet of yeast
    • 1 tsp golden caster sugar
  2. Put in bowl and mix
    • 300g strong white bread flour
    • ½ tsp baking powder
    • 1 tsp golden caster sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
  3. Make well in middle and pour in
    • 25g melted butter
    • 150ml natural yogurt
    • 1 tbsp nigella seeds (optional)
    • yeast mixture
  4. Stir well, if dry add more warm water, if wet add more flour. Should be able to make ball with dough.
  5. Knead for 10 mins, first in bowl then on floured board
  6. Place in buttered bowl as large ball and leave for 1 hour until doubled in size
  7. Divide into 6 balls
  8. One by one shape balls into tear drop shape, 21cm x 13cm
  9. Dry fry one at a time in hot pan for about 3mins then turn over for another 3mins until cooked and charred in patches
  10. Once cooked place in oven on lowest heat, each brushed with butter

Notes

Needs salt for flavour
