Coconut flatbread

  1. Put in bowl
    • 200g tin of coconut milk
    • salt
  2. Add up to 220g self-raising flour to create dough
  3. Divide into 8
  4. Cook on skillet for 2-3 mins each side in butter

Notes

  • Heavier than yogurt flabread (maybe because no baking powder)
  • Make bread when need it so doesn't dry out
