Chicken + tarragon pasta

  1. Heat in pan, 5 mins
    • 1 onion
    • 4 tbsp olive oil
  2. Add, heat 5 mins
    • 4 chicken breasts
  3. Add, heat 10 mins
    • bunch of tarragon
    • juice and zest of 2 lemons
    • 1 crushed chicken stock cube
  4. Add, heat 2 mins
    • 1 tin artichokes
    • 4 courgettes, peeled into ribbons
  5. Mix with
    • 500g tagliatelle
    • 100g grated parmesan
