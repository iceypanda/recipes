Miso & anise broth

  1. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 400ml water
    • 10g ginger paste
    • 1 unsalted stock cube
    • 2 tsp tomato puree
    • 2g star anise smashed
  2. Sieve
  3. Strain into broth to taste
    • red miso
  4. Add gradually to taste
    • juice 1 lime

Serving

  • 2 portions
  • Drizzle with chilli oil
