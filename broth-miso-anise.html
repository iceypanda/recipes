Miso & anise broth

  • Amounts per portion
  1. Simmer for 5 mins
    • 250ml veg / chicken stock (½ salt cube)
    • ½ tsp tomato puree
    • 1g star anise smashed
  2. Sieve
  3. Cook veg in broth if required
  4. Strain into broth
    • 3 tbsp red miso
  5. Add gradually to taste
    • juice of lime
  6. Serve with
    • coriander leaves chopped (optional)
    • chilli oil (optional)
