Chicken & rice pilaf

    Chicken

  1. Sear 400g chicken breast (with skin on) for 5 mins on each side until golden brown in hot olive oil
  2. Add 4 shallots halved for 2 mins

    3. Pilaf

  3. Add to slow cooker
    • 1½ cups mixture basmati and brown rice
    • 10g butter
    • 250g mini carrots
    • 150g mushrooms chopped
    • juice of 1 lemon
    • 2 sprigs rosemary chopped
    • 2 tbsp fresh sage chopped
    • 2 tsp dried parsely
    • 2 tsp dried thyme
    • 750ml stock
  4. Add chicken skin side up
  5. Cook for 4 hours in slow cooker on high
  6. If rice is still hard and more water and cook for 30 mins more
