Tiramisu

Cream

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 300ml double cream
    • 250g mascarpone
    • 1 tbsp marsala (optional)
    • 60g caster sugar
    • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  2. Whisk till consistency of thickly whipped cream

Tiramisu

  1. Pour into shallow bowl
    • 300ml coffee
    • 1 tbsp kahlua/brandy/rum (optional)
  2. Dip half of the sponge fingers in coffee and make layer in dish
  3. Spread over half of cream mixture
  4. Scatter with
    • cocoa powder
    • chocolate finely grated
  5. Dip other half of the sponge fingers in coffee and make second layer
  6. Spread over other half of cream mixture
  7. Cover and chill overnight
  8. Before serving scatter with
    • cocoa powder
    • chocolate finely grated

Serving

  • 6 portions
