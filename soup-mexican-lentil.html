Mexican red lentil stew

  1. Chop tomatoes and celery before starting
  2. Rinse 2 cups dry red lentils until water clear
  3. Add 4 cups water and bring to boil then turn off heat and leave for 10 mins with lid on
  4. Saute in large pot:
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 4 cloves garlic
  5. Add 3 stalks celery untils softens
  6. Stir in:
    • 10 chopped tomatoes
    • ½ tbsp chilli powder
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • 10 dashes hot sauce
  7. Add:
    • drained cooked lentils
    • 4 cups vegetable broth
  8. Simmer for 15 mins then remove from heat
  9. Add:
    • fresh coriander
    • juice from 1 lime
