Fish, leek & potato soup

Pan 1

  1. Bring to boil
    • 300g smoked pollack or haddock fillet
    • 650ml vegetable stock
  2. Turn off the heat, flip the fish over, cover and leave for 3 mins or until fish cooked
  3. Remove fish from stock, remove from skin in large flakes

Pan 2

  1. Heat large knob of butter
  2. Sweat 2 leeks for 10 mins
  3. Add
    • the reserved stock
    • 500g potatoes in small cubes
  4. Simmer for 10 mins until potatoes are soft
  5. Add
    • the fish
    • 4 tbsp double cream
  6. Warm without boiling, then season
  7. Serve with fine chopped parsley
