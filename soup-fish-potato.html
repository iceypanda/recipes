Fish, leek & potato soup

Pan 1

  1. Bring to boil
    • 300g smoked pollack or haddock fillet
    • 650ml vegetable stock
  2. Turn off the heat, cover and leave fish to cook

Pan 2

  1. Heat large knob of butter
  2. Add and sweat for 10 mins
    • 2 leeks halved and sliced
  3. Add stock through strainer, keeping back the fish
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins until soft
    • 500g potatoes in small cubes
  5. Remove from heat and wait until soup has stopped bubbling
  6. Add
    • the fish
    • 4 tbsp double cream
    • cracked black pepper

Serving

  • parsley finely chopped
