King prawn watermelon salad

Zest

  1. Grate
    • zest 1 lime

Dressing

  1. Mix (2 portions)
    • 1 clove garlic grated
    • 1 red chilli chopped
    • juice 1 lime
    • 2 tsp olive oil

Prawns

  1. Cook (2 portions)
    • 200g king prawns
  2. Mix with
    • lime zest

Salad

  1. Add to bowl (per portion)
    • 1 handful green salad leaves
  2. Arrange (per portion)
    • 1 wedge watermelon chopped
  3. Add (per portion)
    • prawns
    • 1 spring onion sliced
    • black sesame seeds
  4. Drizzle
    • dressing

Serving

  • 2 portions
