Bombay potato frittata

  1. Cook 4 new potatoes cut into 5mm rounds for 6 mins
  2. Heat 100g spinach with 1 tbsp water in microwave until wilted
  3. Heat until soft
    • 1 tbsp oil
    • 1 onion
  4. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 1 clove garlic
    • ½ tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp ground cumin
    • ¼ tsp black mustard seeds
    • ¼ tsp turmeric
  5. Add and heat for 3 min
    • 3 chopped tomatoes
    • wilted spinach
  6. Add
    • 1 chopped green chilli
    • potatoes
  7. Add 2 eggs beaten with fresh coriander and cook until just set
