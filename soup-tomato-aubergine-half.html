Tomato & aubergine soup

Roast veg

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C
  2. Zebra peel 1 aubergine and cut into bite-size chunks
  3. Mix well, heat in oven for 45 mins, stirring halfway
    • olive oil
    • aubergine
    • 250g cherry tomatoes
    • 1 chopped large red chilli
    • black pepper
    • sprinkle with oregano

Broth

  1. Fry on low heat for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 garlic clove
    • 2 anchovies
  2. Add 1¼ tbsp tomato paste and fry for 3 mins (with lid on)
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 500ml vegetable stock
    • ½ tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  4. Add roasted veg
  5. Serve with croutons and chopped parsely
