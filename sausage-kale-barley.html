Sausage, kale & barley stew

  1. Heat in pan for 6 mins
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 red / white onions chopped
    • 2 carrots chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • kale stalks chopped
  2. Add 250g pearl barley and heat for 1 min
  3. Add and simmer for 30 mins
    • 3 sprigs rosemary chopped
    • 1l veg / chicken stock
    • 6 veg sausages
    • ground black pepper
  4. Add until for 5-10 mins until wilted
    • 300g kale
    • extra water if barley still hard

Serve with

  • new potatoes

Notes

  • add 2 cooked chipolatas at end for meat eaters
