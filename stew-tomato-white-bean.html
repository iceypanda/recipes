White bean & tomato stew

  1. Heat for 8 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
    • 1 leek or fennel chopped
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 150g cherry tomatoes
    • 3 bay leaves
    • ½ stock cube & 50ml water
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • salt and pepper
  4. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 1 tin cannellini beans drained & rinsed
    • 1 tin butter beans drained & rinsed
  5. Add chopped parsely

Serving

  • Baked salmon

Notes

  • Optional ingredients: red wine vinegar; sun-dried tomatoes; red pepper; veg stock; red wine; pine nuts; pesto; harissa
  • Optional herbs: basil; thyme; rosemary; oregano; paprika
  • Beans are already cooked so only need warming
  • Tried slow cooking but beans overcook
  • Nice: fennel, ¼ tsp basil
