Tomato & white bean stew

  1. Heat for 5 min
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and heat for 5 min
    • 1 chopped leek or fennel
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 100g cherry tomatoes
    • 3 bay leaves
    • ½ stock cube & 50ml water
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • salt and pepper
  4. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 1 tin cannellini beans
    • 1 tin butter beans
  5. Add chopped parsely
  6. Serve with salmon baked at 160°C for 15 mins

Slow cook

  1. Fry onion then leek and garlic
  2. Put all ingredients in slow cooker
  3. Cooked for 3 hours on high then 30 mins on low (next time try 3 hours on low)

Notes

  • Optional ingredients: red wine vinegar; sun-dried tomatoes; red pepper; veg stock; red wine; pine nuts
  • Optional herbs: basil; thyme; rosemary; oregano; paprika
