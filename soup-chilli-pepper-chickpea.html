Chilli pepper chickpea soup

  1. Fry for 10 mins till soft but not coloured
    • olive oil
    • 2 red onions chopped
    • 6 carrots diced
    • 2 red peppers chopped
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 1 chilli deseeded & chopped
  3. Add and simmer for 20 mins
    • 1.6L vegetable stock (2 salted, 2 unsalted)
    • 2 tins chickpeas drained
    • 60g pearl barley presoaked
    • 1 parmesan rind
    • 2 tsp majoram
    • ¼ tsp cumin
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 4 sticks asparagus chopped
  5. Blitz portion
  6. Mix in
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • lemon juice
    • black pepper
    • parsely chopped
  7. Garnish
    • black pepper
    • chilli flakes
