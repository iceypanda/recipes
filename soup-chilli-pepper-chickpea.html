Chilli pepper chickpea soup

  1. Fry for 10 mins till soft but not coloured
    • olive oil
    • 1 red onion chopped
    • 3 carrots diced
    • 1 red pepper chopped
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 chilli deseeded & chopped
  3. Add and simmer for 20 mins
    • 1.2L vegetable stock (2 cubes)
    • 1 tin chickpeas drained
  4. Blitz portion
  5. Mix in
    • black pepper
    • parsely chopped
