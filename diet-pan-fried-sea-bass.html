Pan-fried sea bass

Veg

  1. Fry till cooked
    • 1 tsp olive oil
    • 1 red pepper chopped
    • 1 large courgette chopped
    • 1 large red onion chopped
    • dried herbs
  2. Mix in
    • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar

Sea bass

  1. Fry low 10 mins starting skin-side down
    • 1 tsp olive oil
    • 2 sea bass fillets
    • 1 clove garlic
    • pinch chilli flakes
  2. Serve bass on top of veg
  3. Garnish with
    • lemon juice
    • fresh coriander

Serving

  • 2 portions
