Clafoutis

Batter

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C
  2. Add to bowl
    • 70g plain flour
    • ½ tsp salt
  3. Whisk in
    • 180ml (185g) milk / single cream
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Whisk in one at a time
    • 2 eggs
  5. Whisk in
    • 45g caster sugar
    • 150ml single cream

Clafoutis

  1. Butter oven dish
  2. Scatter
    • 300g blueberries / blackberries / raspberries / cherries
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds coarsely ground (optional)
  3. Pour over batter
  4. Bake for 30 mins, test with stick in middle to make sure cooked
  5. After cooling, dust with
    • 1 tbsp icing sugar

Serving

  • 6 portions

Notes

  • made: 17 Nov 2022, no coriander, used less salt, 50 mins at 180°C
  • next time: use more cream, sugar increased by 5g, all salt can be used, cook in creuset dish because larger
  • get ingredients to room temp before cooking, can warm milk & cream in microwave
    • coriander seeds
  • could add liqueur or spirit
