Oatola

  1. Mix well and spread on baking tray
    • 6 tbsp jumbo oats
    • 12 pecans chopped
    • 1 tbsp chia seeds
    • 1 tbsp linseeds
    • 2 tsp honey
  2. Cook at 150°C for 10 mins
  3. Mix and cook for 8 mins more
  4. Leave to cool
  5. Mix in
    • small handful apple chopped
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  6. Serve with
    • 3 tbsp (45g) yogurt

Serving

  • 2 portions
