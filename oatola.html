Oatola

  1. Mix well
    • 6 tbsp (52g) jumbo oats
    • 12 (23g) pecans chopped
    • 1 tbsp chia seeds
    • 1 tbsp linseeds
  2. Spread on baking tray
  3. Mix in
    • 2 tsp (10g) honey
  4. Cook at 140°C for 7 mins
  5. Mix and cook at 155°C for 7 mins more
  6. Leave to cool
  7. Mix in
    • small handful apple chopped
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  8. Serve with
    • 3 tbsp (45g) yogurt

Serving

  • 2 portions

Notes

  • Cooking reduced from 150°C for 18 mins as oatola was burnt
