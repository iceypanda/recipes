Burrata & lentils

Lentils

  1. Cook for 45 mins
    • 400g puy lentils
    • water enough to cover lentils by 7cm

Dressing

  1. Mix
    • 35ml (32.5g) olive oil
    • 8.5ml red wine vinegar
    • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
    • 1 tsp caster sugar
    • salt
    • pepper

Basil oil

  1. Blitz with enough oil to make a thin sauce
    • bunch of basil leaves
    • olive oil
    • salt

Veg

  1. Heat until soft
    • olive oil
    • 2 carrots diced
    • 3 sticks celery diced
    • 1 small onion diced
    • 3 cloves garlic grated
    • 1 tsp thyme
    • ¼ tsp salt
  2. Add
    • lentils
    • splash of water
  3. Mix in
    • dressing

Serving

  1. Burrata broken up on top of lentils
  2. Drizzle with basil oil and scatter with basil leaves

Notes

  • Original recipe: Polpo, p218
