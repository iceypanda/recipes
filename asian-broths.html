Asian broths

Recipes

  • Prawn miso hotpot
  • Kombu dashi & miso stew
  • Tofu & mushroom broth

Cooking

  • Large pot: Cover udon with boiling water
  • Large saucepan: Heat veg with broth
  • Frying pan: tempeh / prawns / mini corn / carrot batons

Notes

  • Wash veg in 1l Pyrex bowl
  • 500ml Pyrex bowl for cooked ingredients

Ingredients

  • Velvet chicken
  • Stir fry veg
  • Carrot ribboned or julienned
  • Broad beans skin removed
  • Baby corn
  • Pak choi
  • Sugar snap peas
  • Water chestnuts
  • Kale
  • Nori
  • Mangetout
  • Tenderstem broccoli
  • Daikon / radish
  • Ginger pickled

Noodles

  • Udon 150g per portion
  • Soba 100g per portion

Made

  1. Velvet chicken, prawns, tempeh, pak choi, kale, carrot, udon, Itsu veg broth
  2. Prawns sauted in sesame oil, baby corn sauted in sesame oil, pak choi, bamboo shoots, udon, Itsu veg broth
  3. Tofu, fried mushrooms, pak choi, udon noodles, broth
  4. Prawns, baby corn, sugar snap peas, ribbon noodles, Itsu veg broth
