Notes

  • Small & medium Pyrex bowls for cooked ingredients

Noodles

Veg

  1. Cook green veg in water in biggest pan
  2. Frying pan: tempeh / prawns / mini corn / carrot batons / chicken
  3. Heat veg with broth and tofu in large pan
  4. Add veg and chicken to reheat

  • Prawns

Ingredients

  • Stir fry veg
  • Baby corn
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Broad beans, skin removed
  • Broccoli
  • Carrot ribboned or julienned
  • Chilli, sliced
  • Daikon / radish
  • Kale
  • Mangetout
  • Nori, cut into squares, 1 sheet per portion
  • Pak choi, leaves separated and sliced, cook stems longer then green leaves
  • Sugar snap peas
  • Tenderstem broccoli
  • Water chestnuts

Garnish

  • Spring onions
  • Ginger pickled

Made

  2. Velvet chicken, tofu, sprouting broccoli, sugar snap peas, udon, Itsu veg broth

  4. Prawns sauted in sesame oil, baby corn sauted in sesame oil, pak choi, bamboo shoots, udon, Itsu veg broth
  5. Tofu, fried mushrooms, pak choi, udon noodles, broth
  6. Prawns, baby corn, sugar snap peas, ribbon noodles, Itsu veg broth
