Mushroom & barley

Porcini mushrooms

  1. Soak for 30 mins
    • 500ml water boiled
    • 25-30g dried porcini mushrooms
  2. Remove mushrooms and chop, discarding any hard bits
  3. Pour liquid over to soak leaving any grit behind
    • 225g pearled barley

Stew

  1. Heat for 15 mins
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 500g mixed mushrooms chopped
    • 3 carrots chopped
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add and simmer for 40 mins till barley is soft
    • 900ml vegetable stock (1 salted & 1 unsalted stock cube)
    • porcini soaking liquid
    • pearled barley soaked
    • porcini musrooms chopped
    • 3 bay leaves
  4. Add
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Serving

  • Asparagus / green beans / warmed bruschetta tomatoes
  • Fish cakes
  • 6 portions

Notes

  • For fresh mushrooms can use shiitake, cremini, portobello, chestnut, button mushrooms
