Mushroom & barley

Porcini mushrooms

  1. Soak 25g dried porcini mushrooms in 700ml hot water
  2. When soft lift mushrooms out of liquid, remove any hard bits and chop

Stew

  1. Heat for 15 mins
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 500g mixed mushrooms chopped
    • 3 carrots chopped
  2. Add 1 cup (225g) pearled barley and cook for 2 mins
  3. Add porcini mushrooms and cook for 1 min
  4. Add and simmer for 20-30 mins until barley is soft
    • 1 bay leaf
    • porcini soaking liquid (without the grit at bottom)
    • 700ml vegetable stock
  5. Add 1 tbsp soy sauce

Notes

  • Serve with asparagus / green beans / warmed bruschetta tomatoes
  • For fresh mushrooms can use shiitake, cremini, portobello, chestnut, button mushrooms
