Tofu and mushroom stir fry

  1. Cook noodles then cool and set aside
  2. Make dressing:
    • juice of 1 lime
    • chopped coriander
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  3. Drain 150g firm tofu well and pat dry
  4. Fry for few mins until tofu is golden:
    • olive oil
    • tofu
    • 150g halved chestnut mushrooms
  5. Scatter 1 tbsp sesame seeds and toast for 1 min then set aside
  6. Fry for 30 seconds:
    • oil
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 red chilli
    • 2cm chopped ginger
  7. Add and cook until soft:
    • 1 small red pepper
    • 1 tin 220g water chestnuts
  8. Add noodles and cook until hot then remove heat
  9. Stir in half the dressing
  10. Top with tofu and mushrooms
