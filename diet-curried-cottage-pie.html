Curried cottage pie

Root veg

  1. Boil till soft
    • 1½ handful (300g) parsnips peeled, diced
    • 1½ handful (300g) swede peeled, diced
  2. Mash

Filling

  1. Heat till soft
    • 4 tsp (22g) coconut oil
    • 1 (140g) red onion
    • 4 cloves garlic grated
    • 2 tsp ginger grated
  2. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • 200g soya mince
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp coriander
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes

Pie

  1. Add filling to 22x22cm dish
  2. Cover filling with mash
  3. Bake at 180°C for 10 mins

Serving

  • 4 portions
