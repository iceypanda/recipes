Pavlova - rectangle

Meringue

  1. Heat oven to 160°C
  2. Whisk meringue
  3. Scrape onto a 30x20cm baking tin lined with baking paper
  4. Level off with knife so even thickness
  5. Cook 10 mins at 160°C
  6. Turn down heat to 140°C and cook for 50 mins
  7. Turn off heat and leave to cool in oven

Pavlova

  1. Whisk 300g sweetened cream to soft peaks
  2. Spread cream evenly over meringue
  3. Pour or place fruit onto the cream

Serving

  • 200g blueberry compote plus 100g blackberries
  • Sainsbury's taste the difference cherry compote: some juice drained and rest sweetened with sugar
  • Raspberry coulis with 200g fruit
  • Opines summer berry compote
  • Pear & toffee sauce
  • Roasted pineapple
  • Lemon curd
