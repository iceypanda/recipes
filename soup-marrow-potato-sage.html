Marrow, potato & sage soup

  1. Heat in a pan without browning until soft
    • olive oil
    • 20g butter
    • 1 onion
    • 2 cloves garlic
  2. Add and low heat covered for 10 mins
    • 750g diced marrow
    • 350g diced potato
    • 4 sage leaves
  3. Add and bring to boil and simmer for 25 mins
    • 1.2l vegetable stock
    • 2 tsp sugar
    • salt & pepper
  4. Remove sage leaves
  5. Liquidise the soup
  6. When ready to servce
    • 150ml single cream
    • fresh chopped parsley
