White chocolate pots

Chocolate pots

  1. Heat until steaming
    • 150ml double cream
    • 7.5g honey
  2. Remove heat and add
    • 113g white chocolate
  3. Leave for 30 secs
  4. Whisk until combined
  5. Pour into 4 small pots
  6. Cool in fridge for 3 hours until set

Serving

  • Roasted nectarines on top
