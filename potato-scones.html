Potato scones

    1. Mash together
    • 225g potato cooked
    • 20g butter melted
  2. Mix in
    • 60g plain flour
    • ½ tsp baking powder
    • <¼ tsp salt
    • grinds of black pepper
  3. On surface press divide dough into 2 or 4
  4. Press each half into cicle
  5. Flatten circles to 1 cm thick
  6. Fry or cook in oven
    • Fry each wedge for 5 mins each side till golden
    • Cover in olive oil and cook in oven at 180°C for 15 mins

Serving

  • Smoked salmon
  • Poached egg
  • Soured cream / cream cheese & chives / Hollandaise
  • Sauted spinach
  • 2 portions
