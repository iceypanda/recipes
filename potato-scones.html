Potato scones

    1. Mash together
    • 225g potato cooked
    • 20g butter
  2. Mix in
    • 60g plain flour
    • ½ tsp baking powder
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • grinds of black pepper
  3. On surface press divide dough into 2 or 4
  4. Press each half into cicle
  5. Flatten circles to 1 cm thick
  6. Fry each wedge for 5 mins each side until golden

Serving

  • Soured cream & chives
  • Smoked salmon & lemon juice
  • Poached egg on top
  • Sauted spinach
  • 2 portions
