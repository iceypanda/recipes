Potato scones

    1. Mash together
    • 225g potato cooked
    • 20g butter melted
  2. Mix in
    • 60g plain flour
    • ½ tsp baking powder
    • <¼ tsp salt
    • white/black pepper
  3. Divide dough into 2
  4. Press each portion into circle with back of spoon
  5. Roll circles to 1 cm thick
  6. Fry scones for 5 mins each side

Serving

  • Smoked salmon
  • Poached egg
  • Soured cream / cream cheese & chives / Hollandaise / creme fraiche
  • Sauted spinach
  • 2 portions
