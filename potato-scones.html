Potato scones

    1. Mash together
    • 225g potato cooked
    • 2 tbsp butter
  2. Mix in
    • 60g plain flour
    • ½ tsp baking powder
    • ¼-½ tsp salt
    • grinds of black pepper
  3. On surface press dough into 15cm circle, 1cm thick
  4. Cut into 4 wedges
  5. Fry each wedge for 4 mins each side until golden

Serving

  • Soured cream & chives
  • Smoked salmon & lemon juice
  • 2 portions
