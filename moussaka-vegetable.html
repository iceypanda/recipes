Vegetable moussaka

Vegetables

  1. Dice
    • 2 medium aubergines
    • 3 peppers
    • 2 large corgettes
    • 2 potatoes
  2. Bake in oven at 190°C for 35 mins until tender

Lentil sauce

  1. Fry until soft
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  3. Add, bring to boil then simmer 20 mins
    • 110g green lentils precooked 15 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 150ml stock
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • ½ tsp nutmeg
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp salt

Bechamel

  1. Warm in pan then empty into bowl
    • 400ml milk
  2. Warm in pan
    • 50g butter
  3. Stir in and cook until golden
    • 50g flour
  4. Gradually add back then milk whisking continuously then cook for 5 mins
  5. Add some mixture, whisk then add back to pan
    • 2 egg yolks
  6. Add
    • 1 tsp nutmeg
    • 25g parmesan
  7. Cook for 2 more mins
  8. Allow to cool and thicken
  9. Beat to stiff peaks then fold into sauce
    • 2 egg whites
    • ½ tsp white wine vinegar

Moussaka

  1. Put layer of sauce in 9"x13" dish
  2. Add veg
  3. Pour over rest of sauce
  4. Pour over bechamel
  5. Sprinkle 40g cheese / breadcrumbs on top
  6. Bake in oven at 190°C for 45 mins or until bechamel golden (turn halfway)

Serving

  • With rice / potatoes / buckwheat
  • Cool for 20 mins before serving
  • 6 portions

Notes

  • takes 2½ hours from start to serving
  • Don't need to fully cook lentils
  • Can add parmesan to bechamel or on top of moussaka
  • Can add parmesan / feta between veg and bechamel
  • Original recipe

    • 130g flour
