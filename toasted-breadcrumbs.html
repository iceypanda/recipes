Toasted breadcrumbs

Dry bread

  • Dry on counter overnight or at 140°C in oven for 20 mins
    • 3 slices of thick sandwich bread

Breadcrumbs

  1. Tear bread into 2" pieces and pulse in blitzer till crumbs
  2. Melt in pan
    • 3 tbsp butter
  3. Cook on medium-low for 3 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 4 anchovies finely chopped
  4. Add bread crumbs and cook on medium-high for 3-8 mins stirring constantly
  5. Transfer to plate to cool
  6. Season with salt and pepper
