Smoked tofu paella

  1. Fry till soft
    • 4 tsp (18g) olive oil
    • 1 red onion chopped
    • 1 green pepper chopped
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
    • 1 tsp turmeric
  3. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 80g bulgar wheat
    • 2 tsp tomato puree
    • 1 tsp chipotle chilli flakes
    • ½ tsp black pepper
    • 500g water hot
  4. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 2 handfuls (140g) peas
    • 6 palm-size amounts (2 x 280g packs) smoked tofu chopped
  5. Add
    • juice 1 lemon
    • handful parsely

Serving

  • 4 portions
