Minestrone soup

  1. Heat for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 red onion
    • 2 sticks chopped celery
    • plenty of rosemary
  2. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 2 garlic cloves
    • 250g pancetta
  3. Add and heat for 2 mins
    • 1 swede
    • 1 carrot
    • 1 leek
  4. Simmer for 10 mins
    • 1.5l chicken stock
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  5. Add 70g spaghetti broken into 1cm pieces and heat for 6 mins
  6. Add 250g mange tout and heat for 8 mins
  7. Add 15 cherry tomatoes
