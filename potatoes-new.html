New potatoes

Portions

  • 600g - 4 people

Baked

Prep

  1. Drain potatoes and coat with
    • olive oil
  2. Drain potatoes and coat with any of
    • jerk seasoning
    • garlic salt
    • paprika

Air fryer

  1. Roast at 190°C for 22 mins

Oven

  1. Roast in oven at 200°C for 25-30 mins until golden

Notes

  • Do not need to parboil before roasting
  • Potatoes will go slimy inside if overcooked

Potato varieties

  • Celandine - not good for baking, sweet and slimy when cooked (but were parboiled first so maybe too much)
