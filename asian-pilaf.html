Asian pilaf

  1. Fry for 1 min
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 1 tsp ginger minced
    • ½ red chilli chopped
    • 1 clove garlic minced
    • splash water
  3. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • ½ red onion chopped
  4. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 3 palm-size amounts tofu
    • 2 carrots diced
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
    • pinch white pepper
  5. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 70g peas
    • 40g bulgur wheat
    • 250ml water hot
  6. Add
    • juice ½ lemon
    • 1 tbsp pomegranate seeds

Serving

  • 2 portions
