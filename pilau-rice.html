Pilau rice

Spices

  1. Grind ½ tsp coriander seeds in pestle and mortar until course powder
  2. Add 6 cardamom pods and grind until husks split and seeds lightly crushed
  3. Add and pound lightly to mix
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
    • 8 cloves
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 1 cinnamon stick
  4. Add
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • ½ tsp black mustard seeds

Rice

  1. Fry until soft
    • sunflower oil
    • 20g butter
    • 1 onion
    • 1 clove garlic
  2. Add spices and fry gently for 2 mins
  3. Add 300g basmati rice and coat with spices
  4. Add, bring to boil then simmer for 15 mins
    • 500ml veg or chicken stock
    • pinch black pepper
  5. When rice is cooked leave to stand then fluff up and leave to stand for 5 mins
