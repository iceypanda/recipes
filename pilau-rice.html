Pilau rice

Spices

  1. Grind ½ tsp coriander seeds in pestle and mortar until course powder
  2. Add 6 cardamom pods and grind until husks split and seeds lightly crushed
  3. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds and pound lightly to mix
  4. Add
    • 8 cloves
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 2 bay leaves
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • ½ tsp black mustard seeds

Rice

  1. Fry until soft
    • sunflower oil
    • 20g butter
    • 1 onion
    • 1 clove garlic
  2. Add spices and fry gently for 2 mins
  3. Add 300g basmati rice and coat with spices
  4. Add, bring to boil then simmer for 15 mins
    • 500ml veg or chicken stock
    • pinch black pepper
  5. When rice is cooked leave to cool for 10 mins
  6. Remove all cloves, pods, leaves, etc from top of rice
  7. Stir, fluff up and stir
