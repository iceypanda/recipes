Parma ham & peas pasta

  1. Start boiling linguine
  2. Cook for 2-3 mins then rinse in cold water:
    • peas
  3. Fry for 1 min:
    • 1 clove garlic
    • olive oil
  4. Add the peas and cook for 3 mins
  5. Mix peas and linguine
  6. Add:
    • mint
    • parma ham
    • parmesan
    • olive oil
