Rice Pudding

  1. Mix in jug
    • 1 pint whole milk
    • 410g evapourated milk
  2. Place in overproof dish
    • 40g caster sugar
    • 110g short grain rice
    • sprinkle of nutmeg (optional)
  3. Pour milk mixture over rice and sugar and stir well
  4. Heat in oven at 150°C for 30 mins
  5. Stir then heat for another 30 mins
  6. Stir then heat for 1 more hour

Notes

Short grain rice and pudding rice are the same

Try brown sugar instead of caster
