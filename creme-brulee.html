Crème brûlée

  • Set custard

Yolks

  1. Whisk in bowl for 1 min
    • 5 egg yolks
    • 50g golden caster sugar

Creme

  1. Heat in pan till almost boiling
    • 300ml + 150ml double cream
    • 100ml (103g) milk
    • 2 tsp vanilla bean paste
  2. Slowly pour cream mixture into egg mixture whisking constantly
  3. Sieve into jug
  4. Remove any foam
  5. Divide into 4 ramekins
  6. Cover each ramekin with foil squares with corners chopped off (size 1½ width of ramekins)
  7. Pour warm water into baking dish to halfway up sides of ramekins

Bake

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C
  2. Bake for 45 mins till custards are set, rotate tray 180° halfway
  3. Leave to cool for 90 mins (without foil)
  4. Chill in fridge for 1 hour (with foil)

Brûlée

  1. Scatter over each ramekin to cover completely
    • 1½ tsp golden caster sugar
  2. Spray with a little water
  3. Caramelise sugar with blow torch moving it round and round
  4. Leave till cool for 1 hour

Serving

  • 4 portions
