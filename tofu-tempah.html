Tofu & tempah

Tofu

  1. Press for 30 mins
  2. Cut into large chunks
  3. Marinate for 15 mins in
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • 1 clove garlic chopped
  4. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 12 mins

Notes

  • Large chunks so crispy on outside and soft in middle, small chunks cook hard and not nice
  • could also roll in sesame seeds & maple syrup before cooking

Tempah

  1. Cut into 1.5cm chunks
  2. Fry in neutral oil for 6-7 mins until golden
