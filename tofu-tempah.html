Tofu & tempah

Tofu

  1. Press for 30 mins
  2. Cut into large chunks
  3. Marinate for 15 mins in
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • 1 clove garlic chopped
  4. Dip one side in maple syrup to coat in pre-roasted sesame seeds
  5. Roast in air fryer at 180°C for 10 / 11 / 12 mins (take piece out every min to find out which best)

Notes

  • Large chunks so crispy on outside and soft in middle, small chunks cook hard and not nice
  • Need to pre-roast sesame seeds as won't roast in air fryer

Tempah

  1. Cut into 1.5cm chunks
  2. Fry in neutral oil for 6-7 mins until golden
