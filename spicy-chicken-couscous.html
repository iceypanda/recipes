Spicy chicken couscous

  1. Prepare 250g of couscous with chicken stock (~500ml)
  2. Heat in pan for 2-3 mins
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 chopped onion
  3. Add and cook for 5-6 mins
    • 2 large chicken breasts cut into strips
  4. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 1½ tbsp curry paste
    • 85g roasted cashew nuts
  5. Add
    • the couscous
    • 100g halved ready-to-eat apricots
    • 20g chopped fresh coriander
  6. Toss till hot and then serve with plain yogurt

Notes

Only need to roast cashews on one side - be very careful they don't burn!!!

Maybe 1½ tbsp curry paste. Too much is as bad as too little.

¼ tsp cayenne pepper for kick.

Lots of couscous.

Do not add too much water or else get a mush.
