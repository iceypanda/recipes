Boulangère potatoes

  • Preheat oven to 180°C

Potatoes & onions

  1. Heat for 10 mins
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 onions thinly sliced
    • 4 cloves garlic
  2. Transfer to bowl
  3. Add and mix well so each slice coated
    • 1kg potatoes unpeeled & thinly sliced
    • pepper

Baking

  1. Butter 26cm round baking dish
  2. Arrange in circles till packed in tight
    • potatoes & onions
  3. Pour over
    • 400ml water boiled
    • 1 salted stock cube
    • ½ tsp thyme
  4. Place around in pieces
    • 50g unsalted butter
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Bake at 180°C for 45 mins
  7. Remove foil
  8. Scatter
    • 75g gruyère grated
  9. Bake at 200°C for 45 mins till top is golden and crispy
