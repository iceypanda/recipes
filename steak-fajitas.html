Steak fajitas

Steak marinade

  1. Whisk to combine
    • 60ml (60g) soy sauce
    • 60ml (55g) rape seed oil
    • juice of 2 limes
    • 40g brown sugar
    • 3 cloves garlic minced
    • 1 tsp cumin seed
    • 1 tsp black pepper freshly ground
    • ½ tsp chilli powder
  2. Set aside ¼ cup marinade for the veg
  3. Seal together in plastic bag
    • remaining marinade
    • 450g bavette / skirt steak
  4. Marinade for 3-10 hours

Veg marinade

  1. Mix in bowl
    • reserved ½ cup marinade
    • 1 red pepper cut into ½" slices
    • 1 yellow pepper cut into ½" slices
    • 1 onion cut into ½" slices
  2. Refridgerate until needed

Steak

  1. Heat up cast-iron skillet
  2. Heat steak for 1 min covered
  3. Flip steak
  4. Repeat 1 min cooking and flipping until 50-55°C in middle
  5. Rest under foil for 10-15 mins

Veg

  1. Air fry like Chicken fajitas

Serving

  • tortillas warmed
  • sour cream / natural yogurt
  • cheddar cheese grated
  • Salsa
  • lettuce
  • Portions: 200g steak per person

Notes

  • Original recipe: The Food Lab, p349
