Potato, tomato & onion frittata

Potatoes

  1. Boil then mash
    • 1kg potatoes

Tomatoes

  1. Fry
    • bunch spring onions chopped
    • 4 tbsp olive oil
  2. Add
    • 250g (2 medium) tomatoes chopped
    • fresh basil / parsely chopped
  3. Mash everything till sauce

Frittata

  1. Add tomato and onions to potatoes
  2. Leave for 10 mins
  3. Add
    • 2 large eggs beaten
    • 2 tbsp pecorino / parmesan
    • black pepper
    • salt
  4. Press mixture into well-oiled frying pan
  5. Cook until crust forms
  6. Flip and cook until crust forms on other side
  7. Invert onto plate

Serving

  • Salad / watercress
  • Buratta
