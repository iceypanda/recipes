Leek & bean romesco

Sauce

  • Romesco sauce

Leeks & beans

  1. Heat till golden
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 4 cloves garlic sliced
  2. Heat till soft
    • 500g leeks sliced
  3. Add and cook for 8 mins till beans soft
    • 1 tin black beans with liquid
    • 1 tin cannellini / butter beans drained
  4. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • sauce
    • 100g water

Serving

  • Polenta / bread
