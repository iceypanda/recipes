Leek & bean romesco

Sauce

  • Blitz
    • 250g jar of roasted peppers rinsed, drained, chopped
    • 4 tbsp olive oil
    • 40g blanched hazelnuts / pinenuts
    • 2½ tbsp sun-dried tomato paste
    • 1 tbsp sherry vinegar (optional)
    • 1½ tsp paprika
    • 1 red chilli / chilli powder
    • ½ tsp salt

Leeks & beans

  1. Heat until golden
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 4 cloves garlic sliced
  2. Heat until soft
    • 500g leeks sliced
  3. Add and heat for 8 mins until beans soft
    • 1 tin black beans drained
    • 1 tin cannellini / butter beans with liquid
  4. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • sauce
    • 150g water

Serving

  • Polenta / bread
