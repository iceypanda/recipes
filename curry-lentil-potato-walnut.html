Lentil, potato & walnut curry

Lentils & potatoes

  1. Cook for 14 mins (do not drain)
    • 75g red lentils
    • 160g potatoes in small cubes
    • 480ml water

Curry

  1. Cook till soft
    • vegetable oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and toast for 2-3 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 tsp fennel seeds
    • ½ tsp ground pepper
    • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
    • ½ tsp ground cumin
    • ½ tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp poppy seeds
    • ½ tsp sage
  3. Add and cook for 6 mins
    • 2 medium tomatoes chopped
    • 2 tbsp desiccated coconut
    • 2 tbsp water
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 40g walnuts
    • 40g grated carrots
  5. Add potatoes & lentils and ½ cup of cooking water and cook for 5 mins
  6. Garnish with
    • lime juice
    • pepper flakes or cayenne
    • chopped fresh coriander
