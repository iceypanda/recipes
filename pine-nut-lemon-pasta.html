Pine nut & lemon pasta

  1. Stir in a heat proof bowl
    • 60g pine nuts crushed in pestle & mortar
    • 60g whole pine nuts
    • juice of 1 lemon
    • zest of ¾ lemon
    • 80ml olive oil
    • chopped flat leaf parsley
    • 100g Parmesan
    • ground black pepper
  2. Put bowl on top of saucepan while water is heating up for pasta
  3. When water is boiling remove boil and add
    • 350g pasta
  4. Drain pasta and add to sauce
  5. If sauce too thick add water from drained pasta
  6. Sprinkle with parsley leaves and grated parmesan
