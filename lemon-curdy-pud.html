Lemon curdy pud

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C
  2. Separate 2 large eggs
  3. Cream in mixing bowl
    • ¼ cup of butter
    • rind of 1 lemon
    • ⅓ cup of sugar
  4. Mix in
    • egg yolks
    • 6 tbsp self-raising flour
  5. Add and mix well
    • 1¼ cup of milk
    • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  6. Whisk egg whites until stiff the mix in
  7. Pour into buttered oven proof dish
  8. Set dish in roasting pan filled with ⅓ water
  9. Bake in oven for 45 mins until top is set and golden
