Blackberry & sage crumble bars

Crumble base

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 300g plain flour
    • 70g caster sugar
    • 50g light brown sugar
    • 60g oats
  2. Add and rub until like course breadcrumbs
    • 220 cold salted butter
  3. Tip ¾ of crumble into 20x20cm baking tin and press down (can line with paper)
  4. To other ¼ of crumble add 8-10 sage leaves finely chopped
  5. Chill both for 20 mins
  6. Bake base for 20 mins at 190°C until set

Fruit bars

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 350g blackberries
    • 45g caster sugar
    • 1 tsp cornflour
    • juice of ½ lemon
  2. Pour fruit over cooled base
  3. Top fruit with remaining ¼ of crumble
  4. Bake for 40-45 mins at 190°C
  5. Leave to cool for 2 hours before slicing

Serving

  • 12 portions
