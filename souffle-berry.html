Berry souffle

Ramekins

  1. Butter inside of pots and place in fridge (brush butter upwards in direction souffles will rise)
  2. Roll sugar around each pot after cooling to coat

Cornflour

  • Mix 2 tbsp cornflour with 3¼ tbsp cold water adjust for other amounts, ratio cornflour:water 1.6 by volume

Puree

  1. Blitz in bowl
    • 200g raspberries
    • 200g strawberries cored and halved (weight after coring)
  2. Sieve mixture and add to saucepan
  3. Add and bring to boil whisking continuously
    • 80g caster sugar
  4. Add
    • 1 vanilla pod cut in half and seeds scraped out (or 2 tsp extract)
    • cornflour mixture
  5. Whisk until puree starts to thicken
  6. Remove from heat, transfer to container with lid and allow to cool for 90 mins
  7. Transfer to fridge to chill for 30 mins

Souffle

  1. Whisk until soft peaks
    • 4 (165g) egg whites
    • few drops of lemon juice
  2. Add 1 tbsp at a time and whisk until stiff peaks
    • 30g caster sugar
  3. Add spoon of egg whites to puree and mix well
  4. Add puree to egg whites and combine
  5. Fill ramekins with mixture
    1. bang pots down to get even fill
    2. level off with knife
    3. clean pots
    4. run knife around rim of each pot
  6. Cook in preheated oven at 180°C for 15 mins or until risen by half, turning tray around halfway
  7. Dust with icing sugar and serve immediately

Notes

  • Used 165g egg whites and 400g fruit, too much, could reduce by ⅓ (110g whites, 260g fruit)
  • Use container with lid so skin does not form
  • Can put raspberry at bottom of each ramekin
