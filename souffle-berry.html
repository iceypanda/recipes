Raspberry & strawberry souffle

Dishes

  • Grease 4 ramekins with butter and place in fridge

Cornflour

  • mix 2½ tbsp cornflour with 4 tsp cold water

Puree

  1. Blitz in bowl
    • 250g raspberries
    • 250g strawberries cored and halved
  2. Add and blitz further
    • 100g caster sugar
  3. Sieve mixture and add to saucepan
  4. Bring to boil whisking continuously
  5. Add
    • 1 vanilla pod cut in half and seeds scraped out
    • cornflour mixture
  6. Whisk until puree starts to thicken then remove from heat and allow to cool
  7. Once cooled transfer to fridge to chill for 10 mins

Souffle

  1. Whisk
    • 4 egg whites
    • 50g caster sugar
  2. Add egg whites to puree and combine
  3. Put into ramekins and cook in preheated oven at 200°C for 10 mins or until risen by half
  4. Dust with icing sugar to serve
