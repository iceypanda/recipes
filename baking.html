Baking

  • Buttering tin helps baking paper to stick
  • Do not move sponge or open oven while cooking or middle will collapse
  • Cakes done when 98°C in middle
  • Sinking reasons: underbaking, not in long enough, temp too low
  • Preheat oven to 10°C hotter for cake then reduce when cake goes in because oven cools when opened

Flour

  • Sponge flour for baking
  • Sieve into mixture to remove lumps

Eggs

  • Eggs must be room temp when combined with butter
  • For room temp eggs take out of fridge night before
