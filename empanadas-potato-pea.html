Potato & pea empanadas

Potatoes

  1. Boil two large potatoes (750g) in skins for 10 mins
  2. Chop potatoes into 1cm cubes

Filling

  1. Heat untils seeds splutter
    • vegetable oil
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  2. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 large onion
    • 1 green chilli
    • 1 tbsp ginger
  3. Add, stir to coat potatoes and heat for 5 mins until potatoes cooked
    • potato cubes
    • 200g peas
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • 1 tsp amchoor
    • ½ tsp chilli powder
  4. Remove heat, allow to cool, then add
    • 2 tbsp lemon juice
    • chopped fresh coriander
    • ½ tsp salt
