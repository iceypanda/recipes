Chorizo, bean & potato stew

  1. Heat for 5 mins until chorizo oozes its oil
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 large onion
    • 450g thick sliced chorizo
  2. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 tsp chilli powder
    • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  3. Add ¼ cup water and pour everything into slow cooker
  4. Add to cooker and cook for 7 hours on high
    • 1 large chopped red pepper
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • 1 tin cannellini beans
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • 450g new potatoes
    • 150g green beans
  5. Serve with chopped parsely
