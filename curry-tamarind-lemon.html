Tamarind lemon curry

Pickled spring onions

  1. Fry for 2 mins until coloured
    • 7 spring onions cut in half at an angle
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  2. Remove and set aside
  3. Mix separately then drizzle over the onions
    • 3 tbsp vinegar
    • 1 tsp sugar

Chicken

  1. Add and heat for 5 mins until browned
    • 400g chicken breast in large pieces
    • vegetable oil
  2. Remove and set aside

Curry

  1. Add and heat for 10 mins until brown
    • 1 large onion chopped
  2. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 3 cloves garlic
  3. Mix in
    • 4 tbsp tamarind paste
    • 1 tbsp fish sauce
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 2 tsp chilli powder
    • 2 tsp curry powder
    • ½ tsp salt
  4. Mix in
    • zest of 1 lemon
    • juice of 1 lemon
  5. Add in
    • chicken
  6. Cook on low heat for 45 mins

Serving

  • Pickled spring onions
  • Roast potatoes

Notes

  • Original recipe: Fast Flavours, p136
