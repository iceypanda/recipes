Chilli cornbread pie

Filling

  1. Heat in pan until browned all over
    • olive oil
    • 500g beef mince
  2. Pour away liquid from mince
  3. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 large onion
    • 1 green pepper
    • 1 red pepper
    • 2 cloves garlic
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 tsp oregano
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp mild chilli powder
    • 1 tsp celery salt
  5. Add and simmer for 20 mins with lid off to reduce
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 100ml salted veg stock (½ cube)
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 tsp brown sugar
  6. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • 1½ tbsp jalapenos chopped
    • fresh coriander chopped (some stalks)
  7. Pour into oven dish
  8. Flatten with wooden spatula
  9. Preheat oven to 170°C

Topping

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 170g plain flour
    • 90g polenta
    • 2 tsp baking powder
  2. Mix in (whisk to remove lumps)
    • 215g whole milk warmed
    • 90g natural yogurt
    • 2 eggs
  3. Mix in
    • 35g butter melted
  4. Mix in
    • fresh coriander chopped (no stalks)
    • 1½ tbsp jalapenos chopped
  5. Mix in
    • 90g Red Leicester grated
    • 45g mature Cheddar grated

Pie

  1. Pour topping over filling
  2. Bake at 170°C for 35-40 mins until risen and golden (check middle with cocktail stick)
  3. Leave for 10 mins before serving

Serving

  • greens / peas / sweetcorn
  • roasted carrots / potatoes
  • 6 portions

  • timing: 60 mins until topping can go on, 1hr 35 min total
