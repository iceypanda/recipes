Chilli cornbread pie

Filling

  1. Heat in pan until browned all over
    • 500g beef mince
  2. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 large onion
    • 1 green pepper
    • 1 red pepper
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 tsp oregano
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 2 tsp chilli powder
    • 1 tsp celery salt
  4. Add and simmer for 20 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 300ml chicken stock (½ cube)
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 tsp brown sugar
  5. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • 1½ tbsp jalapenos chopped
    • fresh coriander chopped
  6. Pour into oven dish
  7. Preheat oven to 170°C

Topping

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 100g polenta
    • 200g plain flour
    • 2 tsp baking powder
    • 2 eggs
    • 250ml whole milk
    • 100ml natural yogurt
    • 50g butter melted
  2. Mix in
    • 175g red leicester / mature cheddar grated
    • 1½ tbsp jalapenos chopped
    • fresh coriander chopped

Pie

  1. Spoon topping over filling
  2. Bake at 170°C for 25-30 mins until risen and golden
  3. Leave for 5-10 mins before serving

Serving

  • soured cream
  • greens / peas
