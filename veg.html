Vegetables

Cooking methods

  • Blanching / steaming
  • Searing / sauteing
  • Braising: sear in hot oil then slow cook in pot of liquid
  • Glazing
  • Roasting / broiling

Green veg

  • large pot of water to disperse released acids and so water doesn't cool when veg added
  • high temp to denature chlorophyllase
  • lid off so acidic compounds evaporate
  • when cooked cool by running under cold water

Asparagus

  • To trim cut where stalk begins to fade to white, don't snap stem
  • Peel from below tip

Blanch

  • Boil in water for 5 mins

Roasting

  1. Mix bunch with
    • olive oil
    • lemon juice / balsamic vinegar
    • ground black pepper
    • grated parmesan / cheddar
  2. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 8 mins

Aubergine

  • 450g for large aubergine
  • slice baby aubergines

Roasting

  1. Zebra peel, because skin is tough and bitter when roasted
  2. Cut into cubes and toss in olive oil
  3. Roast in oven at 180°C for 25 mins

Pan fry

  1. Heat oil
  2. 3 mins each side, adding more oil as required

Broccoli / sprouting broccoli

  • 10 mins in steamer
  • 6 mins in boiling water (try after 6 mins)

Cabbage

Carrots

  • roast at 170-180°C for 30-40 mins
  • Pack close so cook evenly
  • Lower shelf and high sided tin so don't burn
  • Mix halfway and remove any liquid

Honey & butter roasted

  • 250g carrots
  • 1½ tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 12g butter melted
  • black pepper

Roasted with coriander & cumin seeds and mandarin

  • 250g carrots
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp coriander seeds
  • ½ tbsp honey
  • mandarin zest and juice

400g - 35 min - 170°C

Fennel

Roasting

  1. Coat slices in olive oil, balsamic vinegar and seasoning
  2. Roast in oven at 180°C for 30 mins

Fine beans

Microwave

  1. Place in bowl with enough water at bottom to cover the bottom of the bowl
  2. Cover bowl with plate
  3. 2 mins on hot then stir
  4. Repeat 2-3 times: 1 min on hot then stir and leave for 1 min to steam
  5. Knob of butter and seasoning to finish (optional)

Steamer

  • 15 mins in steamer

Leeks

Braised

  1. Simmer in pan for 15 mins
    • 1 leek outer skin removed and quartered
    • clove garlic sliced
    • ¼ tsp thyme
    • 300ml stock
    • pepper
  2. Add and heat for 5 more mins
    • 100g peas
  3. Drizzle with olive oil (optional) and season with pepper

Mushrooms

  • 250g = 15 medium mushrooms
  • ~17g per mushroom

Peas

  • 70g per portion
  • heat in microwave without extra water, stirring every 30 secs

Peppers

Roasting

  1. Cut in half and place skin side down
  2. Roast in oven at 200°C for 25 mins until skins are black (air fryer)
  3. Flip after 10 mins
  4. Leave to cool for 10 mins then remove skins

Spinach

  • remove stalks
  • 70-100g per portion

Sauted

  • 2 portions spinach : 1 clove garlic
  • don't want garlic to brown, slice not grate
  • baby spinach too watery to saute
  1. Gently fry 1 min
    • olive oil
    • garlic sliced
  2. Add and heat, spinach
  3. Remove heat
  4. Add
    • squeeze lemon juice
    • salt
    • black pepper

Spring onions

  • remove roots and top 1 inch of green

Sweetcorn

Loose

  • heat in microwave without extra water, stirring every 30 secs

Corn on cob

  • Roast: 15 mins at 180°C in air fryer (try less)
  • Blanch: 6 mins in boiling water
