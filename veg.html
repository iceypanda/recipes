Cooking methods

  • Blanching / steaming / boiling

  • Braising: sear in hot oil then slow cook in pot of liquid
  • Glazing

Green veg

  • large pot of water to disperse released acids and so water doesn't cool when veg added
  • high temp to denature chlorophyllase
  • lid off so acidic compounds evaporate
  • when cooked cool by running under cold water

Asparagus

Aubergine

  • 450g for large aubergine
  • Small aubergines best
  • Large aubergines are less dense and less flavourful and more difficult to cook
  • Less dense aubergines absorb more oil and turn mushy more easily

Roasting

  1. Zebra peel, because skin is tough and bitter when roasted
  2. Cut into cubes and toss in olive oil
  3. Roast in oven at 180°C for 25 mins

Pan fry

  1. Heat oil
  2. 3 mins each side, adding more oil as required

Broccoli / sprouting broccoli

  • 10 mins in steamer
  • 6 mins in boiling water (try after 6 mins)

Cauliflower

Fennel

Roasting

  1. Coat slices in olive oil, balsamic vinegar and seasoning
  2. Roast in oven at 180°C for 30 mins

Fine beans

Microwave

  1. Place in bowl with enough water at bottom to cover the bottom of the bowl
  2. Cover bowl with plate
  3. 2 mins on hot then stir
  4. Repeat 2-3 times: 1 min on hot then stir and leave for 1 min to steam
  5. Knob of butter and seasoning to finish (optional)

Steamer

  • 15 mins in steamer

Leeks

Mushrooms

Peas

  • 70g per portion
  • heat in microwave without extra water, stirring every 30 secs

Peppers

Roasting

  1. Cut in half and place skin side down
  2. Roast in oven at 200°C for 25 mins until skins are black (air fryer)
  3. Flip after 10 mins
  4. Leave to cool for 10 mins then remove skins

Spinach

  • remove stalks
  • 70-100g per portion

Sauted

  • 2 portions spinach : 1 clove garlic
  • don't want garlic to brown, slice not grate
  • baby spinach too watery to saute
  1. Gently fry 1 min
    • olive oil
    • garlic sliced
  2. Add and heat, spinach
  3. Remove heat
  4. Add
    • squeeze lemon juice
    • salt
    • black pepper

Spring onions

  • remove roots and top 1 inch of green

Sweetcorn

Loose

  • heat in microwave without extra water, stirring every 30 secs

Corn on cob

  • Roast: 15 mins at 180°C in air fryer (try less)
  • Blanch: 6 mins in boiling water
