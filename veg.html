Vegetables

Asparagus

  • Boil in water for 5 mins

Roasting

  1. Mix bunch with
    • olive oil
    • lemon juice / balsamic vinegar
    • ground black pepper
    • grated parmesan / cheddar
  2. Roast in air fryer at 190°C for 8 mins

Aubergine

  • 450g for large aubergine
  • slice baby aubergines

Roasting

  1. Zebra peel, because skin gets tough when roasted
  2. Cut into cubes and toss in olive oil
  3. Roast in oven at 180°C for 25 mins

Pan fry

  1. Heat oil
  2. 3 mins each side, adding more oil as required

Broccoli / sprouting broccoli

  • 10 mins in steamer
  • 6 mins in boiling water (try after 6 mins)

Cabbage

Carrots

  • Roasted baby carrots: parboil for 5 mins then roast in oven at 180°C for 30 mins

Fennel

Roasting

  1. Coat slices in olive oil, balsamic vinegar and seasoning
  2. Roast in oven at 180°C for 30 mins

Fine beans

  • 15 mins in steamer

Mushrooms

  • 250g = 15 medium mushrooms
  • ~17g per mushroom

Peas

  • 70g per portion

Peppers

Roasting

  1. Cut in half and place skin side down
  2. Roast in oven at 200°C for 25 mins until skins are black (air fryer)
  3. Flip after 10 mins
  4. Leave to cool for 10 mins then remove skins

Sweetcorn

  • 15 mins at 180°C in air fryer (try less)
  • 6 mins in boiling water
