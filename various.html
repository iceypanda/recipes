Various

Measurements

  • 1 cup = 240ml
  • ½ cup = 120ml
  • ⅓ cup = 80ml
  • ¼ cup = 60ml
  • 1 vanilla pod = 2 tsp extract

Methods

  • Heat pan before adding oil then heat oil then add food

Meals

  • Main, carb, sauce, green
  • Carbs
    • potatoes, rice, buckwheat, polenta, pearl barley, oats, quinoa
    • Wheat: pasta, bread, couscous, bulgar wheat

Densities

  • Milk 1.03 g/ml
  • Cream 0.98 g/ml
  • Olive oil 0.92 g/ml

Couscous

  • Ratio 1.4 water to couscous
    • 90g couscous, 127g water
    • 80g couscous, 112g water
  1. Add boiling water
  2. Cover and leave for 5 mins
  3. Don't use a stock cube

Fregola

  1. Soak 90g per portion in water for 2 hours
  2. Microwave until boiling
  3. Let stand for 5 mins then drain

Buckwheat

  • Ratio 2.3 water to buckwheat
    • Lunch: 70g buckwheat, 160g water
  • Presoak for 2-4 hours then rinse

Hob

  1. Bring to boil then simmer for 8-10 mins
  2. Leave in pan till all water absorbed

Microwave

  1. Add buckwheat and 200ml boiling water to Pyrex bowl
  2. Cook on high for 1 min
  3. Repeat till soft
    1. Cook on medium for 30 secs
    2. Let stand for 1 min
    3. Stir

Bulgar wheat

  • Lunch: 70g
  • Try microwaving
  1. Presoak to reduce cooking time
  2. Bring to boil then simmer for 10 mins
  3. Leave in pan for 10 mins then drain

Pearl barley

  1. Use 6:1 ratio of water:barley
  2. Add 1½ tsp miso
  3. Cook in slow cooker for 2 hours on high

Fish fingers / Scampi

Air fryer

  1. Fish fingers at 190°C for 15 mins on roast

Serving

  • Lemon wedges with scampi

Fries

  • 120g fries portion
  • Air fry on roast for 10-12 mins, shaking every few mins

Hot chocolate

  • 35g chocolate from pouch
  • 22-25g Whittards hot chocolate, 2 tbsp

Squash

  • 40g double concentrate in water bottle
