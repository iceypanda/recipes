Tomato & mozzarella gnocchi bake

  • Preheat oven to 180°C
  1. Heat for 5 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 medium onion chopped
  2. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic
  3. Add and heat for 2 mins
    • basil stalks finely chopped
    • 50g sun-dried tomatoes
  4. Add and heat for 2 mins
    • 200g cherry tomatoes
  5. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 200ml water
  6. Add
    • 500g gnocchi
    • bunch basil leaves chopped
    • salt
    • pepper
  7. Pour into oven dish
  8. Scatter over top
    • 150g mozzarella in chunks
  9. Bake at 180°C for 25 mins until gnocchi tender

Serving

  • Chicken pieces
  • 2 portions
