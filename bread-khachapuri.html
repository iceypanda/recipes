Khachapuri

Dough

  1. Mix in bowl and warm to 45°C
    • 130g milk
    • 125ml water
  2. Mix in
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 5g yeast
  3. Mix in
    • 190g strong bread flour
    • ½ tsp salt
  4. Add gradually to make wet dough
    • 190g strong bread flour
  5. Knead for 5 mins
  6. Leave to prove for 40 mins

Cheese

  1. Mix
    • 200g mozzarella grated
    • 100g feta crumbled
    • 1 egg

Bread

  1. Divide dough into 4 on floured surface
  2. Roll each piece into 25x12cm oval
  3. Put same amount of cheese into centre of each piece with wide rim around edges
  4. Smooth top of each piece
  5. Fold edges of dough inward to make thick crust border
  6. Pinch ends to create boats
  7. Leave to prove for 15 mins
  8. Bake in oven at 220°C for 10-12 mins until crusts golden
  9. Remove from oven
  10. Make well in centre of each bread with egg and fill with 1 egg yolk
  11. Return to oven for 2½ mins to warm yolk but not set

Serving

  • Chilli flakes / chilli sauce
  • 4 portions
