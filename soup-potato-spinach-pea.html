Potato, spinach & pea soup

  1. Heat for 1 min until seeds pop
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 2 tsp black mustard seeds
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 1 large onion
    • 1 green chilli
    • 5cm grated ginger
    • 2 tbsp korma curry paste
    • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  3. Add 800ml boiling water and bring to boil
  4. Simmer for 20 mins
    • 900g new potatoes
    • 400ml coconut milk
    • 50g rice
  5. Add 225g frozen peas and simmer for 5 mins
  6. Stir in 225g spinach (defrosted if frozen)

Notes

  • Added rice, might need to add more water
