Blueberry compote

Blueberry

  1. Cook over medium heat for 10 mins
    • 200g blueberries
    • 2 tbsp golden caster sugar
    • 1 tbsp water
  2. Add lemon juice to taste

Blueberry & blackberry

  1. Cook over medium heat for 10 mins
    • 200g blueberries
    • 100g blackberries
    • 3 tbsp golden caster sugar
    • 1 tbsp water
  2. Add lemon juice to taste

Notes

  • lemon juice important for taste
  • water helps the blueberries to cook and dissolves the sugar
  • can add cornflour or more sugar to thicken if necessary
