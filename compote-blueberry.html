Blueberry compote

  1. Cook over medium heat for 10 mins
    • 200g blueberries
    • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  2. Add lemon juice to taste

Notes

  • can keep ⅕ of blueberries to 2 mins from end so not all broken down
  • can add cornflour or more sugar to thicken
  • can add water to thin
  • Alternative ratios
    • 125g blueberries
    • 100g granulated sugar
    • 200ml water
