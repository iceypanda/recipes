Plums & ginger

  1. Heat in pan till bubbling
    • 12 plums halved & destoned
    • 80g caster sugar
    • 250ml water
    • 2 star anise
    • 4 cloves
  2. Lower heat to simmer
  3. Add
    • 3 lumps preserved ginger
    • 4 tbsp preserved ginger syrup

Serving

  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Ginger cake / toasted brioche
