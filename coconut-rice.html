Coconut rice salad

  1. Soak 250g jasmine rice in water for 30 mins
  2. Soak 80g raisins in warm water for ? mins
  3. Add to pot covered by 3cm of water
    • rice
    • 400ml coconut milk
    • 325ml milk
  4. Cook with lid on
    • Bring to boil and cook on high heat for 5 mins
    • Turn heat to lowest setting for 8 mins
    • Turn off heat and leave till rice is soft, about 10 mins
  5. Mix in
    • raisins
    • 2 grated carrots
    • 2 finely sliced spring onions
  6. Add to taste
    • juice 1 lime
    • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  7. Add 80g roasted cashew nuts

Notes

Serve with honey chicken (chicken fried in pan with honey poured on top with lemon or lime)

Very stodgy, very rich
