Sticky buns

Buttermilk

    Whisk
    • 180ml milk
    • ¾ lemon juice / white vinegar
  2. Warm in microwave

Dough

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 3 large eggs
  2. Add and whisk in
    • buttermilk
    • ⅓ cup light brown sugar
    • 7g packet dried yeast
    • 1 tsp salt
  3. Gradually whisk in
    • 90ml (82g) unsalted butter melted
  4. Mix in
    • 720g flour
  5. Knead dough for 2 mins to combine
  6. Leave to rise in bowl for 2 hours at room temperature till volume doubled

Baking dish sauce

  1. Heat in pan until sugar disolved and mixture bubbling
    • 60ml (55g) unsalted butter
    • ⅔ cup light brown sugar
  2. Mix in
    • 45ml (44g) single cream
    • 1 cup pecans toasted, chopped (optional)
  3. Pour evenly over base of 13"x9" baking dish

Cinnamon sugar

  • Mix
    • ⅔ cup light brown sugar
    • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon

Buns

  1. Roll out dough to 16"x12" with short edge towards you
  2. Brush with leaving 1" border at top
    • 27g unsalted butter melted
  3. Sprinkle evenly with cinnamon sugar
  4. Roll up tightly using bench scraper to help
  5. Put seam side down and even out shape
  6. Cut into 12 slices
  7. Arrange in sauced baking dish and cover with cling film
  8. Leave for 2 hours till volume doubled
  9. Cook in preheated oven at 180°C for 30 mins rotating tray once
  10. Rest for 5 mins
  11. Invert buns onto rack and cover with scraped out sauce

Notes

  • Original recipe: The Food Lab, p170
