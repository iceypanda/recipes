Thai steamed fish

  1. Place 300g trout fillets on square of foil
  2. Scatter with
    • knob of ginger
    • 1 clove of garlic
    • 1 red chilli
    • grated zest and juice of lime
  3. Place 3 quartered pak choi next to fish and drizzle with
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  4. Seal foil leaving air above fish for steam to circulate
  5. Cook for 15 mins in steamer
