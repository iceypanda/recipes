Lemon treacle sponge

Bowl

  1. Grease 1100ml Pyrex bowl with 10g butter
  2. Add
    • 200g golden syrup

Flour

  1. Measure out
    • 125g self-raising flour sieved
    • ¼ tsp salt

Pudding

  1. Cream together with wooden spoon
    • 125g unsalted butter very soft
    • 125g golden caster sugar
    • zest 1 lemon
  2. Beat in
    • 1 large egg
  3. Beat in 1 tbsp flour
  4. Beat in
    • 1 large egg
  5. Beat in remaining flour 1 tbsp at a time
  6. Beat in
    • 30g lemon juice
  7. Gently pour the batter into the bowl with the syrup so they do not mix
  8. Tightly double wrap the entire bowl with cling film
  9. Cook in microwave at either
    • 750W for 6:00 mins
    • 600W for 7:30 mins
  10. Leave to stand for 2 mins
  11. Remove cling film
  12. Place plate over bowl and flip
  13. Shake till pudding drops out

Notes

  • Made: 12 Dec 2022
  • Eggs and butter at room temperature
  • Original recipe
