Red lentil daal

Lentils

  • Soak for 1-3 hours
    • 500g water
    • 250g red lentils

Daal

  1. Cook for 10 mins
    • 1 small onion
  2. Cook for 5 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 tsp chopped ginger
    • 1 tsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp tumeric
  3. Cook till lentils done, 30 mins
    • 250g red lentils
    • 500g water
    • 300ml stock (1 salted cube)
  4. Remove heat
  5. Add to taste
    • lemon juice

Notes

  • Add more water if too thick
  • Do not need a lot of heat, red lentils easily cook to mush
