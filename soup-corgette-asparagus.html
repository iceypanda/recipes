Corgette, asparagus & pea soup

  1. Cook on low heat for 10 mins
    • olive oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • ½ tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  3. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 diced corgette
  4. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 250g chopped asparagus
  5. Add and bring to boil, then turn off heat
    • 300g defrosted peas
    • 750ml veg stock
  6. Blend half of soup
