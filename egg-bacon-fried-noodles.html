Egg bacon fried noodles

Sauce

  1. Mix well
    • 3 tbsp soy sauce
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
    • 20g ginger minced
    • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tbsp brown sugar
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • ½ tsp sesame seeds
    • pinch ground seaweed (optional)
    • pinch chilli flakes

Noodles

  1. Fry until crispy
    • vegetable oil
    • 4 rashers streaky bacon cut into 1cm strips
  2. Remove from heat to allow fat to seep out
  3. Crack into pan, break up and leave for 30 secs
    • 2 eggs
  4. Take off heat and continue to break up eggs
  5. Cover with
    • 2 portions udon noodles cooked
  6. Return to heat and don't mix
  7. Cover noodles with
    • 2 pak choi finely chopped
  8. Sprinkle some sauce over
  9. Sprinkle over noodles
    • spring onions finely sliced at an angle
  10. Sprinkle over noodles
    • 50g beansprouts
  11. Mix by tossing
  12. Sprinkle over more sauce (not too much so soggy) and cook 1 min more
  13. Remove heat and sprinkle over little more sauce

Serving

  • 2 portions

Notes

  • Mix sauce while bacon cooks
  • Chop pak choi and spring onions while pan contents cook
  • Original recipe
