Fish, potato & white wine soup

Potatoes

  • Heat until just soft
    • 300g (after peeling) potatoes cubed
    • 400ml water
    • ½ veg salted stock cube
    • ½ veg unsalted stock cube

Soup

  1. Heat until soft (without browning)
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 medium onion chopped
    • 1 leek sliced
    • 1 stick celery chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic
  2. Add and simmer for 5 mins
    • 187ml white wine
    • strip of orange peel (optional)
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • ¼ tsp paprika
    • 2 bay leaves
  3. Add and cook to simmer
    • potatoes & stock
  4. Add and cook for 8 mins
    • 450g fish fillets cubed
    • prawns when 3 mins left (optional)
    • ½ tsp saffron (optional)
  5. Remove orange peel and bay leaves
  6. Mix in
    • 150ml single cream
  7. Blitz portion
  8. Serve with
    • black pepper

Serving

  • 4 portions
  • Bread

Notes

  • Fish: hake / monkfish / cod / haddock
  • Double up and use smoked fish, unsmoked fish and prawns
  • Can swap 100g fish for prawns
  • Original recipe
