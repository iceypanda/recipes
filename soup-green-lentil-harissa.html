Green lentil & harissa soup

  1. Cook for 10 mins until softened
    • olive oil
    • 1 large onion
    • 1 carrot diced
    • 2 sticks celery diced
    • 2 parsnips diced
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 tsp ground coriander
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 150g green lentils presoaked
    • 1l stock
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 2 leeks halved lengthways and diced
    • 1 tin chickpeas
  5. Add and cook until kale wilted
    • 80g kale leaves stalks discarded
    • 3 tbsp harissa
  6. Season with
    • coriander chopped
    • juice ½ lemon
    • salt
    • black pepper cracked
  7. Blitz small amount

Serving

  • 6 portions, 60 mins
  • with flatbreads / rice / fregola
