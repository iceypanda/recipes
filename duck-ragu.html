Duck ragu

Duck

  1. Cook 4 duck legs in oven
  2. Shred meat with 2 forks

Sauce

  1. Fry until soft
    • 1 onion
    • 2 celery sticks
  2. Fry for 2 mins
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  3. Add and reduce by half
    • 360ml red wine
    • 2 bay leafs
    • ¼ oregano
    • ¼ thyme
    • strip of lemon zest (optional)
  4. Add and simmer for 15 mins
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • duck
    • salt

Serving

  • with parpadelle

Notes

  • let duck get to room temperature before cooking
