Fish pie

  • Preheat oven to 200°C

Fish

  1. Saute in pan for 5 mins
    • 25g butter
    • 1 large leek
  2. Sprinkle and stir constantly for 1 min
    • 2 tbsp plain flour
  3. Gradually stir in until smooth and thick
    • 125g full-fat cream cheese
    • 300ml full-fat milk
  4. Remove from heat and add
    • 450g haddock skinned chopped
    • 125g prawns cooked peeled
  5. Transfer to oven dish and cook for 25 mins at 200°C until fish flakes

Potatoes

  1. Boil until cooked
    • 675g potatoes
    • large pinch saffron threads
  2. Drain well and mash with
    • 25g butter melted
    • 3 tbsp creme fraiche
    • ¼-½ tsp salt

Pie

  1. Cover fish with mash
  2. Sprinkle mash with
    • breadcrumbs (optional)
  3. Grill on hot until golden brown
