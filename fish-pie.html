Fish pie

  • Preheat oven to 180°C

Potatoes

  1. Boil until cooked
    • 675g (peeled) potatoes
    • large pinch saffron threads
  2. Drain well and mash with
    • 25g butter melted
    • 3 tbsp creme fraiche
    • ¼ tsp salt

Sauce

  1. Saute on low for 5 mins taking care not to brown
    • 25g butter
    • 1 large leek
  2. Sprinkle and stir constantly for 1 min
    • 2 tbsp plain flour
  3. Stir in gradually until mixed well
    • 300ml full-fat milk
  4. Stir in gradually until smooth and mixed well
    • 125g full-fat cream cheese
    • 1 tsp Dijon / English mustard (optional)
    • ¼ tsp salt
  5. Remove heat

Pie

  1. Add to oven dish
    • 220g cod skinned chopped
    • 220g smoked haddock skinned chopped
    • 125g prawns cooked peeled
  2. Pour sauce over fish
  3. Cover with foil or lid
  4. Cook at 180°C for 15 mins
  5. Stir in
    • 2 tbsp creme fraiche
  6. Cover pie filling with mash and use fork to even out surface
  7. Sprinkle mash with optional topping
    • breadcrumbs
    • grated cheese
  8. Cook at 180°C for 20 mins till top golden

Serving

  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Careful not to overheat in oven or sauce will split
  • Can use ramekins for indidual portions
  • Prep veg and potatoes before fish
  • Can use cream cheese with garlic and herbs
