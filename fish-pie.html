Fish pie

  • Preheat oven to 180°C

Potatoes

  1. Boil until cooked
    • 675g (peeled) potatoes
    • large pinch saffron threads
  2. Drain well and mash with
    • 25g butter melted
    • 3 tbsp creme fraiche
    • ¼ tsp salt

Fish

  1. Saute in pan for 5 mins
    • 25g butter
    • 1 large leek
  2. Sprinkle and stir constantly for 1 min
    • 2 tbsp plain flour
  3. Gradually stir in until smooth and mixed well
    • 125g full-fat cream cheese
    • 300ml full-fat milk
    • 1 tsp Dijon / English mustard (optional)
    • ¼ tsp salt
  4. Remove from heat and transfer to oven dish
  5. Add
    • 220g cod skinned chopped
    • 220g smoked haddock skinned chopped
    • 125g prawns cooked peeled
  6. Transfer to oven dish and cover with foil or lid
  7. Cook for 15 mins at 180°C

Pie

  1. Stir in
    • 2 tbsp creme fraiche
  2. Cover pie filling with mash and use fork to even out surface
  3. Sprinkle mash with optional topping
    • breadcrumbs
    • grated cheese
  4. Cook for 15-20 mins at 180°C till top golden

Serving

  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Can use ramekins for indidual portions
  • Prep veg and potatoes before fish
  • Can use cream cheese with garlic and herbs
