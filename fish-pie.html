Fish pie

  1. Saute for 5 mins
    • 25g butter
    • 1 large leek
  2. Sprinkle over 2 tbsp plain flour stirring constantly for 1 min
  3. Gradually stir in until smooth and thick
    • 125g full-fat cream cheese
    • 300ml full-fat milk
  4. Remove from heat and add
    • 450g skinned chopped haddock
    • 125g cooked peeled prawns
  5. Cook for 2 hours on high in slow cooker until fish flakes
  6. Boil until cooked
    • 675g potatoes
    • large pinch saffron threads
  7. Drain well and mash with
    • 25g butter
    • 3 tbsp creme fraiche
  8. Cover fish with mash and grill on hot until golden brown
