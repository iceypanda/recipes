Black bean and sweet potato quesadillas

  1. Bake on parchment paper in oven for 45 mins
    • 1 large peeled, quartered sweet potato
  2. Mash potato with
    • 1 cup salsa
    • 1 cup cooked brown rice
    • 1 cup spinach
  3. Add then heat thoroughly
    • 1 tin black beans
    • 1 tin refried beans
    • 1 cup spinach
    • ¼ tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp chilli powder
    • ¼ tsp onion powder
  4. Place tortilla in pan, spread mixture and add jalapenos
  5. Cover with another tortilla and cook each side for 3 mins
