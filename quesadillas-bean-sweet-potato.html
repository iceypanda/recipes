Black bean and sweet potato quesadillas

Sweet potato

  1. Bake on parchment paper in oven for 45 mins
    • 1 large sweet potato peeled, quartered
  2. Mash potato

Filling

  1. Mix with potato then heat thoroughly
    • sweet potato mashed
    • 1 cup salsa
    • 1 cup brown rice cooked
    • 1 tin black beans
    • 1 tin refried beans
    • 1 cup spinach
    • ¼ tsp cumin
    • ¼ tsp chilli powder
    • ¼ tsp onion powder
