Cream, white wine & lemon sauce

  • Preheat oven to 140°C
  1. Melt in small pan
    • 60ml unsalted butter
  2. Add and cook for 30 secs
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
  3. Add and cook for 2-3 mins
    • 2 tbsp white wine
  4. Add and cook until sauce thickens slightly
    • 120ml double cream
  5. Remove heat
  6. Stir in
    • 1-2 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tbsp parsely chopped
    • salt to taste

Serving

  • Salmon
  • 4 portions
