Cream, white wine & lemon sauce

  1. Melt in small pan
    • 60ml unsalted butter
  2. Add and cook for 30 secs
    • 1½ cloves garlic minced
  3. Add and cook for 2-3 mins
    • 2 tbsp white wine
  4. Add and cook till sauce thickens slightly
    • 120ml single / double cream
  5. Remove heat
  6. Stir in to taste
    • salt
  7. Stir in to taste
    • up to 1½ tbsp lemon juice
  8. Stir in
    • 1 tbsp parsely chopped

Serving

  • Salmon
  • 4 portions
