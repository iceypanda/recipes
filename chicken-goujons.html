Chicken goujons

  1. Blitz until smooth
    • 1 large egg
    • 15g fresh basil
    • 20g parmesan grated
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 lemon zest and juice
  2. Pour over 400g chicken breast cut into 1cm strips
  3. Leave to marinate in fridge
  4. Coat all chicken pieces in breadcrumbs
  5. Air fry for 15 mins at 200°C

Notes

  • Air fried goujons ok but more oil might make crispier
