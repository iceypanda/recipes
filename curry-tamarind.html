Tamarind curry

  1. Cook until soft
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1 large onion chopped
    • 1 pepper chopped
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 10g ginger grated
    • 3 cloves garlic grated
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
    • ½ tsp cumin
  3. Add and simmer to reduce for 20 mins
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 1 tbsp tamarind sauce
    • 1 tbsp fish sauce

Serving

  • Rice
  • Naan
  • 3 portions
