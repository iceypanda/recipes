Aubergine & lentil bake

Aubergine

  1. Brush with olive oil
    • 1 aubergine thinly sliced
  2. Cook in oven at 180°C for 10 mins

Lentils

  1. Fry for 5 mins
    • 1 red onion chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic chopped
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 2 tins green lentils drained
    • 400g passata
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • pinch dill

Tofu

  1. Blitz
    • 3 palm-size amounts silken tofu
    • splash soya/pea milk
    • ½ tsp nutmeg

Bake

  1. Layer lentils and aubergine in baking dish, starting and finishing with aubergine layer
  2. Pour over tofu
  3. Sprinkle over
    • 12 hazelnuts chopped
    • black pepper
  4. Bake at 160°C for 20 mins
