Chickpea & lentil daal

  1. Fry for 8 mins
    • oil
    • 1 large onion
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 tsp ground cumin
    • 2 tsp ground coriander
    • 2 tsp ground ginger
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1 tsp chilli flakes
    • splash of water
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
  4. Add and simmer for up to 18 mins
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 300g red lentils
    • 750ml water with 1 stock cube
  5. Add 150g spinach for few mins until wilted

Serving

  • rice
  • chopped coriander

Notes

  • if use frozen spinach then make sure defrosted before adding or lentils will go to mush
  • don't overcook lentils or will go to mush, texture is good
