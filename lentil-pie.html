Lentil pie

Prep

  1. Cook 350g green or brown lentils 20 mins and drain
  2. Cook 350g spinach for 2 mins until wilted then squeeze all liquid out
  3. Cook and then mash
    • 750g potatoes
    • 750g sweet potatoes
    • 50g butter
    • small bunch of parsley

Pie

  1. Heat for 15 mins
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 medium onions
  2. Add and heat for 10 mins
    • 150g diced carrots
    • 1 diced celery
    • 2 cloves of garlic
  3. Add and heat for 10 mins
    • 1 tin of tomatoes
    • 3 bay leaves
    • leaves from 8 springs of parsley
    • leaves from 6 springs of thyme
  4. Add 500ml vegetable stock bring to boil then lower to simmer
  5. Add
    • lentils
    • spinach
  6. Transfer to deep baking dish 24 x 28 cm
  7. Spoon mash on top and bake for 20 mins at 200°C

Comments

For mash can also include parsnips and swede and vary amounts of each
