Pork loin steaks

Seared

  1. Pat dry
  2. Season well
  3. Cook on fat side to render a little
  4. Cook 5 mins each side

Air fry

  1. Pat dry
  2. Spread with butter, dijon mustard and honey
  3. Cook in air fryer at 180°C for 9 mins
  4. Spread with whole grain mustard before serving

Pork chops

  1. Trim skin and fry in pan with oil for 1 min each side
  2. Cook pan in oven at 180°C for 15 mins

Serve with

  • apple sauce

Gammon

  1. Cook pan in air fryer at 190°C for 16-18 mins

Serve with

  • dry-fried pineapple rings
