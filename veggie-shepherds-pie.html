Veggie shepherd's pie

Veg mince

  • Fry in pan for 15-30 mins
    • olive oil
    • 250-300g veg mince

Mash

  • Make mash from 1.7kg of unpeeled potatoes

Sauce

  1. Melt in pan
    • 25ml olive oil
    • 25g butter
  2. Heat gently for 15 mins
    • 2 onions chopped
    • 2 carrots diced
    • 2 parsnips diced
    • 2 celery chopped
  3. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 200g mushrooms sliced
    • 4 cloves garlic chopped
  4. Add and simmer for 30-40 mins until lentils are soft
    • 500ml stock (1½ low-salt stock cubes)
    • 1.87cl red wine
    • 100g green lentils presoaked
    • 200g passata
    • 4 bay leaves
    • 1½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 tsp thyme
    • ½ tsp oregano
    • ¼ tsp dill
    • ¼ tsp allspice
  5. Remove heat and add
    • cooked veg mince
    • 1 tsp soy sauce
    • seasoning to taste

Pie

  1. Divide sauce into casserole dishes
  2. Cover with mash and pattern top with fork
  3. Scatter with
    • 40g cheddar cheese grated
  4. Bake at 170°C in preheated oven for 30 mins until top is golden

Serving

  • 6+ portions

Notes

  • Made: 19 Sep 2022, 1.4kg unpeeled potatoes not enough, Feb 2024
  • Try: 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Mince cooked separate to sauce so does not go too soft, will soak up liquid when added to stew
