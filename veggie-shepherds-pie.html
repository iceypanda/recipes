Veggie shepherd's pie

Veg mince

  • Fry in pan for 15 mins
    • olive oil
    • 400g veg mince

Mash

  • Make mash from 2kg of potatoes (sounds like a lot)
  • Season mash and add butter and milk

Sauce

  1. Melt in pan
    • 50g butter
  2. Heat for 15 mins
    • 2 onions chopped
    • 4 carrots diced
    • 1 celery chopped
  3. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 200g mushrooms sliced
    • 4 cloves garlic chopped
  4. Add and simmer for 30-40 mins until lentils soft (if sauce too dry add more water, if too watery simmer without lid)
    • 600ml stock (1½ stock cubes)
    • 187ml red wine
    • 100g green lentils presoaked
    • 3 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 1 tbsp thyme
  5. Remove from heat and add
    • cooked veg mince
    • seasoning to taste

Pie

  1. Divide sauce into casserole dishes
  2. Top with mash
  3. Scatter with
    • 50g Cheddar cheese grated
  4. Bake at 170°C in preheated oven for 30 mins until top is golden

Notes

  • mince cooked separate to sauce so does not go too soft
