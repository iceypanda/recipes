Guacamole

Salsa

  1. Leave in bowl for 20 mins
    • ½ red onion
    • juice of ¼ lime/lemon
    • splash white wine vinegar
  2. Add and blitz in food processor
    • 2 tomatoes deseeded & chopped
    • ½ red chilli
    • fresh coriander (to taste)
    • pinch chilli flakes
    • pinch oregano ground up
  3. Add to taste
    • salt
  4. Strain to remove excess liquid

Guacamole

  1. Mash up
    • 1-2 avocado chopped
  2. Add as needed
    • salsa
  3. Add to taste
    • salt

Notes

  • Best served room temperature, not nice fridge cold
  • Salt very important
