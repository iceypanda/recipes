Mushroom pate

Mushrooms

  1. Fry for 10 mins
    • handful mushrooms chopped
    • 3 shallots chopped
    • 2 tsp olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 clove garlic chopped
    • 1 sprig fresh (½ tsp dried) rosemary chopped
  3. Add
    • black pepper
  4. Leave to cool

Pate

  1. Blitz
    • 6 tbsp (108g) full-fat cottage cheese
    • mushrooms
  2. Transfer to 2 ramekins
  3. Garnish
    • small cluster rosemary leaves
    • black pepper

Serving

  • Wholemeal tortilla/pitta
  • 2 portions
