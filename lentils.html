Lentils

General

  • Soak in saucepan so don't need extra bowl
  • 3 hours soaking may be enough
  • Wash and rinse thoroughly after soaking
  • To cook fill saucepan to 2cm above lentils
  • Simmer until soft, may not need full cooking time

Red lentils

  1. Simmer for 30 mins to cook

Green lentils

  1. Soak in to cold water for 12 hours
  2. Simmer for 20 mins to cook

Puy lentils / lentilles vertes

Type of green lentil

  1. Simmer for 20 mins to cook

Brown lentils

  1. Soak in to cold water for 12 hours
  2. Simmer for 45 mins to cook

Yellow split peas

  1. Soak in to cold water for 12 hours
  2. Simmer for 1 hour to cook
