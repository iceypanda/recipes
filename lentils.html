Lentils

Amount

  • 80g dry = 1 cooked tin
  • Red lentils triple in weight when cooked

Soaking

  • Soak in saucepan so don't need extra bowl
  • 12 hours soaking overnight ideal
  • 3 hours soaking probably enough if not done overnight
  • Red lentils need less soaking
  • Wash and rinse thoroughly after soaking

Cooking

  • Don't add salt while cooking or lentils will harden
  • To cook fill saucepan to 2cm above lentils
  • Simmer till soft, may not need full cooking time

Types

Red lentils

  1. Simmer for 30 mins to cook

Green lentils

  1. Simmer for 20 mins to cook

Puy lentils / lentilles vertes

  • Type of green lentil
  1. Simmer for 20 mins to cook

Brown lentils

  1. Simmer for 45 mins to cook

Yellow split peas

  1. Simmer for 1 hour to cook
