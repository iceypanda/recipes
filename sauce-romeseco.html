Romesco sauce

  • 4 tbsp oil if using sun-dried tomatoes instead of paste
  • Peppers will add salt if brined

Sauce

  1. Blitz
    • 250g (4) jar of roasted peppers rinsed, drained, chopped
    • 3 tbsp (42g) olive oil
    • 40g hazelnuts / pinenuts roasted
    • 2½ tbsp sun-dried tomato paste / 25g sundried tomatoes
    • 2 tsp sherry vinegar
    • 1½ tsp paprika
    • 1 red chilli / ½ tsp chilli flakes
  2. Add gradually to taste
    • salt
