Pastry pies

Pastry

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 125ml olive oil (114g)
    • 125ml warm water
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Gradually work in 375g plain flour in make dough that does not stick, start with fork then use hands
  3. Knead briefly

Pies

  1. Beat 2 egg yolks
  2. Divide dough into 6 pieces
  3. Roll out thin and cut 10cm circles with pastry cutter (dough is oily so don't need more flour)
  4. Fill each circle with generous tsp of filling
  5. Paint edges with yolk to help pastry stick
  6. Fold over circle to make half-moon
  7. Seal edges with fork prongs
  8. Roll any offcuts into ball and cut more circles
  9. Place pies of baking paper
  10. Brush pies with egg yolk
  11. Bake in preheated oven at 180°C for 30 mins until golden

Notes

  • can use small bowl to make circles for larger pies
  • Use pastry sticking to chopping board to maintain shape while filling
  • Use egg whites for meringue
