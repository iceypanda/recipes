Pastry pies

Pastry 1

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 125ml olive oil (114g)
    • 125ml warm water
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Gradually work in 375g plain flour in make dough that does not stick
  3. Knead briefly

Pastry 2

  1. Mix and leave for 5-10 mins
    • 1 sachet yeast
    • 170ml 43°C water
    • 1 tsp caster sugar
  2. Mix in bowl
    • 320g plain flour
    • 1 tbsp milk powder
    • ¾ tsp salt
    • ⅛ turmeric
  3. Add yeast mixture and mix
  4. Add and mix for 5 mins
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 3 tbsp sunflower oil
  5. Remove from bowl, add olive oil then coat dough in oil
  6. Leave for 1 hour or put in bread maker

Pies

  1. Beat 2 egg yolks
  2. Divide dough into 6 pieces
  3. Roll out thin and cut 10cm circles with pastry cutter (dough is oily so don't need more flour)
  4. Fill each circle with generous tsp of filling
  5. Paint edges with yolk to help pastry stick
  6. Fold over circle to make half-moon
  7. Seal edges with fork prongs
  8. Roll any offcuts into ball and cut more circles
  9. Place pies on baking paper
  10. Brush pies liberally with egg yolk
  11. Bake in preheated oven at 180°C for 30 mins until golden

Notes

  • Can use small bowl to make circles for larger pies
  • Use pastry sticking to chopping board to maintain shape while filling
  • Use egg whites for meringue
