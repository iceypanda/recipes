Chicken korma

Chicken

  • Marinate for 1 hour
    • 600g chicken breast trimmed & cubed
    • juice 1 lemon
    • 3 tsp garlic & ginger
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp coriander
    • ½ tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp salt
    • 100g yogurt
    • tandoori marinade

Curry

  1. Fry till light brown
    • sunflower oil
    • 2 onions chopped
  2. Add
    • ½ tsp garam masala
    • ½ tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
    • 3 cardamom pods
    • 1 bay leaf
  3. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • chicken
  4. Add and cook till soft
    • 400g potatoes
  5. Add to taste
    • honey

Notes

  • Potatoes cook in water released by the chicken
  • Original recipe: Rosanna's mum
