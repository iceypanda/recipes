Veg biryani

Rice prep

  1. Wash
    • 280g basmati rice
  2. Soak for 30 mins in water
  3. Drain

Cooking rice

  1. Bring to boil
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 5 cloves
    • 5 cardamom pods
    • cinnamon stick
    • ½ tsp slightly heaped caraway seeds
    • 1 tsp salt
  2. Add
    • rice
    • boiling water to cover rice
  3. Cook 7 mins till rice almost done
  4. Drain rice and rinse under cold water

Veg

  1. Heat in pot for 2 mins
    • veg oil
    • 5 cloves
    • 5 cardamom pods
    • 7g garam masala
    • 7g curry powder
    • 2g caraway seeds
  2. Add and cook covered for 15 mins
    • 2 carrots diced
    • 2 medium potatoes / sweet potatoes diced
    • ½ butternut squash diced
    • 2 green peppers diced
    • 1 small onion diced
    • 2 tsp garlic ginger paste
    • cinnamon stick from rice
  3. Add
    • 200g yogurt
    • 2 tbsp coriander leaves chopped
    • 5g coriander
    • 2g paprika
    • 2g dill
    • 1g dried mint
  4. Add and bring to boil
    • 100ml water
    • 65g cashew raw (optional)
  5. Remove heat
  6. Add in even layer
    • rice
  7. Spinkle over
    • 100ml coconut milk
    • saffron (optional)
  8. Cook high for 5 mins
  9. Cook low for 10 mins
  10. Remove heat
  11. Leave for 10 mins

Notes

  • Original recipe
  • 15g garam masala alone flavour dominated by turmeric
