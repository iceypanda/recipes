Carrot & raisin flapjacks

  • Preheat oven to 180°C
  • Use large saucepan so do not need extra mixing bowl
  1. Heat gently to simmer
    • 150ml (137g) olive oil
    • 150g coconut sugar
    • zest of 1 orange
  2. Remove heat
  3. Stir in and mix well
    • 125g carrots grated
  4. Mix in
    • 275g oats
    • 75g flour
    • 75g raisins
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • pinch salt
  5. Line tin with butter and baking paper
  6. Spoon mixture into 20x20cm baking tin and smooth the surface with wooden spoon at flat angle
  7. Bake at 180°C for 25 mins
  8. Leave to cool for 20 mins
  9. Cut into segments
  10. Leave to cool completely

Notes

  • Original recipe: Healthy Made Simple, p34
