Somerset cream sauce

  1. Fry until soft
    • 1 onion
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 10g butter
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 tbsp plain flour
    • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 50g mushrooms
    • 1 dessert apple peeled, cored, sliced into batons
  4. Add 125ml chicken stock and bring to boil
  5. Add 150ml cider and bring to boil and cook for 2 mins
  6. Lower heat and add then simmer for 6 mins
    • ½ tbsp chopped fresh sage
    • 125ml double cream

Serving

  • Pan-fry chicken breasts
  • potatoes & veg
