Miso, mushroom & lentil stew

Mushrooms

  1. Fry for 6 mins
    • 300g mushrooms sliced

Spring onions

  1. Fry till charred
    • bunch spring onions thinly sliced

Stew

  1. Fry till starting to brown
    • 30g ginger minced
  2. Add and simmer for 15 mins
    • 150g green lentils presoaked
    • 500ml water
  3. Stir in
    • 80g miso paste
    • mushrooms

Serving

  • top with fried spring onions
  • rice
  • chilli oil
