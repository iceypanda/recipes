Fennel & chickpea pasta

  1. Heat in pan till soft
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 large onion
    • 2 small fennel bulbs
    • 1 chopped green chilli
    • 1 large broccoli stalk
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 250ml white wine
    • 1 tin chickpeas
    • 750ml vegetable stock
    • 100g orzo
  3. Add broccoli florets
  4. Cook till pasta is cooked and stew has thickened
  5. Squeeze over lemon juice and drizzle with olive oil
