Tofu Thai red curry

  1. Heat for 10 mins until tofu is browned
    • sunflower oil
    • 800g tofu
    • 8 tbsp thai red curry paste
    • 12 peeled and quartered shallots
  2. Add 800ml (2 tins) coconut milk and bring to boil then reduce heat
  3. Add and stir in
    • 2 tbsp fish sauce
    • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  4. Add
    • 2 red peppers
    • 500g halved new potatoes
    • 200g green beans
  5. Cook for 25 mins until potatoes soft (or 4 hours on high in slow cooker)
  6. Add fresh chopped basil leaves
