Tofu Thai red curry

  1. Heat for 10 mins until tofu is browned
    • sunflower oil
    • 800g tofu
    • 12 peeled and quartered shallots
  2. Add
    • 800ml (2 tins) coconut milk
    • 8 tbsp thai red curry paste
    • 2 red peppers chopped
    • 500g new potatoes quartered
    • 200g fine green beans cut into thirds
    • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Bring to boil then reduce heat and cook for 25 mins until potatoes soft
  4. Add fresh chopped basil leaves
