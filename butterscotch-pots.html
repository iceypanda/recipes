Butterscotch pots

Custard

  1. Whisk in bowl
    • 3 egg yolks
    • 25g cornflour
  2. Whisk in slowly
    • 400ml whole milk warmed

Butterscotch

  1. Bring to boil in pan without stirring
    • 40g caster sugar
    • 50g light brown sugar
    • 1 tbsp water
  2. Allow to bubble for 10 mins
  3. Slowly pour in while whisking
    • 120ml double cream warmed
  4. Remove from heat
  5. Pour butterscotch slowly into the custard while whisking

Pots

  1. Return mixture to pan
  2. Heat over medium heat stirring constantly until thickened
  3. Remove from heat
  4. Stir in
    • 20g unsalted butter
  5. Pour back into the bowl and cover surface with cling film
  6. Allow to cool to room temperature
  7. Whisk to remove lumps
  8. Spoon into ramekins
  9. Chill for 2 hours in fridge

Serving

  • Creme fraiche dollop on top
  • 6 portions

Notes

  • Warm milk and cream in microwave
  • Original recipe
