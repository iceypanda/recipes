Huevos rancheros

  1. Toss in olive oil and place under hot grill for 20 mins to scorch:
    • ½ large onion
    • 2 large red peppers
  2. Blend peppers and onions with:
    • 1 heaped tbsp chipotles in adobo
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 3 tbsp cider vinegar
    • 1 tbsp muscavado sugar
    • salt to taste (1 tbsp)
  3. Fry in oil 2 eggs in hot pan so get crispy on bottom but yolk stays runny on top
  4. Fry in same pan 2 10 cm tortillas for 15 secs on each side
  5. Sprinkle over chopped coriander

Comments

Serve with guacamole if available
