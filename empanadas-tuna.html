Tuna empanadas

Filling

  1. Fry until soft
    • 1 small onion
    • 2 red peppers roasted, chopped
    • olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic
  2. Add 1 tin chopped tomatoes and cook over medium heat for 30 mins until liquid disappeared
  3. Add, mix well, warm then leave to cool
    • 100g drained weight tuna
    • 16 green / black olives quartered
    • 2 tbsp chopped parsely
    • ¼ tsp sugar
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • black pepper

Pies

  • see Pastry pies

Serving

  • chilli jam

Notes

  • Makes about 24
  • Make pastry while tomatoes are reducing, takes 20 mins
  • Use Waitrose roasted red peppers in olive oil
  • Use small tin tuna
