Peach & mango curry

  1. Heat for 8 mins until soft
    • sunflower or vegetable oil
    • 1 large, chopped onion
    • 2 tbsp chopped ginger
  2. Add 2 tbsp curry powder and cook for 1 min
  3. Add and heat for 10 mins until fruit is soft
    • 3 chopped peaches (peeling optional)
    • 1 chopped & peeled mango
    • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
    • 200ml coconut milk
  4. Add
    • chopped fresh coriander
    • chopped pistachios

Notes

Fruit can either be under or over ripe
