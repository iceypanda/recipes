Pasta puttanesca

  1. Heat until butter melts and foaming subsides
    • 55g olive oil
    • 27g butter
  2. Add and heat for 3 mins, stirring frequently
    • 1 onion or 3 shallots
  3. Add and heat for 3 mins
    • 8 anchovies
    • 3 cloves garlic sliced
    • ½ chilli flakes
  4. Simmer for 15 mins to reduce, stirring frequently
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • ½ cup (?g) black olives chopped
    • 3 tbsp capers
  5. Remove heat and add
    • 1 tin tuna
    • salt
    • black pepper
  6. Add cooked spaghetti and mix well
  7. Mix in some pasta cooking water
  8. Top with
    • fresh parsley chopped
    • parmesan grated
