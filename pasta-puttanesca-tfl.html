Pasta puttanesca

  1. Heat until butter melts and foaming subsides
    • 42g olive oil
    • 22g butter
  2. Add and heat for 6 mins, stirring frequently
    • 1 onion or 3 shallots diced
  3. Add and heat for 3 mins
    • 8 anchovies chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic sliced
    • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  4. Simmer for 15 mins to reduce, stirring frequently
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • 75g black olives rinsed & quartered
    • 3 tbsp capers rinsed
    • ¼ tsp sugar
  5. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 2 small tins tuna drained
    • salt (if required)
    • black pepper
  6. Add cooked spaghetti and mix well
  7. Mix in some pasta cooking water if sauce too thick
  8. Top with
    • fresh parsley chopped (optional)
    • parmesan grated (optional)

Serving

  • 4 portions
