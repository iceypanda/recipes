Pineapple & coconut curry

Paste

  1. Blitz
    • 1 green chilli
    • thumb-size piece ginger
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 stalk lemongrass

Curry

  1. Fry for 10 mins till soft
    • 1 small aubergine cubed
    • 1 tsp coconut oil
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 3 palm-size amounts quorn / soya mince
    • paste
    • 30g pineapple juice
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 large tin pineapple drained
    • 3 tbsp coconut milk
  4. Add
    • juice ½ lime
  5. Garnish
    • coriander chopped

Serving

  • 2 portions
