Roasted potato, lentil & fennel

Lentils

  • Cook 230g of green / puy lentils

Dressing

  • Blitz
    • 2 green chillies
    • 50g parsely
    • 40g unsalted pistachios
    • 3 tbsp olive oil

Roasting

  1. Roast at 200°C for 50 mins
    • 600g new potatoes halved
    • 2 bulbs fennel cut into 2cm wedges
    • 2 red onions cut into 2cm wedges
    • 1 lemon cut into wedges
    • 8 cloves garlic minced
    • 75ml (5 tbsp) olive oil
  2. Mix in most of dressing and serve rest on top
