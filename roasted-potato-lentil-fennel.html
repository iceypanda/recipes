Roasted potato, lentil & fennel

Lentils

  • Cook 200g of green / puy lentils

Dressing

  • Blitz
    • 2 green chillies chopped
    • 30g parsely chopped
    • 40g unsalted pistachios
    • 50g olive oil
    • juice ½ lemon
    • ½ tsp salt

Roasting

  1. Roast at 180°C for 10 mins
    • 650g new potatoes halved
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Stir in and roast at 180°C for 30-40 mins
    • 2 bulbs fennel cut into 2cm wedges
    • 2 red onions cut into 2cm wedges
    • 1 lemon cut into wedges and deseeded
    • 8 cloves garlic minced
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Remove lemon
  4. Mix in lentils
  5. Mix in dressing

Serving

  • Garlic prawns
  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Made: 1 Oct 2022
  • Potatoes added first as take longer and do not burn
  • Do not break up onions or fennel else they'll burn
  • Original recipe
