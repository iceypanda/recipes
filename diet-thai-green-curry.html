Thai green curry

Paste

  1. Blitz
    • ½ red onion
    • handful coriander
    • 2 sticks lemongrass
    • thumb piece ginger
    • 2 lime leaves
    • 1 red chilli
    • 1 clove garlic
    • zest ½ lime
    • 1 tsp olive oil

Curry

  1. Fry for 5 mins
    • 1 tsp coconut oil
    • paste
  2. Add and cook for 15 mins
    • 4 tbsp coconut milk
    • 2 portions tofu/chicken
    • splash water
  3. Add
    • 100g bulgar wheat cooked
    • juice ½ lime
    • pinch white pepper
  4. Garnish
    • pinch desiccated coconut

Serving

  • 2 portions
