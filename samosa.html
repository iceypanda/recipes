Vegetable samosas

Veg

  1. Boil for 2 mins
    • 100g carrots in 1cm cubes
  2. Add and boil for 10 mins until carrots are tender
    • 340g potatoes in 1cm cubes
  3. Add for 30 seconds
    • 100g peas
  4. Drain with cold water to cool

Filling

  1. Heat until seeds crackle for 30 seconds
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  2. Gently heat until soft
    • 1 red onion
  3. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 1 green chilli finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 5g ginger grated
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
    • ¼ tsp garam masala
    • ¼ tsp ground cumin
    • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
    • ¼ tsp amchur
    • ½ tsp salt
  5. Add
    • 1 tbsp lime juice
    • 10g coriander leaves chopped
  6. Add potatoes, carrot and peas then remove from heat
  7. Allow mixture to cool and chill in fridge

Notes

  • Reduced: 1 tsp cinnamon, ½ tsp garam masala, ¼ tsp chilli powder
  • Air fried spring rolls not good, texture chewy
