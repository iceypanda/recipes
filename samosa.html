Vegetable samosa

Veg

  1. Boil for 4 mins until tender
    • 340g potatoes in 1cm cubes
    • 100g carrots in 1cm cubes
  2. Add for 20 seconds
    • 100g peas
  3. Drain with cold water to cool

Filling

  1. Heat until seeds crackle for 30 seconds
    • vegetable oil
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  2. Gently heat until soft
    • 1 red onion
  3. Add and cook for 3 mins
    • 2 green chillis finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
    • 5g ginger grated
  4. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp garam masala
    • ¼ tsp ground cumin
    • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
    • ¼ tsp amchur
    • ¼ tsp chilli powder
    • ½ tsp salt
  5. Add
    • 20ml lime juice
    • 10g coriander leaves chopped
  6. Add potatoes, carrot and peas then remove from heat
  7. Allow mixture to cool and chill in fridge

Samosas

  1. Mix to make glue for sealing wraps
    • 2 tbsp plain flour
    • 1-2 tbsp water
  2. Make samosas
  3. Brush with vegetable oil and air fry for 5 mins?

Serving

  • Chicken tikka
  • Raita
