Chorizo & bacon risotto

  1. Cook until fat rendered
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 100g smoked bacon diced
  2. Divide large bacon chunks and remove any pieces of pure fat
  3. Cook for 10 mins
    • 200g chorizo sliced
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
  4. Mix in
    • 250g arborio rice
  5. Add one cup at a time letting rice absorb each cup cook before adding more
    • ~900ml chicken stock (2 cubes, 1 salted, 1 unsalted)
  6. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 175g peas
  7. Add
    • juice ½ lemon
    • black pepper

Serving

  • 4 portions
