Chorizo & bacon risotto

  1. Cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 200g chorizo sliced
    • 100g smoked bacon diced
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic grated
  2. Mix in
    • 250g arborio rice
  3. Add one cup at a time letting rice absorb each cup cook before adding more
    • 1L chicken stock
  4. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 200g peas

Serving

  • 4 portions
