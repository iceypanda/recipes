Mushrooms

Notes

  • 250g = 15 medium mushrooms
  • ~17g per mushroom
  • Mushrooms halve in size after cooking
  • Cannot be overcooked

Pan-roasted mushrooms

  • Try air fry
  • Original recipe: The Food Lab, p441
  1. Heat until shimmery
    • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  2. Heat until lose liquid and then turn golden brown, about 18 mins
    • 150g mushrooms quartered
  3. Add and heat for 1-2 mins
    • 1 shallot chopped
    • 1 clove garlic
    • ½ tsp fresh thyme leaves
  4. Remove heat and mix with
    • ½ tsp soy sauce
    • ¼ tsp lemon juice
    • 1 tsp butter
  5. Season to taste

Chilli & garlic mushrooms

  1. Fry
    • olive oil
    • 200g mushrooms sliced
    • 1 shallot diced
    • 2 cloves garlic sliced
    • ½ red chilli chopped
  2. To serve add
    • ½ tbsp lemon juice
    • seasoning
