Mushroom & black-eyed bean curry

  1. Fry on low heat for 5 mins
    • vegetable oil
    • 2 onions
  2. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 large knob ginger
  3. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp mild chilli powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric
  4. Add, bring to boil then simmer for 20 mins
    • 300g chestnut mushrooms
    • 2 tins black-eyed beans drained and rinsed
    • 1 tin coconut milk
    • 1 tsp cornflour in 2 tsp cold water
    • ¾ tsp salt
  5. Add
    • 100g spinach

Serving

  • Rice, bread, mango chutney
