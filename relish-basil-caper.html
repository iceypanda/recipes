Basil & caper relish

  1. Mix in bowl
    • ½ cup fresh basil chopped
    • 2 spring onions sliced
    • 1 shallot diced
    • 1 red chilli deseeded, chopped
    • 3 anchovies finely chopped
    • 2 tbsp capers rinsed, drained, chopped
    • 2 tbsp kalamata olives rinsed, drained, chopped
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • 1 tsp honey
  2. Add in thin stream, whisking constantly
    • 70g olive oil
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste

Serving

  • Pan-fried cod or salmon

Notes

  • 30 mins
  • Original recipe: The Food Lab, p381
