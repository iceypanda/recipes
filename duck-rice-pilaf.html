Duck & rice pilaf

    Duck

  1. Fry 2 banana shallots halved for 5 mins
  2. Separately sear 400g duck breast

    3. Pilaf

  3. Add to slow cooker
    • 250g basmati and wild rice
    • 10g butter
    • 250g mini carrots
    • 150g mushrooms chopped
    • juice of 1 lemon
    • 1 sprig rosemary chopped
    • 1 tsp fresh sage chopped
    • 2 tsp dried parsely
    • 1 tsp dried thyme
    • 650ml stock
  4. Add shallots
  5. Add duck skin side up
  6. Cook for 4 hours in slow cooker on high
  7. If rice is still hard and more water and cook for 30 mins more

Notes

  • see duck
  • herbs reduced
  • try cooking on low so rice doesn't overcook
  • carrots may need precooking to be soft
  • Original recipe
