Thai green curry

  1. Heat for 10 mins until meat is browned
    • sunflower oil
    • 400g chicken
    • 6 shallots chopped
  2. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 400ml coconut milk
    • 1 red / yellow pepper chopped
    • 1 green chilli chopped
    • 4 tbsp thai green curry paste (use whole small pot)
    • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 150g sugar snap peas cut diagonally in half
    • 1 tsp cornflour in 2 tsp cold water
  4. Add and simmer for 2 mins
    • 150g peas defrosted

Serving

  • Spring rolls

Notes

  • Made: 10 Dec 2022
  • Use good coconut milk (Blue Dragon), not essential kind
