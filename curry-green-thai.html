Thai green curry

Chicken

  1. Heat until almost cooked
    • sunflower oil
    • 400g chicken
  2. Set aside and cover

Curry

  1. Heat gently for 5 mins
    • sunflower oil
    • 6 shallots chopped
  2. Add and heat for 5 mins
    • 1 yellow pepper chopped
    • 1 green chilli chopped
    • splash of water
  3. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 400ml coconut milk
    • 4 tbsp thai green curry paste (use whole small pot)
    • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins
    • 150g sugar snap peas cut diagonally in half
    • chicken
    • 1 tsp cornflour in 2 tsp cold water (mix in paste bottle)
    • ¼ tsp fish sauce
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes if no heat
  5. Add and simmer for 2 mins
    • 150g peas defrosted
  6. Cool for 10 mins for chicken to rest

Serving

  • Spring rolls
  • 4 portions

Notes

  • Made: 9 Jul 2023, 22 Apr 2023, 10 Dec 2022
  • Use good coconut milk (Blue Dragon), not essential kind
