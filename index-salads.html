Salads

Notes

  • Red onion: soak at 60°C for 2 mins to reduce bite
  • Green veg: blanch then cool in ice water
  • Fruits: raw, dried, pan-roasted
  • Citrus fruits: cut into supremes to remove bitter pith

Greens

  1. Crisp lettuces: iceberg, cos, little gem, green leaf, red leaf
  2. Peppery greens: rocket, watercress
  3. Mild greens: spinach, lamb's lettuce
  4. Bitter greens: endive, radicchio
