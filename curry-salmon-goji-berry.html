Salmon & goji berry curry

Bulgar

  1. Cook in microwave
    • 75g bulgar wheat

Curry

  1. Fry until popping
    • 2 tsp olive oil
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds
    • ½ tsp coriander seeds
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 tsp ginger grated
    • ½ tsp turmeric
    • ½ tsp curry powder
  3. Add for 5 mins
    • 2 salmon fillets skin removed & in chunks
    • 100ml water
    • 1 tbsp tomato puree
    • 2 tsp (18g) goji berries
    • ½ red chilli chopped
  4. Add
    • bulgur cooked
    • squeeze lemon juice
  5. Serve
    • coriander chopped
    • rocket

Serving

  • 2 portions
