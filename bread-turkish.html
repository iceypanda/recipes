Turkish bread - pide

Yeast

  1. Whisk in jug and leave for 5 mins until frothy
    • 144ml (146g) milk microwave to 43°C
    • 7g sachet of yeast
    • 1 tsp caster sugar

Bread

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 250g strong bread flour
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp caster sugar
    • ¾ tsp salt
  2. Mix in yeast mixture
  3. Cover bowl with tea towel and leave for 1 hour
  4. Tip dough onto floured surface and shape in flat circle (careful not to knock air out)
  5. Place dough on lined baking tray
  6. Cover with tea towel and leave for 45 mins
  7. Preheat oven to 200°C
  8. Make criss-cross shapes on top of dough
  9. Brush top of bread with olive oil
  10. Sprinkle over centre of bread and push in
    • white & black sesame seeds (optional)
    • herbs & cheese (optional)
  11. Bake at 200°C for 12 mins until golden brown

Serving

  • Cut into squares not slices by pressing down with chef's knife not sawing with bread knife

Notes

  • Can use nigella seeds if no black sesame seeds
