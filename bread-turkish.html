Turkish bread

Yeast

  1. Put in jug and leave for 10 mins until frothy
    • 70ml water 43°C
    • 70ml (72g) milk 43°C
    • 7g sachet of yeast
    • 2 tsp golden caster sugar

Bread

  1. Mix in bowl
    • 250g strong bread flour
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Mix in yeast mixture
  3. Cover bowl with tea towel and leave for 1 hour
  4. Tip dough onto floured surface and shape in flat circle
  5. Place dough on lined baking tray
  6. Cover with tea towel and leave for 45 mins
  7. Preheat oven to 240°C
  8. Make criss-cross shapes on top of dough
  9. Brush top of bread with olive oil
  10. Sprinkle over centre of bread and push in
    • white & black sesame seeds
  11. Bake at 240°C for 8 mins
  12. Turn down to 200°C for 5 mins until golden brown

Serving

  • Cut into squares not slices

Notes

  • Can use nigella seeds if no black sesame seeds
  • Original recipe
