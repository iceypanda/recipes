Grains

Buckwheat

  • Ratio 2.3 water to buckwheat
    • Lunch: 70g buckwheat, 160g water
  • Presoak for 2-4 hours then rinse well

Hob

  1. Bring to boil then simmer for 8-10 mins
  2. Leave in pan till all water absorbed

Microwave

  1. Add 180g boiling water
  2. Heat for 30-60 secs > rest > stir > repeat
  3. Rinse well

Bulgar wheat

  • Lunch: 70g
  • Presoak to reduce cooking time

Hob

  1. Bring to boil then simmer for 10 mins
  2. Leave in pan for 10 mins then drain

Microwave

  1. Heat for 30-60 secs > rest > stir > repeat
  2. Drain

Couscous

  • Ratio 1.4 water to couscous
    • 90g couscous, 127g water
    • 80g couscous, 112g water
  • Don't use a stock cube
  1. Add boiling water
  2. Cover and leave for 5 mins

Fregola

  1. Soak 90g per portion in water for 2 hours
  2. Microwave until boiling
  3. Let stand for 5 mins then drain

Pearl barley

  1. Use 6:1 ratio of boiling water:barley
  2. Does not need presoaking
  3. Add 1½ tsp miso
  4. Cook in slow cooker for 2 hours on high
