Set custard

Notes

  • Custard cooked when 80°C in centre
  • Cook at 150°C
  • Gentler cook takes longer but easier to get correct bake time
  • Cover with foil and will cook quicker
  • Cook in water bath
  • Cool for 2 hours without cover
  • Cover and chill in fridge
  • Move ramekins with tongs

Examples

  • Cooked at 150°C 45 mins to 80°C then cooked 3 more mins to 84°C. Set well and didn't curdle.

Recipes

  • Sugar custards
  • Creme caramel
  • Ancho & orange flan
