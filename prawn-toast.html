Prawn Toast

  1. Mix up with food processor
    • 250g of washed prawns
    • 1 egg white
    • 1 clove chopped garlic
    • 1 tbsp cornflour
    • good pinch of salt
  2. Remove crust from 6 slices of bread
  3. Spread paste on bread and press down
  4. Cover with sesame seeds
  5. Fry on prawn side
  6. Fry on bread side

Notes

Wash the prawns thoroughly

Fry toast 3 slices at a time

Transfer toast to frying pan on spatula with prawn side facing down then slide off

More oil and sesame seeds for more flavour

Careful not to burn bread side
