Fruit scones

Milk

  1. Warm in microwave
    • 175ml (180g) milk
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • squeeze lemon / lime juice

Scones

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C
  2. Mix in bowl
    • 350g self-raising flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • ½ tsp (small) salt
  3. Add to flour
    • 85g unsalted butter chilled and cubed
  4. Blitz flour and butter in portions to crumbs, transferring between two bowls
  5. Mix in
    • 1¼ tsp caster sugar
    • 80g (try 90g) sultanas / raisins separated
  6. Mix in the milk
  7. Fold dough over few times on work surface
  8. Flatten to 4cm height
  9. Cut out circles with 4-7cm cutter dipped in flour
  10. Brush tops with egg yolk
  11. Cook on baking tray for 12-13 mins
  12. Once cool enough store scones in container so do not dry out

Serving

  • Unsweetened cream
  • Jam

Notes

  • Don't want milk too warm or butter will soften and become evenly distributed
  • Fold don't knead
