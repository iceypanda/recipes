Scones

Milk

  1. Warm in microwave
    • 175ml milk
  2. Mix in
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • squeeze lemon juice

Scones

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C
  2. Mix in bowl
    • 350g self-raising flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • ¼ tsp salt
  3. Rub into the flour until fine crumbs
    • 85g salted butter chilled and cubed
  4. Mix in
    • 1 tsp caster sugar
    • 60-80g sultanas / raisins / glace cherries (optional)
  5. Mix in the milk
  6. Fold dough over few times on work surface
  7. Flatten to 4cm height
  8. Cut out 8 circles with 5cm cutter dipped in flour
  9. Brush tops with beaten egg
  10. Cook on baking tray for 12-13 mins

Serving

  • Cream and jam
