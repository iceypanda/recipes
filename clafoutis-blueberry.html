Blueberry & coriander clafoutis

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C
  2. Add to bowl
    • 70g plain flour
    • ½ tsp salt
  3. Whisk in
    • 165ml (170g) whole milk
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Whisk in one at a time
    • 2 eggs
  5. Whisk in
    • 40g caster sugar
  6. Whisk in
    • 165ml (170g) whole milk
  7. Butter oven dish, lightly dust with flour then scatter
    • 200g blueberries
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds coarsely ground
  8. Pour over batter
  9. Dot with
    • 15g unsalted butter
  10. Bake for 25 mins
  11. After cooling, dust with
    • 1 tbsp icing sugar
    • 1 tsp cocoa

Serving

  • 6 portions

Notes

  • not included are extra 1 tsp coriander seeds scattered after cooking
