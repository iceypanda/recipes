Blueberry & coriander clafoutis

Batter

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Add to bowl
    • 70g plain flour
    • ½ tsp salt
  3. Whisk in
    • 180ml (185g) milk
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Whisk in one at a time
    • 2 eggs
  5. Whisk in
    • 40g caster sugar
    • 150ml single cream

Clafoutis

  1. Butter oven dish
  2. Scatter
    • 300g blueberries / blackberries / raspberries
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds coarsely ground (optional)
  3. Pour over batter
  4. Bake for 25 mins
  5. After cooling, dust with
    • 1 tbsp icing sugar
    • 1 tsp cocoa (optional)

Serving

  • 6 portions

Notes

  • can also use cherries
  • can use more or less fruit
  • not included are extra 1 tsp coriander seeds scattered after cooking
  • could use double cream or 300ml single cream instead of milk
  • changes: single cream not all milk, 180°C not 220°C, more fruit, not 2 tsp vanilla, not 2 tsp coriander seeds
