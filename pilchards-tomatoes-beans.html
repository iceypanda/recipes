Pilchards, tomatoes & green beans

Salsa

  1. Mix until sugar dissolved
    • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
    • 1 clove garlic minced
    • ¼ tsp caster sugar
  2. Mix in
    • 1½ tbsp olive oil
    • 4 spring onions finely chopped
    • 30g coriander finely chopped

Pilchards

  1. Separate fish and liquid
    • 400g tin Glenryck pilchards in tomato sauce

Spices

  1. Roast for 2-3 mins in pan on medium heat
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  2. Lightly grind with pestle and mortar

Sauce

  1. Heat for 2 mins on medium-high
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 50g rose harissa
    • 1 clove garlic minced
  2. Add and cook for 15 mins until thickened
    • reserved tomato sauce
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • toasted spices
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ¼ tsp sugar
    • black pepper
  3. Add and cook for 10 mins on medium until beans cooked and sauce thickened again
    • 150ml water boiled
    • 200g green beans
    • pilchards

Serving

  • Bread
  • Pasta
  • Polenta
