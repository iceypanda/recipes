Polenta, cheese & tomato sauce

Tomato sauce

  1. Heat 2 tins chopped tomatoes for 15 mins
  2. Add and heat for 1 min
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • salt and pepper
  3. Add any fresh chopped herbs, e.g. basil, parsely

Polenta

  1. Get 800ml water boiling in saucepan
  2. Add
    • 200g polenta
    • 1 tsp salt
  3. Reduce to low heat and cook for 4 mins, stirring regularly with wooden spoon
  4. Pour onto oiled baking tray, spread evenly and leave to cool for 5 mins
  5. Flatten while still soft and leave to cool for 15 mins
  6. Cut into 5cm squares
  7. Place squares onto oiled oven-dish in slightly overlapping arrangement
  8. Sprinkle tomato sauce over the top
  9. Add
    • 250g ricotta
    • 25g parmesan
  10. Bake for 30 mins at 200°C until golden

Notes

Salt reduced from 1 tbsp for polenta

Polenta reduced from 225g

Water reduced from 1.5l

Ricotta reduced from 350g
