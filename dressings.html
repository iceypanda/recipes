Dressings

Tahini dressing

Dressing for meals with cumin / turmeric

  • 100g natural yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp smooth tahini
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • salt

Balsamic dressing

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp dijon / wholegrain mustard

Lime dressing

  • 2½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic crushed
  • salt
