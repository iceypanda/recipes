Dressings

Tahini dressing

  • Dressing for meals with cumin / turmeric
    • 100g natural yoghurt
    • 2 tbsp smooth tahini
    • juice of 1 lemon
    • salt

Balsamic dressing

  • Shake in bottle
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • 1 tsp Dijon / English / wholegrain mustard
    • ½ clove garlic grated / crushed
    • ½ tsp maple syrup / honey
    • ¼ tsp salt

Lime dressing

  • Shake in bottle
    • 2½ tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp lime juice
    • 1 clove garlic crushed
    • salt
