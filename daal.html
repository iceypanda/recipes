Daal

  1. Cook for 30 mins stirring regularly:
    • 1 onion
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 1 tbsp chopped ginger
    • 1 tsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp tumeric
    • 500g red lentils
    • bunch chopped coriander
    • 1.25l veg stock
  2. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice to taste
