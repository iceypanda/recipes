Bombay mix

Chickpeas

  • Drain and place on kitchen roll
    • 1 tin chickpeas
  • Leave to dry overnight

Bombay mix

  1. Mix in bowl
    • chickpeas dried
    • ½ tsp cumin seeds
    • ½ tsp fennel seeds
    • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  2. Bake on lined tray at 160°C for 30 mins
  3. Mix in
    • 2 tsp (14g) peanuts
    • handful (40g) raisins
    • 1 tsp pine nuts
    • 1 tsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • ½ tsp chilli powder
  4. Bake at 160°C for 15 mins
  5. Leave to cool
