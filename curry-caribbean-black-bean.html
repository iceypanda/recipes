Caribbean black bean curry

Roasted peppers

  • Roast 2 peppers

Curry

  1. Cook for 10 mins
    • vegetable oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 20g ginger grated
    • ¼ tsp allspice
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 bay leaf
    • ½ ground cinnamon
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds lightly ground
    • 1 tsp dried thyme
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins until reduced
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tin black beans with liquid
    • 2 red peppers roasted
    • 1½ tbsp soy sauce
    • 1½ tbsp maple syrup
  5. Season with
    • juice from ½ lime

Serving

  • rice

Notes

  • added more maple & soy
  • replaced cinnamon stick with ground
  • Original recipe
