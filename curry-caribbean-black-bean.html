Caribbean black bean curry

  1. Cook for 10 mins
    • vegetable oil
    • 1 onion
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 20g ginger grated
  3. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp maple syrup
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds
    • 1 tsp dried thyme
    • ¼ tsp allspice
  4. Add and simmer for 10 mins until reduced
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tin black beans
    • 150g roasted red peppers
  5. Season with
    • salt
    • lime juice

Serving

  • rice
