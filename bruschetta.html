Bruschetta

Tomatoes

  1. Mix and leave to chill for 1 hour
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp balsamic or white wine vinegar
    • fresh basil leaves chopped
    • 200g cherry tomatoes quartered
    • black pepper

Bread

  1. Heat in oven at 160°C until bread toasted
    • ciabatta or sourdough bread cut into slices

Assemble

  1. Rub toasted bread with cut clove garlic and brush one side with olive oil
  2. Cover oiled side with tomatoes

Notes

  • Oiling bread prevents it getting soggy
  • Could roast tomatoes or warm in oven with bread before serving
