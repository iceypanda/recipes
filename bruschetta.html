Bruschetta

Tomatoes

  1. Mix and leave to chill for 1 hour
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp balsamic or white wine vinegar
    • fresh basil leaves chopped
    • 300g cherry tomatoes quartered
    • cracked black pepper

Bread

  1. Heat in oven at 160°C until bread toasted, about 12 mins
    • ciabatta or sourdough bread cut into slices
    • tomatoes

Assemble

  1. Rub toasted bread with cut garlic clove
  2. Cover with tomatoes
  3. Grate over parmesan or mozzarella

Notes

  • 70g tomatoes per slice of bread, 3 slices per person
  • Do not leave in sun to ripen further, they split and go mushy
  • Can chop garlic and add to tomatoes instead of rubbing bread
