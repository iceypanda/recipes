Bruschetta

Tomatoes

  1. Mix and leave to chill for 1 hour
    • 300g cherry tomatoes quartered
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    • 1 glove garlic grated
    • fresh basil leaves chopped (optional)
    • 50g black olives quarter sliced (optional)

Cook

  1. Heat in oven at 160°C until bread toasted, about 12 mins
    • ciabatta / sourdough cut into slices
    • tomatoes

Assemble

  1. Cover with tomatoes with juice
  2. Extra dash (optional)
    • olive oil
    • balsamic vinegar
  3. Top with
    • parmesan / mozzarella grated
  4. Sprinkle
    • black pepper cracked

Notes

  • 70g tomatoes per slice of bread, 3 slices per person
  • Can chop garlic and add to tomatoes instead of rubbing bread
