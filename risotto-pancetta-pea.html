Pancetta & pea risotto

  1. Fry until cooked
    • 10g butter
    • 10g olive oil
    • 65g pancetta cubes
  2. Add and fry for 10 mins
    • 1 onion chopped
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 350g risotto rice
  4. Add and cook until almost reduced
    • 100ml white wine
  5. Add 1 cup at a time until all absorbed, stirring constantly
    • 1.2l chicken stock
  6. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 75g peas defrosted
  7. Add to taste
    • lemon juice
    • black pepper
  8. To serve sprinkle with
    • parsely chopped
    • parmesan grated

Serving

  • Roasted chicken wrapped in parma ham with pesto, sliced
