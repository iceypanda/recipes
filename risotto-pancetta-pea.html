Pancetta & pea risotto

  1. Fry till cooked
    • 10g butter
    • 10g olive oil
    • 65g pancetta cubes
  2. Remove pancetta pieces of pure fat
  3. Add and fry for 10 mins
    • 1 onion chopped
  4. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 2 cloves garlic minced
    • 300g risotto rice
  5. Add and cook till almost reduced
    • 100ml white wine
  6. Add 1 cup at a time till all absorbed, stirring constantly
    • 1200ml chicken stock (1 salted cube + 1 unsalted)
  7. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 75g peas defrosted
  8. Add to taste
    • lemon juice
    • black pepper
  9. To serve sprinkle with
    • parsely chopped
    • parmesan grated

Serving

  • Roasted chicken wrapped in parma ham with pesto
  • 4 portions: 75g rice = 1 portion

Notes

  • Not too much salt
  • Made:6 May 2023, stodgy
