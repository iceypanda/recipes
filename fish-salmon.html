Salmon

Recipes

  • Salmon goujons
  • Hot smoked salmon, chickpeas, peppers & maple syrup

Notes

  • Salmon expels white albumin when overcooking, remove heat if see any white gunk

Baked

  1. Air fry on bake at 160°C for 15 mins skin side up (same for oven)
  2. Take out and rest for 2 mins
  3. Remove skin and grey flesh before serving

Pan-fried

  • See Food lab p374
  1. Dry fillets well
  2. Preheat oil on high heat
  3. Place fillets skin side down then immediately turn heat low
  4. Press fillet down with fish spatula for first 2 mins
  5. Cook slow and steady to allow fat in skin to render
  6. Flip only when skin easily detaches from pan

Poached

  1. Bring to simmer
    • 500ml stock
    • dill chopped
    • 1 bay leaf
    • pepper
  2. Cook salmon fillets for 5-6 mins (check cooked, might need longer)
  3. Can keep warm in foil till ready to serve
