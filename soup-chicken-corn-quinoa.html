Chicken, potato, corn, & quinoa soup

Spices

  1. Pound in pestle and mortar until fine
    • 2 whole cloves
    • 1 tsp coriander seeds
    • ¼ tsp cumin seeds
    • black pepper

Soup

  1. In large pot heat for 2 mins
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 skinless, boneless chicken thighs finely sliced
  2. Add spices and heat until chicken cooked
  3. Add and cook for 15 mins stirring regularly
    • 1 large red onion chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 red chilli sliced
    • 2 mixed-colour peppers 2cm diced
    • 500g potatoes 2cm diced with skin
    • 500g sweet potatoes 2cm diced with skin
  4. Add and simmer for 30 mins
    • 1500ml stock (2 cubes salt chicken + 1 cube veg no salt)
    • 60g red lentils
  5. Add and cook for 15 mins
    • 1 tin (160g) sweetcorn
    • 150g quinoa
    • 1 lime sliced thickly
  6. Remove from heat and add
    • 80g feta (half pack)
    • fresh coriander chopped
