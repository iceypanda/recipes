Buckwheat, tofu & kale stew

    Liquid

  1. Mix
    • 350 ml vegetable stock
    • 120 ml white wine

    2. Stew

  2. Heat for 5 mins until everything dry
    • ¼ cup vegetable oil
    • 1 chopped onion
    • 1 cubed potato
    • 8 cloves garlic
    • ½ cup buckwheat
  3. Add 4 cups chopped kale with stems removed and heat for 5 mins
  4. Add 250g cubed tofu and heat for 5 mins until browned
  5. Add half the liquid
  6. Simmer for 20 mins, adding more liquid as necessary until buckwheat tender
  7. Add and heat for 5 mins until mixture is saucy
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp harissa
