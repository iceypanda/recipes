Stuffing

Notes

  • Use less water than recommended: 0.75
  • Start by adding half the water and then increase gradually till desired consistency
  • 2.35 water : 1 stuffing
  • Divide stuffing before rolling into balls: eg half > half > thirds
  • Oil spot on tray then put ball on top
  • Roll into balls then flatten
  • 200°C for 25 mins

Amounts

  • 15g per portion
  • 60g + 140ml water = 4 balls
  • 85g + 200ml water = 6 balls
  • 170g = 12 balls
