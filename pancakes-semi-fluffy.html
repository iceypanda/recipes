Semi-fluffy pancakes

Portions

  • 2 pancakes
    • 55g self-raising flour
    • 83g milk
    • 1 large egg
  • 4 pancakes
    • 110g self-raising flour
    • 165g milk
    • 2 large eggs

Chia-seed pancakes

  • Soak for 1-2 hours before making pancakes
    • ½-1 tbsp chia seeds
    • 80g milk

Cooking

  1. Melt butter and spread over pan base with silicon spatula
  2. Pour in mix
  3. When cooked lift and flip just before top is cooked in middle
  4. Don’t move until cooked — only turn once
  5. Serve first cooked side up

Serving

  • Maple, pistachio & blueberry pancakes
  • Compote

Notes

  • Melt knob of butter in pan before cooking each pancake
  • Original recipe
