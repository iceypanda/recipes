Toffee sauce

    1. Heat in pan over medium heat stirring as little as possible till sugar dissolved
    • 150ml double cream
    • 142g dark brown sugar
    • 68g unsalted butter
  2. Increase heat to medium high
  3. Increase stirring as sauce darkens to prevent burning
  4. Remove from heat when dark amber
  5. Add
    • ½ tsp vanilla extract
