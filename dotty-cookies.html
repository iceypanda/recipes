Dotty cookies

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Line baking trays with baking paper
  3. Combine in a bowl
    • 100g melted butter
    • ½ cup caster sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 lightly beaten egg
  4. Add 1½ cups of self-raising flour and mix until firm
  5. Put mixture in fridge to cool (optional)
  6. Roll into balls, place on baking tray and press down slightly
  7. Place 4 smarties on each
  8. Bake for 10-12 mins
  9. Leave to cool for 5 mins then place on cooling rack

Comments

Careful not to overbake
