Ancho & orange set custard

  • Preheat oven to 150°C

Chilli

  1. Soak in hot water until soft
    • 20g Ancho chilli de-stemmed
  2. Squeeze out liquid

Caramel

  1. Make caramel with
    • 120g white caster sugar
  2. Pour caramel into baking tray to set

Custard

  1. Blend together
    • 400g condensed milk
    • 150ml (147g) double cream
    • 534ml (550g) whole milk
    • 4 medium eggs
    • zest of 1 orange
    • 1½ tsp vanilla extract
    • ancho chlli softened and chopped
  2. Strain into baking tray
  3. Arrange water bath with tray covered up to halfway
  4. Bake for 40-50 mins at 150°C
  5. Remove from water bath and leave to cool
  6. Chill for 3 hours or overnight in fridge

Serving

  1. Go round custard to bottom of tray with sharp knife to help release
  2. Place serving plate on top of tray
  3. Turnover and move quickly to side to release, might take >1 tries
  4. Cut into 6 portions
