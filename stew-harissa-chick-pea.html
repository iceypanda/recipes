Harissa chickpea stew

  1. Heat for 15-20 mins until soft
    • 3 tbsp oil
    • 2 large onions
    • 2 red peppers finely chopped
    • 1 yellow / orange pepper finely chopped
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 1½ tbsp tomato puree
    • 1½ tsp paprika
    • 1 tsp bashed cumin seeds
    • ¼ tsp salt
  3. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 2 tomatoes
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 2 sun-dried tomatoes chopped (optional)
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 1 tin chickpeas with liquid from tin
    • 2+ tbsp rose harissa

Serving

  • 4 portions
  • flatbread
  • polenta chips
  • potato wedges / cubes

Notes

  • bulked up with extra pepper and fresh tomatoes so 4 portions
  • Belazu rose harissa recommended
