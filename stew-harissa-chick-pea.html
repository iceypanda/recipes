Harissa chick pea stew

  1. Heat for 15-20 mins until soft:
    • 3 tbsp oil
    • 2 onions
    • 2 finely chopped red peppers
  2. Add and cook for 5 mins:
    • 3 cloves of garlic
    • 1½ tbsp tomato puree
    • 2½ tbsp rose harissa
    • 1½ tsp paprika
    • 1 tsp bashed cumin seeds
    • salt
  3. Add and cook for 10 mins:
    • 1 tin cherry tomatoes
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins:
    • 1 tin chick peas with liquid from tin
  5. Serve with 200g rainbow chard:
    1. Fry the stems in hot oil for 2 mins stirring once until charred
    2. Add the leaves until they blister for two mins then turn and fry for another 2 mins
    3. ½ tbsp lemon juice
  6. Serve with flatbread
