Rice and peas

  1. Cook in pan for 25 mins until rice soft
    • 125g basmati rice
    • 1 red onion
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • ½ tsp thyme
    • ½ salt-free vegetable stock cube in 400ml water
  2. Stir in and heat through
    • tin of black-eyed beans

Notes

  • water reduced from 600ml
  • thyme reduced from 2 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
