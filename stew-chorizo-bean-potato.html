Chorizo, bean & potato stew

Potatoes

  1. Boil till cooked
    • 400g new potatoes in bite-size pieces

Stew

  1. Heat till chorizo oozes its oil
    • olive oil
    • 200g chorizo chopped
  2. Add and cook till soft
    • 1 onion
    • 1 red pepper chopped
    • 2 sticks celery chopped (optional)
  3. Add and cook for 1 min
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 2 tsp smoked paprika (up from 1½ tsp)
    • ¼ tsp cumin (try)
    • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  4. Add and cook for 20 mins
    • potatoes
    • 150g green beans chopped
    • 1 red chilli chopped (optional)
    • 1 tin tomatoes
    • 1 tin cannellini beans
    • 200ml passata
    • ½ salted stock cube in 100ml water
    • 2 sun-dried tomatoes chopped (optional)
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 tsp light brown sugar
    • ¼ tsp salt

Serving

  • 6 portions
  • Chopped parsely
  • Buckwheat / bulgar wheat / pearl barley / couscous / rice

Notes

  • Lemon juice not needed, acid in tomatoes
