  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Whisk 2 egg whites
  3. Whisk in 120g golden caster sugar gradually until meringue thick and glossy
  4. Tip in and fold in with metal spoon
    • 120g hazelnuts / pecans
    • 150g dark chocolate (at least 70% cocoa solids)
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Cover tray with baking paper and heap teaspoons of meringue
  6. Put tray in oven
  7. Turn oven off and wait for 3 hours until oven is cold
