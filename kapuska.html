Kapuska

Peppers

  1. Roast in oven
    • 3 red peppers
  2. Leave to cool
  3. Remove skins
  4. Blitz with
    • 50ml water

Mince

  1. Fry till brown
    • 500g mince beef

Kapuska

  1. Fry till soft
    • 2 onions chopped
    • 2 carrots diced
    • olive oil
  2. Add and cook for 2 mins
    • 4 cloves garlic grated
  3. Add and cook for 40 mins
    • 2 tins tomatoes
    • ¾ cabbage shredded
    • 150g olives sliced
    • pepper paste
    • mince
    • 150ml water
    • 1 salted stock cube
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1½ tsp fennel seeds
    • 1 tsp chilli flakes
    • 1 tsp majoram
    • ½ tsp fish sauce
  4. Add and cook for 10 mins
    • 150g dried apricots chopped
  5. Add
    • black pepper
    • ½ tsp soy sauce

Serving

  • 7 portions
