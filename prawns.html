Prawns

Notes

  • Count out prawns from 225g bag to 3 portions (75g per portion)
  • Precooked prawns are overcooked prawns
  • Fresh prawns are already defrosted so better to buy frozen
  • Headless shrimp better because head contains enzymes that break tissues down

Precooked prawns

  1. Put boiled water in pan
  2. Add cold prawns
  3. Heat to 70°C
  4. Drain

Brining

  1. Brine and alkalise prawns for 15-30 mins before cooking
    • 225g prawns
    • 100g water cold
    • ½ tsp baking soda
    • ½ tsp salt
  2. Rinse well in cold water

Air fry

  1. Pat dry
  2. Combine with
    • oil
    • spices
  3. 180°C for 4 mins

Recipes

  • Garlic prawns
  • Red prawn spaghetti
  • Sea bass / prawns & tomato sauce
